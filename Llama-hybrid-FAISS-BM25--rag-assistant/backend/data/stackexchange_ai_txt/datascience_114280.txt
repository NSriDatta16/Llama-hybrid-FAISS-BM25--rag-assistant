You are looking for a generative models. Generative models are typically trained to create "similar" data without plainly copying it. These days, generative Models create astonishing results, including art, music, text. But back to your case: There are multiple variants. You could look into Variational Autoencoders (VAEs) or Generative Adversarial Networks (GANs). Depending on your data, ctGAN might be a good start. It is designed to work with tabular data which you seem to have.
