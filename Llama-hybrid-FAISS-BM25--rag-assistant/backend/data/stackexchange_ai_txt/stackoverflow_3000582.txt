So, you have a field in your main report and you want to pass it to your subreport? Not a problem. Right-click on your subreport. Go to the Change Subreport Links submenu. Drag your field from the left menu to the right. Unselect the Select data in subreport based on field: option. Go into your subreport. Your field will appear in there as a parameter field . So many ways to describe and answer a question. Does this help?
