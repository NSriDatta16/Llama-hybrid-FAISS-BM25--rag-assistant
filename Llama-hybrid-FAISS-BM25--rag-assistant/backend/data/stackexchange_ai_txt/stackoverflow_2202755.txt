It says "on lines that contain 'scattered' replace zero or more of any character followed by 'p3=' with nothing (delete it, in other words) and print the result" ( -n says don't print lines unless there's an explicit print command). For this example input: abc organized p3=123 def scattered p3=456 ghi ordered p3=789 The output would be: 456
