Set the value in your onComplete handler. Something like: $('#modal-form-holder').dialog({ bgiframe:true, width:width, title: title, modal:true, resizable: false, closeOnEscape: true, draggable: false, autoOpen:false }).load("my_affiliate_form.html", null, function(responseText, textStatus, XMLHttpRequest) { onComplete(responseText, textStatus, XMLHttpRequest, key); }); Then, in onComplete (which should be defined to take all four parameters), fill the affiliate_key field with key.
