Check User-Agent . Use this header value to distinguish bots from regular browsers/users. For example, Google bot: Mozilla/5.0 (compatible; Googlebot/2.1; +http://www.google.com/bot.html) Safari: Mozilla/5.0 (Macintosh; U; Intel Mac OS X 10_6_3; lv-lv) AppleWebKit/531.22.7 (KHTML, like Gecko) Version/4.0.5 Safari/531.22.7
