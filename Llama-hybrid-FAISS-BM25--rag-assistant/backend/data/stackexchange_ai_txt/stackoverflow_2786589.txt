SQL 2000 lacks the sys.procedures system table - the closest you'll find is sysobjects , and you could do your select WHERE name=? AND XTYPE='P' . To get SQL agent jobs, you can do this: SELECT job_id, [name] FROM msdb.dbo.sysjobs WHERE NAME=?;
