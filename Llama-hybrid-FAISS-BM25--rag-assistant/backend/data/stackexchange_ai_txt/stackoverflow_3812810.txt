Check the current documentation for 3.5 http://www.phpunit.de/manual/current/en/appendixes.configuration.html#appendixes.configuration.blacklist-whitelist They refactored a lot to different packages. Thus PHPUnit_Util_Filter::addDirectoryToFilter("$foot/tests"); becomes PHP_CodeCoverage_Filter::getInstance()->addDirectoryToBlacklist("$foot/tests"); Regards, Markus
