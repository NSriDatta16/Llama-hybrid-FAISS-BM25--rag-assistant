It is not recommended to store large files/pictures in the DB, but you can. For that use a column of type varbinary . As of SQL Server 2008, you can also save BLOBs=files on the disk but still have SQL server refer to them with the help of the filestream storage attribute . You can check basics of the performance differences here .
