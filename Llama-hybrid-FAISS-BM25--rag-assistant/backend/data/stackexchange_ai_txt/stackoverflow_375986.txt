You cannot mix those plugins: they process the same events, and cannot cooperate together. Either rethink your UI, or use different tools. Is it possible to do it? Yes, of course. For example, Dojo DnD allows both sorting and drag-and-drop using just one component: test_dnd.html (link to the debugging server).
