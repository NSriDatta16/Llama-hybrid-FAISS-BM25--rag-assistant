Depending on your browser, you can use the following plugins or tools (linked where necessary): Firefox - Firebug (download the addon, restart and press F12 to open) Google Chrome, Safari - Webkit Inspector (CTRL+SHIFT+I to bring it up) Opera - Dragonfly (CTRL+SHIFT+I) Internet Explorer 5.5-7 - IE Developer Toolbar (press the icon near your address bar) Internet Explorer 8+ - IE Developer Tools (press F12 to open)
