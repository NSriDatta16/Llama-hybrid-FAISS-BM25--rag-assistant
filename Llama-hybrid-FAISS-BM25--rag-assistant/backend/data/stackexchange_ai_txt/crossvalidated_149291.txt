Do you have a test set with $y = 0$ examples? If not, it will be pretty hard to evaluate your classifier! Anyway, logistic regression isn't the greatest tool for this, but there are other neat techniques that were designed for problems like this, usually falling under the umbrella term of one-class classification . I think the most commonly used is the one-class support vector machine .
