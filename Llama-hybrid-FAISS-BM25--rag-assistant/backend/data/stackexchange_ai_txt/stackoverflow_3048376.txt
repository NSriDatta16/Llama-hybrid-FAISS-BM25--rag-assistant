You have an infinite loop. The result of your rewrite is being rewritten again. RewriteBase / RewriteCond %{HTTP_USER_AGENT} (?:Googlebot|Googlebot|Mediapartners|Adsbot|Feedfetcher)-?(?:Google|Image)? [NC] RewriteCond $0 !(?:^|/)503\.html$ RewriteRule .* /var/www/503.html [R=503] As @BalusC pointed out, you also need the R flag.
