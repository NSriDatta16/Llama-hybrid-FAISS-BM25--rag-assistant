Unless you're happy with JEditorPane, you're basically looking at a full webbrowser inside of Swing. Ideally, you would use JWebPane which would be a WebKit view as a Swing component, but it isn't out yet. The most recent information I could find was this blog post . The DJ project allows embedding the platform's native browser in Swing. It uses Internet Explorer on Windows and XULRunner on Linux. It does not have any support for Mac.
