An absolute must-read on this topic is Jeff's own You're Probably Storing Passwords Incorrectly . Here's the executive summary: Do not invent your own "clever" password storage scheme. Never store passwords as plaintext. Add a long, unique random salt to each password you store. Use a cryptographically secure hash.
