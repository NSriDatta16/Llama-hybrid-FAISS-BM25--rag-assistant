for k in dic.keys(): if k.startswith('s_'): del dic[k] * EDIT * now in python 3 , years after the original answer, keys() returns a view into the dict so you can't change the dict size. One of the most elegant solutions is a copy of the keys: for k in list(dic.keys()): if k.startswith('s_'): del dic[k]
