You can normalize each criteria independently in values between 0 and 1 without taking into account the other criterias, it will work better for most classification methods k-nearest neighbors, random forest, neural network, etc. $$x^*_{i,j}=\frac{x_{i,j}-x^{min}_j}{x^{max}_j-x^{min}_j}$$
