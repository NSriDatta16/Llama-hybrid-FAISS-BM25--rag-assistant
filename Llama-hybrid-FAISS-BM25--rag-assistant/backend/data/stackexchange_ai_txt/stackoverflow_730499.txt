Another approach would be to use a UIWebView with HTML. Or, if you are daring you can make use of the undocumented setHTML method on UITextView to set small snippets of formatted text... To me this seems more like the future of where supporting formatted text will go, some way to leverage Webkit in outputting formatted strings.
