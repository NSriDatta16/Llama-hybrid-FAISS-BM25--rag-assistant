A few thoughts: are there groups of jobs that can be grouped together - all having the same base requirements? are there people that can also be groups together - all having the basic skills If so, than you can reduce some of the complexity from the outset and then use some form of weighted averages for the preferences. Also, when you recruit, since the min. you can recruit for is 30 minutes, and assumption they are a higher cost, you probably want to make sure they have the highest utilization levels. Here's some articles that might help: Job Shop Scheduling - http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Job_Shop_Scheduling Competative Analysis - http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Competitive_analysis_(online_algorithm) k server problem - http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/K-server_problem
