I resolved this by putting everything on a second "root" layer (called "gameLayer") the same size as the original. Then during the UIPanGestureRecognizer, I move the bomb element from "gameLayer" into my UIView.layer. Then while I am testing for a AIEnemyUnit, I only run the hitTest on the "gameLayer". - UIView.layer --------- gameLayer | | dragObj(bomb) gameElements | bomb
