Typically, InnoDB is used to support foreign keys, and transactions: http://dev.mysql.com/doc/refman/5.1/en/innodb.html You can change the table engine on the fly: ALTER TABLE tableName ENGINE = InnoDB; You can also change the default -- for newly created tables -- as shown here: http://dev.mysql.com/doc/refman/5.1/en/storage-engine-setting.html
