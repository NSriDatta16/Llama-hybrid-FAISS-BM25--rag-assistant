Those should be the same. 0xE1E1E1 and #E1E1E1 mean the same thing. The difference is that in a Flash/Flex stylesheet you need to use the # prefix or your colors won't show up , while elsewhere you are encouraged to use 0x . 0x prefix, by the way, signals a numerical value, and the hexadecimal value for style colors is a uint data type.
