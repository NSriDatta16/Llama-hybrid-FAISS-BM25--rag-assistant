On dimensionality reduction, a good first choice might be principal components analysis . Apart from that, i don't have too much to add, except that if you have any interest in data mining, I strongly recommend you read the elements of statistical learning . Its both rigorous and clear, and although I haven't finished it, it would probably give you much insight into the right way to approach your problem. Chapter 4, linear classifiers would almost certainly enough to get you started.
