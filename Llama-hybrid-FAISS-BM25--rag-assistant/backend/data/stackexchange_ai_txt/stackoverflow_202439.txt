Using ismember() to merge data organized by text identfiers. Useful when you are analyzing differing periods when entries, in my case company symbols, come and go. %Merge B into A based on Text identifiers UniverseA = {'A','B','C','D'}; UniverseB = {'A','C','D'}; DataA = [20 40 60 80]; DataB = [30 50 70]; MergeData = NaN(length(UniverseA),2); MergeData(:,1) = DataA; [tf, loc] = ismember(UniverseA, UniverseB); MergeData(tf,2) = DataB(loc(tf)); MergeData = 20 30 40 NaN 60 50 80 70
