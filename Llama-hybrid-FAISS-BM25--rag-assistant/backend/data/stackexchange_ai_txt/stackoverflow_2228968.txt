That's just a matter of styling after all. In general, I would consider it to be discouraged to make your Android app look like an iPhone app. Every platform has its own UX standards, and Android's standards are different from iPhone's. That quite obviously also reflects in the user interface. Anyhow, you may want to read this article about Android themes and styles . It should get you going with defining own widget styles and stateful drawables to implement custom buttons.
