The TeX FAQ lists several ways of doing this. Read here for full details. I've successfully used the mdwlist package (which is part of mdwtools ) in my own documents. For example: \documentclass{article} \usepackage{mdwlist} \begin{document} \begin{enumerate} \item List item \item Another list item \suspend{enumerate} Paragraph of comments on list items 1 and 2. \resume{enumerate} \item Further item \item Final item \end{enumerate} \end{document} Thanks to Dervin Thunk for providing the FAQ link.
