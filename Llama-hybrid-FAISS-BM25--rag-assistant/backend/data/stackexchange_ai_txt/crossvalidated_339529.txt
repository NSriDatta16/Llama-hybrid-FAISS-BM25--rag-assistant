The z-score for the AME will not be the same as the one for the coefficient, though they are calculated in a very similar way by subtracting zero and dividing by the standard error. Here's an example showing this calculation with Stata for a continuous variable. I won't calculate the standard error of the average marginal effect (AME) by hand since the derivative gets messy.
