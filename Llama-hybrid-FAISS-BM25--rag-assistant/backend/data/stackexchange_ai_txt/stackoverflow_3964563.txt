and , along with abominations like bold pink 18pt text in comic sans on a light purple background, were all the rage with amateur web designers during the 90's because they're "neat", and were supposed to have experienced the true death when they shut down geocities. The point is they're an eyesore. They keep animating forever. A much better plan would be to use javascript to scroll something in once , like when SO tells you you have new replies or that you've earned a badge. Once gets users' attention without distracting them the whole time they're on the page.
