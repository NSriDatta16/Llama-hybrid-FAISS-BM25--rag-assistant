Visual recognition - as in the case of differentiating a watermark from the rest of an image - is in the domain of artificial intelligence, and it requires A LOT of neural network training. If you can write a perfect algorithm for this one, you can break any CAPTCHA too. So good luck if you manage to do it! That said, you might be way over your head on this one.
