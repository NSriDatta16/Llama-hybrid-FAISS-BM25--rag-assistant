The Rocchio equation for iteratively updating a query is $$ \vec{q}_m = \alpha\vec{q}_0 + \beta\frac{1}{|D_r|}\sum_{\vec{d} \in D_r}\vec{d} - \gamma\frac{1}{|D_{nr}|}\sum_{\vec{d} \in D_{nr}}\vec{d}. $$ So if you have no negative examples, just set the $\gamma$ parameter to 0. That is the approach recommended on page 183 of the book An Introduction to Information Retrieval.
