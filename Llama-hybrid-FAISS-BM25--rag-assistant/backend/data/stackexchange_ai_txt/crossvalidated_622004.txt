Hidden layers: are, as explained, layers that are hidden from view on the path from input to output. Hidden states are technically speaking inputs to whatever we do at a given step, and they can only be computed by looking at data at previous time steps. Source: https://d2l.ai/chapter_recurrent-neural-networks/rnn.html
