It seems that this works: RewriteEngine On RewriteCond %{HTTP_USER_AGENT} !.*MSIE.* RewriteRule \.html$ - [T=application/xhtml+xml] RewriteCond %{HTTP_USER_AGENT} .*MSIE.* RewriteRule \.html$ - [T=text/html] If it weren't for internet explorer, I'd know so much less about all sorts of things!
