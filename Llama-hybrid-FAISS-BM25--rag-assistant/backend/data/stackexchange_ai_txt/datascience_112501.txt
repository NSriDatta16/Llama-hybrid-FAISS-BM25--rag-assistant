You can use models from Huggingface, but be aware that a good RAM and GPU could be necessary with large volumes of data. Alternatively, ML cloud services like Paperspace can be helpful. Here is the list of summarizers : https://huggingface.co/models?pipeline_tag=summarization&sort=downloads The most popular one is bart large: https://huggingface.co/facebook/bart-large-cnn You can test it and download it in the option "use in transformers".
