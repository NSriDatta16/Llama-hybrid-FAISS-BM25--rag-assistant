The algorithmic basis is agglomerative clustering or something similar. But there are a number of heuristics on top of that. For example, the vector space is surely comprised of words and phrases (word n-grams). Limiting the search in a strict time period is also very important. And identifying names, and weighing more the title and the paragraph headings are also key parts. On a tangentially related note.
