It's not the most elegant solution, but you could use OPENROWSET to put the stored proc results into a table, then use sp_help to get a description of it. eg select * into tmp_Results from openrowset( 'SQLOLEDB.1' , 'Server=your_server_name;Trusted_Connection=yes;' , 'exec your_stored_proc') exec sp_help 'tmp_Results' drop table tmp_Results
