Do you set margin / padding for your lists ? Browsers may have different "default" styles for UL and OL elements. It is often usefull to apply "reset" style rules and/or specify the styles you want to be applied to common elements such as lists, paragraphs, blockquotes, tables, headings,... see here for basic CSS resets : http://meyerweb.com/eric/tools/css/reset/
