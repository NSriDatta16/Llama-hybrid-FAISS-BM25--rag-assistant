With my credit union, my account access is through a different organization and a different web site, secure.fundsxpress.com However, my back button still functions. The credit union logo is still there in a frameset. To answer your question, a separate pop up window can paradoxically make the bank transactions feel more secure than just using a browser window. Of course, the bank customer has to trust the web site and the bank. You're correct that most people dislike pop up windows. Pop up windows are to be avoided, generally.
