you need to min the value first, then find the time related to that min value in an outer query. I'm not sure why you're rounding the value. select d.data_point_groupid, min_value, timeId from dma.dbo.calculated_average_group_flow d inner join (select data_point_groupid, min([value]) as min_value from dma.dbo.calculated_average_group_flow where night=1 group by data_point_groupid) mnv on d.data_point_groupid = mnv.data_point_groupid and d.[value] = mnv.min_value where night=1
