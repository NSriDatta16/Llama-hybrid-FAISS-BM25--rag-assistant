Did you try time series modelling? If not, then you should. I tried linear regression with only date and sales, but this does not account for seasonality The moving average model is something which would fit nicely to your dataset. However, as you say that your model is exhibiting seasonality, you need to adjust the moving averages so that it takes the seasonality into account. So, the best model for your dataset would be the SARIMA model. It is just the Auto-Regressive Integrated Moving Average (ARIMA) model but with seasonal adjustments.
