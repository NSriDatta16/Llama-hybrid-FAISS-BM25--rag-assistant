The "type mismatch" you are reffering to in the HTML is because they are trying to stop you from scraping the site by limiting your user agent. add this to your request before making the call: request.UserAgent = "Mozilla/4.0 (compatible; MSIE 8.0; Windows NT 6.1)"; That should give you the information you need.
