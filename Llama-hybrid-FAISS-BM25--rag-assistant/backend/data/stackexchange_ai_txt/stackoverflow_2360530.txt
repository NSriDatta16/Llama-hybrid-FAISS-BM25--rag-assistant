If you want to be quick and inelegant, just use the cells to the right to concatenate with separation (semi-colon, say). Assuming your data starts in A2, put this in B2: =A1 And put this in B3 and fill down: =B2&";"&A3 The text you want is in the bottom cell of the B column.
