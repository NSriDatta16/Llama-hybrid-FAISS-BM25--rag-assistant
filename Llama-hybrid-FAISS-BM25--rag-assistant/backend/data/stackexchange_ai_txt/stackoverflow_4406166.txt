Anything wrong with string.Template ? This is in the standard Python distribution and covered by PEP 292 : from string import Template form=Template('''Dear $john, I am sorry to imform you, $john, but you will not be my husband when you return from the $theater war. So sorry about that. Your $action has caused me to reconsider. Yours [NOT!!] forever, Becky ''') first={'john':'Joe','theater':'Afgan','action':'love'} second={'john':'Robert','theater':'Iraq','action':'kiss'} third={'john':'Jose','theater':'Korean','action':'discussion'} print form.substitute(first) print form.substitute(second) print form.substitute(third)
