No you can't. The _an9 and _anx parts are randomly generated. (When I compile again I got _ane and _anC .) You could use the SCC (set-cost-center) pragma to insert a cost center manually: data Fast = Fast instance Show Fast where show _ = {-# SCC "show(Fast)" #-} show $ sum' [1 .. 10] data Slow = Slow instance Show Slow where show _ = {-# SCC "show(Slow)" #-} show $ sum' [1 .. 100000000] The profile should show: main show_an9 show(Slow) sum' show_anx show(Fast)
