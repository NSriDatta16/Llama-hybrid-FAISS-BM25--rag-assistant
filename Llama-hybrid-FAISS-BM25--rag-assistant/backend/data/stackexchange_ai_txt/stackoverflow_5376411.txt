Yes, that's the UTF-8 representation of รถ (U+00F6 LATIN SMALL LETTER O WITH DIAERESIS). It consists of a 0xC3 octet followed by a 0xB6 octet. UTF-8 is a very elegant encoding, I think, and worth reading up on. The history of its design (on a placemat in a diner) is described here by Rob Pike .
