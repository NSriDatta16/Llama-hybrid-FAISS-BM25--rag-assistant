I am currently wrapping up an asp.net mvc application. We used the S#arp Architecture. It is a "framework" that enables developers to leverage NHibernate, Fluent, and a myriad of other best practices tools (DI, etc.) Here is a link to the site. http://wiki.sharparchitecture.net/ Our team absolutely loves this product and recommends anyone to add it to their technical arsenal when deciding on architecture paths.
