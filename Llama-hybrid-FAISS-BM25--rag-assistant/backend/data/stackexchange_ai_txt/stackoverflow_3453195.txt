Yes: simply press and hold the Alt key, click and drag to select the lines whose columns you wish to edit, and begin typing. You can also go to Settings > Preferences... , and in the Editing tab, turn on multi-editing, to enable selection of multiple separate regions or columns of text to edit at once. It's much more intuitive, as you can see your edits live as you type.
