Your best bet is to use event receivers. The ItemAdded(Synchronous) or ItemAdding(Asynchronous) event receivers will allow you to access the data from the item that was just added. You can use this information to create a different item, either in the same list, in a new list, or in any type of storage medium you can get too. Custom workflows will also give you much of the same ability. Excellent article on ER's --> http://developers.de/blogs/adis_jugo/archive/2009/03/12/develop-and-deploy-a-sharepoint-event-receiver-from-the-scratch.aspx
