They go to the system log. You can see it from the organizer in Xcode. If you really need the file, you can redirect stderr: NSString* myLogfile = [[NSSearchPathForDirectoriesInDomains(NSDocumentDirectory, NSUserDomainMask, YES) objectAtIndex:0] stringByAppendingPathComponent:@"log.txt"]; int fd = open([myLogfile fileSystemRepresentation], O_RDWR | O_CREAT, 0664); dup2(fd, 2);
