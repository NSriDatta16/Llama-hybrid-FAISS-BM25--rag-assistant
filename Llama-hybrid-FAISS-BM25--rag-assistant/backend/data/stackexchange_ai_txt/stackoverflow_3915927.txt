For Access 2003: 2 GB. However, because your database can include linked tables in other files, its total size is limited only by available storage capacity. per this article: http://www.databasezone.com/techdocs/acclimit.html You can also find the info here: http://office.microsoft.com/en-us/access-help/access-specifications-HP005186808.aspx
