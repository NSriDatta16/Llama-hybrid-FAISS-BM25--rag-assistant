Since you're okay with using extensions, but you're worried about going all-out with OpenGL 2.0, consider providing a simple fragment shader with the old-school ARB_fragment_program extension. Alternatively, you could use a library like DevIL, ImageMagick, or FreeImage to perform the conversion for you.
