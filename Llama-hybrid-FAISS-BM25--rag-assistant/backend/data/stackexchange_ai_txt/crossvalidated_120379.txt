There are some things that you can check in your data. 1 - correlation between variables 2 - categorical variables or continuous variables? 3 - relation between number of samples and number of variables 4 - are the samples independent or is it a time series? According to these points and to the kind of information you want to extract from your data you can decide what algorithm to use.
