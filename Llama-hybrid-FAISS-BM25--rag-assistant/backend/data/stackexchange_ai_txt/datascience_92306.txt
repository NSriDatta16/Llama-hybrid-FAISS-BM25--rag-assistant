The full method is explained in the paper Emergence of Locomotion Behaviours in Rich Environments by the DeepMind team. Quoting from that paper: Using a novel scalable variant of policy gradient reinforcement learning, our agents learn to run, jump, crouch and turn as required by the environment . . . So to answer your question, the researchers used a policy gradient method.
