Another inelegant solution (but without the drawbacks of signaling your UI application) is to have another service that is installed to interact with the desktop whose job it is to listen for a signal to lock the desktop. I agree that it's not great to have to run with local system credentials, but if all the service does is lock the desktop, there's a pretty small footprint that needs secured.
