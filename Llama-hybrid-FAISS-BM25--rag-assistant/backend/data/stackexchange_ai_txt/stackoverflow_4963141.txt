Yes, you can create two different sets by simply using a different radio-button name: radio_button_tag 'gender', 'male' radio_button_tag 'gender', 'female' radio_button_tag 'food', 'none' radio_button_tag 'food', 'vegetarian' radio_button_tag 'food', 'vegan' This will result in params[:gender] being 'male' or 'female' and params[:food] being 'none', 'vegetarian' or 'vegan'. You can do the same thing with the radio_button function.
