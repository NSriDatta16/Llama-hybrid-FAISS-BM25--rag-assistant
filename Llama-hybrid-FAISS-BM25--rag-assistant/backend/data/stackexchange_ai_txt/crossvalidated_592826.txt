32 data points are far too little to use any neural network, and it's very hard to extract any reliable information from such a tiny dataset with any method. Anything that comes out of your analysis is very likely simply noise. Sorry. That said, your best bet will likely be a classical logistic regression with regularization. Take a look at GLMNet implementations, available in the glmnet package in R .
