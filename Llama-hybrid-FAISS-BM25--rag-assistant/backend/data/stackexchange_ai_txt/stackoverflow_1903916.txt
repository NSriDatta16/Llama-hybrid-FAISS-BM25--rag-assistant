XHTML Strict forces you to use semantic markup, where as Transitional is a bit more flexible and still allows you to use legacy presentational elements like and . People still use Transitional to maintain support older markup, but it doesn't make any difference to SEO. Strict is considered better for encouraging more accessible XHTML and better separation of content and presentation.
