optimize text in iframe-container-page and de-optimize text in iframe-content-page. use the iframe title-property and insert alternative content between opening and closing iframe-tags. add javascript to iframe-content-page to check if it is loaded standalone, redirect to iframe-container-page if not: if(self.location==top.location) top.location.replace(' http://contain.er/page-url/here '); optionally (but I wouldn't recommend it) you could add the iframe-content-page to robots.txt and declare it not to be indexed: User-agent: * Disallow: /iframe-content-page.html
