Bugs : flaws anywhere within the process (application, database, reporting, etc.) that will prevent 100% of desired functionality from occurring. Also known and referred to as defects. Issues : potentially caused by a bug or bugs, an issue is a report of some form of loss of functionality in the system that would be tied to a user. These are also referred to as help desk tickets in some organizations. WIKIPEDIA LINKS - Software Bug - Issue Tracking
