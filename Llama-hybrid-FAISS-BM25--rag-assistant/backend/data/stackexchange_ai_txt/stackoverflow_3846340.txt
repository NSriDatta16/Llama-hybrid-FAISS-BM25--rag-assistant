It's the navigation bar from the UINavigationController. You can do stuff like [self.navigationController setNavigationBarHidden:YES animated:YES] and self.navigationController.navigationBar.tintColor = [UIColor magentaColor] . You should not set the navigationBar.hidden/alpha directly, since those are handled by the UINavigationController. Additionally, UINavigationController may not re-layout your views appropriately.
