No; selectors either do or don't match a given element. The retrieval mechanism doesn't go out multiple times and build multiple lists. Besides, you're not specifying "all classA and classB elements", you're specifying "classB elements within classA elements". What you're implying would look like: var formElements = $('#MyForm').find('.classA, .classB')
