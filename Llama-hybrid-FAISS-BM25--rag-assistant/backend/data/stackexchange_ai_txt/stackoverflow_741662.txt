I believe those buffers don't exists when those windows are off-screen. Or only partially exist when partially off-screen. If you pay attention to the window thumbnails, you will find it will not update when those windows are minimized or off-screen. WM_PAINT will fire as a window is being dragged from off-screen to on, again suggesting that this data was not already buffered somewhere.
