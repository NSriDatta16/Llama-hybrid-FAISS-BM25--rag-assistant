For others reference this is what i added as per Vinai's code. $collection = Mage::getModel('catalog/product')->getCollection()->addAttributeToSelect('*'); Mage::getSingleton('catalog/product_visibility')->addVisibleInCatalogFilterToCollection($collection); $magento_block = Mage::getSingleton('core/layout'); $productsHtml = $magento_block->createBlock('catalog/product_list'); $productsHtml ->setTemplate('catalog/product/list.phtml')->setCollection($collection); echo $productsHtml ->toHTML(); It renders the pagination toolbar perfectly.
