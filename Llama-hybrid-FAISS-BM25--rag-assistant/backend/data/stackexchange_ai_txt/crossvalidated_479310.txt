What you have is called repeated measurements , and there are indeed special models for repeated measurements data. Nowadays we most use mixed models, and such models do not have any requirement for equal number of measurements pr sample. There are already many questions here, see this list The question about how to organize the data frame: Use long format , that is, one observation pr row, and an additional Group variable identifying to which group that obs belongs.
