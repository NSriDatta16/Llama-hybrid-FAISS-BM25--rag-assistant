You definitely need to check whether the user is allowed to view the page. But, what if you separated the ids for the different catalogs? URL's could look like books.php?cat=foo&id=1. That wouldn't necessarily make things any more secure, but it would prevent people from accidentally finding the wrong pages and organize things a little better.
