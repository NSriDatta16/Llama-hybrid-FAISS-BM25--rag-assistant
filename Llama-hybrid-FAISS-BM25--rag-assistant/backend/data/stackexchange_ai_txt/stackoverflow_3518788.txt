Michelle Ufford (aka "SQL Fool") has a great repository of scripts: http://sqlfool.com/category/sql-scripts/ Her Index Defrag Script v4.0 is probably the best around and it's well tested. You can let it loose on your entire database, and it will sniff out those indices in need of REBUILD (defrag) or REINDEX (complete re-creation).
