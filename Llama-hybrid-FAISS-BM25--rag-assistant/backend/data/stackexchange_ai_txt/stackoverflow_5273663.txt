liner datastructure has nothing to to with it's implementation detail or its memory model. It just means all its elements has its preceding or following, exclude the head and end. Not like a tree or gragh. Liner datastructure includes queue, stack. If somebody mis uses the liner as "Continouse memory", he/she is wrong.
