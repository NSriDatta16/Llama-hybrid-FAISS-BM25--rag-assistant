Don't we love IE? :) Try using those headers: header("Pragma: public"); header("Expires: 0"); header("Cache-Control: must-revalidate, post-check=0, pre-check=0"); header("Cache-Control: private",false); header("Content-Type: application/octet-stream"); header("Content-Disposition: attachment; filename=\"exportevent.csv\";" ); header("Content-Transfer-Encoding: binary"); I think that the octet-stream content type forces IE to download the file.
