You can add in a count for some arbitrary value that's involved the aggregation, or even do SELECT x.name, y.name, z.name, AVG(x.tacos) AS average_tacos, SUM(1) AS aggregated_row_count FROM .... WHERE ... GROUP BY x.name, y.name, z.name
