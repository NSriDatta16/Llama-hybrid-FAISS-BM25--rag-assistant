For all us non-CS majors: wikipedia on De Morgan's laws : De Morgan's laws are rules relating the logical operators "and" and "or" in terms of each other via negation, namely: NOT (P OR Q) = (NOT P) AND (NOT Q) NOT (P AND Q) = (NOT P) OR (NOT Q)
