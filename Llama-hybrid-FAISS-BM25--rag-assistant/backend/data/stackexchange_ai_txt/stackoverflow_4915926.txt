I ran in to Tonttu's problem importing a bunch of names, I went with: strs = "first lastname".split(" ") return_string = "" strs.each do |str| return_string += "#{str[0].upcase}#{str[1..str.length].downcase} " end return_string.chop EDIT : The inevitable refactor (over a year) later. "first lastname".split(" ").map do |str| "#{str[0].upcase}#{str[1..str.length].downcase}" end.join(' ') while definitely not easier to read, it gets the same result while declaring fewer temporary variables.
