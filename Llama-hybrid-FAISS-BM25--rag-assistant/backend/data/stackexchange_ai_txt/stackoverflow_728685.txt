Browser caching strategies can be controlled by HTTP headers. Remember that they are just a hint, really. Since browsers are terribly inconsistent in this (and any other) field, you'll need several headers to get the desired effect on a range of browsers. header ("Pragma-directive: no-cache"); header ("Cache-directive: no-cache"); header ("Cache-control: no-cache"); header ("Pragma: no-cache"); header ("Expires: 0");
