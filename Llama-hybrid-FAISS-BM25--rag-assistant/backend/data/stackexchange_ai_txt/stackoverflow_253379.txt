Your input elements should be within a fieldset. This validates and has the added benefit of making the document more accessible to non-visual user agents. As an aside, your markup is suffering from divitis a bit. You could put classes on the table cells directly rather than nesting div elements within them (I'm not going to comment on the use of tables for layout).
