In terms of view Blocks : Mage_Adminhtml_Block_Sales_Order_Create_Items for the product selection grid Mage_Adminhtml_Block_Sales_Order_Create_Items_Grid for the already selected products grid In terms of view template files : app/design/adminhtml/default/default/sales/orders/create/items.phtml app/design/adminhtml/default/default/sales/orders/create/items/grid.phtml If you want to add some JS logic to the "Add Products" button, you may want to look at the getButtonsHtml() method of Mage_Adminhtml_Block_Sales_Order_Create_Items where you will be able to update the 'onclick' of the $addButtonData array. If you want to add some JS loic to the Update Items and Qty's button, you may tweak the app/design/adminhtml/default/default/sales/orders/create/items/grid.phtml file between lines 428 (Magento CE 1.4.1.1)
