Yet another reason R.java might not get autogenerated is if you have directories like res/drawable-hdpi , res/drawable-mdpi , or res/drawable-ldpi . 1.6+ seems to be OK with these directories, but 1.5 doesn't want them. When I removed those directories, R.java started autogenerating for me again.
