That's completely impossible, and you should be grateful for it. Your mouse is your system's primary input, and if browsers were able to control or commandeer it there is virtually nothing a site couldn't do. It is possible, however, to constrain a dragged object within another DIV - jQueryUI's draggable(), for instance, makes this incredibly easy.
