Read some books. The Pragmatic Programmer. From Journeyman to Master , Thomas, Hunt - to make your brain think in a pragmatic way, not PHP or other technology way. PHP or Ruby will die, the knowledge from this book won't as it's universal. Apprenticeship Patterns , Hoover, Oshineye - to plan your career, get to know what's important, what to avoid and what to do to make yourself better.
