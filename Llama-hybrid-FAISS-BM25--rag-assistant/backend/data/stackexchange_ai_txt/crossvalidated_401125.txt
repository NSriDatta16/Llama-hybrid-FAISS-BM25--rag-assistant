Typo: $$\pi(\xi)=\frac{1}{Z_\beta}e^{\beta\sum_{x,y=x}{\xi_x\xi_y}}$$ should be $$\pi(\xi)=\frac{1}{Z_\beta}e^{\beta\sum_{x,y\sim x}{\xi_x\xi_y}}$$ where $x\sim y$ denotes the neighbourhood relation.
