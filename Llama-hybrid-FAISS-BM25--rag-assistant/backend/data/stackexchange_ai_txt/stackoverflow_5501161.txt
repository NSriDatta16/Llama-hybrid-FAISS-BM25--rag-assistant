You are sending a message whose content is form-urlencoded. Try creating first an unencoded request: use LWP::UserAgent; my $ua = LWP::UserAgent->new; my $req = HTTP::Request->new(POST => 'http://192.168.1.1/something'); $req->content('array[]=123&something=abc'); my $res = $ua->request($req);
