There's no way to identify that struct. You need to put the struct somewhere it can be found, on the stack or on the heap. Sometimes data structures are tagged with identifying information to assist with debugging or memory management. As a means of data organization, it is among the worst possible approaches. You probably need to a lot of general reading on memory management .
