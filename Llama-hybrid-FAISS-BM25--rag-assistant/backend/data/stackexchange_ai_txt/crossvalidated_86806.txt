This is certainly a question that will provoke controversy. When neural networks are used in deep learning they are typically trained in ways that weren't used in the 1980's. In particular strategies that pretrain individual layers of the neural network to recognize features at different level are claimed to make it easier to train networks with several layers. That's certainly a new development since the 1980's.
