It's also random between draws, to ensure lower $\rho$ . In random forests, random subsets of variables (eg $\sqrt{n_{features}}$ ) are used as candidates to build trees to reduce $\rho$ (ie tree 1 is only allowed to use features 1,5,6, tree 2 is only allowed 4,7,8, etc)
