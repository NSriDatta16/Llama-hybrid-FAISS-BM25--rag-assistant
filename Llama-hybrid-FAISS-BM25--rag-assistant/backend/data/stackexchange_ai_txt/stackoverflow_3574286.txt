In theory, yes. EJB 2.x is still part of the current JavaEE spec, and JBoss6 supports it fully. However, JBossAS 3.x and 6.x are architecturally very different to each other. Do not assume the upgrade will be a smooth one, it may well feel like migrating to a completely new appserver.
