Use the - (void)setDefaultParagraphStyle:(NSParagraphStyle *)paragraphStyle method in your NSTextView. Documentation on NSParagraphStyle Documentation on NSMutableParagraphStyle There is a setLineSpacing: method in NSMutableParagraphStyle. There are also methods relating to line height, the methods under "Setting Other Style Information" in the NSMutableParagraphStyle documentation should prove useful. I think that's what you're looking for.
