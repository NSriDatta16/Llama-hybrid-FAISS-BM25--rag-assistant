These are all data structures used for quickly searching binary data. Many are used by different data management systems. They differ in their approach for storage of data. I'd recommend reading up on each. Splay: http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Splay_tree Red-Black: http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Red-black_tree AVL: http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Avl_tree B-Tree: http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/B-Tree T-Tree: http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/T-tree
