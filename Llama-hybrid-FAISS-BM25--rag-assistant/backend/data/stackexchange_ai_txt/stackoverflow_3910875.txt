Well, we figured this one out. Two things are necessary: On the page when embedding the swf: allowScriptAccess="always" In the swf itself: System.security.allowDomain("ourdomain.com") I guess this is sort of obvious, but I figured I'd post it here in case some other poor lost soul such as myself is having the same problem.
