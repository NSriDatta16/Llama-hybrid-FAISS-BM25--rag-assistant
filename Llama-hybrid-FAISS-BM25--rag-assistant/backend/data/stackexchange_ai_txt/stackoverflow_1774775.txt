If you want to leverage your C#/.NET knowledge (IronPython,...), you should go with Mono . Its IDE is called MonoDevelop and works in Linux, Mac and Windows. You can keep using WinForms or switch to Gtk#, which is a nice wrapper around gtk+ . Also, make sure you read the application portability guidelines which covers different strategies and common pitfalls. Enjoy!
