user_agent = environ.get('HTTP_USER_AGENT', None) Or if environ is wrapped in some sort of Request object: user_agent = request.user_agent btw, You should probably look at HTTP_ACCEPT header instead of HTTP_USER_AGENT to find out what representation should be sent.
