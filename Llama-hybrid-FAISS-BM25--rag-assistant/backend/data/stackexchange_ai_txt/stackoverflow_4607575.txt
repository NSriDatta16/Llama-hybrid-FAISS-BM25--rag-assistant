Although the point of MVC is simple - having data access (Model), GUI creation (View) and flow control (Controller) as separate components - it can be implemented in very different ways. From a purely technical viewpoint, MVC has very little to do with computational scalability. It's more about the human factor and having a way to organize huge piles of code into meaningful sub-units that are much easier to understand.
