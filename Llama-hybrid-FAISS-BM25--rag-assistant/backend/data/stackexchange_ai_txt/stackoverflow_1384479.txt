The fulltext search comes with the same set of builtin dictionaries as on Unix/Linux, which is pretty good coverage. If these cover your needs, you will have the exact same functionality. If you require custom dictionaries, they also work equally well on Windows, but you are likely going to have a harder time building the installation of them there.
