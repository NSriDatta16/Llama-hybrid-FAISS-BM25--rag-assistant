1) Be warned that validating email addresses is extremely hard. In fact it's impossible to do perfectly. It's a trade-off between expression complexity, coverage, and accuracy (false positives). See the zillions of other questions on SO about validating e-mail addresses, and also see here: http://www.regular-expressions.info/email.html 2) You have a flat file (.csv). You can't read this in with javascript and process in a browser. You'll need to look at some other language. Perl and Java to randomly mention two languages have good regex support.
