I think you want a multilevel ordinal logistic regression model with difficulty as the dependent variable and number of pedestrians, skill of driver and their interaction as independent variables. You need a multilevel model to account for the non-independence of the data: Drivers may have different typical perceptions of difficulty, even within skill level.
