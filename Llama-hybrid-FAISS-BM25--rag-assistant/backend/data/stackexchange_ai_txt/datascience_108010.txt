From the NLP side, i.e. assuming you have the data as text, this would be typically treated as a NER problem , i.e. with a sequence labeling model . The task look similar to text anonymization . This is a area of active research. If the goal is to recognize all the potentially sensitive information in a text, it's important to keep in mind that there's no perfect method. In general performance is higher with a specific domain than with a broad domain.
