Your problem fits the domain of recommendation engines. Based on user's used software, you wish to evaluate his chance of using another one(and maybe buying it). This document describes the logic pretty well. The item profiling phase can be done using a pairwise correlation, and other heuristics. Be aware that usually these method need more data than standard classifiers(logistic regression/decision trees/~SVM)
