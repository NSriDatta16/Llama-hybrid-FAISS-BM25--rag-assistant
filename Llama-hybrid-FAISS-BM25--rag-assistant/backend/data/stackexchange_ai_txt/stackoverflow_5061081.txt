The /importer.php document is pointing you to the right place. If you look at that page again, you can see at the bottom there is this line: Categories and Tags Converter - Install the category/tag converter to convert existing categories to tags or tags to categories, selectively. It's a plugin you can install. Once you've done that, you can do all kinds of modifications to your tags/categories. The link to the plugin once installed is: /wp-admin/admin.php?import=wpcat2tag
