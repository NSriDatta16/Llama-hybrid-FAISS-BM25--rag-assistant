According to the Code Style Font Family Survey , Tahoma is available on about 98% of Windows and 74% of Macintosh systems. Their Build Better CSS Font Stacks page lists common stacks organized by category. They also have a Font Stack Builder that tells you the likelihood of a visitor seeing a particular font in the stack.
