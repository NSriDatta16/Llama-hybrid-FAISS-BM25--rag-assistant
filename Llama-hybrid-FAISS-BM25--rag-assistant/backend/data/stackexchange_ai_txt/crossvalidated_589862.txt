how to calculate b in SVM if we have the optimum solution

In a SVM classifier. $$\begin{align} \min_{\mathbf{w},b}\quad &\frac{\|\mathbf{w}\|}{2}+ C \sum_{i=1}^{N} \xi^{(i)}, \\ s.t.\quad&y^{(i)} (\mathbf{w^T}\mathbf{x}^{(i)}+b) \ge 1 - \xi^{(i)},&\forall i \in \{1,\dots,N\} \\ \quad&\xi^{(i)}\ge0, &\forall i \in \{1,\dots,N\} \end{align}$$ if we have optimal $w$ and $\xi^{(i)}$ can we compute $b$ ? My guess is using train samples in $O(N)$ complexity we can compute it, but I can't prove it.
