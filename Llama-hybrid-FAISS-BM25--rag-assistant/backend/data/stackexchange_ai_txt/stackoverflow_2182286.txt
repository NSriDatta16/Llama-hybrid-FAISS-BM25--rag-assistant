Just as an extra option, consider that wxPython can produce some pretty good applications on Mac as well as on Linux and Windows. For the most part you can get native appearance but maintain portability with little or no attention to platform-specific issues. In other words, PyObjC + Python is not the only way to do Mac development with Python.
