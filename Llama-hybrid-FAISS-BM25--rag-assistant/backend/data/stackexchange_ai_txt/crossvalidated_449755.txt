One of the factors to consider is computational simplicity. By not introducing the square root, the gradient has a more elegant form. Also, minimizing $MSE$ subject to $\|\theta\|_2\le c$ is equivalent to minimizing $MSE$ subject to $\|\theta\|_2^2\le c^2$ .
