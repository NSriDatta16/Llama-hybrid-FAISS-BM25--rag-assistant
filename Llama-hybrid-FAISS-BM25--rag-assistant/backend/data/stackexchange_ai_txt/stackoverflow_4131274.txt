GameKit and cocos2d serve totally different purposes. cocos2d are for drawing graphics, while GameKit is to support social gaming (Game Center, P2P connection, Voice Chat). Game Kit offers features that you can use to create great social games. Social games allow players to share their experiences with other players. When players tell their friends about their favorite games, this encourages more players to download and play those games.
