My preferred method is the same as Robert Harvey proposes in his answer. You can still use the Task Parallel Library , but spin the task up in a separate process outside of IIS (the reason being that IIS has a limited number of worker threads to hand out and imposes other limitations that can make long running tasks unpredictable).
