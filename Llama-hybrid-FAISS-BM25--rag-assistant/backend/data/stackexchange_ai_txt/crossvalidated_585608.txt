You can do heterogeneous variance in a variety of ways in R. A simple way is through the gls package library(nlme) mod = gls(score~treatment, data=DF, weights = varIdent(form = ~1|treatment), method="ML") emmeans(mod, ~treatment) although lme4 is more efficient and popular, nlme offers a variety of structures for the residuals. Of course you can always go the Bayesian route if you need even more flexibility library(brms) modb
