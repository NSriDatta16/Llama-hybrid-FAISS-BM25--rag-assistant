The way I do this (in VB.NET) is to declare the dropdown: Private WithEvents dd As New UltraDropDown Then set the DataSource , ValueMember , DisplayMember , etc. Then set the ValueList property of your column to the dropdown: Me.ultragrid.DisplayLayout.Bands(0).Columns("Name").ValueList = dd Then on the RowSelected event of the dropdown, call the Update() method for the ActiveRow of the UltraGrid: Me.ultragrid.ActiveRow.Update()
