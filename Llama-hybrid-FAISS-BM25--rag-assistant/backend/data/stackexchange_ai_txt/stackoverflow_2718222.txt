There are a lot of nuances to how people prefer to do that, but the main benefit of what you're calling the module pattern (a named scope), is that your not cluttering up the global namespace, which helps keep things clean if you bring in other libraries etc, and avoids name collisions. How you organize the names and nested scopes within that is largely a matter of personal preference.
