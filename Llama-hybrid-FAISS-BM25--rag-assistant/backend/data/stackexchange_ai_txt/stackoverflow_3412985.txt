Finally found it at http://groups.google.com/group/android-developers/browse_thread/thread/c1a385226f3df482 . It is a one-liner: sendBroadcast(new Intent(Intent.ACTION_MEDIA_MOUNTED, Uri.parse ("file://" + Environment.getExternalStorageDirectory())));
