The Elements of Statistical Learning is probably a very good starting point if you are going to be working in machine learning and/or data science. Friedman, J., Hastie, T., & Tibshirani, R. (2001). The elements of statistical learning (Vol. 1, No. 10). New York: Springer series in statistics. This is also available free online, and has many further references.
