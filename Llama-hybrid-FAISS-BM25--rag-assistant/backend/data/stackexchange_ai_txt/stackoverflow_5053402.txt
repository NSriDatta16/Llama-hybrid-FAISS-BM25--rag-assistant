I think you're looking for the containment option: Constrains dragging to within the bounds of the specified element - can be a DOM element, 'parent', 'document', 'window', or a jQuery selector. Here's a demo: http://jsfiddle.net/andrewwhitaker/ZB5Tm/1/ Notice what happens when you remove the containment option.
