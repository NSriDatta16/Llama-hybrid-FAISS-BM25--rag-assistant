Yes, I think you are right. You need to build your app signed not as developer but with adhoc distribution certificate. You can find more about it on iPhone Developers Portal. Then you can find your app on your disk. You and your beta-testers can install your app via iTunes - Drag and Drop your adhoc certificate file on iTunes Drag and Drop your app package on iTunes. Sync iPhone with iTunes - and you have your app installed and sync.
