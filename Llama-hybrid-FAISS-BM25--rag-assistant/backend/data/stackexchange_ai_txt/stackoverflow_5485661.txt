Just catch and retry. This is normal SQLite behaviour. [edit:] SQLite will retry itself; this error is thrown if the retries don't work within a certain period. You can increase the period in various ways: http://www.sqlite.org/pragma.html#pragma_busy_timeout http://www.sqlite.org/c3ref/busy_timeout.html
