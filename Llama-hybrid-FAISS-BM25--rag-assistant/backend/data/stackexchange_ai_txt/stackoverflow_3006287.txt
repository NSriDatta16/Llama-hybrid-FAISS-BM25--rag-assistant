AppEngine doesn't use a relational database. You should really read the docs about how AppEngine works, starting with this Java-oriented overview of AppEngine . AppEngine delivers on its scalability promise by leveraging Google-style infrastructure. If you're looking to use a more traditional stack, you'll probably be better off with Amazon EC2 .
