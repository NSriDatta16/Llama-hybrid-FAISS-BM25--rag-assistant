You could try treating each object's time series as a function, and use tools from functional time series analysis (or functional data analysis) to analyze it. Here is one paper Clustering and Forecasting Multiple Functional Time Series which attempts to do this. Also there is an R package for functional time series analysis here . Possibly you could look at running principal component analysis and then classify time series based on the coefficients.
