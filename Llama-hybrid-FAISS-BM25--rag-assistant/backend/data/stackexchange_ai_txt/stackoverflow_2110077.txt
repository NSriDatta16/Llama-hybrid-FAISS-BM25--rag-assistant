UINavigationBar has a UINavigationItem with a leftBarButtonItem and a rightBarButtonItem. To set your edit button you init a bar button item with the style: UIBarButtonSystemItemEdit. Follow the delete button example here: http://dragonforged.com/devblog/?p=34
