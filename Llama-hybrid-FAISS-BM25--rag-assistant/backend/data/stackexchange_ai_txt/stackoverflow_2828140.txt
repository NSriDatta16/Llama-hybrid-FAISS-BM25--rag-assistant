You would have to make use of either JSONP or a middle-agent to retrieve the data (i.e. a PHP script using the CURL library). JSONP functionality is included in numerous Javascript libraries such as MooTools and jQuery. That is the method I would use. MooTools: http://mootools.net/docs/more/Request/Request.JSONP jQuery: http://docs.jquery.com/Release:jQuery_1.2/Ajax Once you have retrieved the body of the page the DIV resides on, you could use REGEX to extract the information from the specific DIV element.
