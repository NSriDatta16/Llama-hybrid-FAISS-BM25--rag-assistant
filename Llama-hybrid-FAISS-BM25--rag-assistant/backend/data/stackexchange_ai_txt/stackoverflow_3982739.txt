Manual Solution: 1- Open the diagram in SSMS 2- Right-Click and select 'View Page Breaks' (If your diagram is displayed across multiple pages, go to #3) 3- drag-and-drop all of you table items into one page space (you are done!) 4- Use the Zoom drop-down control to view your tables (i.e. Fit to, 10%, ... 100%) 5- save As Whatever name you want 6- Print your diagram, and it will fit to one page. -Each one teach one,
