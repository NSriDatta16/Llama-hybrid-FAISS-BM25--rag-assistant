Creating your own threads in a J2EE application is strongly discouraged and is outside the J2EE specification. I don't think you'll find a J2EE library to do what you want. One of the main benefits of J2EE is that the server manages all your threads (and other resources) for you. If you start creating your own threads, the server doesn't know about them and can't look after them, so you're opening yourself up for potential resource leak problems amongst other headaches. To coin a phrase, you don't get a dog and bark yourself. You'd be better off trying to create a solution that uses JMS . JMS is all about processing queues of messages so should be a good match to your requirements. Here's an article that talks about this a bit.
