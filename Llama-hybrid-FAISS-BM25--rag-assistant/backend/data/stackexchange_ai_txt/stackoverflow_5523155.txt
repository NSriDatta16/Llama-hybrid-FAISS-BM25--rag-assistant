"At least one of" can also be written as "not none of", so: !(!B1 && !B2 && !B3) This can also be written as (applying De Morgan's Rule ): B1 || B2 || B3 The rest of what you've specified can more or less be translated directly, with appropriate use of && , || , and parentheses to resolve ambiguity.
