Ok I found a way, not sure if its the most efficient or most elegant but here goes: -sorted_tags = Contact.tag_counts.sort{ |x,y| x.name.upcase y.name.upcase }.map(&:name) %ul -"A".upto("Z") do |l| %li="#{l} = #{ sorted_tags.select{ |x| x.upcase.starts_with?(l)}.map{|k| link_to k, k}.join(" ") }"
