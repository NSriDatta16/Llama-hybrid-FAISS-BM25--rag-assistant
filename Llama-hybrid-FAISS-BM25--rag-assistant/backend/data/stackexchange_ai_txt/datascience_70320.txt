+1 to @Djib2011: LabelEncoder is for the targets/labels, not for other data columns. Also, I agree that generally you don't want an ordinal encoding, when one-hot is more faithful to the original data. But, if you do want to ordinal encode, there's a better way: OrdinalEncoder . And if you want it to only apply to certain columns, you can use ColumnTransformer , e.g. encoder = ColumnTransformer( transformers=[('ord_enc', OrdinalEncoder(), object_columns)], remainder='passthrough' ) df_enc = encoder.fit_transform(df)
