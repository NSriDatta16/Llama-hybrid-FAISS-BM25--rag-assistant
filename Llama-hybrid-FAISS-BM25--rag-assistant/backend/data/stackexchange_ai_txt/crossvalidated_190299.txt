You could use the rfe() function of the caret R package, with SVM as classifier method. With this function, you could choose the resampling method. The function proposes the optimal number of predictors, averaged the results of the resampling. The method is based in the RFE method of Guyon, but incoporates the importance of the variables in each subsample of the resampling loop
