Quasi-separation occurs when one cell has a 0 in it. Since the population probability of one cell in the model you show is 0.001, unless the sample is quite large it is likely that this cell will have 0 in the sample. As to your second question - well, that is how coefficients are calculated in logistic regression. See any good book on the subject; e.g. Kleinbaum and Klein
