Include is similar to inheriting from a another class, in the sense that the methods of the class you include a module into have precedence over the included methods. You can even call super in your class to access the method from the module: class User attr_accessor :name def name super end include UpcasedName end u = User.new u.name = 'john' puts u.name # outputs 'JOHN' Here's an article about it: include vs. extend in Ruby
