take a look at jQuery plugin "Swapable" http://code.google.com/p/jquery-swapable/ it's built on "Sortable" and looks like sortable (drag-n-drop, placeholder, etc.) but only swap two elements: dragged and dropped. All other elements are not affected and stay on their current position.
