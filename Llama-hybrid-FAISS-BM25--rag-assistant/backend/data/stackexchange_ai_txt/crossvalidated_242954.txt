My suggestion would be: 1. Use the median F1 score on each combination of parameters and CV-fold. See: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/F1_score 2. Of each of the 10 F1-scores you get for each parameter set, take the median (average of the middle 2 scores). 3. Pick the parameter set with the highest median F1-score.
