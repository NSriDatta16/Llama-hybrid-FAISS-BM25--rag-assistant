The exception comes from the GDI+ library returning a NotImplemented status from its GdipCreateCustomLineCap() function. Try passing a stroke path instead of Nothing : Dim path2 As GraphicsPath = New GraphicsPath() path2.AddLines(points); _HlpCap = New CustomLineCap(path, path2)
