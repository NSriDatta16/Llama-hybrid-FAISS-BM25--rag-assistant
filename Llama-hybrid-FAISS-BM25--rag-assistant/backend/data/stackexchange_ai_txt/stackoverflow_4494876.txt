The cookies should be archived and persisted when the app is terminated and later reloaded when the app becomes active. You can achieve this by: [[NSHTTPCookieStorage sharedHTTPCookieStorage] setCookies:cookies forURL:url mainDocumentURL:nil]; // where cookies is the unarchived array of cookies
