You cant send messages directly through asp.net. What you can do is send a web request or an email to an online SMS provider, or to a local gateway (like a phone) and it can then pass the message onto the GSM network. Try Clickatell to get an idea of what I mean. http://www.clickatell.com/brochure/coverage.php
