You seem to have a time series of counts . Quoting from book above: All of the methods discussed in this book assume that the data have a continuous sample space. But often data comes in the form of counts. For example, we may wish to forecast the number of customers who enter a store each day. We could have 0, 1, 2, , customers, but we cannot have 3.45693 customers. The author suggest an approach using Croston's methods, usually applied to time series with high number of zeros.
