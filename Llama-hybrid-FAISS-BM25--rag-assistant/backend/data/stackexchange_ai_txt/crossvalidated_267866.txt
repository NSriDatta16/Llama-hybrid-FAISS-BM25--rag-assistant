One thing you must consider is the "level" at which these different variables are measured. Are CO2 emissions measured at the country level? Regional? At what level is agricultural machinery measured? By country? Also, are these variables measured at the same time. Generally, I'd first pay attention to the definitions of these measures and their respective validity.
