I find it more convinient to make vim write both opening and closing tag for me, instead of just the closing one. You can use excellent ragtag plugin by Tim Pope. Usage looks like this (let | mark cursor position) you type: span| press CTRL + x SPACE and you get | You can also use CTRL + x ENTER instead of CTRL + x SPACE , and you get | Ragtag can do more than just it (eg. insert or DOCTYPE). You probably want to check out other plugins by author of ragtag , especially surround .
