Logistic regression does NOT assume a linear relationship between the dependent and independent variables. It does assume a linear relationship between the log odds of the dependent variable and the independent variables (This is mainly an issue with continuous independent variables.) There is a test called the Box-Tidwell that you can use for this. The stata command is boxtid. I don't know the SPSS command, sorry. This resource may be of help -- resource .
