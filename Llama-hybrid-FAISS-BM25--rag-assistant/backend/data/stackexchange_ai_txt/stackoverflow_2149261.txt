The type system is your friend, and the warning is letting you know your function has cracks. The very best approach is to go for a cleaner, more elegant fit between types. Consider ghc's definition of groupBy : groupBy :: (a -> a -> Bool) -> [a] -> [[a]] groupBy _ [] = [] groupBy eq (x:xs) = (x:ys) : groupBy eq zs where (ys,zs) = span (eq x) xs
