Passion Distribution

Suppose the number of tsunami in a season follows a Poisson distribution and the average number of tsunami that hit a region is 5 in every tsunami season A tsunami season lasts for 3 months. In the first month, 2 tsunamis occurred. Write an expression to evaluate the probability that in total, there will be more than 5 tsunami this season, 3 months.
