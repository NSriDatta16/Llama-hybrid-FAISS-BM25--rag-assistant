You describe a very common database construct; it's called a "many-to-many relation". Indexes shouldn't impact this schema at all. In fact, indexes shouldn't impact any schema. Indexes are a trade-off between space and time: indexes specify that you're willing to use extra storage space, in exchange for faster searches through the database. Wikipedia has an excellent article about what database indexes are: Index (database)
