Geoff Hinton is the man when it comes to multilayer perceptrons. His Science paper from 2006 used a special class of MLP called an "autoencoder" that was successful in digit recognition, facial recognition, and document classification (all of which have real world applications): Reducing the Dimensionality of Data with Neural Networks Fortunately, they also published the Matlab code .
