If you're mainly interested in manipulating the tags in a program, and don't need the TreePrint functionality, you can just get the tagged words as a List: LexicalizedParser lp = LexicalizedParser.loadModel("edu/stanford/nlp/models/lexparser/englishPCFG.ser.gz"); Tree parse = lp.apply(Arrays.asList(sent)); List taggedWords = parse.taggedYield();
