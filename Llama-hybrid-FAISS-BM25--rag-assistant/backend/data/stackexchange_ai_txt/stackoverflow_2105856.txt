Much simpler, though there may be gotchas: var index:int = ... var renderer:IListItemRenderer = tree.indexToItemRenderer(index); var item:Object = renderer.data; This won't work if the index is offscreen (since there might not be an active itemRenderer); shouldn't be an issue for drag and drop.
