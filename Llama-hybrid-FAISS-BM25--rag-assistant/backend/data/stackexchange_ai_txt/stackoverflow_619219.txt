Have a look at the exponential smoothing . Fairly simple, and might be sufficient for your needs. Basically recent observations are given relatively more weight than the older ones. Also (depending on the application) you may want to look at various reinforcement learning techniques, for example Q-Learning or TD-Learning or generally speaking any method involving the discount .
