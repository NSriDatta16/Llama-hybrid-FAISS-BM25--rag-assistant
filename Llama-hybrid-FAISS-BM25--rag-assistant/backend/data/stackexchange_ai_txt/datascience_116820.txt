There are many ways to create a vector (i.e., "extract 1 dimensional features ") out of the data: Concatenate the 2 diminesional matrix. Model with Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) and dense layer output The specific choices will have to the actual data (e.g., images, text, or time series).
