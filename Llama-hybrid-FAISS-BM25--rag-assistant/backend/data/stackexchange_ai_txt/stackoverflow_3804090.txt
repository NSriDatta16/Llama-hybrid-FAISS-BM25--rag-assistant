If you're asking for full featured docking, you should probably edit your question title to reflect that. Docking is pretty complicated as it involves dynamic layout, drag-n-drop, and many other things. You could roll your own, essentially with nested JSplitPane and JTabbedPane and a lot of time. Besides JavaDocking , consider the open source (MIT licensed) FlexDock , or the commercial JIDE Docking .
