Text summarization is an active research topic and the question is very broad. For a detailed answer one has to study the state of the art, there's a rich literature on the topic. From a recent survey paper : Deep Learning Models capture both the syntactic and semantic structure. Requirement of large data set limits the use of Deep Learning Models.
