For large projects the approach I like to take is to have one Domain namespace for my business objects and then use Data Transfer Objects (DTO's) in my layers where storage and retrieval of the business object is needed. A DTO is a simple object that doesn't contain any business logic. Here is a link that explains a DTO: http://martinfowler.com/eaaCatalog/dataTransferObject.html
