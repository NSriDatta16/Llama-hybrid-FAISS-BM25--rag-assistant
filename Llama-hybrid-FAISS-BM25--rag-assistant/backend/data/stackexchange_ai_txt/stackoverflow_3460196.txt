It is possible to do this with regexes, however it's fragile. my $string = "var1=100 var2=90 var5=hello var3=\"a, b, c\" var7=test var3=hello"; my $regexp = qr/( (?:\w+=[\w\,]+) | (?:\w+=\"[^\"]*\") )/x; my @matches = $string =~ /$regexp/g;
