Build your string up in steps, doing each encoding layer separately. Much more manageable than trying to cope with the multiple levels of escaping in one go. xml= ' ' % cgi.escape(guid, True) query= 'xml=%s' % urllib2.quote(xml) url= 'http://%s/Altiris/NS/Agent/GetClientPolicies.aspx?%s' % (ns, query) fetch= urllib2.urlopen(url)
