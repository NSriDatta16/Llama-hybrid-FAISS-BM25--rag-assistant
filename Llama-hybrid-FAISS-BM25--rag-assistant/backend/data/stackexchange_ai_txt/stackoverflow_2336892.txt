There are (research) tools based on abstract interpretation or model checking that can show coverage properties without execution, for subsets of language. See, e.g. "Analyzing Functional Coverage in Bounded Model Checking", Grosse, D. Kuhne, U. Drechsler, R. 2008 In general, yes, there are approaches, but they're specialized, and may require some formal methods experience. This kind of stuff is still cutting edge research.
