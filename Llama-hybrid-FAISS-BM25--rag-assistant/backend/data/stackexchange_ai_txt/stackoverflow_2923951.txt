You can get the URI but only without the fragment since that is not transmitted to the server. Try this rule: RewriteRule ^http:/ index.php [L] Then the requested URI path plus query (so the part from the third / up to the first # or the end of the URI) is available at $_SERVER['REQUEST_URI'] .
