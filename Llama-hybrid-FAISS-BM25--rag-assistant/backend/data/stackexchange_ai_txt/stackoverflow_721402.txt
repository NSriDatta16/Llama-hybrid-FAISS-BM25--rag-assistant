HTML::Form works nicely, too. The synopsis of the module is an excellent example: use HTML::Form; $form = HTML::Form->parse($html, $base_uri); $form->value(query => "Perl"); use LWP::UserAgent; $ua = LWP::UserAgent->new; $response = $ua->request($form->click);
