Edit: Obviously Colin Hebert solved the problem, but as a general tip I`d like to say: In Camera.h write #ifndef _CAMERA_H #define _CAMERA_H above all other includes. And include all header files needed in your .cpp file in your .h file. At least that`s what I think is best practice.
