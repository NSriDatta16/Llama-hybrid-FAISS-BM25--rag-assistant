Using a notification icon for this case seems wrong to me. The user's attention is, when entering something into a cell, on the cell. If you display the notification on the lower right of the screen the user is very likely to miss it, or worse, it disrupts his work flow. You might instead consider adding a balloon tip to the cell the user is editing.
