Add the Singleline option: html = Regex.Replace(html, " .*? ", "", RegexOptions.IgnoreCase Or RegexOptions.Singleline) From MSDN : Singleline: Specifies single-line mode. Changes the meaning of the dot (.) so it matches every character (instead of every character except \n). PS: Parsing HTML with regular expressions is discouraged .
