If your signal is very 'spiky', you can remove the peaks by applying a median filter. Here is an example from the MATLAB documentation which should give you an idea how it works. There are also other types of filters for signal smoothing such as using a moving average or the slightly more sophisticated Hampel filter. Again the MATLAB documentation has some very good examples .
