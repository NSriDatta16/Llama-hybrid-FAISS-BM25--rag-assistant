This has already been done and it's free. Download (if you haven't already) the JEDI 's JVCL+JCL from SourceForge and install them. In File | New | Other | Delphi Files... you'll have a new item: 'Jcl Exception dialog for Delphi' . And (imho) this is much more mature/advanced than you can do 'if you'll have time'. Also you can customize the generated Exception Handler to fit to your needs. There is also a support newsgroup for this. As an aside, IIRC, you need only the JCL for the dialog but I'm not sure. Also, you have also commercial options for this (madExcept, EurekaLog etc.) but these are only advanced Error Loggers while JVCL, besides that's free, will give you also over 600 components to play with.
