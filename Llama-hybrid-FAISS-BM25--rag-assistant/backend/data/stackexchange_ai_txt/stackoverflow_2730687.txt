To answer the " Update: " portion of the question, yes, Hudson supports build agents: "Hudson supports the "master/slave" mode, where the workload of building projects are delegated to multiple "slave" nodes, allowing single Hudson installation to host a large number of projects, or provide different environments needed for builds/tests." -- http://wiki.hudson-ci.org/display/HUDSON/Distributed+builds
