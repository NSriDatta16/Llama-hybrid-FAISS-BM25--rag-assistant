Use [NSDate date] to get the current date and time. Store it in touchesBegan: , and fetch the duration in touchesEnded: as this NSTimeInterval duration = [[NSDate date] timeIntervalSinceDate:beganDate]; You will now have the duration between the events measured in seconds in length .
