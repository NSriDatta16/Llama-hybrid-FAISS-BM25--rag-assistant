SELECT * FROM room WHERE room_no NOT IN ( SELECT room_no FROM booking WHERE check_outdate >= @req_fdate AND check_indate = @req_fdate AND check_indate Pay attention to the order or the arguments: @req_fdate here is the first date here ( from ), @req_tdate is the last date ( till ). To check for availability from Aug 16 to Aug 19 , use this: SELECT * FROM room WHERE room_no NOT IN ( SELECT room_no FROM booking WHERE check_outdate >= '2010-08-16' AND check_indate = '2010-08-16' AND check_indate
