It could be indexes. If you have many indexed properties, especially list properties, the data storage number can easily be several times higher than the stats stored data number. There is a good article explaining how space is used. http://code.google.com/appengine/articles/storage_breakdown.html You can also star issue 2740 to request that statistics are provided for indexes too. http://code.google.com/p/googleappengine/issues/detail?id=2740
