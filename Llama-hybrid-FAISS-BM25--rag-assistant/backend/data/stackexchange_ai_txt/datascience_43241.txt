f1-score combines precision and recall in a single figure. As both are pretty similar in A and C cases, f1-score is similar too. Your choice depends on what it is less harmful in your categorization: false positives or false negatives. I do recommend you to read the 3rd chapter of "DEEP LEARNING:From Basics to Practice" volume 1 by Andrew Glassner . There you have the three concepts (precision, recall and f1-score) described in a very illustrative way.
