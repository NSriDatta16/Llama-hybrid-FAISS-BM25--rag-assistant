Are you a paid up developer ? Have you created a provisioning profile for your application ? Have you installed a provisioning profile via the Organizer? if you have, then you need to go to the KeyChain utility, ensure that Login is the default Keychain, and that iPhone Developer:yournamehere certificate is installed along with the public and private keys of your dev certificate. if you haven't, then you need to follow the steps in the provisioning utility http://developer.apple.com/iphone/manage/overview/index.action
