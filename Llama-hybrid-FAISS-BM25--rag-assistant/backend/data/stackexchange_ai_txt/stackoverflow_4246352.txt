The GetList() method returns a CAML fragment that includes the list's field (column) definitions. You might want to try an XPath expression : XmlNode list = yourListService.GetList("yourListName"); XmlNodeList visibleColumns = list.SelectNodes("Fields/Field[not(@Hidden) or @Hidden='FALSE']");
