Project Euler is a fun way for computer programmers to find out more about maths. Try working through the first few problems in the language of your choice. Solving a problem will unlock discussion and sometimes a PDF explaining the mathematical background. For stuff that's specifically relevant to machine learning and NLP, you might want to check out some of the answers to this question .
