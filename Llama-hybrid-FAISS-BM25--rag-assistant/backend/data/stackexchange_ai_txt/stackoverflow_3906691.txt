Well, there is a solution backed by Hibernate providing history, it's called Envers , but from what I remember, there are some minor drawbacks. There also is another solution : JCR. A Java Content Repository is a non-relationnal DB, that is far more compatible with your history needs (as it can use, as an example, a SVN repository as storage back-end). You can take a look at modeshape , as an example.
