need the correct path for your file, if on SD card then... sendIntent.putExtra(Intent.EXTRA_STREAM, Uri.parse ("file://"+Environment.getExternalStorageDirectory()+getString(R.string.csv_title)")); Look here for addition information on setting the appropriate file path http://developer.android.com/guide/topics/data/data-storage.html#filesExternal
