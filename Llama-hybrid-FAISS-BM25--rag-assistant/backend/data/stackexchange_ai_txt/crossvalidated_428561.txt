Let alternative $j$ have indirect utility $v_j$ for $j=1,...,J$ and let $\epsilon =(\epsilon_1,...,\epsilon_J)$ be multivariate normal with covariance $\Omega$ then choice probability is $$Pr(j=\arg \max_h \{v_h + \epsilon_h\})$$ defines choice probability of agent choosing alternative $j$ in a multinomial probit model. See page 111 of Kenneth Trains book Discrete Choice Methods with Simulation here
