len = max(key for (item, key) in list) newlist = [[] for i in range(len+1)] for item,key in list: newlist[key].append(item) You can do it in a single list comprehension, perhaps more elegant but O(n**2): [[item for (item,key) in list if key==i] for i in range(max(key for (item,key) in list)+1)]
