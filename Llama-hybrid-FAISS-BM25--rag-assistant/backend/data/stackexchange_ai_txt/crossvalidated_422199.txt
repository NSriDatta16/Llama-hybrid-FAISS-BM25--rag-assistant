Lots of good references: Gelman and Hill, Data Analysis Using Regression and Multilevel/Hierarchical Models Pesaran, H. M., Time Series and Panel Data Econometrics Gelman and Hill's book is more applied while Pesaran has made original contributions by developing and extending classic univariate time series tests for stationarity, autoregression, unit roots, weak dependence between cross-sections, and so on, to panel data models.
