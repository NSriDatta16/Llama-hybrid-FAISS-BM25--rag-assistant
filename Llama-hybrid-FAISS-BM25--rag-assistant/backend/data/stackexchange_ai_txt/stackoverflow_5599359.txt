The real problem is that omitting around function calls is rather fragile. Expect weird errors if you do. $ perl -we'$file="abc"; open(my $fh, $file);' $ perl -we'$file="abc"; open my $fh, $file;' Parentheses missing around "my" list at -e line 1.
