It might help to put breakpoints in your buttonPressed method to know if it even makes it that far and on which line it'll break. You might not have set up your referencing outlets. Control-click on File's Owner and drag the blue arrow to your different components and, in the popup window, select the object in your ViewController to link it to. For example, the button should link up to mashButton, one textfield to word1, another to word2, etc.
