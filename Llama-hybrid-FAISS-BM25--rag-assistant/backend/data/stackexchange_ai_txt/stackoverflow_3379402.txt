Say, you have the month number from 1 to 12 in row 1. Add a helper row, containing the formula =MOD(A1-8+12,12), =MOD(B1-8+12,12), ... (add the formula in the first cell and drag it to the right; "8" is for "September") Sort the columns by this value.
