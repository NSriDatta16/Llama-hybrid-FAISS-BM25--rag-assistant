I'm not too sure how you want to retrieve the info, but something like the below may work. (It's probably close to what Hogan mentioned.) If you want to retrieve data for a type, you can just JOIN two tables. If you want to retrieve data for all types (with all fields), you can LEFT JOIN all tables. ----Items +Item_id +Item_Type_Id +Item_Common_Field1 +Item_Common_Field1 ... ----Item_Type_A +Item_id +Item_Type_A_Specific_Field1 +Item_Type_A_Specific_Field2 ... ----Item_Type_B +Item_id +Item_Type_B_Specific_Field1 ...
