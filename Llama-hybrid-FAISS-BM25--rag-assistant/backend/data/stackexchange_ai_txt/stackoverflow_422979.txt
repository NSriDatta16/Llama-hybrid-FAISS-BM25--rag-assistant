Have you tried identifying them by their user-agent information? A simple google search should give you the user-agents used by Google etc. This, of course, is not foolproof, but most crawlers by major companies supply a distinct user-agent. EDIT: Assuming you do not want to restrict the bots access, but just not count its visit in your statistc.
