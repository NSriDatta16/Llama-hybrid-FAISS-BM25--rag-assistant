Many major aggregators report user stats by including them as part of the useragent string. Examples: Bloglines reporting description in blog comment Google Reader: Tips for Publishers PostRank: Reporting Subscription Counts There's no standard for this at this time. To the best of my knowledge, folks will contact major feed analytics vendors like Feedburner directly, to make sure their useragent-based reporting is being counted.
