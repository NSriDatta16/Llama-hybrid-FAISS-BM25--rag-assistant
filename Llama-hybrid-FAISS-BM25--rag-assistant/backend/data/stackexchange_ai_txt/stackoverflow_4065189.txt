This should work: extended_user = ExtendedUser(user_ptr_id=auth_user.pk) extended_user.__dict__.update(auth_user.__dict__) extended_user.save() Here you're basically just copying over the values from the auth_user version into the extended_user one, and re-saving it. Not very elegant, but it works.
