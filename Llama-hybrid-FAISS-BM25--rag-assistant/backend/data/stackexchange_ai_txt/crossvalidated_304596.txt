By default it's cosine similarity, with vectors averaged over the document for missing words. You can also customize this, by setting a hook to doc.user_hooks['similarity'] . This pipeline component wraps similarity functions, making it easy to customise the similarity: https://github.com/explosion/spaCy/blob/develop/spacy/pipeline.pyx#L50
