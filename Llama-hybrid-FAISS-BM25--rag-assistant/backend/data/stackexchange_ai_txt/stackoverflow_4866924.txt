Determining the speed of a particular operation is often known as profiling.The best solution for profiling an operation is to use a profiler. Visual Studio has a good profiler. Linux has gprof . If your compiler doesn't have a profiler, it might be worthwhile purchasing a compiler that does if you will often be profiling your code. If you have to get by without using a professional profiler, then you can usually get by embedding your own into your program check this out for codes of some profilers.
