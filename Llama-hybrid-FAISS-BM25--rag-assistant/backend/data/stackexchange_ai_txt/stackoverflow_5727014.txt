Subclass the UITableViewCell and override the UIResponder with these: - (void)touchesBegan:(NSSet *)touches withEvent:(UIEvent *)event; - (void)touchesEnded:(NSSet *)touches withEvent:(UIEvent *)event; Then you will have 2 separated events when press and when release.
