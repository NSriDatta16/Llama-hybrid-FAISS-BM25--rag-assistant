HASHBYTES with MD5 is 5 times slower than CHECKSUM, I've tested this on a table with over 1 million rows, and ran each test 5 times to get an average. Interestingly CHECKSUM takes exactly the same time as BINARY_CHECKSUM. Here is my post with the full results published: http://networkprogramming.wordpress.com/2011/01/14/binary_checksum-vs-hashbytes-in-sql/
