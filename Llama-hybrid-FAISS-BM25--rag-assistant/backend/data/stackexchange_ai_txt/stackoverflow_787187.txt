No, floats can be automatically upcast to doubles, but doubles can never be floats without explicit casting because doubles have the larger range. float range is 1.40129846432481707e-45 to 3.40282346638528860e+38 double range is 4.94065645841246544e-324d to 1.79769313486231570e+308d
