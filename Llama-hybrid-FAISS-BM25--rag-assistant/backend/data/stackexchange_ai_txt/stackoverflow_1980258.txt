If you just want to overlay images, you can simply drag UIImageViews around and position them within the view hierarchy such that the appropriate images are overlaid one on top of the other. For generating a new image out of those composited images, you may want to use something like CGContextDrawImage() within a UIView subclass's -drawRect: method or in a custom CGContext. You can also refer to these similar questions: How can we combine images and get image object from this two images in iphone apps Combining images As far as selectively replacing a specific color within a UIImage, that's a commonly asked question here: In a UIImage (or its derivatives), how can I replace one color with another? How to make one color transparent on a UIImage?
