#3 is the key: 2-3 char passwords are too vulnerable to dictionary attacks (too few combinations, and thus able to include the universe in the attack combinations). Upper limit is irrelevant but anything over 20 characters means that the user is likely to be copying/pasting most, which is bad practice that should not be encouraged.
