media="handheld" doesn't work with modern smartphones like the iphone which pretend to be a desktop browser (uses the screen media type). http://detectmobilebrowser.com/ Free & open source, has a comprehensive mobile user-agent checker available in many languages - javascript, php, asp.net, ruby, etc.
