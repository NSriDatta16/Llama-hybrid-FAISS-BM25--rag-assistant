Well, the short of it.. $ch = curl_init("https://api.twitter.com/1/users/show.json?screen_name=USERNAME"); curl_setopt($ch, CURLOPT_RETURNTRANSFER, true); curl_setopt($ch, CURLOPT_USERAGENT, 'SOmehitn/3.2'); $ret = json_decode(curl_exec($ch)); curl_close($ch); unset($ch); print_r($ret); Just like hitting any other RESTful API.
