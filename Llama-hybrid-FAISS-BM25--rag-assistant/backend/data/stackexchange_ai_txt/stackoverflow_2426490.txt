Check out this link: http://www.codeproject.com/KB/applications/xcmyk.aspx . It gives this formula. Black = minimum(1-Red,1-Green,1-Blue) Cyan = (1-Red-Black)/(1-Black) Magenta = (1-Green-Black)/(1-Black) Yellow = (1-Blue-Black)/(1-Black)
