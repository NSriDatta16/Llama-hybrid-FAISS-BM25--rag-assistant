Yes. The number of lines deleted is significant if you consider line counts significant. But that begs the question of what use you are going to make of these line counts. I certainly hope that you don't say that the person with the largest line counts is the most productive. That certainly isn't true. Indeed, if you make it clear that people are being assessed on the number of lines they commit, you only encourage people to do things to inflate their commit line counts.
