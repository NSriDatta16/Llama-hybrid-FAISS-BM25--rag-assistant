By all means write code-first to gain experience with a platform - just be brave and throw it away once you're sure you can tackle most tasks on that platform. I've just done that with a small Java program I've started. Now that I've gained some experience I can see what I have to do to get very high line coverage now that I'm starting to write it test-first, even most of the plumbing.
