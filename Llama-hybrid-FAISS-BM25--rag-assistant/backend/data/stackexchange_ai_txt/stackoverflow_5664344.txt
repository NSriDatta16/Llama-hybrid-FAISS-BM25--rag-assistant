You can't, it's hard-wired at 5MB. This is a design decision by the Chrome developers . In Chrome, the Web SQL db and cache manifest also have low limits by default, but if you package the app for the Chrome App Store you can increase them . See also Managing HTML5 Offline Storage - Google Chrome .
