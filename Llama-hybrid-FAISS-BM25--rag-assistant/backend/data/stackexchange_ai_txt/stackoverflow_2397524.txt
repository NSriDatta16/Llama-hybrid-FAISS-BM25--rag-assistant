Well, yes - you can definitely add your indices later on. However: if you develop your app without any indices, and only a handful of test rows in your tables, you might not get a good feeling for how it'll behave under load. Using the right indices, you can gain a lot of performance boosts - and having no indices, or the wrong ones, can severely and negatively impact your app. So in my opinion, being able to have the indices in place during development, and paying attention to them, is crucial for your app to get a good understanding of where and how you need to possibly tweak your settings.
