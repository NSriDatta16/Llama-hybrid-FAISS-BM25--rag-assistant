You could do a project on ad-hoc communication networks between vehicles. There is an open-source traffic simulation model called SUMO which you can leverage. The wiki for SUMO lists a bunch of existing research and tools that you can use as a starting point: http://sourceforge.net/apps/mediawiki/sumo/index.php?title=Main_Page#Related_to_C2C
