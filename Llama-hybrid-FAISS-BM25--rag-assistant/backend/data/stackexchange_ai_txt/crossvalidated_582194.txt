Since the symptoms are mutually exclusive, a chisquare test on the complete table is meaningful, but not very informative. I would fit a logistic regression, but a loglinear model as suggested by @Christian Hennig: Is probably equivalent. There are many posts here about contingency tables and logistic regression, see Logistic regression vs chi-square in a 2x2 and Ix2 (single factor - binary response) contingency tables? and chi square test vs logistic regression result
