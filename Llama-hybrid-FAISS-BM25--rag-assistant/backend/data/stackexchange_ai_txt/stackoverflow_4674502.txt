I think this might work for you. You can choose where to append your sortable helper with the following: $( ".selector" ).sortable({ appendTo: 'div.wrapper' }); From the docs: "appendTo: Defines where the helper that moves with the mouse is being appended to during the drag (for example, to resolve overlap/zIndex issues)."
