Bayesian model comparison refers to comparing two or more models; Bayesian model criticism refers to evaluating the fit of a single model (without a competing model to compare with). Regarding your second question about hypothesis testing, the use of Bayes factors has been likened to hypothesis testing. Bayes factors is a form of model selection or model comparison.
