Yes. The template will also contain the definition of event receivers that are registered when you save the template. If you deactivate your feature you remove those event receiver registrations, and so your template won't contain those receiver registrations. It's one of the awkward things about creating site templates in 2010 - you've gott pay attention to what features are active.
