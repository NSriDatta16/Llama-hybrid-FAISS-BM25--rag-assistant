While this doesn't exactly answer your question, Runescape uses a white list of phrases, rather than words. The implementation in Runescape is awkward, because there are so many phrases to choose from. You have to go through 3 or 4 menus sometimes to get to the phrase you want. If you can come up with a better organization of phrases, then this might work for you.
