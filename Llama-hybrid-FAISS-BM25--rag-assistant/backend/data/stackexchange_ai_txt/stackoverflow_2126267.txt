You can add references to external stylesheets simply by adding dynamic HTML content in the head: $('head').append(' '); This content gets inserted in the DOM, and subsequently rendered normally, in this case causing a fetch of an external stylesheet. Pay attention to cletus' answer as well however, as if you're not careful with dynamic loading of static content, it can end up costing you in page load time and excessive resource transfer.
