"keg_only" will keep everything from being linked in. To keep "most" stuff from being linked in, you can install everything to "libexec" and then symlink from there to bin, or write shim-scripts to bin that call things out of libexec. Lots of Java brews use this method, see "ActiveMQ" or "Gant" for examples.
