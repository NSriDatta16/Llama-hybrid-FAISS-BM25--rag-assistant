You can use any number of local settings on the dev box that won't be present in the official build or test boxes, and read those to determine if you need to switch to dev-box specific behavior. For example, a file, an XML File, a registry key, a preprocessor directive (as you mentioned). As Robert Harvey suggested, application settings are another way to do it.
