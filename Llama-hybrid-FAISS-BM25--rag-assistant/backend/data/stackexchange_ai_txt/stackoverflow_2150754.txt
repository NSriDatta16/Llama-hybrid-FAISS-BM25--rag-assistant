Simple and elegant? Not so much...but that's T-SQL for you: DECLARE @number decimal(5,2) = 123.00 DECLARE @formatted varchar(5) = CAST(@number as varchar) SELECT LEFT( @formatted, LEN(@formatted) - PATINDEX('%[^0.]%', REVERSE(@formatted)) + 1 )
