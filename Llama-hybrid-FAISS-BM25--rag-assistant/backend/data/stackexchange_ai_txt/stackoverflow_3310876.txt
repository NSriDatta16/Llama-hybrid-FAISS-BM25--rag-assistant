Not sure exactly what you want to achieve. Ruby uses references for all custom objects already. You may want to look at the delegate library. require 'delegate' obj = "foo" ptr = SimpleDelegator.new(obj) ptr.upcase # => "FOO" other_obj = "bar" ptr.__setobj__(other_obj) # make ptr point to another object
