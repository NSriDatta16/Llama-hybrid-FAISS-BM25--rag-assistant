Let $x_t$ denote the value of the time series of interest at time $t$ . Let $\Delta x_t:=x_t-x_{t-1}$ denote the increment in $x_t$ from $t-1$ to $t$ . Let hats ( $\widehat{}$ ) denote predictions. If you have $x_t$ and $\widehat{\Delta x}_{t+1},\dots,\widehat{\Delta x}_{t+5}$ available, then it is straightforward to obtain $\hat x_{t+5}:$ $$ \hat x_{t+5}=x_t+\widehat{\Delta x}_{t+1}+\dots+\widehat{\Delta x}_{t+5}. $$
