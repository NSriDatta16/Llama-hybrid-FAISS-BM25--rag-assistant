The training is based on the boxes content only, but during the detection process, the algorithm has to scan all the image. Consequently, there is no learning of the environment outside the box. Such algorithms only focus on detecting specific objects , independently from their surrounding environment. However, tensorflow could be used to apply contextual object recognition , but it requires additional function such as an attention mechanism.
