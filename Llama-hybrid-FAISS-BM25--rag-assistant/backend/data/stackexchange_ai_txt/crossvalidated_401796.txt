Revenue is a continuous (actually ratio scale) variable, so logistic regression is not appropriate. You should look into (multiple) linear regression. I have been told to use logistic Regression to predict the revenue of the restaurant Who told you? Ask him/her to show you how! When you have fitted a linear regression, you should criticize the model with residual plots and so on. If that shows problems with linear regression you can come back here and ask.
