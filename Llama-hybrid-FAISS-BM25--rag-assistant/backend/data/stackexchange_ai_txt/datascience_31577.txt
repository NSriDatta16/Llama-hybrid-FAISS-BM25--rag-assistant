You can do following the two approaches. As others mentioned already, you can change the distribution to 50-50 in the training dataset. You can use weighted random forest algorithm for creating a balance between the two classes. In this algorithm, Random Forest itself adds weight to the two classes in order to achieve 50-50 percent weight.
