After consideration of wrapper workaround I found that using existing COM Automation object has some advantages versus developing your own API. Since I already use Excel.Application object for other purposes I just created a couple of macros in VBA and execute them as needed. More details in my blog posts: http://automation-beyond.com/2009/09/21/gp-qtp-automation-sanscript/ http://automation-beyond.com/2009/09/23/gp-automation-vbscript-limitation/ Thank you, Albert Gareev
