You can specify the parameters along with which extension files you want and which to exclude. Example %1\Paraffin.exe -dir "..\..\..\..\Application" -dirref INSTALLLOCATION -custom App ..\..\AppFragment.wxs -guids -ext .vspscc -ext .cs -ext .resx -ext .csproj -ext .exclude -ext LocalAppSettings.config -ext .pdb -direXclude UITests -direXclude obj -direXclude Netrics -direXclude XSLT -multiple
