No - session storage should not be used for transaction data. Even if you can ensure that users do not use the back and forward buttons, even if you can ensure that they will not open multiple windows, your code will get tied in knots. It's not hard to... echo ' Remove '; is it? Although really, that kind of operation should be done in a POST rather than a GET (you can have lost of forms on the page, or a single form and javascripts to write a hidden filed and submit the form).
