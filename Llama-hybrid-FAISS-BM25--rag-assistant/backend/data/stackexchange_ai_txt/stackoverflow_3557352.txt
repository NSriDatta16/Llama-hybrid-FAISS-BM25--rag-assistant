The 100% guaranteed way is to render it to an image. CSS3 supports font-face but this will not give you wide support. @font-face { font-family: 'Myriad Pro'; src: url('Myriad-Pro.ttf'); } You will also need to check legal agreements to see if they allow embedding this font in your pages.
