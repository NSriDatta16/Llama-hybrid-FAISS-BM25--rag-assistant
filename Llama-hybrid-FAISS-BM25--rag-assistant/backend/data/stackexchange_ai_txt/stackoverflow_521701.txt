Domain objects should be independent of storage, you should use the Repostiory pattern, or DAO to persist the objects. That way you are enforcing separation of concerns, the object itself should not know about how it is stored. Ideally, it would be a good idea to put query construction inside of the repository, though I would use an ORM inside there. Here's Martin Fowler's definition of the Repository Pattern.
