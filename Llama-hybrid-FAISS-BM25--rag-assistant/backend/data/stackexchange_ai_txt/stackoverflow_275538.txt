It depends on the OS and whether or not roaming user profiles are enabled. For example, on XP, with non-roaming profiles, the location is \Documents and Settings\ \Local Settings\Application Data\Microsoft\IsolatedStorage On Vista with roaming profile storage, \Users\ \AppData\Roaming\Microsoft\IsolatedStorage See an Introduction to Isolated Storage for more info.
