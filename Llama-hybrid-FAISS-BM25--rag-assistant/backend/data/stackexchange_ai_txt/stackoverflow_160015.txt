In Xcode select window->organizer and expand the node next to your application in the applications section on your phone. Select the black downward pointing arrow next to application data and save the file anywhere on your desktop. Your sqlite database should be in there somewhere. As for how to go about getting it back on the phone once your done i have no clue.
