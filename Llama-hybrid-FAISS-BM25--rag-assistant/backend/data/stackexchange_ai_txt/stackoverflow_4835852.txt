For prosperity, the Silverlight Toolkit offers a set of DragDropTarget's which can be used to reorder items within a ListBox , TreeView , or a simple ItemsControl . You can also drag items between ItemsControls . Run the Silverligh 4 samples project and look at 'Drag and Drop' under the Toolkit section. http://silverlight.codeplex.com/ http://www.silverlight.net/content/samples/sl4/toolkitcontrolsamples/run/default.html The controls you want are: ListBoxDragDropTarget TreeViewDragDropTarget DataPointSeriesDragDropTarget
