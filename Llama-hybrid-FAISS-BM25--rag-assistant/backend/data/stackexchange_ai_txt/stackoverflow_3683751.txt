If I understand the question correctly, you want to implement your report in a format similar to a Pivot table (as can be found in Excel). The Crystal equivalent of this is call a Cross-Tab - you can add one to your report by selecting Insert>Cross-Tab... . This will bring up the Cross-Tab Expert, which will enable you to drag and drop the fields you want to see down and across the page into the appropriate places.
