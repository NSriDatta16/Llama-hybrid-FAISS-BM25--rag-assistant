AI models, particularly deep learning models need large amounts of training data. The performance of such models do not usually show intelligence on unseen test data. So, when we say prediction performance " In The Wild ", it means how it performs on real-world scenario. In computer vision, static refers to image processing. Usually, Static data does not exhibit stochastic behavior. The opposite of static is " Dynamic ".
