you can also programmatically direct the browser to ditch the cache for the selected page (though it's up to the browser to actually honor your directive). Public Sub KillCache() Response.Cache.SetCacheability(System.Web.HttpCacheability.NoCache) Response.Cache.SetExpires(New Date(1900, 1, 1)) Response.Cache.SetMaxAge(New TimeSpan(0, 0, 5)) '// 5 SECONDS' Response.Cache.SetNoServerCaching() Response.Cache.SetNoStore() Response.Cache.SetRevalidation(HttpCacheRevalidation.AllCaches) End Sub
