Other proposition of mine using Fold. It is linear function O(N). But definitely not so elegant and simple as DannyAsher's solution: let each5 xs = let fu (left, next, acc) x = left@[x], List.tl next, (x, left@(List.tl next))::acc let (x, y, res) = List.fold fu ([], xs, []) xs res
