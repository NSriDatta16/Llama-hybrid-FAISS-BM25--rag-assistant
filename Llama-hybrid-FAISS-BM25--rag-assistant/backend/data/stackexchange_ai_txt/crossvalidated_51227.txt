Models such as these don't usually come off-the-shelf. There are general techniques, but you have to adapt them to your use case, and that means you have to get familiar with modeling. The other answers are excellent; for a take on these issues from the Bayesian perspective, see: Marc Kery's Introduction to WinBUGS for Ecologists Marc Kery's Bayesian Population Analysis McCarthy's Bayesian Methods for Ecology King et al's Bayesian Analysis for Population Ecology Kery's first book is an really good introduction.
