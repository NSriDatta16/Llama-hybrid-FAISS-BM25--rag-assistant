Make the TBitmap, named b, a global variable and remove the line ImageList1.Add(b, nil); from the Label1StartDrag procedure and place it in an OnDragOverProcedure. This will allow ImageList1 to remain blank until the mouse has moved the four pixels specified in Label1.BeginDrag(False, 4);
