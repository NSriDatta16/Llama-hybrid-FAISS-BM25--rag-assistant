This problem is called text classification (it belongs to the more general case of document classification ). There are plenty of resources online about this, e.g. here , here or here . There are also a lot of research papers on the topic. General text classification consists in two steps: Represent the text as features Train a classification model The first step is specific to text, as opposed to the second step which is general ML. There are plenty of options to represent text as features, from traditional bag of words representation to word embeddings . In this question I explained the principle of the traditional BoW representation.
