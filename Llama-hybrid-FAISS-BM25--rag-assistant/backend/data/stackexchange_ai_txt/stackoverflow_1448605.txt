The RSS feed should be formatted like any other with enclosures. There are hundreds of resources on the web describing how to make such a feed. This here tells you about the iTunes-specific tags needed in your RSS feed. Note, Apple has seriously broken podcast support in iTunes 9, so don't get discouraged if it doesn't appear to work properly: it's likely Apple's fault.
