Fraud Analytics Using Descriptive, Predictive, and Social Network Techniques: A Guide to Data Science for Fraud Detection Bart Baesens, Veronique Van Vlasselaer, Wouter Verbeke ISBN: 978-1-119-13312-4 Aug 2015
