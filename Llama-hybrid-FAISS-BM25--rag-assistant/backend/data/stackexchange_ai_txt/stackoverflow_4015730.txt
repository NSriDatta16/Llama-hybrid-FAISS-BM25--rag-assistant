Turns out cocos2d moves in mysterious ways. Adding the background to an otherwise empty wrapping CCSprite gets the framerate back up to 60: CCSprite *spback = [(CCSprite*)[CCSprite alloc] init]; [self addChild:spback]; CCSprite *sp = [CCSprite spriteWithFile:@"Background.png"]; sp.position = ccp(1024/2, 768/2); [spback addChild:sp]; Credits for this goes to yaoligang on the cocos2d forums.
