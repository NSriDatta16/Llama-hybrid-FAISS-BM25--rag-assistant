The specific solutions posted here address your current situation, I'd just like to touch on the reasoning behind them. Javascript logic is executed in your browser. PHP logic is executed on the server. Embedding conditional PHP statements directly in javascript won't do what you want, but you can use PHP to generate the javascript your browser needs to execute.
