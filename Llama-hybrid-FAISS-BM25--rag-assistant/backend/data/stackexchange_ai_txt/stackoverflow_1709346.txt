If possible, pair up junior members with more senior members. Either way, definitely have code reviews. I'd also encourage them to have scheduled workshops or discussions so that they can get more well-rounded skills and to increase their exposure to different areas that they might not currently be aware of. I'd also encourage them to go to user group meetings.
