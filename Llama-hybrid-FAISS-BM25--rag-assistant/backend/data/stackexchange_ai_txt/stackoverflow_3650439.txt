The following is how i do it in NHibernate DetachedCriteria newCriteria = CriteriaTransformer.TransformToRowCount(criteria); Further more if you want to add some other information like SUM's you can add those with projections like thus: newCriteria.SetProjection(Projections.ProjectionList() .Add(Projections.RowCount(), "rows") .Add(Projections.Sum("TaxAmount.Decimal")) .Add(Projections.Sum("NetAmount.Decimal")) .Add(Projections.Sum("GrossAmount.Decimal")));
