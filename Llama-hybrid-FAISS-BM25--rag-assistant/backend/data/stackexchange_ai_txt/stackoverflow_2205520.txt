If the "some text here" sums up the section(s) or paragraph(s) that come directly after it, then it can be considered a header. Otherwise, it's just another piece of text. Another good rule of thumb is that headers should not contain colons. So: Inquiries Please send your inquiries to blabla. But: Send your inquiries to: blabla
