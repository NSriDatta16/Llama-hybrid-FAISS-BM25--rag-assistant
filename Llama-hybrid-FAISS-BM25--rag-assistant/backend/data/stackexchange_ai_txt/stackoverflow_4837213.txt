Backbone.js works just as well with local storage as it does with RESTful queries. I'm a learn-by-example kind of guy so here are some links to get you started: Todos , a todo application that uses local storage and backbone.js, check out the annotated source to see how it works. The localStorage adapter is all you need to get started, take a look at the annotated source of that too.
