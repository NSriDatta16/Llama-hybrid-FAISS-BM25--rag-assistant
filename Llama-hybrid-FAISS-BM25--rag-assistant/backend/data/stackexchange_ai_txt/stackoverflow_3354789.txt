There are two things you could do to solve your problem: require comma-separated arguments which are going to be grouped into the following option value, you could use getopt , or any other module then. or do more fragile own processing: sys.argv.pop() cmd = {} while sys.argv: arg = sys.argv.pop(0) if arg == '--arg1': cmd[arg] = sys.argv.pop(0), sys.argv.pop(0) elif: pass print(cmd)
