You can't. Not since IE7. Click on the "down" arrow just to the right of the "house" icon in your Internet Explorer toolbar and choose "Add or Change Home Page." Then click on "Use this webpage as your only home page" or "Add this webpage to your home page tabs," depending on your preference. To make this page your home page, just drag the icon to the left of the URL in your location bar onto the "Home" icon in the "house" icon in your toolbar. Some browsers do this differently. You can always set your home page through the preferences of your browser.
