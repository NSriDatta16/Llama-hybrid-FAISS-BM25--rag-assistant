MLE (Maximum Likelihood Estimation) is discussed a lot because it is relatively straightforward and commonly applicable. The downside is that MLE is not always the best method for a given context or it might not even be possible to be applied. In practice, gradient descent and other methods are commonly used for estimating parameters in logistic regression.
