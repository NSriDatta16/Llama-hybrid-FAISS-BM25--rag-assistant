Do a subject-wise cross validation using both discriminant analysis and logistic regression. Whichever is giving better performance (accuracy, sensitivity, specificity and AUC) in cross-validation, use that. Refer to this link, for more information on why to use subject-wise cross validation https://stats.stackexchange.com/a/240033/86202
