Disqus is built on Django, and we use a combination of Bayesian filtering (with a steady stream of ham and spam fed through our comments), rate and likeness limiting, selective captchas, and--at the user's discretion--akismet. We're still fighting, as are all spam-susceptible web services, but those are some fairly common techniques.
