You should definitely not use one hot encoding with values which represent numbers, as this removes the natural order between your intervals. So these values should be represented as numbers: Either with the average of the limits indeed Or a simple integer encoding of the intervals, e.g. tumor sizes 0-4, 5-9, 10-14,... would be represented as 0,1,2,...
