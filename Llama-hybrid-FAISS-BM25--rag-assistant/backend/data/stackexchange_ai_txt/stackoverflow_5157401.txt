Rotating AdSense ad units on a page is probably a violation of the AdSense Program Policies , specifically "Direct user attention to the ads using arrows or other graphical gimmicks." It could jeopardize your account. Besides, what would be the point of rotating the ad units in the first place? The same ads would show, because the content of the page isn't changing.
