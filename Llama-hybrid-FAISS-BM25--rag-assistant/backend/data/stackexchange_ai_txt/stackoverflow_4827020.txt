Flash menu with animated pop out on hover

I have a problem with a Flash menu I have inherited from our graphics designer. It seems to be working to a certain extent but I can't seem to pick out the error in the Actionscript 3.0 code. Basically we need each bar in the menu (which you can find at the link) to pop out when hovered over, and when the mouse moves away from the bar, it needs to roll back in. This works when you do it slowly but when you hover over each menu item quickly it does some strange things. I don't have much experience in Flash but from a programming point of view, my theory is that no threading is being used. By this I mean that the mouse over is happening so quickly that it ignores the mouse out for one menu item and skips to the mouse in for the other. I hope this makes sense.
