Make sure the new file is included in the target you're trying to build. Click on the filename in the left pane of XCode and open the inspector (command-I), then look at the "targets" tab to verify that the target you're building is checked. If necessary, you can also expand the target (in the left pane) to see the build steps, and drag the file directly into the build step (for example "Compile Sources") where it needs to be.
