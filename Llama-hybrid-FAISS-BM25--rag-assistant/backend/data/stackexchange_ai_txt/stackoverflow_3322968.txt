Reia is a scripting language for distributed system: Reia aims to expose the powerful capabilities of Erlang in a way which is easier for your average programmer to understand. It aims to bring the beauty and simplicity of Ruby, a language which is easy and fun to program in, to Erlang, a language which very few will think of as easy or fun to use.
