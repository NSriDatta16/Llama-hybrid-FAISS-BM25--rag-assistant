Firstly.. I did not understand the concept of unbinding onPause(). If you do this, the service will start and stop with your activity (Which also means the service is essentially an activity). This is what my application did. When user selected Start from (obviously) activity, the service has started with the following code. startService(new Intent(this, MyService.class)); And when the user selects Stop (either same button or another button) stopService(new Intent(this, MUMService.class)); On the Service class, I have implemented onStart and onDestroy methods to control the service process.
