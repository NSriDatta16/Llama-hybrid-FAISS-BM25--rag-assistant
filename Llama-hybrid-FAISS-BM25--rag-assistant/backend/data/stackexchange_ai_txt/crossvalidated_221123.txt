I think this is what you're looking for. This guy proved that a multilayer, or even a single layer feedforward network could approximate any function, provided that the net has enough hidden units. Hornik, K. (1991). Approximation capabilities of multilayer feedforward networks. Neural networks, 4(2), 251-257.
