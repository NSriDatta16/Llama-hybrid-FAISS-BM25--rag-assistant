Whilst it is a cop out answer, I would actually encourage you to make your code thread-safe. The behaviour of MSTest is to ensure isolation as Richard has pointed out. By encountering problems with your unit tests you are proving that there could be some problems in the future. You could ignore them, use NUnit, or deal with them and continue to use MSTest.
