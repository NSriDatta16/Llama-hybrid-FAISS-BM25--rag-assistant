You may want to use a schema where the message body and attachment records can be shared between multiple recipients on the message. It's not uncommon to see email servers where fully 50% of the disk storage is used by duplicate emails. A simple hash of the body/attachment would be enough to see if that record was already in the database. However, you would still need to keep separate headers.
