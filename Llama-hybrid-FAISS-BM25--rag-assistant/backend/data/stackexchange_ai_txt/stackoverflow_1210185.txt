Application bundles are just folders. Navigate to one in finder, and right-click -> 'Show Package Contents'. An exciting world awaits. Dragging the application bundle into the library in iTunes should take care of it, however you can also distribute an .ipa file, which is just a renamed .zip with a directory called Payload in it, with the application bundle inside.
