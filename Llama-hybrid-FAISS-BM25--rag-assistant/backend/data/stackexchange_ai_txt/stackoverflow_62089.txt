I'd ask about the differences between the the various storage engines, their perceived benefits and drawbacks. Defiantly cover replication, and dig into the drawbacks of replication, esp when using tables with auto increment keys. If they are still with you then ask about replication lag, it's effects and standard patterns for monitoring it.
