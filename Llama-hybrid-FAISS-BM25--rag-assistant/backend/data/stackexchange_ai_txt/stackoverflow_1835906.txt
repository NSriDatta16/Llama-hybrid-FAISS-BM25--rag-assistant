I agree with Joerage that your server path is probably the culprit. To get more insight into what's happening, you need to wire up some events on the VersionControlServer object. At minimum you'll want Getting, NonFatalError, and Conflict. Complete list: http://msdn.microsoft.com/en-us/library/microsoft.teamfoundation.versioncontrol.client.versioncontrolserver_events(VS.80).aspx
