PCA does not throw away the unimportant features. In other words, features that you get from PCA are not the original ones. Mathematically PCA is orthogonal linear transformation that transforms the data to a new coordinate system such that the greatest variance by some scalar projection of the data comes to lie on the first coordinate (called the first principal component), the second greatest variance on the second coordinate, and so on...
