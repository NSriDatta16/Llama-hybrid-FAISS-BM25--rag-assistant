Is your HTML well formed? If so you could simply use an XML DomDocument. Use XPath to find the attributes you want to replace. You can actually use JScript serverside as well in ASP, whicdh might give you access to HTMLDom libraries you could use. You should probably have a look at one of the libraries for cleaning up HTML, something like HTML Tidy http://www.w3.org/People/Raggett/tidy/ Your main problem is you need to do a replace on the ampersands, they need to be &amp; in well formed XML/XHTML.
