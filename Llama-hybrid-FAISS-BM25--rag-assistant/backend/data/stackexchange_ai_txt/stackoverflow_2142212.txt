It's common, but it's best to avoid it. At the very least you should log the exception somewhere. Also, avoid using exceptions for flow control. Your code should be written to handle all likely cases without resorting to exceptions. Aside from performance increases, this method also tells you that when an exception happens, it's worth your attention.
