It was used in mootools 1.1 to store values on a DOM element (the tooltip in your case). Since mootools 1.2, the store() and retrieve() methodes are used for this, cause it will prevent you for creating memory leaks, caused by circular references. http://mootools.net/blog/2008/01/22/whats-new-in-12-element-storage/
