Why are you using clients twice? (Maybe explain what you're trying to achieve) Try this: SELECT DISTINCT am.[CreateDate], ru.[UserName], cl.[name], cl.[organisation], cl.[address], am.[IsApproved], am.[UserId] FROM [aspnet_Membership] am INNER JOIN clients cl ON am.userid = cl.userid INNER JOIN [RolesAndAllUsers] ru ON cl.userid = ri.userid WHERE cl.IsApproved = 'False'
