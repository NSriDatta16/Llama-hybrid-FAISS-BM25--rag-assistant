First, the Hosmer-Lemeshow test is considered obsolete . With 50000 observations and only a few predictors you could use cross-validation to evaluate and compare models. And, yes, your plots indicate that using splines could be a good idea. There are many posts discussing cross-validation for logistic regression models, see for instance How should two cross-validated logistic regression models be compared? , How to include an interaction term in GAM? , Cross-Validation in plain english? , How to reduce predictors the right way for a logistic regression model and search this site.
