Unfortunately it's not possible to split on a zero-width match in Python. But you can use re.findall instead: >>> import re >>> re.findall('[A-Z][^A-Z]*', 'TheLongAndWindingRoad') ['The', 'Long', 'And', 'Winding', 'Road'] >>> re.findall('[A-Z][^A-Z]*', 'ABC') ['A', 'B', 'C']
