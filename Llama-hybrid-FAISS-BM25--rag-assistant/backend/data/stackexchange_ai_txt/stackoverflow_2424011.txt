The transpose-paragraph function could help you. You might also want to have a look to the transpose section in the Emacs manual. Essentially: C-t Transpose two characters (transpose-chars). M-t Transpose two words (transpose-words). C-M-t Transpose two balanced expressions (transpose-sexps). C-x C-t Transpose two lines (transpose-lines).
