If you want a custom appearance for your application, I would just make my form borderless and handle everything myself, either by drawing on the form itself in the Paint event, or else moving controls around (panels etc.) in the Resize event. You have to handle things like dragging, resizing, closing/minimizing etc., but none of this is especially difficult.
