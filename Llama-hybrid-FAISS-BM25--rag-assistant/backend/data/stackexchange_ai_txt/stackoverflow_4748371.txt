mod_rewrite has no RewriteHeader directive, so I used it in conjunction with mod_headers to achieve the desired result: RewriteEngine on RewriteCond %{HTTP_USER_AGENT} MSIE\ [56] RewriteCond %{HTTP_USER_AGENT} !SV1 RewriteCond %{REQUEST_URI} \.(css|js)$ RewriteRule .* - [E=REMOVE_IE_ACCEPT_ENCODING:1] RequestHeader set Accept-Encoding "" env=REMOVE_IE_ACCEPT_ENCODING Hope it helps!
