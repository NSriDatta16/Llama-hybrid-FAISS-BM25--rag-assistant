No. Presumably you want to apply the click handlers to matching elements within each dragbox. You can do that with: $('.dragbox').each(function(){ $('.close', this).click(function(){ $(this).parent().hide(); }), $('.colpase', this).click(function(){ $(this).siblings('.dragbox_content').toggle(); }) }); If you just wanted to add the handlers globally, you wouldn't want the each.
