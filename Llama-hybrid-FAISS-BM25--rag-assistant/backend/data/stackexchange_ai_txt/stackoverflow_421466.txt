Many JDBC-capable database management systems (e.g. Oracle) provide compression in the physical storage engine. Oracle, for example, has the notion of a "compressed" table without decompression overhead: http://www.ardentperf.com/wp-content/uploads/2007/07/advanced-compression-datasheet.pdf
