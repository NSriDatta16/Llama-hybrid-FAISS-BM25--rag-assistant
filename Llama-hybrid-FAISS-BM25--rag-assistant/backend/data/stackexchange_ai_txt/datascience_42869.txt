Actually, loss is used by the model to decide the probability of the class. So, logloss just indicates how much is your model certain in comparison to the correct labels of the classes in test samples. Accuracy indicates what percentage of test samples are classified correctly. Look at this: What is the relationship between the accuracy and the loss in deep learning?
