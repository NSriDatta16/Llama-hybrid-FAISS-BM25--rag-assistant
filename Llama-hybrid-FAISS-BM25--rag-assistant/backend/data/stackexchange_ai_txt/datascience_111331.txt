What you ask for is related to what is termed as explainable AI (especially for deep models, like CNNs). Explainable AI methods try to provide (quantitative) insight as to why the model makes this or that prediction. So you can search into this type of literature for approaches. Effectively what you do with the regression model is trying to quantify how this or that feature affects prediction, which is an approach to explainable AI
