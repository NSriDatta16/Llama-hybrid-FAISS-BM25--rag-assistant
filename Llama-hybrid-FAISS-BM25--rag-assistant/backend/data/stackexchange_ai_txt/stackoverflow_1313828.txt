@temoto To specify a 503 for bots and a maintenance page for humans, try the following RewriteEngine on RewriteBase / #for bots such as google RewriteCond %{HTTP_USER_AGENT} ^.*(Googlebot|Googlebot|Mediapartners|Adsbot|Feedfetcher|bingbot)-?(Google|Image)? [NC] RewriteCond %{ENV:REDIRECT_STATUS} !=503 RewriteRule !^/down/for/maintenance$ %{DOCUMENT_ROOT}down/for/maintenance [L,R=503] #for humans RewriteCond %{REMOTE_HOST} !^1\.1\.1\.1 RewriteCond %{REQUEST_URI} !^/maint.html [NC] RewriteRule .* /maint.html [R=302,L]
