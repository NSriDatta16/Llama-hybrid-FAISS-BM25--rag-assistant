One solution is to keep the object model at the RDF triplestore level, for example by having classes for nodes: uris, blanks and literals statements: collections of three or more nodes (three for the RDF triple, additional node(s) for meta) Running queries on such a storage is essentially doing triple/n-tuple pattern-matching on the statements.
