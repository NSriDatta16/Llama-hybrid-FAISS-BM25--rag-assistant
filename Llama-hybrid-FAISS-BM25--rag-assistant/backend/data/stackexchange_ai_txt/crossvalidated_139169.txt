There is no bias concerning the misclassification rate, it just measures what it's supposed to measure. As you indicate, misclassification rate or accuracy can be a very misleading measure, especially if the distribution of the classes are unbalanced. Here are some links about common measures to assess prediction quality: http://webdocs.cs.ualberta.ca/~eisner/measures.html http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Receiver_operating_characteristic
