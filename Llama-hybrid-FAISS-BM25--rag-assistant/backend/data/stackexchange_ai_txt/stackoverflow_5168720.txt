NCHAR and NVARCHAR use 2 bytes per character for storage and have a limit of 4000 characters. CHAR and VARCHAR use one byte, and have a limit of 8000 characters. If your application is English ONLY, or is in a language that does not require Unicode extended characters, you can use CHAR or VARCHAR.
