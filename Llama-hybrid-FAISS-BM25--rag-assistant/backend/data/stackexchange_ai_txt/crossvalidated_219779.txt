"The maximum correlation between these two is about 0.6 at a fixed lag. Is this sufficient to establish that one time series affects the other" Correlation does not imply causation. Just because to sets of data are correlated it does not mean one affects the other. You could take a look at the Granger Causality test. This might be more aligned with what you want to do
