Most UNIX utilities pay attention to SIGPIPE , which, when received, indicates that a piped stream to which the process is writing has nothing listening on the other end, so there's no need to keep working. Searching for something like "java posix signals" yields some libraries that might allow you to add signal handlers to your Java code.
