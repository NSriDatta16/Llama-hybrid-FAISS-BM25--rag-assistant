Named Entity Recognition ( NER ) models should be able to identify money amounts. Other NLP techniques such as Dependency Parsing or Constituency Parsing can be used to identify the subject of the sentence - or the person the amount is referred to. For the months amount, I think once you have other informations that's something you could extract with a "hard coded" script.
