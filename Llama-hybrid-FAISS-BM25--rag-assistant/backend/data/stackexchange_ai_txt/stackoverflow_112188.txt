Domain Driven Design is interesting here, since it encourages programmers to embrace the domain vocabulary. On top of that, there is some design conventions, which allow you to refer parts of your application using well known terms, like services, repositories, factories, etc. Combining domain vocabulary and using technical conventions above it could be a good solution.
