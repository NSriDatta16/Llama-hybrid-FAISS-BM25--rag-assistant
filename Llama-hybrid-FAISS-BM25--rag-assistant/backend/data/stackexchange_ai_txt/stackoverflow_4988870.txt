Set a couple of headers: $filename = ...; $mime_type = ...; //whichever applicable MIME type header('Pragma: public'); header('Expires: 0'); header("Content-Disposition: attachment; filename=\"$filename\""); header("Content-Type: $mime_type"); header('Content-Length: ' . filesize($filename)); readfile($filename);
