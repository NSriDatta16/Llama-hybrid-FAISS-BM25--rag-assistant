Google "learning to rank", "Optimizing for the AUC" or similar. One idea is to predict probability that person $i$ wins over person $j$ as $NN(x_{ir},x_{jr})$ with $x_{ir}$ being features of subject $i$ at race $r$, NN some neural network or model. Ground truth is binary. Predicting who wins a race is then pairwise comparisons among all contestants (=ranking) Related [answer]
