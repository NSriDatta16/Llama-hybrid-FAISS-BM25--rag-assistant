Model prediction on meshgrid in python

Suppose I have data with two independent variable $X_1$ , $X_2$ and one dependent variable say $y$ , as follows: $X_1$ : $x_{1,1}$ , $x_{1,2}$ , $x_{1,3}$ $X_2$ : $x_{2,1}$ , $x_{2,2}$ , $x_{2,3}$ $y$ : $y_1$ , $y_2$ , $y_3$ I built some Machine learning model which is good . Now I want to generate predictions not just for test data but for all possible combinations of test data for example, if our test data looks like $X_1$ : $a$ , $b$ , $c$ $X_2$ : $p$ , $q$ , $r$ then I want predictions for pairs $(a,p)$$(a,q)$ , $(a,r)$ , $(b,p)$ , $(b,q)$ ....etc I have tried np.ravel, Meshgrid kind of commands but find it difficult.
