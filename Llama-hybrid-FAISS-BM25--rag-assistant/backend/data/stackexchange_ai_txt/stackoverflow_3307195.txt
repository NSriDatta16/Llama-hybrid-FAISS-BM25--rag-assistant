This is a complex question, so just a few notes about our practice at $work: JUnit is indeed still the standard. Most documentation and literature treats JUnit. Mockito seems to be the new star in Java mocking, although we still use JMock and think it's fine for our needs. We use the EclEmma Eclipse plugin for checking our test coverage, and like it.
