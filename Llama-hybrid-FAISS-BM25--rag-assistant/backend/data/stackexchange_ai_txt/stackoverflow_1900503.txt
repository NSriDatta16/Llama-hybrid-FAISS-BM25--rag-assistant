There's no substitute for taste, talent and experience in figuring out exactly how many safety checks should be in your code for best cost/benefit ratio for your organization. A good quality APIs are expected to be fool-proof, and to guide the user with proper amount of warnings. Sometimes, safety precautions may impair performance. Performance is one of the most counter-intuitive things in programming. Optimize with care, only when performance really matters.
