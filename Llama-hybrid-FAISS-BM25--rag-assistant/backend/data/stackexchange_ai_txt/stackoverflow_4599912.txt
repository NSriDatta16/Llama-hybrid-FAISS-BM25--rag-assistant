There's no general way to locate the source of a message the way you are asking for. There are however alternative architectures for your app which might solve this problem and I encourage you to consider them. What you're describing sounds like a mess of interdependent classes. All of your levels are aware of and use this MasterScene singleton and now you're trying to make the singleton aware of every level as well? Every piece of your applications shouldn't need to be aware of every other.
