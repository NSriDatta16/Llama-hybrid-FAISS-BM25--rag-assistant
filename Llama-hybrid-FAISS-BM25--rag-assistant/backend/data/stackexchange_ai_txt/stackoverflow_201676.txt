The solution I came up with is fragile; it relies on django's naming convention for foreign keys. USE information_schema; tee mysql_output SELECT * FROM TABLE_CONSTRAINTS WHERE CONSTRAINT_TYPE = 'FOREIGN KEY' AND TABLE_SCHEMA = 'database_name'; notee Then, in the shell, grep 'refs_tablename_id' mysql_output
