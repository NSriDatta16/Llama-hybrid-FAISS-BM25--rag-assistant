Total average of daily maximal values: SELECT AVG(dailyMax) AS avgOfDailyMax FROM (SELECT Date, MAX(Score) AS dailyMax FROM MyTable GROUP BY Date) as DailyMaxTable and daily maximal values: SELECT Date, MAX(Score) AS dailyMax FROM MyTable GROUP BY Date
