sortable lists, masked input fields and date pickers can all be added to your existing jQuery setup without having to rely on jQueryUI in the form of jQuery plugins. (add http:// before each url) datepicker - plugins.jquery.com/project/datepick draggable sortable lists - plugins.jquery.com/project/listsort numerous validation/form plugins - plugins.jquery.com/taxonomy/term/68
