I'm not sure, but what be on your mind are the Win32 console APIs. This are what you have to call to interact in detail with the character-mode console window. If you want something shell-like that isn't, in fact, a Console Window, then you need to learn about Win32 pipes and the CreateProcess API. You will use the pipes to capture output and to feed in input. If you want to mix in GUI apps, then you've got a gigantic job ahead of you, if you really want to force their windows to be in your app's window tree.
