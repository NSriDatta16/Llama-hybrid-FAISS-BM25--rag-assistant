I probably prefer to invoke the ToTitleCase from CultureInfo ( System.Globalization ) than Thread.CurrentThread ( System.Threading ): string s = "THIS IS MY TEXT RIGHT NOW"; s = CultureInfo.CurrentCulture.TextInfo.ToTitleCase(s.ToLower()); But it should be the same as jspcal's solution .
