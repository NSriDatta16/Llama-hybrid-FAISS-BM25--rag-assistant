It's a balance between the cost of storing and using Long integers, versus the likelihood of overflowing a 32-bit integer. Consider that an unsigned 32-bit integer stores over 4 billion values. If you think you are going to average more than 1 new row every second in this table for the next 136 years, then you need to use a Long.
