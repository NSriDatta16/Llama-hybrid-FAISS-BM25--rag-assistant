As far as tree traversal goes, I think recursion is a fantastic fit. (The directory structure is a tree) As long as you know that the directory structure isn't unbelievably huge, you shouldn't have to worry about overflowing the stack. Recursive solutions to navigate trees are almost always more elegant than iterative solutions
