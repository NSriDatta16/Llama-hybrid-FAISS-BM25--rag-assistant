By choosing a custom file storage system you will lose the benefits of using a relational database. Also your code might not be easy maintainable. Nonetheless, there are many who believe that relational databases offer too much complexity at the cost of speed. Have a look at the NoSQL entry in wikipedia and read about possible alternatives.
