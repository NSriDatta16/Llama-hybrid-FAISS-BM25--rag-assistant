I recommend embedding only a reference to your script into your HtmlHelper and keep your ParentChildSelectList.js script in the HEAD of your page. If you need to use a string literal, remember that two quotes ("") equals a single quote ("), or use only single-quotes (') to avoid any conflicts... string jScript = @" $(function() { var elCount = $(""body *"").size(); alert(""You have "" + elCount + "" elements on your page""); }); ";
