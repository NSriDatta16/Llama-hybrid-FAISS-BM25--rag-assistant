This is better solved at the summary stage. There is really a hidden coefficient for the omitted level, but as it is fixed by zero, the usual summaries ignores it. There is a special R package that does include those, gtsummary . For details and examples see What to do in a multinomial logistic regression when all levels of DV are of interest?
