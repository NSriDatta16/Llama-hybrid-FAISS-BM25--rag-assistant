As far as I know, this is not possible. GEE uses estimating equations for the various moments. The benefit of this approach is that you don't have to write down a likelihood and make the assumptions therein, however this also makes it limited in terms of using Bayesian methods that require specification of a likelihood. Here is a link https://ete-online.biomedcentral.com/articles/10.1186/s12982-015-0030-y
