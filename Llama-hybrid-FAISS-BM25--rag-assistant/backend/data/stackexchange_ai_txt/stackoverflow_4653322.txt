Use -setAttributes:ofItemAtPath:error: . What’s not elegant about it? Another possibility is to use chmod(2) , a POSIX function. The solution you’ve listed in your question is expensive — running a task is equivalent to creating a new process on the operating system, which is definitely more expensive than using NSFileManager or chmod(2) .
