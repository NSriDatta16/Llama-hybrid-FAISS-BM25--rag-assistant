According to CWA , the average US resident has a 1.9Mbps download speed. They have data by state, so if you have money then you can probably get a more specific report for your intended audience. Keep in mind, however, that more and more people are sharing this with multiple computers, using VOIP devices, and running background processes that consume bandwidth. -Adam
