The issue is probably due to using "Name" as your "name" in teh "name_value_list". The "Name" field is just a concactonation of the "first_name" and "last_name" fields. Try: $set_entry_params = array( 'session' => $result_array->id, 'module_name' => 'Contacts', 'name_value_list'=>array( array('name'=>'first_name','value'=>'Brian') ) ); $result = $soapClient->__soapCall('set_entry', $set_entry_params);
