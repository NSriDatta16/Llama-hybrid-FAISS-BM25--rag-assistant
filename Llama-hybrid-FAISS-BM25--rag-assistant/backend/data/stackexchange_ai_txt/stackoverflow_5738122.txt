This may seem obvious, but: Why not just use Django's testing client (django.test.client.Client)? then instead of manually 'debugging' in your browser, you can write unit tests with expectations and get leverage out of those further down the track. e.g. from django.test.client import Client client = Client() resp = client.put('/employee/2/', data={'email': 'here@there.com'}, follow=True) #... etc
