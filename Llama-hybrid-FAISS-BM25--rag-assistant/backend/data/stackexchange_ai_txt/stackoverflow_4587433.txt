Sure, it's easy. Just add your .txt files to your project (drag them to the Files tree). To access them, you need to first get the path: NSString *path = [[NSBundle mainBundle] pathForResource: @"filename" ofType: @"txt"]; Then load the string: NSError *error = nil; NSString *fileData = [NSString stringWithContentsOfFile: path encoding: NSUTF8StringEncoding error: &error];
