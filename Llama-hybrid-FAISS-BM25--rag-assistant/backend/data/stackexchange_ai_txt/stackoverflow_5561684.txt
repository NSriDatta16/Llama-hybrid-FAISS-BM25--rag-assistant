I realize you want ruby code, but I present to you a one liner to run in your shell: for i in `find * -depth`; do (mv $i `echo $i|tr [:upper:] [:lower:]`); done as found here: http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=244738 Run it once, and it should do the trick.
