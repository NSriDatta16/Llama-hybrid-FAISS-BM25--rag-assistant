Since XML::Simple is a pure Perl module, you could download the package, extract and use it. You will have to use the lib pragma to specify the directory containing the module. Suppose your directory structure is (only relevant files have been included): my_app.pl XML-Simple XML-Simple/lib XML-Simple/lib/XML XML-Simple/lib/XML/Simple.pm Your my_app.pl will have access to XML::Simple by including the following code: use lib 'XML-Simple/lib'; use XML::Simple;
