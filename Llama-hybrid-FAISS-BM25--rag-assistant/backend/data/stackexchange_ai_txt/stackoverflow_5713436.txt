What you're after is data detection. UITextView performs data detection providing it the text is uneditable. You can create a UITableViewCell subclass that contains a UITextField . Have a look at the dataDetectorTypes in the UITextView documentation . Both UITableView and UITextField are both subclasses of UIScrollView so you'll have to pay attention to scrolling behaviour.
