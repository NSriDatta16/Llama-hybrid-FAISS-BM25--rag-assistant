Yes, with the Azure SDK, you're installing what is called a "local fabric" which allows you to create and test your Visual Studio apps in a local environment before posting them out into the cloud. Google for "local fabric" or "development fabric" [ EDIT: since SDK 1.3 the fabric is called "Compute Emulator", the Development Storage is renamed to "Storage Emnulator"] - I remember several blog posts on this, at least one by Scott Guthrie.
