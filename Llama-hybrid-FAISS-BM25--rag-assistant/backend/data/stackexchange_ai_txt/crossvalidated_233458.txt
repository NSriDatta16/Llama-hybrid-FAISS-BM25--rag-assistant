You can use the icc() -function from the sjstats-package . In the help-file ?sjstats::icc you find a reference to the formula for mixed models with binary response: Wu S, Crespi CM, Wong WK. 2012. Comparison of methods for estimating the intraclass correlation coefficient for binary responses in cancer prevention cluster randomized trials. Contempory Clinical Trials 33: 869-880 (doi: 10.1016/j.cct.2012.05.004) The residual deviance in logistic regression is fixed to (pi ^ 2) / 3 .
