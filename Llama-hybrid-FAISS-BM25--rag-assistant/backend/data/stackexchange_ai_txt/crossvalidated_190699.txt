At each stage a person either moves one step to the right with probability 0.6 or one step to the left with probability 0.4.Assuming the direction of each step is independent I want to find the expected number of steps it takes a person to be r steps to the right of where she began. Answer:-15r steps. Is this right answer? If wrong, what would be correct answer?
