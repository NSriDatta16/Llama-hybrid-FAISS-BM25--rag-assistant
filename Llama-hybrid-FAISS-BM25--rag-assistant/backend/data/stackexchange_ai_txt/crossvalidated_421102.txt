Mean residual life in terms of incomplete moments

In a paper by Braga, Cordeiro, and Ortega 2017, they representing the Mean Residual Life in Terms of the incomplete moments $$\operatorname{MRL}(t)= \frac{1-m_1(t)}{1-F(t)}-t$$ where $m_1(t)=\int_0^t x f(x) \, dx$ How can we get this result?
