Try getting rid of the authentication token: FormsAuthentication.SignOut(); Context.Response.Cookies.Item(FormsAuthentication.FormsCookieName).Expires = Date.Now; return RedirectToAction("LogOut"); Also, make sure the page is not cached: Response.Cache.SetExpires(DateTime.UtcNow.AddMinutes(-1)); Response.Cache.SetCacheability(HttpCacheability.NoCache); Response.Cache.SetNoStore();
