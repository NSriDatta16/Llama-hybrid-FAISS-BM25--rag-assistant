Opening new IndexSearcher and IndexReader whenever somebody executes a search is very inefficient. Best to have a single reader that is shared by all calls to search(). This reader instance is closed and replaced whenever you need to rebuild the index. Of course, this will need to be synchronized. This is elaborated more at: http://wiki.apache.org/lucene-java/UpdatingAnIndex
