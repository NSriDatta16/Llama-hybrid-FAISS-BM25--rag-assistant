It depends on what you are testing and what your assumptions are. Here are couple of scenarios: If you are looking at single day, then it does not matter. If you are comparing multiple days but looking at each day independently, they you might want to control for the multiple comparisons problem. If you are looking for changes over days, then you might want to treat it as a time series problem.
