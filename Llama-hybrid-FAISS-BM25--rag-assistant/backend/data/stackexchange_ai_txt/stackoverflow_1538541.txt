From the searching that I've done, the best way to approach this without having to do something ugly like: window.location = href + '?id=avalue' would be to update the anchor hrefs on the page load. Grab whatever ID/value you need, and just append it; $(document).ready(function(){ value = "?id=foobar" $("#tabs ul li a").attr('href', ($(this).attr('href') + value) ); }); Not the most elegant solution, but it does work.
