Controls that Are Not Compatible with UpdatePanel Controls The following ASP.NET controls are not compatible with partial-page updates, and are therefore not supported inside an UpdatePanel control: GridView and DetailsView controls when their EnableSortingAndPagingCallbacks property is set to true. The default is false. http://www.asp.net/Ajax/Documentation/Live/overview/UpdatePanelOverview.aspx
