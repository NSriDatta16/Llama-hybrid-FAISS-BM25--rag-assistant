You want to leverage the standard http header "Content-Disposition". For example, to stream bits to the browser and force a 'save-as' dialog, add the following headers to the response. Content-Disposition: attachment; filename=checkimage.jpg Content-Length: 76127 Content-Type: image/JPEG More information: http://www.hanselman.com/blog/TheContentDispositionSagaControllingTheSuggestedFileNameInTheBrowsersSaveAsDialog.aspx
