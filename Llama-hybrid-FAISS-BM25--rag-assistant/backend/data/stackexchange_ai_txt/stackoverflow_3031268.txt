In case other people are interested, somebody adapted a 32-bit block cipher a few years back that's especially useful for this task. http://www.qualcomm.com.au/PublicationsDocs/skip32.c There is also Perl and Ruby port of the above available: https://github.com/gitpan/Crypt-Skip32 https://github.com/patdeegan/integer-obfuscator If you need the result in 8 characters or less, you can use a hex or base64 representation.
