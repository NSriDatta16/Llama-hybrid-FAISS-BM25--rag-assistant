At the most basic level your comparing how C# is compiled into a specific framework feature. Areas are simply built in routing/finding/searching customizations against so you can separate your app into different folders. You could provide your MVC application with a VirtualPathProvider and use views embedded in class libraries to segment your application but it isn't the standard way of organizing things.
