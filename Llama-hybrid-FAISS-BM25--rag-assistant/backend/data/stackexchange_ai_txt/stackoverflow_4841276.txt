Try the following For Activity: InputMethodManager mgr = (InputMethodManager) getSystemService(Context.INPUT_METHOD_SERVICE); mgr.hideSoftInputFromWindow(curEditText.getWindowToken(), 0); In case of Fragment : InputMethodManager mgr = (InputMethodManager) getActivity().getSystemService(Context.INPUT_METHOD_SERVICE); mgr.hideSoftInputFromWindow(mEditText.getWindowToken(), 0);
