Well, it's a bit old, but this article makes a few decent arguments for BLOB storage: http://www.dreamwerx.net/site/article01 . While not a performance gain per se, having your images and whatnot in a DB as opposed to in a directory should also eliminate problems with hotlinking (assuming this is a web app that's publicly available).
