Before adding text to the cell, Create a new paragraph with the text you want to add. Paragraph paragraph=new Paragraph("Test in the Cell"); paragraph.getFont().setStyle(Font.BOLD); Cell c=new Cell(paragraph); table.addCell(c); For those Cells in which you need not want the bold just simply remove the line paragraph.getFont().setStyle(Font.BOLD);
