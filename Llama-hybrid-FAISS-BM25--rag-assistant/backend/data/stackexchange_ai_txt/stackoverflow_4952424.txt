Use Fiddler2 to look at your page loading you've got a whole lot of JS and CSS there is it really all necessary? Are the resources minified and compressed? Every HTTPRequest you make takes time even when the response is 200 and the cached resource can be used. Look at loading resources on demand or bundling them into single files. jQuery is fairly well optimised generally speaking, IE6 was slow unless you specific an element along with and id or selector. Your page felt about equally slow for me in FF4, Chrome and IE9.
