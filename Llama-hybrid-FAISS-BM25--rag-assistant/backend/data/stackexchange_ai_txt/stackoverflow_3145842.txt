This paper gives a survey of different text categorization techniques and their accuracies. In short, you can categorize text with decision trees, but there are other algorithms that are much better. Sebastiani, F. (2002). Machine learning in automated text categorization. ACM Computing Surveys, cs.IR/0110053v1. Available from: http://arxiv.org/abs/cs.IR/0110053v1 .
