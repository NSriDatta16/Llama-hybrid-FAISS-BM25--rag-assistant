In this document, it's literally called the "question-mark" (hyphen included) ( http://www.ietf.org/rfc/rfc2396.txt -- scroll down to 3.2 Authority Component). I agree it's a bad name. The hash (#) is called the "Fragment Identifier" (scroll down to 4.1). So maybe it should be called the "Query Identifier" .
