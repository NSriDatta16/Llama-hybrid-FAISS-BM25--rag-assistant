To rebuild use: ALTER INDEX __NAME_OF_INDEX__ ON __NAME_OF_TABLE__ REBUILD or to reorganize use: ALTER INDEX __NAME_OF_INDEX__ ON __NAME_OF_TABLE__ REORGANIZE Reorganizing should be used at lower ( For further information see https://msdn.microsoft.com/en-us/library/ms189858.aspx
