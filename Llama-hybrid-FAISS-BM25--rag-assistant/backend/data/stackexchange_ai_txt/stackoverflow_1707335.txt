the following will work in 9i and above, it uses Tom Kyte's concatenation function : SELECT c.car_id, c.make, c.model, stragg(e.extra) FROM table_car c LEFT JOIN table_car_extras_link ce ON c.car_id = ce.car_id LEFT JOIN table_extras e ON ce.extra_id = e.extra_id GROUP BY c.car_id, c.make, c.model
