That should work on IE, Firefox, Opera, Safari and Chrome. The data attribute of and the movie parameter must both be present and must have the same value. In IE this prevents the SWF from streaming and, for example, presenting a pre-loader, but that should be a non-issue for YouTube. For more, and for a fix for that last problem, see Flash Satay: Embedding Flash While Supporting Standards .
