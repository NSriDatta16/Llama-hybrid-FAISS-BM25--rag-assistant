awk '{print $2}' | sort -rn | perl -e '$d=.1;@l=<>;print $l[int($d*$#l)]' It would indeed be more elegant to do the whole thing in perl. If you want to use a temporary file, you can use wc + head/tail to select the quantile from the sorted list of numbers.
