Yes. APPLICATIONS will be considered the parent and APPLICATIONS_SRV_STORAGE will be the child, the connection being made through APP_ID . When the parent is deleted, then all the children will also be deleted ( cascading ). Reference: http://www.techonthenet.com/oracle/foreign_keys/foreign_delete.php (Couldn't find the official one.)
