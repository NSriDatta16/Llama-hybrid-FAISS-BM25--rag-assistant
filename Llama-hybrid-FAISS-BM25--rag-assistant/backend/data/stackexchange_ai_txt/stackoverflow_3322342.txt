I generally just put everything into Session variables AFTER I have validated the information. I have always had pretty good luck with that and since there is generally not a lot of user information like you are talking about the overhead really isn't all that bad. Now, if you are talking A LOT of data then you may want to consider a different method. Like I said, this may not be the most elegant solution but it will certainly work.
