Something like this: SELECT DATEPART(W, Data_Date) AS [Week], AVG([Some_DataField]) AS AverageValue FROM [DataTable] WHERE Data_Date >= DATEADD(W, -4, GETDATE()) GROUP BY DATEPART(W, Data_Date) DATEPART(W, [Date]) returns the week number for the month in context
