There are a lot of things to do and I'm not sure you'll find any single technique to get organized and stay focused. But... Do list the things you have to do. Several short lists will be needed (today, later, inbox == to be sorted out, etc...). Review these lists once in the morning, and then in the evening. These related posts are worth a read: The Taste of the Day and The Trickle List Timeboxing: allocate time in your calendar to get the tasks done As suggested by harriyott, switching off email is kind of essential too!
