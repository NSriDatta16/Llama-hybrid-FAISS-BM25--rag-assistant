You have two possibilities: (easy) Host the images somewhere, and reference them in the . (difficult) Encode them in Base64 and build a multipart MIME message with known content IDs, so they can be referenced in the message body via cid: URIs. Each possibility has its downsides: Remote images may not be loaded on the modern e-mail clients for privacy. Probably rises spam score. When the receiving clients are in your control (e.g. same organization), you might be equally fine with either way.
