The XmlWriter class implements the IDisposable interface. That means you must ensure that the Dispose method is called on your instance. In addition, you should see the example at XmLWriterSettings.ConformanceLevel . It shows how to create an XmlWriter which is ok with writing a fragment. You can write a document with one log entry per element: text text Just be sure to flush the writer after each.
