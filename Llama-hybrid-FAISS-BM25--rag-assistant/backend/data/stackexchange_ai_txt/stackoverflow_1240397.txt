The jCrop plugin does most of the work. You just need a little logic to stitch it together with a slider widget (like the jqueryui slider). Here's a quick and dirty example. You'll certainly want to host the files on your own server, but I just wanted to throw something together. One significant difference is that this code forgets where you have dragged the selection and just resizes it around the current midpoint.
