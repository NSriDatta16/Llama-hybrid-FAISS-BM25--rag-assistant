If you're really interested in learning about parsing C, you might want to look into pycparsing . It's built on PLY , so you can probably leverage what you learn from it for parsing lots of things. Parsing C++, though, is way more complicated than parsing C or Python, so you may want to explore Python and C before you start digging into C++.
