Also, letting genetic algorithm adjust the weights of neural network, which determines the decision logic. This approach is very suitable for poker AI. I made my own AI like this. At first, I created ~1000 players, who didn't know how to play the game at all. Based on their initial luck during the hands, their fitness was weighted and new generation created. New "brains" were playing better than previous generation. Eventually, the best individuals played very good.
