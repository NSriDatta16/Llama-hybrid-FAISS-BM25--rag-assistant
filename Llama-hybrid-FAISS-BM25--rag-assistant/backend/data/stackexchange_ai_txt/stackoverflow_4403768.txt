A simple solution would be to update the link with a version number on update. So, the first time you write the file, you point to file.rtf?1. When you update the file, update the link to file.rtf?2 If you aren't keeping track of version numbers, you could use a random integer similarly. No, it's not elegant. It does, however, work.
