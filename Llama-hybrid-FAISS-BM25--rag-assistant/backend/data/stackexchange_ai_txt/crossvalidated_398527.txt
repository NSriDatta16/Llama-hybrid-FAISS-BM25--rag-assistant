Check out Bayesian Kernel Machine Regression (BKMR) and its many adaptations. There is a fantastic R package to support BKMR with great documentation. It is simply titled 'bkmr'. Check out this citation to learn more: Bobb, Jennifer F., et al. "Bayesian kernel machine regression for estimating the health effects of multi-pollutant mixtures." Biostatistics 16.3 (2014): 493-508.
