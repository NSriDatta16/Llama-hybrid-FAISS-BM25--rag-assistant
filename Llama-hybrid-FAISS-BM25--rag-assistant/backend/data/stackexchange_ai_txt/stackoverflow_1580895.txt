What you need, is to use double quotes (instead those single quotes wrapping the whole selector), for example: $("p:contains('John Resig')"); this will select the correct paragraph, with string 'John Resig' inside or you can inverse it: $('p:contains("John Resig")'); or you can use an old good escaping: $('p:contains(\'John Resig\')');
