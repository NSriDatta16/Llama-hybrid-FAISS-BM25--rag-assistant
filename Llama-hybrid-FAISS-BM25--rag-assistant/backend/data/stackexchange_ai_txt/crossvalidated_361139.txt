If you have $k$ values of $z_i$ each based on $N_i$ as its, possibly differetn sample size then the average $z$ is $$ z_{av} = \frac{\sum_{i=1}^k (N_i - 3) * z_i}{\sum_{i=1}^k (N_i - 3)} $$ and the standard error of $z_{av}$ is $$ \frac{1}{\sqrt{\sum{(N_i - 3)}}} $$
