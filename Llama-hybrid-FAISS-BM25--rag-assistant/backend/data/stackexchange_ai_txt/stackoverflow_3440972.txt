If you haven't changed your site information (url and such) in the backend (or in the database) after the migration, this is the likely problem. http://www.siteground.com/tutorials/magento/magento_configuration.htm#base_url for the database: in the core_config_data table change key "web/unsecure/base_url" to your site base url ex. http://yoursite.com good luck!
