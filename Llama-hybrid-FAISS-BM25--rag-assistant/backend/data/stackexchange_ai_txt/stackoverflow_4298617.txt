From the Rails documentation: Exceptions will force a ROLLBACK that returns the database to the state before the transaction began. Be aware, though, that the objects will not have their instance data returned to their pre-transactional state. http://api.rubyonrails.org/classes/ActiveRecord/Transactions/ClassMethods.html
