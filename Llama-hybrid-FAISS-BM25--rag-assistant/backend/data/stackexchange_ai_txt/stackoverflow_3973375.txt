There's no longer a hard limit on the number of QPS your App Engine app can sustain (and in any case, increases were never temporary). As long as your average request latency is below 1000 milliseconds, we'll provision more instances as needed. As far as polling goes, yes, that won't scale well when it comes to resource consumption. The upcoming Channels API will provide a way to asynchronously notify a user's browser of events.
