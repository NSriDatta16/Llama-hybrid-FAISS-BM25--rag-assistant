It's g~iw with the cursor on the word. Key: g flag (I couldn't find a good reference for this...) ~ toggle case; alternatively use U for to-upper or u for to-lower iw selects the Inner Word, i.e. the word that the cursor is on; ip selects the Inner Paragraph See Michael Jakl's Vim Introduction and Tutorial - concise and has some nice graphical explanations.
