Following up on Robert's idea , you might try adding the env parameter to the Popen call. For example: import subprocess p = subprocess.Popen(["date"], env={'TZ':'America/New_York'}) p.wait() # Fri Jan 14 14:45:44 EST 2011 p = subprocess.Popen(["date"], env={'TZ':'Asia/Taipei'}) p.wait() # Sat Jan 15 03:45:44 CST 2011
