See bigint : Transparent BigInteger support for Perl... All operators (including basic math operations) except the range operator .. are overloaded. Integer constants are created as proper BigInts. Floating point constants are truncated to integer. All parts and results of expressions are also truncated. Unlike integer , this pragma creates integer constants that are only limited in their size by the available memory and CPU time...
