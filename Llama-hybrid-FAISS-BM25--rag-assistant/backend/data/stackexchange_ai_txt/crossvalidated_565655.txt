Here are some standard methods i am aware of when there is a class imbalance of data for classification problems in CNNs: Change the Loss Function (Focal, Dice, ...) Spatial Pyramid Pooling Non-local blocks Initialise the bias term only in the final layer of CNN Are there any others new or old?
