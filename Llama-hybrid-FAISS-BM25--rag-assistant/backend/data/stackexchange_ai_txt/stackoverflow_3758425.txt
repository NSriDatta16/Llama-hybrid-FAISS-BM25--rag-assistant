(very) Simple, but not simplist design (simple enough design if you prefer) : K.I.S.S . Prefer flat hierarchies, avoid deep hierarchies. Separation of concerns is essential. Consider other "paradigms" than OO when it don't seem simple or elegant enough. More generally : D.R.Y. and Y.A.G.N.I help you achieve 1.
