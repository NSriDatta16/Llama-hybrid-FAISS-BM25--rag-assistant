It depends on the further analysis that you want to perform. Increasing amount of occurrences can significantly effect the properties of your time series data, because you have only 10% of the data available. In general, this is an interpolation question. There are fundamentally different statistical models that you can use to find the missing data. The Pandas dataframe.interpolate() is a good way to start.
