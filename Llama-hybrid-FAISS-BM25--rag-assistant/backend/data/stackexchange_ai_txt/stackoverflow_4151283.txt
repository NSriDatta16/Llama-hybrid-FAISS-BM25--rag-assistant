std::queue::front retrieve the Value of the first entry or its Reference. So your code: CDFCQueueMsgs oDfcQueueMsg_TopMsg; CDFCQueueMsgs * poDfcQueueMsg_TopMsg = & oDfcQueueMsg_TopMsg; poDfcQueueMsg_TopMsg = oCDataQueueingAndPersisting.poDFCMsgQUEUE.front(); should be: // take the reference of the queue::front in oDfcQueueMsg_TopMsg CDFCQueueMsgs& oDfcQueueMsg_TopMsg; oDfcQueueMsg_TopMsg = oCDataQueueingAndPersisting.poDFCMsgQUEUE.front(); or // copy the queue::front() into oDfcQueueMsg_TopMsg CDFCQueueMsgs oDfcQueueMsg_TopMsg; oDfcQueueMsg_TopMsg = oCDataQueueingAndPersisting.poDFCMsgQUEUE.front();
