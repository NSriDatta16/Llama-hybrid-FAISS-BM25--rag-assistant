You can either float all the li elements or give them display: inline-block , then give them some top and bottom margin: .letterlist li { float: left; /* or */ display: inline-block; margin: 20px 0; } See: http://www.jsfiddle.net/yijiang/z8Gfe/ for a simple example. And by the way, ul elements are not valid in p paragraphs
