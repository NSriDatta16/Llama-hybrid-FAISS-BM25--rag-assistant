whats the difference between these two value function definisions?

I've seen in literature two different yet similar approaches when writing the value function in an MDP: $V_\pi(s)=\sum\limits_{a\in A}\pi(a|s)\sum\limits_{s'\in S}\sum\limits_{r\in R} Pr[s',r|s,a][r+\gamma V_\pi(s')]$ $V_\pi(s)=\sum\limits_{a\in A}\pi(a|s)\sum\limits_{s'\in S} Pr[s'|s,a][r(s,a,s')+\gamma V_\pi(s')]$ Are those equivalent? what is the meaning of adding the sum over $r$ ?
