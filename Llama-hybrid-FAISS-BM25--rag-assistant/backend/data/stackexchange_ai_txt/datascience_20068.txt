A common step in "feature engineering" is parsing these out into multiple values that might give you additional information during ML. For example: Hour of the day Minute of the hour Hours after sunrise Hours before sunset If you also had day, that would open a lot more to detect seasonality like: Month of the year Month of the quarter Day of the week Holiday Y/N
