Is it possible for you to step towards WPF by embedding a WPF app into one of your existing WinForm applications? It can be a lot harder to sell a complete retooling without an example of some of the benefits (in particular, maintainability and flexibility, especially in the UI). Try starting with a well used portion of your current application and giving a demo with it in WPF.
