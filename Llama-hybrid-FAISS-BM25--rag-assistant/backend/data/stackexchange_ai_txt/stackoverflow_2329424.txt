First a function to make one of the condp clauses (defn test-make-adj [x num y] `(~x (make-adj ~num ~y ~x))) Then a macro to assemble the clauses into a condp expression (defmacro adj-condp [pred expr & clauses] `(condp ~pred ~expr ~@(mapcat test-make-adj clauses))) ps: i'm not at my repl so i cant test this, please edit :)
