Text vectorisation is a good way to have a reliable classification. You have several libraries like doc2vec that you can use together with logistic regression or dimensional reduction technique like tSNE or UMAP. https://radimrehurek.com/gensim/auto_examples/tutorials/run_doc2vec_lee.html On the other hand, you can also use libraries like BERT or TF-IDF: https://pypi.org/project/bert-document-classification/ https://medium.com/swlh/text-classification-using-tf-idf-7404e75565b8
