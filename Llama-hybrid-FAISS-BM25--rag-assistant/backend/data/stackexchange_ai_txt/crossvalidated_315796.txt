Econometric Analysis of Cross Section and Pannel Data from Jeffrey M. Woolridge is one of the most famous book about regression and econometrics. It covers cross-section and pannel data while you should read an additional book about time series, e.g. New Introduction to Multiple Time Series by Luetkepohl.
