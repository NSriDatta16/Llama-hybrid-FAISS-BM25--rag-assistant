If you have Xcode 3.2.5 then you can verify and upload your application through Organizer. 1.) Xcode -> Run -> Build and Archive 2.) Xcode -> Organizer -> Your Application (under Archives) -> Verify / Submit Your app must already be setup and in waiting for upload mode in iTunes Connect.
