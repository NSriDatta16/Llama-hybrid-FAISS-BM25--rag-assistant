Transparent database encryption - even if the backup files get stolen (or the entire data files) the database cannot be read. Reduces the cost of data loss and embarrassing media attention. SQL Audit - see who's doing what with the data with no 3rd party tools necessary Policy-based management - reduce the amount of time needed to ensure the servers adhere to a defined policy.
