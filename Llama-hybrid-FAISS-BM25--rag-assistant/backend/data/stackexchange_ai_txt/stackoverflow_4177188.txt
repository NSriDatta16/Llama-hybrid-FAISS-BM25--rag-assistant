Yes you'll use less bandwidth, it'll be better for performance because you can store information in your browser up to 5Mb . It's much more than with cookie. For instance to set an item in the storage you can do: sessionStorage.setItem("name1", "value1"); and to get the item sessionStorage.getItem("name1"); The sessionStorage is relative to each subdomain/domain.
