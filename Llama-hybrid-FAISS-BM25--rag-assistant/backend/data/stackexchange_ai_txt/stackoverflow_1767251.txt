input {font-size: 14px; } But please don't do that. Use ems, or or ex, to size fonts. Otherwise your forms are at the mercy of the resolution of the monitor's display. Ems, or whatever, aren't necessarily perfect, but they're a slightly less fragile means of sizing fonts without, on the user zooming the page, breaking layout. input input {font-size: 14px; } Or, for all text within the form: form {font-size: 1.4em; } Where the 1.4 will be calculated from the parent of the form.
