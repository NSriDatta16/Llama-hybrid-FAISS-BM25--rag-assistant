I think what you are looking for is a packet sniffer, it will intercept almost all communications over a network. If you want to use a library, check out WinPCap , which was meant for exactly this purpose. Also, if you think that you just want something pre-written and just want to modify it, check out Wireshark . Although, reading code is often more difficult than writing it.
