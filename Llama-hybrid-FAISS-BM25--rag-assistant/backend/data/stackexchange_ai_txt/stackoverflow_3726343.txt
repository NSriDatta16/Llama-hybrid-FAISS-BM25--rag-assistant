A Simple way to avoid that is to add a random string on the end of the file, something like /YourFile.csv?rnd=4707 or you can place some cache headers to not let it cache, for example Response.Cache.SetExpires(DateTime.UtcNow.AddYears(-4)); Response.Cache.SetNoStore(); Response.Cache.SetValidUntilExpires(false); Response.Cache.SetCacheability(HttpCacheability.NoCache);
