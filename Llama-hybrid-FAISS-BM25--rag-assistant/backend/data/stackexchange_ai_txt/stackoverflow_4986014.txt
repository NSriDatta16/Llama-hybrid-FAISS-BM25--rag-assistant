You don't have register_globals enabled. Therefore, you cannot implicitly get POST parameters by writing $variable . Change your code to echo $_POST['variable'] . You should also disable it on your server: Warning This feature has been DEPRECATED as of PHP 5.3.0. Relying on this feature is highly discouraged. It will usually create security holes.
