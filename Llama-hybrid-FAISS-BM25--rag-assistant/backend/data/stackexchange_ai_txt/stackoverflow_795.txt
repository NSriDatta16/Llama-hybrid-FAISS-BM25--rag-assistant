I would be wary of automatically upcasing all whitespace-preceded-words in scenarios where I would run the risk of attracting the fury of nitpickers. I would at least consider implementing a dictionary for exception cases like articles and conjunctions. Behold: "Beauty and the Beast" And when it comes to proper nouns, the thing gets much uglier.
