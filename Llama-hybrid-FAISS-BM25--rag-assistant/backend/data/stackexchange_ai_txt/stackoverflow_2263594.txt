The application is quite simple and could be done either with AS2 or AS3 (or even javascript although you may do it with more ease in flash if you're a newbie). You will mainly need to explore MouseEvents to select and drag MovieClips. There's a lot of existing examples related to drag and drop already. Do some research and see how far you can get on your own. You may get back here and ask more problem specific questions once you've got this started.
