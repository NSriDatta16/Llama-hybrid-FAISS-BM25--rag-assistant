Another idea is to move the strings into a "configuration" file, such as in XML or INI format. Modifying the EXE without compilation is hacking and highly discouraged. You could use a hex editor, find the string and modify it. The new text must be have a length less than or equal to the original EXE. Note, some virus checkers perform CRCs or checksums on the executables. Altering the executables is a red flag to these virus checkers.
