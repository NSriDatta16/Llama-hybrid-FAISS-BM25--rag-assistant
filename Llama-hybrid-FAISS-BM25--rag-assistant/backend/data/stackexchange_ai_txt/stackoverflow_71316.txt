That would only work for ManyToOne relations and for them @ManyToOne(fetch=FetchType.EAGER) would probably appropriate. Fetching more than one OneToMany relation eagerly is discouraged and/or does not work as you can read in the link Jeremy posted. Just think about the SQL statement that would be needed to do such a fetch...
