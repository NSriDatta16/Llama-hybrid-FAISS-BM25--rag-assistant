Are you sure the session itself isn't being flushed on the server-side? I had a situation in a shared-hosting environment where the server settings were caused my sessions to disappear. I had to add local settings in my app to overcome this (private rather than common session storage). These are the settings I have in my .htaccess file for the sessions, giving them reasonable time on the disk php_value session.gc_probability 1 php_value session.gc_divisor 100 php_value session.gc_maxlifetime 3600
