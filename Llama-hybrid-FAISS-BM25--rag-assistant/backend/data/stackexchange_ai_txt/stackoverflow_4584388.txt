Java, Hibernate java.lang.ClassCastException: org.hibernate.collection.PersistentSet cannot be cast to java.util.HashSet

I have two tables, DVD and Contact. A DVD can be rented to a contact and a contact can rent many DVD's. The many to one link (dvd-->contact) works fine. But the other way fails: (contact-->dvd) This is the contact mapping: Here is setter getter for Contact: private Set dvds = new HashSet (); public Set getDvds(){ return dvds; } public void setDvds(Set dvds){ this.dvds=dvds; } When I try to get the DVD rented from a contact with this: HashSet tt = (HashSet )dds; I get an Exception: java.lang.ClassCastException: org.hibernate.collection.PersistentSet cannot be cast to java.util.HashSet What does the Exception mean and how do I fix it? Edit: This solved my problem: .setResultTransformer(Criteria.DISTINCT_ROOT_ENTITY)
