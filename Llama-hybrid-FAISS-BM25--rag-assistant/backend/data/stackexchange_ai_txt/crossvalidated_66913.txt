You cannot express state-dependent independencies with standard directed (Bayesian networks) or undirected graphical models (Markov networks). But you can use an extension called gates which are described T. Minka & J. Winn, Gates: A graphical notation for mixture models , 2008 and were designed specifically for this purpose.
