This is telling you that the mailbox you are sending it to, has run out of space. The error is an SMTP error : 552 Requested mail action aborted: exceeded storage allocation outlined in the rfc http://www.ietf.org/rfc/rfc2821.txt . So either the mail box is full or you are sending something that will not fit in it
