A simple (some times simple is the best) solution would be average out your readings. Buffer the last, say five readings, and then take the average of those as your value. Their will be a slight delay between being flat and being quickly rotated, but for a cursor will result in a slight, sort of friction almost, it will sort of resist sudden changes.
