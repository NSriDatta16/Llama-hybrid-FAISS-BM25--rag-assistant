This question has been answered before here . They represent statistical significance, where the null hypothesis is: $$H_0: r_k=0$$ The test statistic is: $$\mid r_k\mid>\frac{1.96}{\sqrt{n}}$$ See Cowpertait & Metcalfe (2009) for more about time series analysis in R .
