You could always put your referenced assemblies in the GAC, then the location would not matter. You can install the component by dragging it into the GAC (C:\windows\assembly) or running GACUtil.exe. This article may help. It describes the " Best Practices for Assembly Loading ." At first I thought you could used the element in the runtime/assemblyBinding in the web.config, but the probing will only allow you to specify subdirectories under the root location.
