sar -q will report queue length, task list length and three load averages. Example: matli@tornado:~$ sar -q 1 0 Linux 2.6.27-9-generic (tornado) 01/13/2009 _i686_ 11:38:32 PM runq-sz plist-sz ldavg-1 ldavg-5 ldavg-15 11:38:33 PM 0 305 1.26 0.95 0.54 11:38:34 PM 4 305 1.26 0.95 0.54 11:38:35 PM 1 306 1.26 0.95 0.54 11:38:36 PM 1 306 1.26 0.95 0.54 ^C
