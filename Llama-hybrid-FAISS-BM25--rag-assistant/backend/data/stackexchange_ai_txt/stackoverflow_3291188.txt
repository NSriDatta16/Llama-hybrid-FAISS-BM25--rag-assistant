I just tested it myself, you need to do: $('#dv').mousedown(function(e){ e.preventDefault (); x1 = e.pageX - parseInt($('#dv').css('left')); y1 = e.pageY - parseInt($('#dv').css('top')); drag = true; }) This will prevent the browser drag-and-drop function. HTH.
