A keychain is an encrypted container that holds passwords for multiple applications and secure services. Keychains are secure storage containers, which means that when the keychain is locked, no one can access its protected contents. -- Keychain Services Programming Guide, Apple 2010. I recommend that you read the Keychain Services Tasks for iOS .
