It is the estimated intercept in the logistic regression. I.e., $$E(Y\vert \vec{x}) = p(\vec{x}), \qquad \log\left(\frac{p(\vec{x})}{1-p(\vec{x})}\right) = \beta_0+\vec{\beta}_1^\top\vec{x} $$ See appendix B.4 and write $\tilde{\vec{x}} = (1, \vec{x}^\top)^\top$ and $\vec{\beta} = (\beta_0,\vec\beta_1^\top)^\top$
