I found the problem: If you symlink your .hgrc somewhere it causes this. Definitely a bug in mercurial (and a stupid error message at that). $ rm -f ~/.hgrc $ hg init foo $ cd foo $ hg log (works) $ ls -l ~/Dropbox/.hgrc -rw-r--r--@ 1 schubert staff 83 Jan 9 02:15 /Users/schubert/Dropbox/.hgrc $ ln -s ~/Dropbox/.hgrc ~/.hgrc $ hg log abort: Is a directory $ rm -f ~/.hgrc $ hg log (works)
