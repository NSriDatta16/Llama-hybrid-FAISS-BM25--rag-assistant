The glob pattern .* includes the special directories . and .. , which you probably didn't mean to include in your pattern. I can't think of an elegant way to exclude them, so here's an inelegant way: sed -i.bakkk -e "s$/sa/#/he/#g" $(ls -d .* | grep -v '^\.\|\.\.$')
