Encryption: to put (a message) into code or to distort so that it cannot be understood without the appropriate decryption equipment By definition, you are trying to encrypt the data. The only difference is you only need an encryption that's strong enough to not allow laymen from accessing it's contents. Like others have mentioned, using Base64 would effectively encrypt the data against your average user.
