You may find value in using a framework if: You work in a team environment and want to be sure everyone uses the same approach You have a large, sophisticated app and want to communicate effectively between parts of the app You want to be able to hire additional programmers and have them be productive quickly You want to ensure your application is built with a proven architecture You want to leverage the knowledge of design patterns and solutions that are known to work well You want to simplify a complex application Here's a great article on Flex frameworks. Flex Framework Comparison And, I agree with the conclusion...Mate is a great Flex Framework. Another interesting framework not mentioned in this article is Spicefactory's Parsley .
