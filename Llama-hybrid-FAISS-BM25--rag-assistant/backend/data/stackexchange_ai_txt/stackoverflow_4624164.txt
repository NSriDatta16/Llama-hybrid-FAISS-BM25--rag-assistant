Suppose that your matrix in the cells A1:D4 in A6 put: =OFFSET($A$1;0;QUOTIENT(ROW()-ROW($A$6);3)+1) in B6 put: =OFFSET($A$1;MOD(ROW()-ROW($A$6);3)+1;0) in C6 put: =VLOOKUP($B6;$A$1:$D$4;MATCH($A6;$A$1:$D$1;0);FALSE) Den drag down (copying formulas) A6:C6 up to A14:C14 (I translate my formulas from italian so there could be some glitch) PS: 3 in the formulas refers to the number of rows (and column) of the example.
