In all likelihood, no it won't be slowed down considerably. However, a better question to ask is: Which method of adding more fields results in a more elegant, understandable, maintainable, cost effective solution? Usually the answer is "It depends." It depends on how the data is accessed, how the requirements will change, how the data is updated, and how fast the tables grow.
