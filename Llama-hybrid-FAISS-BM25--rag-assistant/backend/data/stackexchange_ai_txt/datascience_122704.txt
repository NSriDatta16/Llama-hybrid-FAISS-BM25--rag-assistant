You should determine if the attribute is Missing at Random (MAR) , Missing Completely at Random (MCAR), or Missing Not at Random. For each of these categories there are recommendations when and how to impute. But how to classify attributes in your dataset as MAR,MCAR,MNAR? By doing EDA, e.g., studying the statistical distribution of the attribute values. It is best to read modern academic literature about this topic (I cannot explain it properly and in full here).
