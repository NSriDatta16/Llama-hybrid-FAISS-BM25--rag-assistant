MAPE and MASE are common metrics to use for time series, which you may not be familiar with. MAPE - Mean Absolute Percent Error: MASE - Mean Absolute Scaled Error: Reference: https://blogs.oracle.com/datascience/7-ways-time-series-forecasting-differs-from-machine-learning You can consider also using multiple metrics for your assessment, not just one, as each metric has a slightly different purpose.
