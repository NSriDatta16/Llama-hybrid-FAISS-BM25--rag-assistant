It looks like Avaloq Script, used by (ahem) Swiss banks, and while there is very little about it online, I found a grammar which perfectly matches the terms in your samples. Avaloq Script, the Avaloq Banking System's script language, facilitates entering specific business logic. The structure of the data that can be accessed through Avaloq Script is defined in a DDIC (Data Dictionary), making it unnecessary to know the data storage structure.
