An easy way is to add that product to a category and display that category on the homepage by going to CMS - Manage Pages, select Home Page from the list of pages and add {{block type="catalog/product_list" category_id="8" template="catalog/product/list.phtml"}} where category_id="8" is the category's id (thisid is displayed on the category page in Magento's admin)
