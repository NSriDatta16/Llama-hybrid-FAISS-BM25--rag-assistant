This may not be as nice as what you were hoping but if you put (global-hi-lock-mode 1) in your .emacs file then you can type C-x w h REGEX to highlight all occurances of REGEX , and C-x w r REGEX to unhighlight them again. Again, not as elegant as you'd probably like, but it'll work.
