No idea why it wasn't working with that syntax, but i found a different way to do it that works: Cufon.replace('h2.no-shadow'); Cufon.replace('h3.no-shadow'); Cufon.set('textShadow', '3px 1px 0 #BF6017'); Cufon.replace('h2.with-shadow'); Cufon.replace('h3.with-shadow'); Every replacement configured after the Cufon.set will have the properties that we specify. This can be very useful to organise your Cufon replacements file, as well. Hope this is useful to somebody.
