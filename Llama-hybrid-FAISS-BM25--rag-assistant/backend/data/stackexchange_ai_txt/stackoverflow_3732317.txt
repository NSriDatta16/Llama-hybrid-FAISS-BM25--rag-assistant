Why create your own exception? You create your own exception so that when you throw them, you can have specific catches and hence differentiate them from system thrown (unhandled) exceptions. What class should you derive it from? Earlier, it was standard practice for custom exceptions to be derived from ApplicationException class but over time, MS recommendations have changed encouraging developers to derive from System.Exception itself rather than ApplicationException
