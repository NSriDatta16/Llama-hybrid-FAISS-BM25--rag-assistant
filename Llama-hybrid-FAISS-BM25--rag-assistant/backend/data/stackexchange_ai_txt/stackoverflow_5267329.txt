This is how I did it, am open to more elegant solutions though! @certs = @article.required_certs.collect{|c| c.id} User.all(:joins=>:certifications, :group=>User.attributes_for_sql, :select=>'users.*', :conditions => ['certifications.id in (?)', @certs], :having=> "count(*)=#{@certs.length}") Hope this helps someone!
