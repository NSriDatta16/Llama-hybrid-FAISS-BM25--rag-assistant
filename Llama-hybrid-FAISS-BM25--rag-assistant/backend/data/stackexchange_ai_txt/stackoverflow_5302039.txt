You can use long.Parse() in your Select statement, since from your screenshot it looks like values in the Items collection could be strings: var x = SchedulerMatrixStorage.Resources.Items.Select(col => long.Parse(col.Id.ToString())).ToList(); x will be resolved to type System.Collections.Generic.List .
