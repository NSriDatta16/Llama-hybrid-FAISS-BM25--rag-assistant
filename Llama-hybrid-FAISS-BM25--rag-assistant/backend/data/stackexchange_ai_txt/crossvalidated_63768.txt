The short answer is: An MCMC is a MC, but not all MCs are MCMC. The slightly longer answer: MC methods are a class of methods, of which MCMC is one possibility. Even MCMC does not uniquely define your method as there are different variations of MCMC. You can read more in: Robert, C. P., & Casella, G. (2004). Monte Carlo statistical methods . New York: Springer.
