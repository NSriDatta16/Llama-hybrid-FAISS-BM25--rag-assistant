I know you didn't mention Pylab/Matplotlib, but it works. Here is an example (assumes single-channel signal): x, fs, nbits = audiolab.wavread('schubert.wav') audiolab.play(x, fs) N = 4*fs # four seconds of audio X = scipy.fft(x[:N]) Xdb = 20*scipy.log10(scipy.absolute(X)) f = scipy.linspace(0, fs, N, endpoint=False) pylab.plot(f, Xdb) pylab.xlim(0, 5000) # view up to 5 kHz Y = X*H y = scipy.real(scipy.ifft(Y))
