It seems that Box-Pierce and Ljung-Box tests are mainly univariate tests, but there are some assumptions behind the Breusch-Godfrey test when testing if linear structure is left behind on residuals of time series regression (MA or AR process). Here is link to discussion: http://www.stata.com/meeting/new-orleans13/abstracts/materials/nola13-baum.pdf
