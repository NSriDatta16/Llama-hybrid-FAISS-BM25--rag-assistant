If RTRL means online learning with a recurrent neural network, you could use the R tensorflow interface to python Tensorflow. Or you could use the much more user friendly R keras ( keras.rstudio.com ). Just train an RNN with LSTM or GRU type layer and some form of SGD, using a batch size of $1$ .
