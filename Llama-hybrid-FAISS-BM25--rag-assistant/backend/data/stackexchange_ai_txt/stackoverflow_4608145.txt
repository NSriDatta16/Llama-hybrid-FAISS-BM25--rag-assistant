For me you've found a bug :) It works fine until the mouse cursor stays inside the visible area... then the H1 enable the hyperdrive :D To reduce (but not to avoid completely) the bug's effects you can restrict the draggable area into e tag (eg. body) AND disable the scroll: $(".draggable").draggable(); $(".draggable" ).draggable( "option", "containment", 'body' ); $(".draggable" ).draggable( "option", "scroll" , false ); or an arbitrary area (other option in docs): var area=Array(0,740,300,880); $(".draggable").draggable(); $(".draggable" ).draggable( "option", "containment", area ); $(".draggable" ).draggable( "option", "scroll" , false );
