If your problem is to impute time series data, I think you might try: A specialized R package, such as Amelia. Casting the problem in state-space form, if it lends itself to such treatment and interpolate using the simulation smoother (see for instance simulateSSM in R package KFAS ). For the theory, the book from Durbin-Koopman is an excellent resource.
