Just to expand on Konrad's answer, and to correct Pierre, you can still generate an Expression from an IL-compiled lambda, though it's not terribly elegant. Augmenting Konrad's example: // Gives you a lambda: Func f = x => x * 2; // Gives you an expression tree: Expression > g = x => f(x);
