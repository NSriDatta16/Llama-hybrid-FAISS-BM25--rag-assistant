You might consider embedding a popular JavaScript engine. Not only will they be fast and well-supported, but so many people know how to program in JavaScript that it will be easily adopted and read by a large audience. According to this answer the SpiderMonkey engine is thread-safe, while Google/Chrome's V8 may not be.
