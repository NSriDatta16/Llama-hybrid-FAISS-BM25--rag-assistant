Someone probably has a more elegant solution, but setting the paddingLeft style of your menu to a negative number seems to work just fine (I'm using a PopUpMenuButton to illustrate, but a PopUpButton with a Menu would work just the same): Menu { paddingLeft: -12; } Hope that helps!
