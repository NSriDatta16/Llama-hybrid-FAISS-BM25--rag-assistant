This subject is covered very nicely in Chapters 4 and 8 of Bernhard Pfaff's book, " Analysis of Integrated and Cointegrated Time Series with R ", for which the vars and urca packages were written. The vec2var step is necessary if you want to use the predict functionality that's available. A more complete answer was provided on the R-Sig-Finance list . See also this related thread .
