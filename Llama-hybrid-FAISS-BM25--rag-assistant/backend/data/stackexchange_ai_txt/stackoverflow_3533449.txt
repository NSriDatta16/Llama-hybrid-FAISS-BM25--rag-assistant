You definitely can. With XCode 3.2.3 it's pretty easy now--you can dynamically load the provisioning certificate to the phone. To do this, plug in the phone that you want to use via USB, open the Organizer in XCode, and follow the instructions for provisioning the device. You must be connected to the internet. Note: if there are new developers agreements from Apple that you haven't accepted yet, this certificate provisioning step may fail silently.
