One way to avoid Google from deciding what country you are in, is by setting a different IP address. Just get one of the many US proxy servers from the Web and do something like this: $ch=curl_init(); curl_setopt($ch,CURLOPT_RETURNTRANSFER,1); curl_setopt($ch,CURLOPT_FOLLOWLOCTION,1); curl_setopt($ch,CURLOPT_PROXY,"8.12.33.159"); curl_setopt($ch,CURLOPT_PROXYPORT,"80"); curl_setopt($ch,CURLOPT_USERAGENT,"Mozilla/5.0 (Windows; U; Windows NT 5.1; en-US; rv:1.9.0.3) Gecko/2008092417 Firefox/3.0.3"); curl_setopt($ch,CURLOPT_URL,$URI); $results=curl_exec($ch); curl_close($ch); This way, Google will think you come form a US IP address and not redirect to a local Google.
