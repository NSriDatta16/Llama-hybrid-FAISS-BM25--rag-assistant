Some good reasons that come to mind: They have features that Java doesn't have and that you may find useful in certain circumstances. They use different programming paradigms, different way of thinking. Learning other languages opens your mind. Learning languages makes you conscious about their respective strengths and weaknesses. They are getting more and more attention and better JVM support (with Java 7). Actually, maybe ask yourself the reverse question: Why not learn an addiitional JVM language?
