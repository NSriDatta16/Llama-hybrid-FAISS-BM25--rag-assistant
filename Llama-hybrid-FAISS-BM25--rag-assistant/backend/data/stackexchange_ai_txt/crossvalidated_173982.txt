If you are looking to automate the clustering, you could use a k-means or hierarchical clustering approach. If you want an algorithm that would take your clusters into account, a non-linear approach such as boosting or random forest would probably work well. If the clusters you've identified are important, the trees should split along those features and then you don't have to manually specify the clusters.
