Since the question is tagged as MySQL, you should be able to use a MySQL-specific solution, namely, GROUP_CONCAT. For example, select Org_ID, Org_Address, Org_OtherDetails, GROUP_CONCAT(employees) as Employees from employees a, organization b where a.org_id=b.org_id group by b.org_id;
