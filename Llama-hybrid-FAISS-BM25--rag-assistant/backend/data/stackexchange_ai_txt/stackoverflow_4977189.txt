I am guessing that with "time series plot" you mean you want to get a bar chart instead of a line chart? In that case, you have to modify your code only slightly to pass the correct parameters to geom_bar(). The geom_bar default stat is stat_bin, which will calculate a frequency count of your categories on the x-scale.
