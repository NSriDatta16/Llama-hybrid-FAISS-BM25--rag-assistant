You can also override succesAction in OnepageController from Checkout module in Mage. This action is always called after successed new order in Magento. You can use code: $order = Mage::getModel('sales/order')->load($this->getOnepage()->getCheckout()->getLastOrderId()) $ourFileName = "order_".$order->getId().".txt"; $ourFileHandle = fopen($ourFileName, 'w') or die("can't open file"); fwrite($ourFileHandle, 'Some text'); fclose($ourFileHandle); You can put this part of code just before Mage::getSingleton('checkout/session')->clear();
