FatJar can help you in this case. In addition to the"Export as Jar" function which is included to Eclipse the Plug-In bundles all dependent JARs together into one executable jar. The Plug-In adds the Entry "Build Fat Jar" to the Context-Menu of Java-projects This is useful if your final exported jar includes other external jars.
