total_net_revenue / total_sold or total_net_revenue / total_sold / 100.0 or total_net_revenue.to_f / total_sold / 100 These three methods give an increasing amount of precision, if you want it. Remember that "average price" is "average price / sale. That's money-per-item so you will want to do the division in that specific order.
