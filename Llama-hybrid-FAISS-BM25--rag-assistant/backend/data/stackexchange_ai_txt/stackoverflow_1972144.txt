This is a very common problem in scientific computing. Some of the most effective ideas for reducing the size and complexity of code require leveraging assumptions, and science demands that you constantly change those assumptions. All you can do is try to refactor your code as you go, and try not to write yourself into any corners. Also work with good people who understand the value of not making a mess.
