The open-source Vici CoolStorage ORM library works on Windows (.NET), Mono (Mac,Linux and Windows) and MonoTouch (iPhone) using that platform's SQLite driver. To use it on these different platforms, you don't have to change anything to your source code. Just recompile, and it should work.
