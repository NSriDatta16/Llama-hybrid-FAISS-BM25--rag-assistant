In Common Lisp in what turned out to be perhaps an unfortunate decision, directories are not files. Therefore to obtain a list of directories in you example you need a wildcard in the directory fragment: (directory (make-pathname :directory '(:absolute "cygdrive" "c" "Download" :wild)))
