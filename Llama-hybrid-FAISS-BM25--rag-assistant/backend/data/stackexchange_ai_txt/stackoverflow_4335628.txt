You can try to make the other popup "not topmost" and then show your popup. You will have to find the other popup either via enumerating all open windows with the EnumWindows api func and then remove its topmost status with SetWindowsPos . But this is definitely a dirty and fragile trick (if you can make it work at all). http://pinvoke.net/default.aspx/user32.EnumWindows http://pinvoke.net/default.aspx/user32/SetWindowPos.html
