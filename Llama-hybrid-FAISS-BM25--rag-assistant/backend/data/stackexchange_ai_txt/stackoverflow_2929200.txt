Start by writing a simple functional spec , a few paragraphs from the user's perspective: what they see, how they perform actions, what they expect to happen if they click widget X. This will glue the logic together in your head, and on paper. From there you can work on the technical spec, which details the gritty things like database structure, special controls and components you need, SDK's if any, and all other developer-type details that you need to implement.
