This corresponds to an NLP task called paraphrase detection . It's an active area of research, as far as I know there's no ready-to-use system able to perform this task very well, but there are probably a good few methods and prototypes around. A quick search gives these links for example: https://aclweb.org/aclwiki/Paraphrase_Identification_(State_of_the_art) https://arxiv.org/abs/1712.02820
