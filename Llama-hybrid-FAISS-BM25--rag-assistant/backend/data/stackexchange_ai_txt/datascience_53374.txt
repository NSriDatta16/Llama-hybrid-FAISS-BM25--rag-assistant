I'm currently reading Jurafsky and Martin's Speech and Language Processing on NLP. I suggest you to take a look specifically at Chapter 17: Information Extraction . All models for this task are devoted to the extraction of lexico-syntactic patterns. They describe supervised, semi-supervised and unsupervised approaches.
