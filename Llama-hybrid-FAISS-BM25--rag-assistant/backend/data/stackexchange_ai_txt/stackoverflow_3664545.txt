Did you test your robots.txt following Googles instructions? http://www.google.com/support/webmasters/bin/answer.py?hl=en&answer=156449 To test a site's robots.txt file: On the Webmaster Tools Home page, click the site you want. Under Site configuration, click Crawler access If it's not already selected, click the Test robots.txt tab. Copy the content of your robots.txt file, and paste it into the first box. In the URLs box, list the site to test against. In the User-agents list, select the user-agents you want. Webmaster tools: https://www.google.com/webmasters/tools/home?hl=en
