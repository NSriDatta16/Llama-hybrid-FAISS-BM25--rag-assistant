The 802.11 Sequence Control field is a 16 bit little-endian field that contains two subfields - the upper 12 bits contain the Sequence Number, and the lower 4 bits contain the Fragment Number. In this case: Sequence Control = 0x9170 Sequence Number = 0x917 = 2327 decimal Fragment Number = 0x0 = 0
