If your expression actually looks so, and you don't care about grouping, it can be rewritten as ab+c? . If expression is actually more complex and having (bc) is essential, you can use negative lookahead as follows, I think it would be more elegant than Mike Samuel's solution: ab*(?!c)(bc)? .
