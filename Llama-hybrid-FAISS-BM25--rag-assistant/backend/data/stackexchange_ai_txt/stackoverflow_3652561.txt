I use following setenv.bat contents: ==============setenv.bat============ set JAVA_OPTS=-XX:MaxPermSize=256m -Xms256M -Xmx768M -Xdebug -Xnoagent -Xrunjdwp:transport=dt_socket,address=7777,server=y,suspend=n %JAVA_OPTS% ==================================== It also enables debugging and sets debug port to 7777, and appends previous content of JAVA_OPTS .
