As Roberto mentions, you can use xlwt and a trusty for-loop: import xlwt w = xlwt.Workbook() ws = w.add_sheet('mysheet') for i in range(10): ws.write(i, 0, i) ws.write(i, 1, i+1) ws.write(i, 2, xlwt.Formula("$A$%d+$B$%d" % (i+1, i+1))) w.save('myworkbook.xls')
