Well you can detect touches providing the following implementation: - (void)touchesBegan:(NSSet *)touches withEvent:(UIEvent *)event - (void)touchesEnded:(NSSet *)touches withEvent:(UIEvent *)event - (void)touchesMoved:(NSSet *)touches withEvent:(UIEvent *)event Then to reload you cells, Update your DataSource Array and call [self.tableView reloadData];
