Paste it into a spreadsheet. Some spreadsheets will parse the HTML table for you. Save it as a .CSV file and process it that way. Or . Add a column to the spreadsheet that says something like the following: ="INSERT INTO COUNTRY(CODE,NAME) VALUES=('" & A1 & "','" & B1 & "');" Then you have a column of INSERT statements that you can cut, paste and execute. Edit Be sure to include the tag when pasting into a spreadsheet. country name> Canada (CA) Alberta (AB) Canada (CA) British Columbia (BC) Canada (CA) Manitoba (MB) Processing a CSV file requires almost no parsing. It's got quotes and commas. Much easier to live with than XML/HTML.
