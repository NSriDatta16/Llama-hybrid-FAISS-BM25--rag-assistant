Convert the times to a time interval, average the two numbers, then convert it back to an NSDate object. Assuming your two times are instances of NSDate, that might look like this: NSTimeInterval intA = [timeA timeIntervalSince1970]; NSTimeInterval intB = [timeB timeIntervalSince1970]; NSDate *avgTime = [NSDate dateWithTimeIntervalSince1970:(intA + intB) / 2.0];
