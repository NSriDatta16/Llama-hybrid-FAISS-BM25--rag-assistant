To find the insertion point you could use the interpolation search (treating each date an integer in msecs for example) and when the item is inserted it could fire off a method that updates its neighbors, who then update their neighbors upon receiving the request to update, on down the line. I might be oversimplifying your problem, but from what I understand of your post, this should take on average a little more than O(n) time.
