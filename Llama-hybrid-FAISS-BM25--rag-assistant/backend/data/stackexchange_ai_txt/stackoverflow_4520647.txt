You're on a good way, so far. Building what you need requires various steps : Creating a Service , like you began to do. Tha Javadoc for this class explains how a service works a lot better than be, so I'll let you read it. To display an icon in the status bar, use NotificationManager in your Service. Lastly, the question comes of when to launch your service. You have two possibility : Launching it in your activity, and letting the service work : this is when you have to do a long operation, and want to keep user informed of what happens Launching it periodically, and then letting it come back to sleep : use this method if you only need to check something periodically. For this usage, you can use AlarmManager
