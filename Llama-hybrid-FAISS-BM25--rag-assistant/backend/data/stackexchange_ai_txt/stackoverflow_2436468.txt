I'd recommend taking a look at Akka ( www.akkasource.org ) It's a Scala Actor framework with ALOT of plumbing for making scalable backend applications. Out of the box it supports: Actor Supervision Remote actors Cluster Membership Comet over JAX-RS (Project Atmosphere) HTTP Auth for your services Distributed storage engine support (Cassandra, MongoDB) + a whole lot more
