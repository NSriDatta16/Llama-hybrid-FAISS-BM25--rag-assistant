Create a robots.txt containing the following text: User-Agent: * Disallow: /page1.html Disallow: /page2.html The file must then be placed at http://www.site.com/robots.txt . For more information, see Wikipedia . In addition, Google has a robots.txt tester , available from their Webmaster Tools, which lets you verify that it is formatted correctly.
