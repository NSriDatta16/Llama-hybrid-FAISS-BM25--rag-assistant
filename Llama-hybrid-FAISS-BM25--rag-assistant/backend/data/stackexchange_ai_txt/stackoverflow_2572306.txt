I believe it gets blocked by the User-Agent. You can change User-Agent using the following sample code: USERAGENT = 'something' HEADERS = {'User-Agent': USERAGENT} req = urllib2.Request(URL_HERE, headers=HEADERS) f = urllib2.urlopen(req) s = f.read() f.close()
