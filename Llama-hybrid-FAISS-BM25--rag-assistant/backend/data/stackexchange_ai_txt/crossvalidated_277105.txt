Here's a conceptual description that might help you understand the difference between AR and MA. In the AR world, the next value in a time series is a direct function of the last known values. i.e forecast = f(t1,t2,t3) In the MA world, the next value in a time series is a function of the errors you've made in prediction in the previous instances. i.e. forecast = f(e1,e2,e3) , where e = forecast - actual
