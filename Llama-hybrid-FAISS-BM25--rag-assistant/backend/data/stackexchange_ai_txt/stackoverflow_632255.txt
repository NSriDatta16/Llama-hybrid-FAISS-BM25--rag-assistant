If this is a Unix system, parse the output of uptime . This shows the CPU load which is generally considered to be a good measure of "busy." Anything near or over 1.0 means "completely busy." There are three CPU "load" times in that output giving the load average over 1, 5, and 15 minutes. Pick whichever makes sense for your script. Definition of "load" is here .
