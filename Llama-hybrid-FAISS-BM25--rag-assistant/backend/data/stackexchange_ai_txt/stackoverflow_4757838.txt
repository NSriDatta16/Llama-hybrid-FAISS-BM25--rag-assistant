Database and XML aren't opposites - this looks an ideal application for an XML database! But whichever technology you use, you will need to pay careful attention to performance at every stage of implementation. Poor performance is rarely the consequence of choosing technology that isn't up to the job, but it's often the consequence of using technology that you don't understand well enough.
