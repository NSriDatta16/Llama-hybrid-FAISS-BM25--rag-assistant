Many organization are moving to WebServices and the open standards relating to them (HTTP, WS-*) as alternatives to Corba. This article provides a comparison of the two technologies and offers some recommendations on when to use which. If you really care about platform independence and protocol standardization - then the WS-* standards are something to look into.
