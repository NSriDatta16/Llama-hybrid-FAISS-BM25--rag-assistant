Riffing off of Tim Henigan's answer , put this in your .vimrc cmap / =substitute(substitute(@/, '^\\ $', '', '') Now when you hit CTRL-R / in command line mode, it will drop the word boundry markers, so you should be able to use :!grep CTRL-R / file-list
