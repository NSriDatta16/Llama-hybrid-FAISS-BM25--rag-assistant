There are two steps: You need to know how to apply a Bayesian Network and how to define the nodes and the belief propagation for your game. To do this, you need to read tutorials. Apply software. This is quite easy, there are plenty of free/open source implementations. At the end of the wiki page http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bayesian_network , there are a list of software. You may want to check Infer.Net , which is a mature library developed in MSR Cambridge.
