It rather depends on the desires of the management than on the desires of the customers. Given a choice of 'you can have it working, or you can have it Friday', the average target-and-goal loving manager will prefer to have it Friday. If you actually have a choice, please leave it until it works. You'll save yourself and everyone else a deal of time and trouble. Time(do it right) (Fundamental law of software engineering.)
