You can use urlparse to do that from urlparse import urlparse, parse_qs qs = urlparse("http://example.com/hello?q=1&b=1").query parse_qs(qs) if you must use FieldStorage cgi.FieldStorage(environ={'REQUEST_METHOD':'GET', 'QUERY_STRING':qs})
