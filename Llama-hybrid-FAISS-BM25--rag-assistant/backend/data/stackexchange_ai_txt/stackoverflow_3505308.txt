Mobile webkit doesn't use mousedown/mouseup events that are the basis for conventional web drag and drop. Instead, mobile webkit uses touch events. Have a look at this blog post for details: http://www.sitepen.com/blog/2008/07/10/touching-and-gesturing-on-the-iphone/
