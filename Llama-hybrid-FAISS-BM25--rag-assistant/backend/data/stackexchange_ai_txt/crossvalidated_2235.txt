Popular right now are randomForest and gbm (called MART or Gradient Boosting in machine learning literature), rpart for simple trees. Also popular is bayesglm, which uses MAP with priors for regularization. install.packages(c("randomForest", "gbm", "rpart", "arm")) library(randomForest) library(gbm) library(rpart) library(arm) r1
