Quick way might be to only look at the ones with exactly three matches (with an inner query), and then among those only include the ones that have 19, 20, and 28. Of course, that is sort of a brute force method, and not very elegant. But it has the small benefit of being understandable. None of the approaches I can think of will be easy to customize to various other sets of values.
