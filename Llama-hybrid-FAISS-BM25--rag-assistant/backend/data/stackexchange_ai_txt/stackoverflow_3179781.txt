See if this works for you. All your data is in column A (beginning at row 1). In column B, row 1, enter =A1&"," This will make cell B1 equal A1 with a comma appended. Now select cell B1 and drag from the bottom right of cell down through all your rows (this copies the formula and uses the corresponding column A value.) Select the newly appended data, copy it and paste it where you need using Paste -> By Value That's It!
