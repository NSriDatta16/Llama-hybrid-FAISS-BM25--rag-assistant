Add surrounding quotes on selected text/word Useful when writing emails or during coding... Doubleclick word, hit Win+X, have quotes around ; Win + X #x:: ; Attention: Strips formatting from the clipboard too! Send ^c clipboard = "%clipboard%" ; Remove space introduced by WORD StringReplace, clipboard, clipboard,%A_SPACE%",", All Send ^v return
