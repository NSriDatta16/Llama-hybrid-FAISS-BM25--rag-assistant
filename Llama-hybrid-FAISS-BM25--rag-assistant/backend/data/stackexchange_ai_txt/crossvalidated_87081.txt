The storks example is on page 8 of the first edition (1978) of Box, Hunter & Hunter's book entitled "Statistics for Experimenters..." (Wiley). I don't know whether it's in the 2nd edition. They identify the city as Oldenburg and the time period as 1930-1936. They reference Ornithologische Monatsberichte , 44 , No 2, Jahrgang, 1936, Berlin, and 48 , No 1, Jahrgang, 1940, Berlin, and Statistiches Jahrbuch Deutscher Gemeinden , 27-33, 1932-1938, Gustav Fischer, Jena.
