How to generate a realization from a transition matrix?

Consider a Markov chain of 4 states described by the transition matrix, $$ T_{ij} = \begin{bmatrix} 0.40 & 0.56 & 0.03 & 0.01\\ 0.45 & 0.51 & 0.04 & 0.00\\ 0.25 & 0.25 & 0.25 & 0.25 \\ 0.00 & 0.00 & 0.01 & 0.99 \end{bmatrix} $$ How can I generate a realisation of the Markov chain from it in python using the numpy arrays? What exactly it means by realisation of the Markov chain? import numpy as np x = np.array([ [ 0.40, 0.56, 0.03, 0.01], [0.45, 0.51, 0.04, 0.00], [0.25, 0.25, 0.25, 0.25 ], [0.00, 0.00, 0.01, 0.99 ]]) ```
