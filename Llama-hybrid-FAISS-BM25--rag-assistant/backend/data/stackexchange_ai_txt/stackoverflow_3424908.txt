Not sure this will solve your problem but GlassFish Embedded provides a Java EE 6 implementation. Add this to your pom.xml : ... glassfish-extras-repository http://download.java.net/maven/glassfish/org/glassfish/extras ... org.glassfish.extras glassfish-embedded-all 3.0.1 test javax javaee-api 6.0 provided ... ... It's important to declare the glassfish-embedded-all artifact before the javaee-api .
