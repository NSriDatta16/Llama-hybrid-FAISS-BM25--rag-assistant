If you want to share information between servlets, Why not use ServletContext ? You can use ServletContext.setAttribute(key, object) (for uploading) and ServletContext.getAttribute(key) for retrieval. There is 1 ServletContext per web application, per JVM. So, each servlet, in your webapp, have the same ServletContext .
