There is no secure way to store that just on the client. Best scenario I could think of is the HTML5 storage . But that means you doom your customers to HTML5 browsers . Writing and reading textfiles from the local filesystem is a bad thing (bad bad karma) which requires lots of security tokens / browser settings. I would really consider using serverside database storage.
