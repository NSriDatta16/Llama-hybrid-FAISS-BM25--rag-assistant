The "Console" tab in the Xcode Organizer or iPhone Configuration Utility will give you device log messages. It gives messages for all apps (not just yours!) but that's not the end of the world, really. Your app is missing the entitlement get-task-allow . You might be able to self-sign it with this to make it work. I'm not sure, since I'm not sure what steps you're following to get it to run.
