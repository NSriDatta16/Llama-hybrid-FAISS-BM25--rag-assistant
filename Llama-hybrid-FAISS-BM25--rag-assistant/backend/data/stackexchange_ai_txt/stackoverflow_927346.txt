The answer is storage space and fixed size rows. Fixed-size rows are much, MUCH faster to search than variable length rows, because you can seek directly to the correct byte if you know which record number and field you want. Edit: Having said that, if you use proper indexing in your database tables, the fixed-size rows thing isn't as important as it used to be.
