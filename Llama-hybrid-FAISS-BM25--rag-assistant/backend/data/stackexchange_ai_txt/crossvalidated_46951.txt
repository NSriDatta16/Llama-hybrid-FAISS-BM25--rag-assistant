Some references that might interest you: 1] Authoritative sources in a hyperlinked environment Jon Kleinberg JACM 1999 http://www.cs.cornell.edu/home/kleinber/auth.pdf 2] Truth discovery with multiple conflicting information providers on the web Xiaoxin Yin, Jiawei Han, Philip Yu KDD 2007 http://www.cs.uiuc.edu/~hanj/pdf/kdd07_xyin.pdf 3] A Bayesian approach to discovering truth from conflicting sources for data integration Bo Zhao, Benjamin Rubinstein, Jim Gemmel, Jiawei Han VLDB 2012 http://vldb.org/pvldb/vol5/p550_bozhao_vldb2012.pdf If you check the references and look at papers that cite these papers, you should get lot more papers along these lines.
