Well, unless you are using HTML5 I'm assuming you'll be using Flash to display video. FCKEditor has a built in dialogue box for embedding flash movies. Take a look at the demo http://www.fckeditor.net/demo and look for the Flash control found on the second row, 6th icon in from the right.
