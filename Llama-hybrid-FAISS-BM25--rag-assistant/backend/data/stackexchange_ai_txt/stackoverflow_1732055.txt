Your function works fine for me. Your assignment won't work with spaces around the equal sign. It should be: SOME_STRING=$(padSpaces TEST 10) I took the liberty of replacing the backticks, too. You don't show how you are using the variable or how you obtain the output you showed. However, your problem may be that you need to quote your variables. Here's a demonstration: $ SOME_STRING=$(padSpaces TEST 10) $ sq=\' $ echo $sq$SOME_STRING$sq 'TEST ' $ echo "$sq$SOME_STRING$sq" 'TEST '
