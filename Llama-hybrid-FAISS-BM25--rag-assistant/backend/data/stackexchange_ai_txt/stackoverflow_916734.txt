Starting from Microsoft BizTalk CRM 4.0 adapter onwards, use the adapter auto-generated schema 'CRMAdapterResponse_Response.xsd', instead of 'response.xsd'. This schema will be specific to the organization under consideration. The use of the schema will solve your 'schema does not match expected type' problem. Thanks.
