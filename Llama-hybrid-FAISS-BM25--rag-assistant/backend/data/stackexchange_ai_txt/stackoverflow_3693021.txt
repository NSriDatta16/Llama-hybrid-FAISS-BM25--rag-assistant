Per the Haml documentation, interpolation within filters is normal and expected. To circumvent it, you can just escape the #{} interpolation syntax by prepending a backslash: :markdown like_frags = [fields].flatten.map { |f| "LOWER(\#{f}) LIKE :word\#{count}" } or_frags
