Is a word embedding a vector or a function?

Is a word embedding a vector or a function? I have read contradicting statements: Function : A word embedding $W: \text{words} \rightarrow R^n$ is is a paramaterized function mapping words in some language to high-dimensional vectors. Vector : Most recently, it has been proposed to represent words as dense vector [...]. These representations, referred to as “neural embeddings” or “word embeddings”, have been shown to perform well across a variety of tasks
