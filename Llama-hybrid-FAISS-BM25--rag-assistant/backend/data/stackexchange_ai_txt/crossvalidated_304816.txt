In the context of time series (but why would you use one side filters otherwise?): One side filters : because they only use past data, these filters can be used for backtesting and for online analysis (= what is the current filtered value) Two-sided filters : (among which are the so called "centered filters") are only for post-facto analysis, given that at any point in time they require future data...
