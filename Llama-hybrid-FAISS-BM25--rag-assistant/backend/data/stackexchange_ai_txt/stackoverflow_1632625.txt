There is no rule of thumb. In many cases you can formulate your problem to make use of either of them. Machine learning is still an active area of research and which learning model to use can be debatable. GA's take sexy languages from evolution but you're waiting for your computer to stumble upon a solution through a random process. Study your data, make good assumptions, try to know what you want and pick an approach that can make good use of these. If your first choice gives poor results, know why it was so, and improve the algorithm itself or pick a better one.
