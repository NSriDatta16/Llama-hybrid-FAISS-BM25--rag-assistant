It mostly depends on your labels. Training one classifier with N labels (= multitask) makes more sense when labels are related. But one cannot predict in advance with 100% certainty whether multitask will outperform single task (= training N classifiers for N labels) This paper studied the question: Caruana, R. (1997). Multitask learning: A knowledge-based source of inductive bias.
