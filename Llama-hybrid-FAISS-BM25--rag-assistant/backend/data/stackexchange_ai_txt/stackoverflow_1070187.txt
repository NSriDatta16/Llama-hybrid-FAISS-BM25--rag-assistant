You're trying to prevent CSRF - Cross-Site Request Forgery . Jeff himself has a blog article about this. True XSRF Prevention requires three parts: Hidden Input Fields, to prevent someone from just snatching the form and embedding it Timechecking within an epsilon of the form being generated, otherwise someone can generate a valid form once and use the token (depending on impementation/how it's stored) Cookies: this is to prevent a malicious server from pretending it's a client, and performing a man-in-the-middle attack
