If tables are stored in the InnoDB storage engine, mysqldump provides a way of making an online backup of these (see command below). shell> mysqldump --all-databases --single-transaction > all_databases.sql This may help... specifically the --single-transaction option, and not the --all-databases one... (from the mysqldump manpage)
