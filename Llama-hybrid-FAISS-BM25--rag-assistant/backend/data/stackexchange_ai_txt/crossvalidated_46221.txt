Try the following: Take the log of the PE ratios. Apply a time series model that you already have the software for. For example ARIMA, ETS. Exponentiate the forecasts. Do they look realistic? Repeat the above three steps to test your model out of sample. Disclaimer: Earnings are announced at sparse intervals. There are others factors that are not being measured with the above suggested procedure.
