If your text paragraphs are separated by a blank line, this seems to work: :g!/^\s*$/normal vipJ :g global (multi-repeat) !/^\s*$/ match all lines except blank lines and those containing only whitespace. normal enters 'normal' mode vip visually select inner paragraph J join lines
