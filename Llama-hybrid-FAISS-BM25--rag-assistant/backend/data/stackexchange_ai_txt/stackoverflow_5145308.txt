It's a family of styles that allow components to communicate through asynchronous messages. Systems based on event-based styles are often organized as a loosely coupled federation of components that trigger behavior in other components through events. A variety of event styles exist, for example: Point-to-Point Publish-Subscribe Crosscutting concerns are addressed by aspect style. Aspect style is a module style, event-based style - runtime style, so it's important not to mix them up.
