Few more measures of "Statistical Difference" Permutation test (by Fisher) Central Limit Theorem & Slutsky’s theorem Mann-Whitney-Wilcoxin test Anderson–Darling test Shapiro–Wilk test Hosmer–Lemeshow test Kuiper's test kernelized Stein discrepancy Jaccard similarity Also, hierarchical clustering deals with similarity measures between groups. The most popular measures of group similarity are perhaps the single linkage, complete linkage, and average linkage.
