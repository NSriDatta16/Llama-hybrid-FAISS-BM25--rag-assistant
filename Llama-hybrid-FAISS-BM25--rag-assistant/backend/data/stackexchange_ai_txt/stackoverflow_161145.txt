To complete Peter Kelley answer, you might consider for eclipse IDE the AST solution. You might then write an AST program which parse your source code and does what you want. A full example is available in this eclipse corner article , also more details in the eclipse help . And you can find some examples in Listing 5 of the section "Implementation of in-place translation" of Automating the embedding of Domain Specific Languages in Eclipse JDT , alongside multiple examples in GitHub projects .
