ELKI includes a number of approaches to do this. They are called EigenPairFilter in ELKI, as they are used for splitting PCA results into high variance and low variance vectors. Java package documentation The most common approach seems to be to stop when you've reached at least $\alpha$ of the total variance, with e.g. $\alpha = 0.85$.
