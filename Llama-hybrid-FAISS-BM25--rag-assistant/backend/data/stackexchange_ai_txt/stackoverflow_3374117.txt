AFAIK, as long as you have a reference to a valid Context object, it is safe to create a Toast. However, in your case, I think you might be better off using the Notification Bar's API. http://developer.android.com/reference/android/app/Notification.html It would probably give your users a better experience to not have your app interrupt their screen space to tell them your app didn't do what it was supposed to. To the average user, that's two reasons to uninstall that they suddenly have.
