It appears you'll just use the ratio of some kind (e.g. ratio of vertical and horizontal dimensions in an image) to predict if the object is an hand or foot . With just looking at one variable, your algorithms will do nothing but thresholding. Logistic regression, SVM, or one-level decision trees try to achieve this.
