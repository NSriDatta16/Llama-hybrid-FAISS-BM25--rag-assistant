How can I print single quotes around attribute values instead of double quotes with the javax.xml.transform.Tansformer class?

However, it prints the attributes' values with double quotes around them, as opposed to single quotes. So, that being said, does anyone know of an output property I could set to tell the transformer to print single quotes instead of double quotes? Thanks for your help, B.J.
