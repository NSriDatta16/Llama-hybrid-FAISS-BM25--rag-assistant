A Markov chain is a stochastic process. A stochastic process in discrete time n ∈ $N$ = {0, 1, 2, . . .} is a sequence of random variables (rvs) $X_0, X_1, X_2$ , . . . denoted by $X = \{X_n : n ≥ 0\}$ . So, for your question, each of them is a Markov chain.
