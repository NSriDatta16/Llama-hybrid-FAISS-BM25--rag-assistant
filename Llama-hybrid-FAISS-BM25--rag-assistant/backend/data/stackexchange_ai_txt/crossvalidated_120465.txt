Ben - You can fit $ln(y) = \beta x$ with a generalized linear regression (GLM) model called a Poissson regression. Better yet, since the observed y is either 0 or 1, one uses a logistic regression to estimate probability of y, where the logit function replaces the log: $$ logit(y) = \beta x$$
