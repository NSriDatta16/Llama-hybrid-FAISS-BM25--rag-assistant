Conditionals in constructors aren't problematic per se. However, in this instance, I'd be inclined to write your constructor like this: public PDFParagraph(PDFPhrase phrase, boolean isRtl) { super(phrase); setAlignment(isRtl ? Element.ALIGN_RIGHT : Element.ALIGN_LEFT); }
