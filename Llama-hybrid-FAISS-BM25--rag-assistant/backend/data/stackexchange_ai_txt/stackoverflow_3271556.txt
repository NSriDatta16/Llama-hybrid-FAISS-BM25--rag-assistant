When you say "update the results to a form text field" do you mean you want the deleted word to go to a form text field or the resulting paragraph after the text has been removed? If the former: $("span").click(function () { $('#myfield').val($(this).remove().text()); }); edit - see comment below If the latter: $("span").click(function () { var $p = $(this).parent('p'); $(this).remove(); $('#myfield').val($p.text()); });
