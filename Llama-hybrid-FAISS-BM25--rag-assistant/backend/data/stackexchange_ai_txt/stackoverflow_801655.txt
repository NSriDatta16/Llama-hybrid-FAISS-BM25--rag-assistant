It should be possible to do so if you implement a service on your server that does the retrieval. So your AJAX-request would call a URL like the following: http://myownserver.com/getContent?url=theotherserver.com That way you could get around the XSS-security protection of your browser, but the remote server will then see the IP of your own server instead of that of the client.
