Yes, you can. I do something similar at one of my sites, although with href instead of id , but id works too. A quick example: $('a[id]').click(function(e) { // This will change the URL fragment. The change is reflected // on your browser's address bar as well window.location.hash = this.id; e.preventDefault(); });
