I = imread('peppers.png'); H = fspecial('average', [5 5]); I = imfilter(I, H); imshow(I) Note that filters can be applied to intensity images (2D matrices) using filter2 , while on multi-dimensional images (RGB images or 3D matrices) imfilter is used. Also on Intel processors, imfilter can use the Intel Integrated Performance Primitives (IPP) library to accelerate execution.
