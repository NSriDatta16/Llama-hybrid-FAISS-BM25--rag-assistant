You only need to test floor(y**(0.25)-1) . As y approaches infinity, x approaches y**0.25-1.5 (from underneath). To some extent, this is rather intuitive. x*(x+1)*(x+2)*(x+3) is a product of four numbers whose average is equal to x+1.5 . When x is high, 1.5 looks small.
