How can I make the decimal places of AVG function in sql limit to 2 only?

I want to limit the decimal of the average to 2.. SELECT grade.GStudNo, AVG(grade.Grade) AS Average, students.LName, students.FName, students.MName, students.Course FROM students INNER JOIN grade ON students.StudNo = grade.GStudNo WHERE GSem = '$sem' AND GYear = '$year' GROUP BY grade.GStudNo ORDER BY Average ASC LIMIT 3
