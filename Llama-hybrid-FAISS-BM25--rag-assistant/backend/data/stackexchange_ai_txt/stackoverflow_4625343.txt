How are you accessing your table from code?? The error would indicate that somewhere in your code, you access the table and your code depends on the sequence of the columns. Since you moved away your column to another place, now suddenly your code is trying to access column no. 17 (or whatever) and assumes that this is a uniqueidentifier column - but that's no longer the case, since you reorganized your table's column order....
