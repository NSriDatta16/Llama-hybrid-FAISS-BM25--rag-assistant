You can to write your own ad-hoc transformer. It's not hard, look at the source of AliasToBeanResultTransformer . A better alternative is writing an object model that actually reflects your DB and domain. If there are multiple versions of Bar, then the version number (or whatever is used to identify it) should be part of the key. That way you'd get rid of all the hacks.
