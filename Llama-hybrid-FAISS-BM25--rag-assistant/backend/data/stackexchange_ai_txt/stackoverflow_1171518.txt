You could use the eval() function, but don't do it . Seriously. There's no "safe" way to let any old user run their own PHP on your server. You are exposing yourself to a potential world of hurt. Instead, provide excellent documentation, code samples, and the source for your own demos, and encourage potential users try it out on their own test/development servers.
