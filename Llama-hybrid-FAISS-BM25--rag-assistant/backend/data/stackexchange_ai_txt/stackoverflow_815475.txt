Since you know JavaME, take a look at developing for Android . It doesn't have a large market share, but at least you'll be able to leverage your existing skills. You won't be able to find a common language and development environment that works on the majority of phone platforms. Once you select a platform, you're kind of committed to it unless you want to port your app to an entirely different language.
