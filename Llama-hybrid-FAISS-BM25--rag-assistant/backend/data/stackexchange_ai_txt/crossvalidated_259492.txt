Adj $R^2$ provides Nagelkerke's modification of $R^2$ to correct for cases (esp. logistic regression) where the likelihood-ratio based $R^2$ MuMIn calculates would not otherwise have a maximum value of 1. Found this in help file for function r.squaredLR .
