Perl does not provide a built-in to solve your problem. If you know that the keys cover a particular range 0..N , you can leverage that fact: my $n = keys(%hash) - 1; my @keys_and_values = map { $_ => $hash{$_} } 0 .. $n; my @just_values = @hash{0 .. $n};
