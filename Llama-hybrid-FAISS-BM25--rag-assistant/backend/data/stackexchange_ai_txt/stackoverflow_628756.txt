You need to specify the target folder even if you are at the target folder. For example, if my files were in Foo folder and I want to move them to Bar folder. Let's say that I'm in Foo folder, and here is how the directories are organized: / (root) |--Foo/ |--Bar/ Then, I would do (inside Foo folder): mv Picture_* ../Bar/
