Fun, quick results. Capture the attention span of the kid. Interpretive shells like most scripting languages offer (command line) that lets the student just type 1 or 2 liners is a big deal. python: >>> 1+1 2 Boom, instant feedback, kid thinks "the computer is talking back". Kids love that. Remember Eliza , anyone? If they get bogged down in installing an IDE, creating a project, bleh bleh bleh, sometimes the tangents will take you away from the main topic. BASIC is good too. Look for some things online like "SIMPLE" : http://www.simplecodeworks.com/website.html
