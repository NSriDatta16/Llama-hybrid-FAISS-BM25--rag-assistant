First things first, there is no way that you can estimate the length of every little task in big projects. Categorize your tasks by criticality, and only estimate times for those tasks that are crucial to your project. The next move would be to allocate free time amidst less critical tasks that are simultaneously going on with the critical tasks. Gantt charts would serve this purpose beautifully.
