Yes, there have been studies on online social networks (Facebook and Twitter) that show that two random users are connected are at an average distance of 4.7 hops (which means an average of 3.7 degrees of separation). Some pair of users, however, may be more distant, or even not connected at all. http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Six_degrees_of_separation
