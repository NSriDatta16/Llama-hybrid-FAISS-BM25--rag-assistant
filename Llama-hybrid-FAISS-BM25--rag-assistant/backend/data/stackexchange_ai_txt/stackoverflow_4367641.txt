If you want to distinguish real screenshot from fake, try embedding small change in picture that will be invisible to the eye but you will be able to detect from your code, for example pixel on location (2,2) on the client area of the form could have RGB values slightly altered (+3,+2,+1) - which will be unseen. Look up steganography .
