You need to move your ZIP archive from the "Link Binary with Libraries" build phase to the "Copy Bundle Resources" phase. Open up your target with the disclosure triangle, and drag it from one to the other. I'd guess that it ends up in the Libraries phase as an unexpected side-effect of the Java support built into Xcode.
