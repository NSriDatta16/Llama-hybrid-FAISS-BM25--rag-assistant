Network data is all about statistics. There are node level statistics (describing individual points within the graph) such as degree centrality, and graph-level statistics which describe the overall network, such as average distance between nodes, density, and fragmentation. https://www.amazon.com/Statistical-Analysis-Network-Data-Statistics/dp/038788145X
