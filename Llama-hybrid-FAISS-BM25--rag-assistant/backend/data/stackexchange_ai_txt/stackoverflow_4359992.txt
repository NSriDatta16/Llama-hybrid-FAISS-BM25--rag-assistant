There is no perfect way to do this. Windows has a built in speech recognition system that we have used and works quite well. You have to train it for different users. We also use the (commercial) Dragon /Nuance tools. These systems can interact with the active areas on the screen (e.g. buttons) You don't say whether you want this to create documents or whether you wish to issue commands to the machine.
