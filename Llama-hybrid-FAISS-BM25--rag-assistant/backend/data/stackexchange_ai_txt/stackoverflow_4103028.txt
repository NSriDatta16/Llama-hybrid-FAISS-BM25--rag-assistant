To match up the Lookup columns: Click on the field in the table on the left (Available Input Columns), then drag your mouse pointer to the matching field in the other table (Available Lookup Columns). This defines what fields to match. Then, to specify what column to return (ie. output), click on the checkbox next to that column in the table on the right.
