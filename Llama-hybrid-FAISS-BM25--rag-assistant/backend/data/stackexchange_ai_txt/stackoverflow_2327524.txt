Long story short. You can't replicate the join behavior when you work with the Table Storage; or, more precisely, any solution, that achieves such behavior, comes with terrible performance . Thus, what the point of using Table Storage, if your code does not scale at all? With Table Storage, you have to think your design from the start not to need any Join .
