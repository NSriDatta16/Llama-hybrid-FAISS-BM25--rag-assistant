I agree with Jon's answer, wrt the fact that you shouldn't expect all your data in one read. Here are some blogs that have helped me with this problem in the past: Aviad Ezra has an excellent series on Asynchronous Sockets: .NET Sockets - Two Way - Single Client .NET Sockets in Two Directions with Multiple Client Support Sending Typed (Serialized) Messages over .NET Sockets This blog is particularly useful if you decide to go the length-prefixed route, the Author uses a MemoryStream as his temporary storage between reads: How to Transfer Variable Length Messages With Async Sockets
