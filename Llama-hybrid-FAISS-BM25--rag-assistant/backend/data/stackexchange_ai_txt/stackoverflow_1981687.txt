Yes, using Cookies . But be careful, don't put too much in them (I think there is a limit at 4kb). But a few variables are ok. If you need to store considerably more than that, check out @Annie's great tips in the other answer. For small time data storage, I would say Cookies are the easiest thing. Note that cookies are stored client side.
