If you're sending non-character data (i.e. binary data for example) in your XML, you might encode them using Base64. But I'm not sure I've understood your question correctly. Maybe you just forgot to encode your XML in UTF-8, using transformer.setOutputProperty(OutputKeys.ENCODING, "UTF-8")
