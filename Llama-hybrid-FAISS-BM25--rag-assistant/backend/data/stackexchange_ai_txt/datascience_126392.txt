This was studied in the original BERT article , which concluded that the best approach was to concatenate the states of the last 4 layers: Although BERT preceeded RoBERTa, we may understand this observation to be somewhat applicable to RoBERTa, which is very similar. You may, nonetheless, experiment with the precise number of layer states to concatenate to see what value gives the best results.
