You could calculate the mean using a Cumulative moving average to only iterate through the lists once: from collections import defaultdict averages = defaultdict(float) count = defaultdict(int) for name,result in zip(names,result_values): count[name] += 1 averages[name] += (result - averages[name]) / count[name]
