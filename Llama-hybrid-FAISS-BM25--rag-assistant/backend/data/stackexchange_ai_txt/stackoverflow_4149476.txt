Maybe this sounds too simple, but it works. Randomly distribute values to all the cells until only two are empty. Test for adjacency of all values. Compute the average the percent of successful casts vs. all casts until the variance drops to within an acceptable margin. The risk goes to zero and the that which is at risk is only a little runtime.
