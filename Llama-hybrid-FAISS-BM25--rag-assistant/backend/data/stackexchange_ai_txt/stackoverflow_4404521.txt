The Provisioning Portal Distribution How-To explicitly states: Only Team Agents are authorized to prepare and submit applications for distribution. Team Agents are also the only ones authorized to create an Ad-Hoc distribution profile. However, once the distribution profile is created, the Team Agent should be able to download it and hand it off to you for creating Ad-Hoc distribution builds.
