In the test code itself: With method level comments explaining what the test is testing / covering. At the class level, a comment indicating the actual class being tested (which could actually be inferred from the test class name so that's actually less important than the comments at the method level). With test coverage reports Such as Cobertura . That's also documentation, since it indicates what your tests are covering and what they're not.
