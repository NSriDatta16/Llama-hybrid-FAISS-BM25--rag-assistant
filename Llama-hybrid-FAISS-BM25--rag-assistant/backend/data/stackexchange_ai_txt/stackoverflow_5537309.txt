Yes. Connect to your emulator/device using ADB. Then make the catalog world writable, using chmod 777 You can also add the permission WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE to your manifest file. Read the instructions here: http://www.netmite.com/android/mydroid/2.0/dalvik/docs/heap-profiling.html
