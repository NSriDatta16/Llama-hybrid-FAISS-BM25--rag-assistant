How is fromlen being set when you call recvfrom() ? If fromlen > sizeof(add1) , you are possibly writing over add2 by accident. Beej's Guide suggests that you use local variables of type struct sockaddr_storage , which is guaranteed to be big enough to hold any of the struct sockaddr_foo s in use.
