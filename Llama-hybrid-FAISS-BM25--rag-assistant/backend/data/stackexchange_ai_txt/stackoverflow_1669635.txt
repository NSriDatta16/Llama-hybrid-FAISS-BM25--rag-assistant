The real win that SOA brings is that it reduces the number of linkages between applications. In the past I've worked with organizations who have done it a many different ways. Some data layers are integrated, and some are abstracted. The way I've seen it most successfully done is when you create generic data-layer services for each app/database and you create the higher level services based on your newly created data layer.
