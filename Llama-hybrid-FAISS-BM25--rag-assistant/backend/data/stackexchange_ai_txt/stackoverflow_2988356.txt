To grab multiple list items per iteration, use something like List::MoreUtils::natatime or use splice: my @tmparray = @array; # don't trash original array while ( my ($a,$b,$c) = splice(@tmparray,0,3) ) { print "$a , $b , $c\n"; } Or reorganize your data into multiple arrays and use one of the Algorithm::Loops::MapCar* functions to loop over multiple arrays at once.
