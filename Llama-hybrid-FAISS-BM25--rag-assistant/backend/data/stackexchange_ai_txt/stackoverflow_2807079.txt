If you're using jQuery, why not leverage it? Assuming your version of removeAttribute() does the same thing as the native javascript method removeAttribute() , you could do something like: $(document).ready(function(){ $('a#somelink').click(function(){ $('#foo').removeAttr('bar'); }); });
