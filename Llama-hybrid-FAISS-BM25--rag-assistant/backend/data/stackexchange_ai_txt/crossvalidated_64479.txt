An amazing paper on the subject was written by Leo Breiman, the father of random forests, see Statistical Modeling: The Two Cultures . It would not be correct to say that all statistical inference algorithms are based on statistical models, but this is generally true. This question was asked by others and led to the development of the machine learning community.
