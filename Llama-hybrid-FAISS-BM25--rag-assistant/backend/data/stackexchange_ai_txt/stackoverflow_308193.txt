The proper way to use javascript and Ajax is to first code your pages and delivery content without javascript. All content should show in a logically organized manner. Once this is done you can use JS/Ajax to provide superior usability to the visitors who have JS enabled. That will benefit all your users, javascript enabled and disabled, and the search engines.
