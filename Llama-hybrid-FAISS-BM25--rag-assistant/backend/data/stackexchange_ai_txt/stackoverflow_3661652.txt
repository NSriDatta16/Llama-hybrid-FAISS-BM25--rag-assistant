The bot is discarding session info and ignoring your cookies. This means that it shows up multiple times in your log. Yes, it does hit your site quickly and without session info, so you don't filter it out. Your best option is to have some kind of duplicate filter for bots or IPs that hit quickly and repeatedly this way. You might also try adding explicit ignores to the few bot user agents that cause this problem with your script. By the time you have 10 or 15, you'll have dealt with the majority of the problem UAs.
