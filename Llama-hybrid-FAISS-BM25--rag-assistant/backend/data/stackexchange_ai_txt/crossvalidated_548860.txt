You are thinking about doing an 'individual participant/patient data meta-analysis' (IPDMA). It's a bit more involved though than just combining some datasets and pretending they are one large study. To start, you need to respect the multilevel nature of the data. For a very thorough coverage of the topic, see: Riley, R. D., Tierney, J. F., Stewart, L. A. (Eds) (2021). Individual participant data meta-analysis: A handbook for healthcare research . Wiley. https://www.wiley.com/en-am/Individual+Participant+Data+Meta+Analysis:+A+Handbook+for+Healthcare+Research-p-9781119333722
