You obviously have problem adding namespaces to the namespace manager. Also, your XPath expression will at best select the top element, however you want z:row . One simple and not too-elegant way to avoid namespace processing is the following Use : SelectSingleNode("/*/*/*") or SelectSingleNode("/*/*[local-name()='data']/*[local-name()='row']")
