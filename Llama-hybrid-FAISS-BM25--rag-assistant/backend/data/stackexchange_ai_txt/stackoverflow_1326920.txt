I'd use DateTime::Format::Strptime to convert the date into a DateTime object, then ask it to provide the desired representation of the date. e.g.: my $parser = DateTime::Format::Strptime->new(pattern => '%a, %d %b %Y %T %z'); my $dt = $parser->parse_datetime($original_timestamp); # Postgres-format timestamp for db storage my $pg_timestamp = DateTime::Format::Pg->format_datetime($dt); # Epoch timestamp for if I'm going to do the comparison in code my $epoch = $dt->epoch;
