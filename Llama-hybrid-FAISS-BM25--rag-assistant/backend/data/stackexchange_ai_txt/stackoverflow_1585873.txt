regular expressions are certainly a very powerful way of matching text, namely regular languages. of course, today's regex engines handle far more than regular languages (through recursion, code insertion and other tricks). the next step beyond regular expressions are grammar parsers (yacc, bison et al). if you are interested in parsing, i would encourage you to take a look at the upcoming grammar parser implemented in perl6, which seems to provide a very powerful blend of regex and grammar based parsing, moving far beyond the limitations of conventional regex
