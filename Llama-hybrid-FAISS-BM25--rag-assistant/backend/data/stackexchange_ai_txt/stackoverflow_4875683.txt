I don't know of a book that deals with streams as its major subject. I'm sure you can find a lot of resources on the Internet, but judging their quality may be difficult. The following two books have rather good coverage of streams and might be work looking into. Of course they cover a lot more than just streams. The C++ Programming Language by Bjarne Stroustrup The C++ Standard Library: A Tutorial and Reference by Nicholai Josuttis
