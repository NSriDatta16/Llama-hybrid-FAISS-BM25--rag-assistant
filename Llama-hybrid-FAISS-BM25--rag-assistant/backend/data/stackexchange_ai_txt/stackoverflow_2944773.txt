What kind of Mail Delivery Agent are you using? Are you developing your own? In that case, are you planning to implement IMAP protocol? If you're using Cyrus (or any other product that handles IMAP) with SORT and THREAD extensions , then it's already built in. In both cases, you should take a look at RFC 5256 .
