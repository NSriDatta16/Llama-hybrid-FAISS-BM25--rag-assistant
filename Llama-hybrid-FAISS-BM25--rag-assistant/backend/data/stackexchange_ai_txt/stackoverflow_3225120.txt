You can have an iframe that points to another domain, that's not a problem. However, there are restrictions in terms of what scripting you can do between the domains (i.e. javascript in domain A can't talk to javascript in domain B). Facebook gets around it by using a second hidden iframe and fragment identifiers for IE =8 and other browsers).
