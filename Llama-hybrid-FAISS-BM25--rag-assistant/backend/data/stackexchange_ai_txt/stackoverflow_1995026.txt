Wikipedia has an API . With this API you can get any random article in a given namespace: http://en.wikipedia.org/w/api.php?action=query&list=random&rnnamespace=0&rnlimit=5 and for each article you call also get the wiki text: http://en.wikipedia.org/w/api.php?action=query&prop=revisions&titles=Main%20Page&rvprop=content
