Maybe you want to sign your jar files instead? What you want should be possible via Intrumentation, by adding custom Transformer. See http://java.sun.com/j2se/1.5.0/docs/api/java/lang/instrument/package-summary.html Custom classloader also does the job, as it gets bytecode when class is defined.
