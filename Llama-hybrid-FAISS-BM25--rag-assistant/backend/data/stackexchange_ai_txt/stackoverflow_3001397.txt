I'd worry less about people obtaining the files from some hidden-away cache, and more about the fact you've got an http URL which allows the file to be directly downloaded onto any device. You can dance around this and check for user-agent strings, session cookies etc, but there's not much you can do prevent someone from figuring it out, and writing an easy way around it.
