For a devexpress XtraGrid you can use DevExpress.Utils.FormatInfo: DevExpress.Utils.FormatInfo fi = new DevExpress.Utils.FormatInfo(); fi.FormatType = DevExpress.Utils.FormatType.Numeric; fi.FormatString = "n2"; myColumn.DisplayFormat.Assign(fi);
