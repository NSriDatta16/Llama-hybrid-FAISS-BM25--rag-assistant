May not work in all circumstances, but: (defun my-fill-sentence () "Fill sentence separated by punctuation or blank lines." (interactive) (let (start end) (save-excursion (re-search-backward "\\(^\\s-*$\\|[.?!]\\)" nil t) (skip-syntax-forward "^w") (setq start (point-at-bol))) (save-excursion (re-search-forward "\\(^\\s-*$\\|[.?!]\\)" nil t) (setq end (point-at-eol))) (save-restriction (narrow-to-region start end) (fill-paragraph nil)))) To make it work with auto-fill-mode , add (setq normal-auto-fill-function 'my-fill-sentence) to your LaTeX mode hook (I think).
