The value of UUID_SHORT() is guaranteed to be unique if the following conditions hold: The server_id of the current host is unique among your set of master and slave servers server_id is between 0 and 255 You don't set back your system time for your server between mysqld restarts You do not invoke UUID_SHORT() on average more than 16 million times per second between mysqld restarts
