The reason being is the RSS feed is not secure. Try to get the feed to use SSL, instead of http://www.cnn.com/rss , try https://www.cnn.com/rss . If there is not an SSL port available, request one. If the users are on a Domain, you can use a group policy that will auto allow that RSS feeds url/site.
