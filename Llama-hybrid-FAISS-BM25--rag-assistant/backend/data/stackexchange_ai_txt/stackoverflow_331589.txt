Whenever you feel the need to ask what limits the system has, you have a design problem. If you were asking "How many characters can I fit into a varchar?" then you shouldn't be using varchars at all. If you seriously want to know if 1000 columns is okay, then you desperately need to reorganize the data. (normalization)
