Yes it is certainly possible to automate this. However your service may be broken when the bank decides to update their website in future. To find out what data is required try monitoring your login with a firefox extension like TamperData or HttpFox . Then replicate all the required data and headers in your script. Make sure to set the user-agent and referrer headers because they are often required.
