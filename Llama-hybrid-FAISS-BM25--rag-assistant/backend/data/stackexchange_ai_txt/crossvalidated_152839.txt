You can use the following statistical test for your issue : Sanso, A., Arago, V., and Carrion, J. (2004), “Testing for Changes in the Unconditional Variance of Financial Time Series,” Revista de Economia Financiera,4, 32–53. As a result, you will know if the UNconditional variance of your time series can be considered constant or not (from a given confidence level). Their test statistic can be easily implemented. Enjoy!
