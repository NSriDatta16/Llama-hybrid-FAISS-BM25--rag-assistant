ldapsearch and ldapadd are not necessarily the best tools to clone your LDAP DB. slapcat and slapadd are much better options. Export your DB with slapcat: slapcat > ldif Import the DB with slapadd (make sure the LDAP server is stopped): slapadd -l ldif
