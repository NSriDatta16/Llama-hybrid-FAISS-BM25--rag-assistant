Almost any embedding method can learn the relationship between those discrete actions. For example, Python's gensim package has a word2vec implementation that supports training on your own model . You'll have write your tokenizer that creates pairs of words (e.g., "add wall" or "edit wall") as tokens. Conventional tokenizer will split on whitespace.
