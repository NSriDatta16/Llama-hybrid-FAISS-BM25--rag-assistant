That "certain size" is the \textwidth . In order to get defined centering behaviour, your elements should fit into that. You could adjust that width, or use something like \resizebox to fit your elements into the text area. That is usually seen when you try to write paragraphs without writing paragraphs. You should provide an example so we can see what you are trying to do. Remember, paragraphs of text are to be separated simply by empty lines in the source.
