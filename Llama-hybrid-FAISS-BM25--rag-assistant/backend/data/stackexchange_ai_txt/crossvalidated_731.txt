If you're looking at system faults, you might be interested in the following paper employing machine learning techniques for fault diagnosis at eBay. It may give you a sense of what kind of data to collect or how one team approached a specific problem in a similar domain. Fault Diagnosis Using Decision Trees If you're just getting started, something like RapidMiner or Orange might be a good software system to start playing with your data pretty quickly. Both of them can access the data in a variety of formats (file csv, database, among others).
