This is a classification problem with functional data. The fact that there is a time index is not really important. Think about each time series as a continuous function that is observed at discrete points. There was a review of the literature about 10 years ago by Baillo, Cuevas and Fraiman . I'm sure a lot has been done since then. You can track the citations for that paper at https://scholar.google.com.au/scholar?cites=947432642489427396
