I'm sure someone will probably have a more elegant solution, but I think this will do what you want to done. Where: $subject = " File : i_want_this "; Option 1: $pattern1 = '/( )(.*)( )/i'; preg_match($pattern1, $subject, $matches1); print($matches1[4]); Option 2: $pattern2 = '( )(.*)( )'; ereg($pattern2, $subject, $matches2); print($matches2[4]);
