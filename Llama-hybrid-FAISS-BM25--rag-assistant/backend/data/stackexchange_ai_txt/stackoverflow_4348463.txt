Due to the use of inequalities , and not equalities , a composite index isn't going to do any much better (if not worse) than two individual indexes. I'd advocate for leaning towards two individual indexes on both began_at and finished_at . References for Loose index scan: http://www.mysqlperformanceblog.com/2006/05/09/descending-indexing-and-loose-index-scan/ http://dev.mysql.com/doc/refman/5.5/en/loose-index-scan.html
