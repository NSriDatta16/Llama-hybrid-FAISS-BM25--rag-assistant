I believe the coordinates being passed from DragAndDrop are screen space coordinates. I think you need to cast the points to the client coordinates space. Point dscreen = new Point(e.X, e.Y); Point dclient = dataGridView1.PointToClient(dscreen ); DataGridView.HitTestInfo hitTest = dataGridView1.HitTest(dclient.X,dclient.Y); then hitTest.Row will have the row index
