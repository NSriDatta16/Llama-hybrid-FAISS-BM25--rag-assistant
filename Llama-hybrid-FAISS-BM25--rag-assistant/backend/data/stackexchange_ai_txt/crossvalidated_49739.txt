What you describe is essentially just logistic regression with a scaled output using squared loss rather than the usual log loss. Notice that $\tanh(x) = 2\sigma(x) - 1$ where $$ \sigma(x) = \frac{1}{1 + e^{-x}} $$ is the logistic function. The decision boundary will still be linear.
