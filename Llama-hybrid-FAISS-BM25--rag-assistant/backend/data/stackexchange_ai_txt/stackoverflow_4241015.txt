There's no reason not to and will really help people reading your code. Some things to watch out for: Don't over -nest folders, this can be confusing for readers of your code. Be consistent in the organization of your code, e.g. don't put any view code in the controllers sub-directory, or vice-versa. Keep the layout clean.
