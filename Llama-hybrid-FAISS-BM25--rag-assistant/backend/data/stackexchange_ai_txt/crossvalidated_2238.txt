Not all methods lead to analytic expressions (preferably based on proper asymptotic results) that provides this. But the bootstrap allows you to approximate this via simulation. In essence, you generate (lots of) surrogate 'fake' data sets, employ your estimator on each of these and then use the population of your estimates to make inferences. However, doing bootstrapping in a time series context has its own challenges...
