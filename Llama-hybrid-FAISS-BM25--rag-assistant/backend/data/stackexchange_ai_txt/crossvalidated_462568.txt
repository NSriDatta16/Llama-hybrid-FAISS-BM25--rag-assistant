PCA tells us what features are more important, how? In short: We find the first principal component one (PC1). Now PC1 is a linear combination of the variables (features). The variable with the highest weight (coefficient)(loading scores) in the linear equation is the most important feature. Don't miss this wonderful video from StatQuest.
