This can be an indication that your unit tests are not fine-grained enough. Like, you've written integration test, made it pass, and now you're putting unit tests in place. Or maybe after refactoring you are trying to put tests on something you shouldn't, such as private methods. Refactoring shouldn't change your code coverage anyway.
