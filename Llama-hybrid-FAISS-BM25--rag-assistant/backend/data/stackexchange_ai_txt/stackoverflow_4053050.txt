To prevent bias I would recommend first stopping all unnecessary processes from running in the background. I'm not sure about windows, but under linux you can clear the HDD cache via drop_caches Information on how to use it here Additionally you may want to take an average for several runs of the application, that way any HDD or OS interference won't skew the results.
