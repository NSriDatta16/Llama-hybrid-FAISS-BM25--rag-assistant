Data Analysis: Examines, cleans, transforms and models data to derive insights. Involves techniques such as descriptive statistics, data visualization, and exploratory data analysis. A broader term that encompasses the entire process of examining and interpreting data. Data Analytics: Uses advanced techniques and tools to analyze large and complex datasets. Involves methods like machine learning, statistical modeling, and data mining. Specialized field that focuses on uncovering hidden patterns, correlations, and insights from data.
