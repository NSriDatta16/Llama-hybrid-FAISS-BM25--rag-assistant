Be kind to other programmers, don't embed the escape sequences directly in your strings. Maybe use a library like Rainbow or Term-ANSIColor ? Eg Term-ANSIColor: require 'term/ansicolor' include Term::ANSIColor Rails.logger.add 1, "#{red(ATCHUNG!)}: pay attention to me!"
