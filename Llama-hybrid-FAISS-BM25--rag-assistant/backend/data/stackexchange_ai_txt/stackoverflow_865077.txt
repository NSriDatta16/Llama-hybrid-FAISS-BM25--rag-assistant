One way to do it: Adapt the Document to a Source with DOMSource . Create a StreamResult to adapt a ByteArrayOutputStream . Use a Transformer from TransformerFactory.newTransformer to copy across the data. Retrieve your byte[] and stream with ByteArrayInputStream . Putting the code together is left as an exercise.
