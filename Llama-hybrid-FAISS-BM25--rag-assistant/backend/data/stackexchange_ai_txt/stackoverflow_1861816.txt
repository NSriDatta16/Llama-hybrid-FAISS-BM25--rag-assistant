1. You can use "\b" to check for word boundaries. A word boundary is defined as the boundary between a word character and a non-word character. word-characters are letters, numbers, and underscore. 2. You can do it all at one go, by using "|": $stopwords = preg_match('/\\b(preheat|minutes|stir|heat|..other words..|pan)\\b/i', $paragraph)
