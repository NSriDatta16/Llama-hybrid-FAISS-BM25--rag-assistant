If we're talking about the points inside the scrollview then you can hook with the delegate method: - (void)scrollViewWillBeginDragging:(UIScrollView *)scrollView and inside the method, read the property: @property(nonatomic) CGPoint contentOffset from the scrollView to get the coordination.
