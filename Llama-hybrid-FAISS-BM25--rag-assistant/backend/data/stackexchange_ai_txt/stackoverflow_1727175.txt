Take a look at working sets . In Eclipse, Working Sets provide a convenient way to collect projects together into a sort of holding-folder. This folder exists only in some "meta" sense, and single projects can belong to multiple working sets. The link above shows a video of these in action. I had been looking for convenient way to collect projects together to ease my searching for specific projects in large workspaces and have found this small bit of organization helps quite a bit.
