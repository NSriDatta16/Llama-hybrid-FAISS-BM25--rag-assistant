You can also use jspf (JSP fragment files) to store chunks of JSP. jsp:include is one way to include a JSP. Another way is to use c:include from the JSTL libraries. The differences are covered here: * http://sharat.wordpress.com/2006/09/08/40-what-is-the-difference-between-jspinclude-and-cimport/ Hope that helps. I'm sure there are framework specific approaches, but these are the two JSP approaches I know of.
