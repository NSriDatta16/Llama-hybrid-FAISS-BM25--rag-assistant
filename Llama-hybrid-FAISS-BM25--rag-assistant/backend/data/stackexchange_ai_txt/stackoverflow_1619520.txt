Are you trying to parse some text on cnn's web site? You can get the page with import urllib f = urllib.urlopen('http://www.cnn.com') page = f.read() f.close() You can then use BeautifulSoup to find whatever it is you are looking for on page. Why win32com, dispatch, etc.?
