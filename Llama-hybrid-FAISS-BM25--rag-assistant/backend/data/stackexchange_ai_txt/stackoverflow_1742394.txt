Less elegant and shorter: $ perl -plaF, -e '$r=0;$r+=$F[$_],$c[$_]+=$F[$_]for 1..$#F;$_.=",$r";END{$c[0]="Total";print join",",@c}' Quick and dirty, but should do the trick in basic cases. For anything more complex, use Text::CSV and an actual script.
