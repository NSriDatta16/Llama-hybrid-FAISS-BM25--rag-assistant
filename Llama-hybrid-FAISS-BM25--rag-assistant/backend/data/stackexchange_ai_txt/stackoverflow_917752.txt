It turns out the the mouseDrag event is active regardless of which button on the mouse is pressed. To filter the middle mouse button, you need to call the MiddleIsDown() method from the MouseEvent . def on_mouseDrag( self, event ): do_stuff() if event.MiddleIsDown(): do_other_stuff()
