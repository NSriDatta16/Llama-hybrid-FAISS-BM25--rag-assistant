In your emails, use plaintext with links to your HTML pages. HTML in email is generally widely reviled as a bad idea, because it's a gigantic security hole. Never send passwords in email, even as a reminder. Send a link to the user's registered email address that allows them to reset their password. The absolute fastest way to make me quit using your service is to send me my password via email in plaintext.
