Take a look at result transformers. You should be able to add this to your query and have it get rid of the duplicate entries. SetResultTransformer(CriteriaSpecification.DistinctRootEntity) You should also be able to leave the FetchMany methods out if you want to use lazy-loading. It could cause many queries to be run, however.
