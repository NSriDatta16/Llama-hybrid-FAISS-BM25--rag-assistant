Semantically h1 - h6 are for headings. If the different types of font on your site are all headings then fine, I suspect they are not. Similarly p is used for paragraphs. If you have different paragraphs that have different styles of text in..then yes This is paragraph 1 This is paragraph 2 but remember these tags have meaning. For more info, see this article
