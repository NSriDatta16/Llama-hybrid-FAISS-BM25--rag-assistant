For a set of time series $t_1,...,t_n$, the Covariance Matrix is as follows: $$ \begin{bmatrix} Var(t_1) & Cov(t_1,t_2) & \cdots & Cov(t_1,t_n) \\ Cov(t_2,t_1) & Var(t_2) & \cdots & Cov(t_2,t_n) \\ \cdots \\ Cov(t_n,t_1) & Cov(t_n,t_2) & \cdots & Var(t_n) \\ \end{bmatrix} $$ It looks like your inputing only one time series, so the output is just a 1x1 matrix: $$ \begin{bmatrix} Var(t_1) \end{bmatrix} $$ Of course, a 1x1 matrix has only one eigenvalue & one eigenvector
