It looks correct to me, and this is a regular Bland-Altman plot. A Bland-Altman plot plots the average of the two measures on the X axis, and the difference of the two measures on the Y axis. If there is a correlation in the plot, it suggests that the measurement error is not independent of the underlying true value.
