It is commonly know as FOUC - Flash of unstyled content. The options in the answer above make eminent sense but it may still be impossible to eliminate in all circumstances. Initially it was and IE problem but has recently happened a lot more in Safari and is probably to with the underlying method used in the browser application itself and therefore may not be remediable. Sometime addins such as jQuery and typekit can exacerbate the problem hence paying close attention to and testing different scenarios for loading them.
