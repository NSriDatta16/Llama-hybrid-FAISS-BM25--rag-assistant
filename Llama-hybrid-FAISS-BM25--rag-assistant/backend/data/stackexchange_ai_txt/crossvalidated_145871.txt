Normal distribution. Find the average

In a large group of patients, cholesterol level approximates a normal distribution N(μ, σ). Observed that 20% of the members of this group have a cholesterol level of less than 117.7mg/100ml and 8% had levels above 239mg/100ml. What is the average, μ, of the normal distribution?
