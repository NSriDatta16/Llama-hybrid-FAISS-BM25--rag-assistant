For the beginning try this: Read a paragraph about 1 design pattern (as they listed in the book). Try to understand the problem it solves. Imagine some task with this problem. Code it. Draw a class diagram of your program on a piece of paper (without automated tools). One pattern per day will be a very good velocity.
