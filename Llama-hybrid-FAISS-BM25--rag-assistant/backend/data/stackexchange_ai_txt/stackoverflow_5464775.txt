You can actually do this with an inner container whose width/height is calculated to only allow the image to travel a certain distance. Of course you also have to position the inner container appropriately. Here is my go at it: Markup: Script: var img = $("img").draggable({ containment: '#inner'}), h = img.height(), w = img.width(), outer = $('#outer'), oH = outer.height(), oW = outer.width(), iH = h + (h - oH), iW = w + (w - oW), iT = '-' + ((iH - oH)/2) + 'px', iL = '-' + ((iW - oW)/2) + 'px'; $('#inner').css({ width: iW, height: iH, top: iT, left: iL });
