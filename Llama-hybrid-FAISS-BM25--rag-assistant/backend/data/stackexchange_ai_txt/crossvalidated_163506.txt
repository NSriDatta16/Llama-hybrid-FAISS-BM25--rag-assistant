Linear means you are estimating a relationship by a straight line, or more correctly by linear multiples of the explanatory variables and a constant. Regression is related to regression to the mean : the expected height of children of tall parents (adjusted for gender) estimated from linear regression is above average but lower than the heights of the parents; this has echoes of eugenics.
