You are both right. Good coding conventions are as valuable as elegant code. Your task would be to introduce your idea to the coding convention and change that first, before you add new styles to a potentially big project. What you shouldn't have is 50 ways of declaring singletons, which are considered an anti-pattern by some anyway.
