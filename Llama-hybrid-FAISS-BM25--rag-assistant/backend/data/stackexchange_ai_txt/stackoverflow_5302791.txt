In general the use of !! is often discouraged, as it's not clear to those who haven't seen it before what the actual purpose of it is. That said, it is less than a third the characters of Boolean() . Further, I'm not sure how often you actually need to cast to a boolean in Javascript, as it is often implicitly cast since Javascript is weakly typed.
