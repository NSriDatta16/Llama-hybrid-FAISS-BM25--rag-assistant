I would check to see if you turn the warnings off in some of your units. Depending on the last change in the units, the compiling order can change. In some cases the warnings can remain disabled for a unit that is compiled after while, when freshly checked out of version control, it was compiled before, with the warnings. Pay extra attention to any Include file you may use.
