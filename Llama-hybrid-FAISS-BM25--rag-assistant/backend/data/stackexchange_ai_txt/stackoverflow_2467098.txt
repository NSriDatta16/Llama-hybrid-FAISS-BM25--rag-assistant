There are many ways to do this (as with everything in R). I always recommend using a time series when working with time series data. The zoo package is probably the most popular time series package (although you can also look at others such as xts, timeSeries, its, fts): library(zoo) z You may also find the chart.RollingCorrelation function in the PerformanceAnalytics package useful.
