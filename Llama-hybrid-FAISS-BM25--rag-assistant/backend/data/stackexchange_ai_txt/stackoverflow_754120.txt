If the latest is more important than every picture, UDP should be your first choice. But if you're dealing with frames lager than 64K your have to-do some form of re-framing your self. Don't be concerned with fragmented frames, as you'll have to deal with it or the lower layer will. And you only want completed pictures. What you will want is some form of encapsulation with timestamps/sequences.
