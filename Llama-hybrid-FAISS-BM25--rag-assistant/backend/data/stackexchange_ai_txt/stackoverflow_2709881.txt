First of all, try to get the default charset of your device with: Charset.defaultCharset(); Then try to get the charset of your XML looking in pseudo-attribute of the XML declaration or in Content-Type header of the HTTP call. For example: or Content-Type: text/html; charset=utf-8 If the default charset of your device is different from the charset of XML, you have to pay attention when you handle new strings with: new String( bytes); because if you forget to specify the correct encoding, Dalvik will use the default encoding of the device with plausible display errors. Remember to use: new String( bytes, encoding);
