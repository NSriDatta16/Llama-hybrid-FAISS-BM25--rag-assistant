TimeZoneInfo are pulled from the registry... HKEY_LOCAL_MACHINE\Software\Microsoft\Windows NT\CurrentVersion\Time Zones So its OS Language Dependent. Changing the culture will have no effect in the Time Zone DisplayName. Of course, you can leverage resource files to achieve the desired affect.
