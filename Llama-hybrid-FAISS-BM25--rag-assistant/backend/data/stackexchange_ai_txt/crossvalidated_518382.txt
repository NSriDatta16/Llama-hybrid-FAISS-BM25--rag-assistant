There is a Bayesian equivalent of the t-test. It returns the actual probability that the means are different. You can also use different sample sizes for each distribution. You can find the paper here: https://jkkweb.sitehost.iu.edu/BEST/ An online demo can be found here: http://www.sumsar.net/best_online/
