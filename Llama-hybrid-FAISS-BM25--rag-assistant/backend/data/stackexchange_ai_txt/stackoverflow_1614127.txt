It sounds like you want: dst.rgb = (src.a * src.rgb) * ((1 - src.a) * dst.rgb) You would use D3DRS_BLENDOP to do that, but unfortunately there isn't a D3DBLENDOP_MULTIPLY. I don't think this operation is possible without a fragment shader.
