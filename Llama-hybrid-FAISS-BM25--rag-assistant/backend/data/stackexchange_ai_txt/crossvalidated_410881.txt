Yes, the predictions from the two approaches will be different, in the same line as you mentioned for the odds ratios. Namely, the GEE will give you population-averaged predictions and the mixed effects logistic regression subject-specific predictions. From the latter you can obtain the marginal predictions by marginalizing over the random effects.
