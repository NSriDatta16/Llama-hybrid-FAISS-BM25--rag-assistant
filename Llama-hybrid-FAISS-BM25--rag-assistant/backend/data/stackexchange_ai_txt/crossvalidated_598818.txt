As said in other answers, ML methods required the i.i.d assumption and therefore are unsuitable to be used naively for time series. Not-naive ways have been suggested by DankMasterDan. There is a further option, which is to use supervised predictors inside time-series forecasting. A good overview is found in the Sktime documentation: Forecasting, supervised regression, and pitfalls in confusing the two .
