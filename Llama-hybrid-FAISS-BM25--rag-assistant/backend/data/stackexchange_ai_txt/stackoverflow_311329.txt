When the amount of time the Team is able to put in at every iteration varies too much, the velocity cannot really help to plan the Sprints since it will vary too, especially at the beginning. However, the average velocity may start to stabilize after several Sprints. Nevertheless the burndown charts will be useful still as they show an accurate status of the current iteration. You'll also take advantage of the estimations "calibration" Agile processes bring.
