Find the generated manifest for your specific version of the CRT. Then add a pragma to the main file of your application: #pragma comment(linker,"/manifestdependency:\"type='win32' " \ "name='" __LIBRARIES_ASSEMBLY_NAME_PREFIX ".CRT' " \ "version='" _CRT_ASSEMBLY_VERSION "' " \ "processorArchitecture='x86' " \ "publicKeyToken='" _VC_ASSEMBLY_PUBLICKEYTOKEN "'\"")
