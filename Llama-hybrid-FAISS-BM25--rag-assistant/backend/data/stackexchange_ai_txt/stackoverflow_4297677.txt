GoDaddy SSL certificates are cheap and are great for any small to medium size business endeavor. Get the organization validation options if you are doing millions of dollars a year in transactions. IMO it's not that important or critical as a buyer Standard SSL will allow you to do secure.yourdomain.com OR www.yourdomain.com OR some single domain name. Get a wildcard if you plan on doing more like client1.yourdomain.com or client2.yourdomain.com Warranty / Insurance - no need As for Azure here is a step by step process for doing your Azure SSL certificate The low cost GoDaddy certificates work fine for 99.9% scenarios and they are like $20 to $30 dollars versus $300+
