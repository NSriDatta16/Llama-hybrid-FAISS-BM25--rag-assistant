You could look at your webserver's log files and see what type of 'User Agent' those connections are coming from. Browsers such as IE/Firefox have a User Agent signature of something along the lines of 'Mozilla/4.0 (compatible; MSIE 7.0; Windows NT 6.0)' The bots will likely have a (few) specific User Agent signature(s), you could add these to a black list in your server's configuration files so that your server will just ignore requests from them. Also you should take a minute to read through http://www.kloth.net/internet/bottrap.php
