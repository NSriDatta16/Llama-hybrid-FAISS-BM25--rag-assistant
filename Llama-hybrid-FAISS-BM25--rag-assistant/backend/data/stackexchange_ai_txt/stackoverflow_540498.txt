In short: = JSF = PROS: component architecture; many libraries & tools; somewhat good IDE support CONS: heavy weight, both in CPU/memory and learning curve; when something doesn't work as expected, it's difficult to debug = WICKET = PROS: lightweight; sensible templating system; good tutorials; CONS: reference documentation is not so well organized and deep as are the tutorials; development team had some serious difficulties, especially when becoming and incubated project. This lead to confusion on important aspects of the framework, at that time I had to switch to another framework because of this...
