That should be sufficent. You'll need to register that dll on your machine, add it to the dev studio Toolbox (Right click on the toolbox/Choose Items) and go to the COM tab, if you can't find it in there you can browse to the specific location and select the Type Library to use. After that you can just drag the new control to your form. A Reference to the library will be added to your project automatically. see " Adding ActiveX Controls to Windows Forms "
