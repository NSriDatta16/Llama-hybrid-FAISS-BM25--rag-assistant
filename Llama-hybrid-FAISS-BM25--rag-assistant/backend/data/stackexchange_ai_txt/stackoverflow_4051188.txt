While you really didn't provide enough information, it sounds like you're describing a typical Role Class Model . I suspect that your Client doesn't really have a Person or an Organization, but rather that Person or an Organization is a Client, or IOW, either a Person or an Organization can play a role of a Client in a certain relationship. There are various ways to model this, and no single correct solution. You might find this article helpful
