If built with TBB then TBB.dll is needed - it doesn't mean the specific function is using TBB. To see which functions are TBB enabled you need to look at the code, or follow the opencV minutes - eg http://pr.willowgarage.com/wiki/OpenCVMeetingNotes/Minutes%202010-03-30
