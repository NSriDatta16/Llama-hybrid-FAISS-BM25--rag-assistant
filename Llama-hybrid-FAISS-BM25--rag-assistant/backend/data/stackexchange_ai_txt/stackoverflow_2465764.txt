For you purpose the best choice is Format-Custom . get-date | Format-Custom -Depth 1 -Property * get-childitem . | select-object -first 1 | Format-Custom -Depth 1 -Property * It's maybe too verbose, but useful ;) Or you can really use Get-Member $obj = get-date $obj | gm -MemberType *property | % { write-host ('{0,-12} = {1}' -f $_.Name, $obj.($_.Name)) }
