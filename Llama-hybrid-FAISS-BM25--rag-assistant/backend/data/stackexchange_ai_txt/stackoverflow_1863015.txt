Smells a bit, but I could see cases where this would make sense, especially if you have programmers switching from language to language all the time. For instance, MATLAB is one-indexed, so I could imagine someone getting fed up with making off-by-one mistakes whenever they switch languages, and defining DEFAULT_INDEX in both C++ and MATLAB programs to abstract the difference. Not necessarily elegant, but if that's what it takes...
