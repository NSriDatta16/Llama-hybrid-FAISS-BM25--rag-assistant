Push notifications are sent by the PNS (Push Notification Service) to devices in response to a request by your web server on a channel that your app created on the device and sent to the web service.... That's all a bit of a mouthful, but is the essence of it. For an overview, checkout this online documentation Push Notifications for Windows Phone and for the different types..
