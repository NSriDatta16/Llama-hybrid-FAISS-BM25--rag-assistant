To fix your Firefox issue, you could try using jQuery to scroll to your anchor after unhiding it. It's a little bit hackish, I suppose, but it should work. The 'Interface' plugin for jQuery includes a scrollTo method. Link Example usage. jQuery Smooth Scroll to Anchor Links To solve the elegance problem, here's one idea: var targetDiv = $('#'+($.url.attr('anchor'))).next().attr('id'); $("div[id!='"+targetDiv+"'][id^='answer']").hide(); $("div[id='"+targetDiv+"']").show(); $.scrollTo('#'+($.url.attr('anchor')));
