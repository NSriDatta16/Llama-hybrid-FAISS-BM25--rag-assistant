While this is most likely just due to rounding, there are different methods of calculating confidence intervals for parameter estimates that will give similar, but still different, values and may not both correspond to the p-value significance. For example in logistic regression, one could calculate Wald confidence intervals or profile likelihood-based confidence intervals estimates.
