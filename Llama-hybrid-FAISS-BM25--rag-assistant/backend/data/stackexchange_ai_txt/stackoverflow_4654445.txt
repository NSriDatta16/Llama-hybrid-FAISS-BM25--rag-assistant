You can do gsub, or scan. Either way a regex is most elegant. I recommend a scan, so you will have the option to go through each email address and do whatever you want to it (including deciding whether or not to quote the name). str = Bob Smith , Jones, Rich A. email_addrs = [] str.scan(/\s*(.*?)\s* (?:,|\Z)/) do |match| # yields ["Bob Smith", "bobsmith@gmail.com"] # then ["Jones, Rich A.", "richjones@gmail.com"] email_addrs " end email_addrs.join(", ") #=> "Bob Smith" , "Jones, Rich A." Good luck!
