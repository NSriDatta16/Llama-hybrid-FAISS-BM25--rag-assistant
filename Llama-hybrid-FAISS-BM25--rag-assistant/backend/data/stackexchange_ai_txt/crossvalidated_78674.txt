It's best to use models that can deal with structured output instead of doing a hierarchical classification. The fact there is a structure in the possible outcomes is prior information which you can use to obtain a better overall model. One technique that can handle this kind of information is the structured SVM , an implementation of which is available in SVM$^{struct}$ .
