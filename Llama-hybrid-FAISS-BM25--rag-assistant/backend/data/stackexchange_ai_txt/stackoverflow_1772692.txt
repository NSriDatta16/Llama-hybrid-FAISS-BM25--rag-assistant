1000 users X 10 minutes = 10,000 user minutes 10,000 user minutes / 1440 minutes in a day = 6.944 average # of concurrent users If you are looking for better estimates of concurrent users, I would suggest putting google analytics on your site. It would give you an accurate reading of highs, lows, and averages for your site.
