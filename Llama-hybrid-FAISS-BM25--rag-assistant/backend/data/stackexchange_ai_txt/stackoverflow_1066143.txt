you could replace the quotes with an empty string... SELECT AllRemoved = REPLACE(CAST(MyColumn AS varchar(max)), '"', ''), LeadingAndTrailingRemoved = CASE WHEN MyTest like '"%"' THEN SUBSTRING(Mytest, 2, LEN(CAST(MyTest AS nvarchar(max)))-2) ELSE MyTest END FROM MyTable
