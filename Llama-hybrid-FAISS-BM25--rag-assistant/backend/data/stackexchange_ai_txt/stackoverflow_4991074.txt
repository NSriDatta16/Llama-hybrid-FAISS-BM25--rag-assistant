Make sure that they belong to the same paragraph: .... bla blabla, the equation is: % \begin{equation} ... \end{equation} The percent sign is the important thing here because it prevents a paragraph end. Since \predisplaypenalty is 10000 by default, the last line of the text will always be on the same page as the equation.
