You know, libpcap exists for a reason: It does something that's necessary. You need to set up a system-level hook for TCP/IP events, and the way global hooks work means you need to do this from a DLL. Having hooked those events, you have to figure out the contents of the packets you get. Are you sure you want to re-invent this wheel? I found some introductory info on hooking Windows events here .
