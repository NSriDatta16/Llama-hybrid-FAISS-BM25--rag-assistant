If you just want everything after the # , use split: String s = "Last one: http://abc.imp/Basic2#URPlus1_S2_3," ; System.out.println(s.split("#")[1]); Alternatively , if you want to parse the URI and get the fragment component you can do: URI u = new URI("http://abc.imp/Basic2#URPlus1_S2_3,"); System.out.println(u.getFragment());
