They might have been PNG files that you dragged in, but the xib contains TIFF data: You should remove the images from your nib and include the PNG files in your application bundle and refer to them by name from your code. (That's assuming, of course, that the small snippet of xib you showed is actually relevant to the problem, and not something completely innocent.)
