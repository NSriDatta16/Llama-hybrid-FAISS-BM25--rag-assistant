Edit for Excel 2007: Home->Editing->Find & Select->Replace. Also see the IF function, you could create a copy on another sheet using something like =IF(A1= 7,100, A1) and drag the right-hand corner to create a copy of all the cells (it will automatically change the formula to use B1, etc)
