In this blog post , Regex guru Jan Goyvaerts shows a few ways how to go about matching URLs in plain text. He also shows many common pitfalls. For your case, I'd recommend \b(?:(?:https?|ftp|file)://|www\.|ftp\.)[-A-Z0-9+&@#/%=~_|$?!:,.]*[A-Z0-9+&@#/%=~_|$] (case-insensitive mode turned on)
