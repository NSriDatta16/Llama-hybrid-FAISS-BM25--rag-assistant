The goal of Explainable Artificial Intelligence (AI) is to better understand the decisions that are made by complex AI systems. In contrast to common machine learning approaches, which are often a "black box", explainable AI systems should be modeled in a way that allows humans to retrace how the system arrived at a certain decision. The overarching goal of Explainable AI is to build trust in complex AI systems and increase the overall accountability and transparency of these systems.
