People avoid cursor because they generally are more difficult to write than a simple while loops, however, a while loop can be expensive because your constantly selecting data from a table, temporary or otherwise. With a cursor, which is readonly fast forward, the data is kept in memory and has been specifically designed for looping. This article highlights that an average cursor runs 50 times faster than a while loop.
