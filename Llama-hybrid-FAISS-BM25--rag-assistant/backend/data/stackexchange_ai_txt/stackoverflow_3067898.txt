Better performance NoSQL databases sometimes have better performance, although this depends on the situation and is disputed. Adaptability You can add and remove "columns" without downtime. In most SQL servers, this takes a long time and takes up a load of load. Application design It is desirable to separate the data storage from the logic. If you join and select things in SQL queries, you are mixing business logic with storage.
