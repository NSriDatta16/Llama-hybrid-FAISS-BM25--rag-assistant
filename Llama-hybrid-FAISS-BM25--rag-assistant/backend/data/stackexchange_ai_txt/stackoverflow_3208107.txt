Where pairs is your list of pairs: averages = [float(sum(values)) / len(values) for key, values in pairs] will give you a list of average values. If your numbers are strings, as in your example, replace sum(values) above with sum([int(i) for i in values]) . EDIT: And if you rather want a dictionary then a list of averages: averages = dict([(key, float(sum(values)) / len(values)) for key, values in pairs])
