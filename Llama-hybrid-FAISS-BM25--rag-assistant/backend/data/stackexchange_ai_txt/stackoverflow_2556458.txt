Yes, you can use recursion inside lexer rules. Take the following example: ${a ${b} ${c ${ddd} c} a} which will be parsed correctly by the following grammar: parse : DollarVar ; DollarVar : '${' (DollarVar | EscapeSequence | ~Special)+ '}' ; fragment Special : '\\' | '$' | '{' | '}' ; fragment EscapeSequence : '\\' Special ; as the interpreter inside ANTLRWorks shows: alt text http://img185.imageshack.us/img185/5471/recq.png
