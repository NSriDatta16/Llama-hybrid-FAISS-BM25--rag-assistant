Add/Remove Programs also has to look into this registry key to find installations for the current user: HKEY_CURRENT_USER\Software\Microsoft\Windows\CurrentVersion\Uninstall Applications like Google Chrome, Dropbox, or shortcuts installed through JavaWS (web start) JNLPs can be found only here.
