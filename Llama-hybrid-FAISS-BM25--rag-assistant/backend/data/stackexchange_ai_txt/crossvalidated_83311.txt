Even if changing the metric might be a solution sometimes, the problem - as you noticed - is cause by the method (centroid method in your case). So the appropriate solution is to choose a different method, if this issue occurs. Inversions do not appear, if you use a monotonic method for clustering Essential monotonic hierarchical clustering methods are Single Linkage Complete Linkage Average Linkage Weighted Average Linkage WARD's Linkage One can choose a method out of these depending on which suits the given problem most
