Have you looked at BerkeleyDB ? Fast, embedded, in-process data management. Key/value store, non-relational. Persistent storage. Free, open-source. However, it fails to meet one of your criteria: BDB supports distributed replication, but the data is not partitioned. Each node stores the full data set.
