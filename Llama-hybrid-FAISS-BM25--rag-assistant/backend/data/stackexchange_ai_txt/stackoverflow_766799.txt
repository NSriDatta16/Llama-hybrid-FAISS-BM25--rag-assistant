VB Watch is another tool you might want to consider. These things are most versatile when you can isolate suspect areas of your code. Many tools of this type allow you to limit the coverage of the code instrumentation to modules or individual procedures, or limit monitoring to the procedure rather than statement level. This can help to reduce some of the pain associated with line by line instrumentation of the whole program.
