The specific functionality you are looking for is an "Active Directory Application Partition" . There's a great blog with a book's worth of information and code samples as well. I would discuss this with your Systems Architecture/Operations group before proceeding. You are going to have to modify your AD schema, and I know that process can take a long time at many organizations.
