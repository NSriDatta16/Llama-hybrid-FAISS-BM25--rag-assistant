Read the Dragon Book: http://www.amazon.com/Compilers-Principles-Techniques-Alfred-Aho/dp/0201100886 It covers lexical and syntactical analysis in depth (among other topics). You can use this to help you understand the "language" you are trying to parse to determine the best way to go about it.
