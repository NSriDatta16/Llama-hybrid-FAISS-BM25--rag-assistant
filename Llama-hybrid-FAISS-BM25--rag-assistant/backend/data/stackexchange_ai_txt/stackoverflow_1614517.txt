You might want to take a look at two NSTextStorage delegate methods: - (void)textStorageWillProcessEditing:(NSNotification *)notification; /* Delegate can change the characters or attributes */ - (void)textStorageDidProcessEditing:(NSNotification *)notification; /* Delegate can change the attributes */ Inside textStorageWill/DidProcessEditing , you can call -[NSTextStorage editedMask] and -[NSTextStorage editedRange] to find out what changed and then take action accordingly.
