what you need is just the GestureListener which lies in the Microsoft.Phone.Controls namespace, which can handle a couple gestures like Flick Pinch Drag Swipe etc. You can use it like so var gestureListener = GestureService.GetGestureListener(myCanvas); //registering the Events gestureListener.PinchStarted += new EventHandler (PinchStartedHandler); gestureListener.PinchDelta += new EventHandler (PinchDeltaHandler); gestureListener.PinchCompleted += new EventHandler (PinchCompletedHandler); In the approriate Hanler-Methods you do your rotate- and scale- transformations.
