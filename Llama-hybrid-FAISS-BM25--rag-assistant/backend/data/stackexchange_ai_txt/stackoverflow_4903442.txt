I believe you are not using a SurfaceView. Drawing like that to a canvas was meant for controls and not fast rendering(read >10fps) If you want performance you need either to use a SurfaceView where you'll average a 25-30 fps or opengl Please read : http://developer.android.com/guide/topics/graphics/index.html
