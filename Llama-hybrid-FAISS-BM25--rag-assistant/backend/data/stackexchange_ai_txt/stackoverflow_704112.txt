You may consider reading Wikipedia's article on sunrise equations . The lead paragraph gives the equation: cos(ω o ) = -tan(φ) * tan(δ) where: ω o is the hour angle in degrees at either sunrise (when negative value is taken) or sunset (when positive value is taken) in degree (°) φ is the latitude of the observer on the Earth in degrees δ is the sun declination in degrees
