Only in C99 - it's a new feature called "variable length arrays". Normally, no. I would strongly recommend against using this feature. If you have to do it, either use alloca, or allocate them properly, i.e. Point *V = new Point V[rows]; . BTW: Many people discourage Alloca as well. See here .
