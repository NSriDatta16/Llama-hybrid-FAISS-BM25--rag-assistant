I noticed you mentioned it's a form you're designing. I'm surprised no one has mentioned this, but it's important to make use of the tag and / tags if you're wanting to create a properly semantic HTML form. So, your StyleA might make the most sense as a within a , not an tag. Your StyleB, if it describes a control, definitely should be a . Take a look at some of the example HTML on the W3C pages I've linked above for information on how to properly use these tags. The note at the end would be in a tag, perhaps with some class. Longer informational notes on the form would be in paragraph tags as well.
