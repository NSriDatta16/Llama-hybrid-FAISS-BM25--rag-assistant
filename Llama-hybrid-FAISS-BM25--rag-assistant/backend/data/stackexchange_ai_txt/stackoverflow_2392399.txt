try this (untested) select entity.* , count(*) as relevance from entities , tags , entity_tags where entities.id=entity_tags.entity_id and tags.id=entity_tags.tag_id and tags.name in ('fruit', 'organic', 'item') group by entity.id having count(entities.id) > 1 order by relevance also look here for help on SQLite and search this page for "having"
