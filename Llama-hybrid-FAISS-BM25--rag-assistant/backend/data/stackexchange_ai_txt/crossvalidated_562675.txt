If you have more than 1 other experiment (e.g. you have damage rates for more than 1 other product), you might want to fit a partially pooled hierarchical logistic regression, and estimate the variance of the defect rate across products. This can give insight into where you might expect to see the damage rate of a new, unknown product lie. Here's a link to a discussion about the use of hierarchical modeling for extrapolation .
