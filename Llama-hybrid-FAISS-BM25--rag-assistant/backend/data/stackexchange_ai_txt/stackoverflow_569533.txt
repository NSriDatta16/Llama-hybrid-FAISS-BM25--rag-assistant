This is a bad way to keep membership. But if you still need to live with it, you may try REGEXP matching to test for membership: SELECT ocp.*, oc.*, GROUP_CONCAT( u.username SEPARATOR ', ') AS `memjoined` FROM gangs_ocs_process ocp LEFT JOIN users u ON (ocp.membersin RLIKE CONCAT('(^|,)[[:blank:]]?', userid, '[[:blank:]]?($|,)')) JOIN gangs_ocs oc ON (ocp.ocid = 1 AND ocp.gangid = 1 AND oc.oc_name = ocp.crimename) GROUP BY ocp.ocid LIMIT 0 , 30
