Don't assume you know what needs to be optimized. Let sampling tell you. I've done a couple spelling-correctors, and the way I did it ( outlined here ) was to organize the dictionary as a trie in memory, and search upon that. If the number of words is large, the size of the trie can be much reduced by sharing common suffixes.
