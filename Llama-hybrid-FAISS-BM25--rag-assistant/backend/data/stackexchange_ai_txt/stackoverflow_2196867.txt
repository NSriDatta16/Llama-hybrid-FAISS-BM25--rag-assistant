I found a way to do this. You can load whatever pages you want via the jquery load method. $('#result').load('ajax/test.html #container'); They will then be placed in the div with the #result id. You can also specify a page fragment with #container so you only load the part you want. This allows all your controllers to stay in tact etc with no extra js.
