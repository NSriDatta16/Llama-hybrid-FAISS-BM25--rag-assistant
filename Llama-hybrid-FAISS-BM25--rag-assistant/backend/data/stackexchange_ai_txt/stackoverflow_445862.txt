Some of the best PHP carts: Magento - Full featured. Excellent code quality. Hard to learn. Requires lots of server resources. PrestaShop - PrestaShop is currently used by 250,000 shops worldwide and is available in 60 different languages. OpenCart - OpenCart comes with an inbuilt Affiliate system, where affiliates can promote specific products and get paid for this. InterSpire - Not Free. FoxyCart - Not Free. Hosted checkout that uses your templates. Works well with a CMS like Modx or Expression Engine. LemonStand - Not Free.
