If you control the second (off-site) domain, you can get around the same-origin policy issues by loading a script on the secondary domain. That script should kick off the various AJAXish content updates you want. on example1.com: in stuff.js: $('#stuff-from-exampl2").load('http://www.example2.com/fragment.html');
