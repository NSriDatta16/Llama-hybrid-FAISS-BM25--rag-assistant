Well you could replicate the data from your transactional databases to read-only copies to run reporting queries against as a relatively quick fix solution. With eCommerce however you need to be aware of PCI and ensure you're following their requirements to safeguard sensitive customer / credit card data. PCI Compliance Guide The next step is to build a data warehouse that's organized specifically for reporting purposes - making it easier to write queries as well as have them run much more efficiently to slice and dice your data - seeing it from multiple points of view (dimensions).
