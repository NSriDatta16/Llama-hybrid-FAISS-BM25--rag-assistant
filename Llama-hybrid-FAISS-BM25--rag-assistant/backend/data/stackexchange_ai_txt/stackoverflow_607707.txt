Your e.Data is a IDataObject , not the value you sent with DoDragDrop . To get the value you sent, you must call e.Data.GetData(...) . To fix your code, replace the problem line with: Dim copiedFromCell As DataGridViewCell = _ e.Data.GetData(GetType(DataGridViewTextBoxCell)) (or whatever the type of DataGridView1.CurrentCell is.) You can get a list of types available to be dropped by calling e.Data.GetFormats() .
