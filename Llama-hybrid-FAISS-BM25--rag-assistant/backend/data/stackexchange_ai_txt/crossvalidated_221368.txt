Micro f1 is based on global precision and recall. It treats each test case equally and doesn't give advantages to small classes. I think it's more suitable. This article "Macro- and micro-averaged evaluation measures" from Vincent Van Asch in U of Antwerp explains many different kinds of f1 scores.
