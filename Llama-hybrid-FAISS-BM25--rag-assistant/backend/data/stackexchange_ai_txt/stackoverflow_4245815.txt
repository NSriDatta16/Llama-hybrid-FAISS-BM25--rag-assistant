Another , IMHO more elegant, solution is to cast HttpContext.User to RolePrincipal and call SetDirty method after adding a new role to the user ( read more on RolePrincipal.SetDirty ). The next call of IsInRole or GetRolesForUser methods should trigger request to your default RoleProvider.
