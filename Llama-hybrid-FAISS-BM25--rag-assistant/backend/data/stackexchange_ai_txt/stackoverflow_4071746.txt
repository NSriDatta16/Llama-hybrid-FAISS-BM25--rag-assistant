As an alternative suggestion consider creating a cart price rule that, while not preventing smaller quantities, can offer encouragements to potential customers. For example a rule might have the conditions: Product attribute combination (If an item is FOUND etc...) Quantity in cart equals or is greater than 6 Attribute set is whichever set you use exclusively for cards and then offer free shipping on those cards.
