Purposes of a thread Hide latency (i.e. do something else while waiting) Exploit the concurrency of the hardware (in case of multiple cores, this gives better performance) Discriminate importance levels (i.e. high and low priority threads) Organize structure (i.e. thread per event, thread per resouce, thread per process) There are others, but i think this are the basic uses of a thread
