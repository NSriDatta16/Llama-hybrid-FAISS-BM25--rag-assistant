The answer is in the error message: "access denied" You do not have write permissions to that folder, which makes perfect sense; you shouldn't have write permission to that folder, at least you likely shouldn't. If you need to cache something for later use, either use session for temporary storage, or talk to your hosting company about getting write permission for longer-term storage.
