See RFC 3986 , section 3: The generic URI syntax consists of a hierarchical sequence of components referred to as the scheme, authority, path, query, and fragment. URI = scheme ":" hier-part [ "?" query ] [ "#" fragment ] hier-part = "//" authority path-abempty / path-absolute / path-rootless / path-empty The scheme and path components are required, though the path may be empty (no characters).
