There have been many papers on this topic, but you probably should search with the term language modeling (the name of the main application for smoothing n-grams frequencies). Apparently there's a recent paper , but otherwise you'll most likely find papers from 20 years ago, as this used to be an important question for statistical machine translation (it isn't anymore with neural MT, afaik).
