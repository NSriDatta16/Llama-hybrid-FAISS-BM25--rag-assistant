Ignoring the denominator is related to posterior maximization, i.e. choosing the class with the highest posterior probability. The quote is actually referring to Bayesian and Frequentist methods. Frequentist methodology uses data to explain phenomenon (i.e. likelihood), while Bayesian methodology assumes priors, other than the data.
