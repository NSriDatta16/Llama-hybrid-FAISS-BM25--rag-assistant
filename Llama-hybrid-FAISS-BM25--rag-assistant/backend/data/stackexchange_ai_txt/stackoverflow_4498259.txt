01 is different from 1 . Lexicographically 13 comes before 3 . Presuming that all "paragraphs" are prepended with a non-zero-prefixed integer, you will need to parse each integer and order your list by that. The previous answers will throw FormatException if a list item is not prefixed by an integer.
