I think this encourages people to reuse existing passwords, which is a bad idea. They shouldn't even consider putting in the password to their bank account, just in case they forget one they used specifically for your site. Consider using OpenID as an alternative system. It allows people to use a single password for any group of websites they considered insignificant enough to deserve a unique, secret password. And on top of it, they never have to tell you what their password is.
