If you're doing this for AdSense, here's an article on how to exclude content from the scraper. If you don't want Google to follow links, you can give them a rel="nofollow" attribute. Otherwise, I'm afraid you may be out of luck here. Something else you could do, but I wouldn't necessarily recommend doing, is detecting the user agent before rendering your page, and if it's a spider or bot, not showing the portions of your page you want to exclude.
