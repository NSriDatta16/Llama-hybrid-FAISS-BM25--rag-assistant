Siblings is the right Idea, but you need to first go back to the li element as you want to hide its siblings and not of the a element.. Here is a working example : http://www.jsfiddle.net/MxgpT/ i just add to your code this $('a').click( function(){ $(this).closest('li').siblings(':not(:first-child)').hide(); });
