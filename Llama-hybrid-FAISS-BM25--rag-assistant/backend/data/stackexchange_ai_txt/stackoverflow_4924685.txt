As far as I can tell from the documentation , reference names in ReST documents share a common namespace, so they must be unique. I can't think of anything better than this: foo is a tool used to do foo. `Download foo`_ .. _Download foo: http://example.com/foo blah blah blah (omit a lot of paragraphs) bar is another tool to do bar. `Download bar`_ .. _Download bar: http://example.com/bar
