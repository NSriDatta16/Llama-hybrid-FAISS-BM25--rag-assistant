The specific answer is no, there is not an easy or built in way to do this. You have to write it to disk "yourself". Figure out why you are getting the out of memory it might surprise you. Maybe its string internment, maybe your are fragmenting the GC with all the small object allocations. The CLR Profiler from Microsoft is fantastic for this.
