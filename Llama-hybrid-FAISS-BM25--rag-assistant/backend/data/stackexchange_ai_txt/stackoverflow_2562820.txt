I think it's a problem with your code and not with 10.6.3. I have an app I'm writing that simulates key presses, and I've upgraded to 10.6.3, and my simulated key presses still work just fine. Here's what I'm doing: CGEventSourceRef source = CGEventSourceCreate(kCGEventSourceStateCombinedSessionState); CGEventRef keyDownPress = CGEventCreateKeyboardEvent(source, (CGKeyCode)keyCode, YES); CGEventSetFlags(keyDownPress, (CGEventFlags)flags); CGEventRef keyUpPress = CGEventCreateKeyboardEvent(source, (CGKeyCode)keyCode, NO); CGEventPost(kCGAnnotatedSessionEventTap, keyDownPress); CGEventPost(kCGAnnotatedSessionEventTap, keyUpPress); CFRelease(keyDownPress); CFRelease(keyUpPress); CFRelease(source);
