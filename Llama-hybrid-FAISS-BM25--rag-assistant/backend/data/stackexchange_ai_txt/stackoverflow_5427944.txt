You want pylibpcap but what you have installed is pypcap , a different Python libpcap binding with an incompatible API. There are three Python libpcap bindings used in the wild: pypcap ( recommended by scapy for use on Windowas ), pylibcap and pcapy . See scapy/pcapdnet.py for a taste of their API differences. With their similar names people must have them mixed up pretty regularly ...
