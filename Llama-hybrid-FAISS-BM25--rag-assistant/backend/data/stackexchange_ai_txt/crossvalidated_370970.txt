It is neither classification, nor regression, since you don't want to predict anything, you want to compare groups given some known criteria. Decision trees are not used for this kind of tasks. This is a hypothesis testing problem . You may also find interesting the following thread: Distinguishing between two groups in statistics and machine learning: hypothesis test vs. classification vs. clustering .
