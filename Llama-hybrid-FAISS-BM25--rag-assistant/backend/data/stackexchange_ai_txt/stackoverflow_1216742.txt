Ensure the database is running in the Simple Recovery Model. Doing so negates the need for you to perform transaction log backups. This recovery model automatically ensures that the inactive portions of the transaction log can become immediately available for reuse. No longer concerned with the Transaction Log management, you can focus your attention on your backup strategy. A weekly FULL Database Backup, perhaps with daily Differential Backups may suit your requirements. Recovery Model Overview
