After png_read_image you should technically have a png_read_end call: // ... png_read_image(png,(png_byte**)row); png_infop end_info = png_create_info_struct(png); png_read_end(png, end_info); After that the positions should match. Even the libpng docs (last paragraphs of section 13.7) make it seem unnecessary, though.
