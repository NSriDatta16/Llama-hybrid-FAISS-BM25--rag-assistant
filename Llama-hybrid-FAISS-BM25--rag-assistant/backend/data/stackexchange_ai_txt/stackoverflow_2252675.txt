It certainly does work in Oracle. Quick contrived example: SQL> select ename, job, deptno from emp 2 where (ename, deptno) in 3 ( select ename, deptno from emp 4 where job = 'MANAGER' 5 ); ENAME JOB DEPTNO ---------- --------- ---------- JONES MANAGER 20 CLARK MANAGER 10 PARAG MANAGER 30 This also works: SQL> select ename, job, deptno from emp 2 where (ename, deptno) in (('JONES',20),('CLARK',10)); ENAME JOB DEPTNO ---------- --------- ---------- JONES MANAGER 20 CLARK MANAGER 10 NOT IN too: SQL> select ename, job, deptno from emp 2 where (ename, deptno) not in 3 ( select ename, deptno from emp 4 where job = 'MANAGER' 5 ); ENAME JOB DEPTNO ---------- --------- ---------- SMITH CLEANER 99 SCOTT ANALYST 20 KING PRESIDENT 10 FORD ANALYST 20 MILLER CLERK 10
