With SQL Server 2008 you get a plethora of date types: SMALLDATETIME 4 byte storage, no second precission DATETIME 8 byte storage, 3ms precission DATETIME2 has custom precission DATETIMEOFFSET has timezone info DATE day only, no time TIME no day info, only time of day, good for scheduling So, pick your poison.
