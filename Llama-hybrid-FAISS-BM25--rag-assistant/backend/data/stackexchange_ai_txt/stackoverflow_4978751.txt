You want the IOStorage subsystem of IOKit. The easiest way to get started is to create a filter scheme driver: http://developer.apple.com/library/mac/#documentation/DeviceDrivers/Conceptual/MassStorage/08_Media_Example/MS_Media_Example.html The book "Mac OS X Internals" by Amit Singh also contains some info and an example on this (source available at http://osxbook.com/ ).
