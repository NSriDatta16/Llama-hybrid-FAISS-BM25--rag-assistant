Java Verifieds UTI root certificate is not on all handsets/network combinations, the same is true for other domains in the trusted third party such as Verisign and Thawte (for these bodies in particular Motorola devices) It is fair to say that the UTI certificate is probably the one to choose to give you the most coverage across handsets
