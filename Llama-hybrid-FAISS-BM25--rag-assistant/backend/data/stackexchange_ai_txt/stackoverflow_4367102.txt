Have a look at this documentation: https://developer.mozilla.org/En/DragDrop/Drag_and_Drop Note: It does not work in every browser. If you want a cross-browser support use jQuery: http://jqueryui.com/demos/draggable/ jQuery example: Drag me around $(function() { $( "#draggable" ).draggable({ drag: function(event, ui) {} }); }); Another example: This text may be dragged. document.getElementById('toBeDragged').addEventListener('dragstart', function(event) { event.dataTransfer.setData('text/plain', 'This text may be dragged'); });
