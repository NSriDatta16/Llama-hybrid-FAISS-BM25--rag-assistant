There are definitely more elegant ways, but at 2:40AM: title=`cat test | grep "^\s*title\s*=\s*" | sed 's/^\s*title\s*=\s*{?//' | sed 's/}?\s*,\s*$//'` Grep for the line that interests us, strip everything up to and including the opening curly, then strip everything from the last curly to the end of the line
