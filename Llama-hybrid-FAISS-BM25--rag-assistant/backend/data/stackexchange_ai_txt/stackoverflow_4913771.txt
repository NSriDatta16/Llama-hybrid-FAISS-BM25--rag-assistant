The observer pattern may be a good fit for your situation. If your expressions are referencing one entity, the classic observer pattern will work well. If the entities being referenced are also expressions that reference other entities, and those entities reference other entities, and so on, you can see how it could become a tangled mess. In this case, a specific use of observer called an event bus could provide an elegant solution. This way, your entities register for and send events through a central event bus.
