Simply, you should base your program on a system such as Eclipse RCP, or the Netbeans Platform. Both of these systems already deal with exactly this problem, and both are perfectly adequate for this task. They're not just for IDEs. It's a larger first step as you will need to learn one of these platforms beyond simply just Swing. But, they solve the problem, and their overall organization and technique will serve your program well anyway. Don't reinvent this wheel, just learn one of these instead.
