There is a reason they are junior and you are senior. The ability to realise when a change in design is needed is one of them. I would carry on as you are but encourage them to come to you when things are getting difficult. You can then work with them to alter the design if needed, this will be easier for you than refactoring it and will help you pass on knowledge to your junior developers.
