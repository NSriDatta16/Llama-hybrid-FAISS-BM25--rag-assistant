Look to the load function in jQuery( http://docs.jquery.com/Ajax/load ). Call your controller with load and then inject the result into your html document. $("myDiv").load("/Controller/Action"); Your ViewData will be parsed on the server side. Instead of returning View(), return a PartialView() pointing to a user control. What comes out then is just a HTML fragment.
