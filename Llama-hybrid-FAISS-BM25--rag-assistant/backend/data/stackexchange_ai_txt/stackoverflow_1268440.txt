It appears that the Rails plugin 'has_many_polymorphs' allows me to do exactly what I want. http://github.com/fauna/has_many_polymorphs/tree/master Adding this line to my User model gave me the functionality I wanted: has_many_polymorphs :friendables, :from => [:businesses, :organizations], :through => :friendships
