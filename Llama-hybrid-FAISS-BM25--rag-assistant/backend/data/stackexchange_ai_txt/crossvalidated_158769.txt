Exactly The Same Autocovariance Function of Two Time Series

A MA(2) process : $$X_t=W_t+\frac{5}{2}W_{t-1}-\frac{3}{2}W_{t-2}$$ where $\{W_t\}\sim WN(0,1)$ And another MA(2) process : $$X_t=W_t-\frac{1}{6}W_{t-1}-\frac{1}{6}W_{t-2}$$ where $\{W_t\}\sim WN(0,9)$ Both time series have exactly the same autocovariance function . What does it imply ? What is the message of the example ?
