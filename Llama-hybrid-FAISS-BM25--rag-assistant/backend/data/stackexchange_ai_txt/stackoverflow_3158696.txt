This is due to the internal storage format for a floating point number being inherently inexact when the number is represented in decimal. There are many, many questions on this on Stack Overflow. The number you are returning is probably something like 130.000000000000001 since the numbers in your calculation can't be represented exactly as a binary floating point number.
