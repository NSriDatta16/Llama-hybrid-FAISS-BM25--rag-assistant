You will need to use Facebook connect to access FBJS calls. Facebook encourages Iframe developers to use Facebook Connect to perform client-side facebook interaction, such as publishing feed stories, permission dialogs, etc. See: http://wiki.developers.facebook.com/index.php/Creating_a_Popup_Dialog_in_an_IFrame
