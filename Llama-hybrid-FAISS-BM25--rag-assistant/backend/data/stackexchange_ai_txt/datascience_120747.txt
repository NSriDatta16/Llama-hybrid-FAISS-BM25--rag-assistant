Linear regression excels at extrapolating trends, but can't learn interactions. XGBoost excels at learning interactions, but can't extrapolate trends. You can create a "hybrid" forecasters that combine complementary learning algorithms and let the strengths of one make up for the weakness of the other. Source: https://www.kaggle.com/code/ryanholbrook/hybrid-models
