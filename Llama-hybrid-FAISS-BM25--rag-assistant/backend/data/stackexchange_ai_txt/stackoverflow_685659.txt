See my question and answer here . Basically it depends on your organization and the application. If your company, developers and customers all speak the same native language and you expect it to stay that way, then it would be extremely counter-productive to have everyone become a part-time translator as well. Considerable productivity loss for a purely hypothetical future advantage. YAGNI. If it's a large international company, or if there are concrete plans to expand internationally or have some work done offshore, it's a different matter, of course.
