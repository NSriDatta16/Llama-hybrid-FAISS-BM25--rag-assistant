You could check out SkPictureRecord.cpp . It looks like the details of drawing functions are simply serialized linearly via methods like: void addScalar(SkScalar scalar) { fWriter.writeScalar(scalar); } In any case, as with most private details of the SDK, leveraging this knowledge is likely to get you into trouble when the SDK changes.
