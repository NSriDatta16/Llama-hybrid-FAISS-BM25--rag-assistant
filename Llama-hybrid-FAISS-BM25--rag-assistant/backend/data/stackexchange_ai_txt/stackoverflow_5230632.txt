You very well may be better off starting with an existing open source player, like JW Player . It is going to resolve a lot of issue for you. That said, your problem is probably not the player. Pay close attention the the encoding of the video . Just because your media is MPEG-4 h.264 does not mean it will play on a give device--they all have their own particular restrictions. Video CODEC, resolution, audio CODEC, bit rate, etc. Blackberry media type restrictions iPhone encoding guide If the phones are downloading the file, then you may have a MIME type issue. If it is just not playing, then it is likely to be encoding related.
