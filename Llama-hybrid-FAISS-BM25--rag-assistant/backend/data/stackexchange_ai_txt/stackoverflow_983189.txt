If you want it to be fast, the best way is to derive from Control and implement OnRender - normally this isn't necessary, but for your application it might be. Also, let's take a step back - the screen you're rendering to certainly isn't 300k pixels across; before you go to render, reduce the buffer by averaging n nodes into one until you've got something closer to the resolution of the actual device, then draw it on-screen.
