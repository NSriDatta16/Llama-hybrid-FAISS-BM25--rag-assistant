You can detect the incoming user agent and if it is a bot user agent render the page with the words you don't want to index. However, chances are good that such keywords are going to be much more popular on other sites than your blog - is it really a concern? If so, the simple fact is that "the Internet doesn't forget" - it is better not to post something compromising to begin with.
