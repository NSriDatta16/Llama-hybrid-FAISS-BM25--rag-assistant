How could I predict these five at once? You could use hierarchical time series forecasting . This approach is used when forecasting grouped or related time series (for example closely related products, or the same products in different geographical locations). (If I understand your data correctly) In your case specifically you can use the following approach: have a separate time series for each item/pack size and then roll them up into a total demand per item series.
