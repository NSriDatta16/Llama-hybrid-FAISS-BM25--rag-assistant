OpenCalais is a web service that augments unstructured text with semantic metadata (and can therefore partially automate the creation of a semantic web): http://www.opencalais.com/about You could write about the challenges of implementing named entity recognition and natural language processing for extracting facts and events. This paper proposes using Wikipedia articles as a dictionary of named entities: http://www.aaai.org/Papers/IJCAI/2007/IJCAI07-259.pdf This may also shed some light on the NLP challenge: http://www.cyc.com/cyc/technology/whatiscyc_dir/cycrandd/areasofrandd_dir/nlu
