Most tracking systems of this type work by embedding an image or some other object into participating sites which is served from your domain. At this point you can issue cookies from the single domain that you do control, and track the users by watching the referral data on your web requests. Since the object is served from your domain, there's no need for the cross-domain cookies that you're asking for, which is fortunate because they're not possible to do.
