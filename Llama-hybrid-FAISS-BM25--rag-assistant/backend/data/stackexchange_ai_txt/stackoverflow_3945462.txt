When the linebreak has semantic meaning within the unstyled document. As someone said, poetry is a good example - conventionally, poetry is written with a linebreak between lines. As are addresses. It does not make sense to mark up a line of a poem or an address with a paragraph element, as these are better matches to the whole address or a stanza of the poem.
