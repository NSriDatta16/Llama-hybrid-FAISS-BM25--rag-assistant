Average Partial Effects (APE) are the contribution of each variable on the outcome scale, conditional on the other variables involved in the link functio n transformation of the linear predictor Average Marginal Effects (AME) are the marginal contribution of each variable on the scale of the linear predictor . This documentation from the margins package for R is quite useful for understanding.
