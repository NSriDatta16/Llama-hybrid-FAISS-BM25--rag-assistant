PCA does not get rid of any of the variables, although some may be very unimportant. Suppose you use the first three components from your PCA, each of these is a linear combination of the 20 variables you put into it. If you want to be able to interpret the importance of the original variables in the regression, I don't think PCA is the way to go. I would consider one of the penalized regression methods such as LASSO or LAR
