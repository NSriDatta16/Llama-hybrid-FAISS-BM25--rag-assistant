For editing? You don't need code at all, provided you use the CCK module on your site. Go to Admin > Content Types. Click on "manage fields" on the content type you want to edit, then drag the Taxonomy module form underneath the body. Hit Save, you're done. For maximum control, you can also use the Content Taxonomy module which turns taxonomies into CCK fields.
