For anyone trying to dynamically assign "textarea1", the draggable is in the ui object as ui.item. For one textarea per sortable: tinyMCE.execCommand( 'mceAddControl', false, $('textarea',ui.item)[0].id ); tinyMCE.execCommand( 'mceRemoveControl', false, $('textarea',ui.item)[0].id );
