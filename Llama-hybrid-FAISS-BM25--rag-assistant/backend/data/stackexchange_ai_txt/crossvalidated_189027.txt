There are two chapters that you might find interesting in a book called: Bayesian Nonparametrics by Hjort et al. - ISBN: 978-1107012271 The two chapters are called: Nonparametric Bayes applications to biostatistics by Dunson More nonparametric Bayseian models for biostatistics by Muller et al. There is also a Springer book, Nonparametric Bayesian Inference in Biostatistics by Mitra - ISBN: 9783319195179 I found a fairly good list of papers, many by Muller, Mitra and colleagues: https://www.ma.utexas.edu/users/pmueller/paper.html
