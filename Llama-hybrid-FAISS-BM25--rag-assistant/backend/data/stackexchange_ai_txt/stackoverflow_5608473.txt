Fuego is an open-source monte-carlo tree search (as opposed to alpha-beta tree search) platform that can target 2-player full-information games (originally created to target Go). It may even be more general than that. http://fuego.sourceforge.net/ Edit: I just learned that it also features an alpha-beta searcher. Here is a recent paper: http://webdocs.cs.ualberta.ca/~mmueller/ps/fuego-TCIAIG.pdf
