The lack of a valid DOCTYPE declaration causes IE to render the page in Quirks Mode. As a result, for whatever reason, some version of IE's dreaded three pixel bug is active in full force and padding out your DIVs. The easy solution is to just specify a DOCTYPE and hope nobody will be watching from IE6 or worse. Otherwise, it will take some special hacks.
