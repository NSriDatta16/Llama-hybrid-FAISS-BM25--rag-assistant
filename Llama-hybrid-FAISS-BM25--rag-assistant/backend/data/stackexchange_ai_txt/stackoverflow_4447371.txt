Record the actual times taken and relevant factors; estimate future times based the averages of past runs with in similar situations. In most cases it will never be all that accurate, but it's better than nothing I suppose. As you say, this isn't language specific, but it is very situation specific; the factors that are relevant to burning a disc are probably different to the factors that influence the time to unzip a file.
