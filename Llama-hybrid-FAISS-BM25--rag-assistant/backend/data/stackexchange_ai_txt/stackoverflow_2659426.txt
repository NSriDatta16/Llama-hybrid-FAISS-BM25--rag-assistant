When considering using Office Automation on the server, you might want to take a read through the info in the following KB article. http://support.microsoft.com/kb/257757 A key phrase from the above link Microsoft does not currently recommend, and does not support, Automation of Microsoft Office applications from any unattended, non-interactive client application or component (including ASP, ASP.NET, DCOM, and NT Services), because Office may exhibit unstable behavior and/or deadlock when Office is run in this environment. And I can confirm that the above is very true, and not just MS trying to discourage you...
