Use get/set arguments: http://developer.android.com/reference/android/app/Fragment.html#getArguments () [edit] So when you create Fragment Bob from Fragment Jim you could do something like this: Bob b = Bob.newInstance(); Bundle bundle = new Bundle(); bundle.put("resourceId", R.id.XMLB); b.setArguemnts(bundle); or you could even in the newInstance method take in the resourceId Bob b = new Bob.newInstance(R.id.XMLB);
