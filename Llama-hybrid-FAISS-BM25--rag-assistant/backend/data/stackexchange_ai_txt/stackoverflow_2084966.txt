I second on the Vici MVC framework ! Open source, easy to setup, easy to learn, lightweight, powerfull and with a good support channel through a StackExchange-based webpage. The Vici Project comes with a lot more libraries (e.g. Vici CoolStorage = ORM) which work beautifully together.
