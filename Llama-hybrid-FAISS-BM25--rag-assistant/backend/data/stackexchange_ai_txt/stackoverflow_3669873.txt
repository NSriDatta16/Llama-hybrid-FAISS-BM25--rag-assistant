First of all, you should not subclass UIWebView , as the documentation specifically discourages this practice. You could intercept the touch events by subclassing other classes, such as UIWindow or, in your case, UIScrollView . Take a look at this article which demonstrates how to detect touch events on UIWebView . I took a similar but slightly different approach: I subclassed a UIView , which contains a UIWebView (and other controls), and override the hitTest:withEvent: method.
