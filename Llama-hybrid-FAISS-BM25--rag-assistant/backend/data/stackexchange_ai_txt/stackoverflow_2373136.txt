Less fragile than the actual contents would be if you gave the body tag a class (or ID). For big blocks of stuff a simple, one-line if works: if( $("body").hasClass("doStuff") ) and for anything outside of that area you can just use $("body.doStuff div.example").hide() and suchlike.
