It's most likely that SQL server is doing its best to get the data you asked for. It's not unreasonable to assume at least 1K/record for 30 columns. 3.4M x 1K = 3.4Gb. Just reading 3.4Gb from the disk could take minutes on an average machine (do not forget that this is not just reading, there's obviously some SQL processing overhead in it. But of course in a real world scenario you don't want to retrieve all the data...
