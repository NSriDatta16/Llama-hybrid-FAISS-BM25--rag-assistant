You could use a decision tree: http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Decision_tree_learning It should give similar results as logistic regression, but it's a really easy way to present the effects of binary variables. After you have run both models, you can look at a contingency table of correct and incorrect classifications to determine which model to use.
