You answered your own question; if you feel that calling the service every time you start would be disruptive (which it would, e.g. the user is out of coverage), then don't do it. Google make no recommendations about how often to use the licensing service; it's down to how paranoid you as the application developer are about piracy, balanced with how much you feel constantly checking would annoy the user.
