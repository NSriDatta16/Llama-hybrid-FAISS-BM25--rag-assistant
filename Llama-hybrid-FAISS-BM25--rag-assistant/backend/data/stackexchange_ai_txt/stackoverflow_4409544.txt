Not directly. About the only thing you can do is have one database inherit from the other, and set only the lotusscript libraries to be inherited. Then you could make changes on the parent, and have them carry over to the child (or children) databases. You can, however, run agents on another database. Agents in turn can make calls to script libraries in their own databases. Depending on your goals, you may be able to get what you need from that.
