Many releases have taken out significant components and put them in plugins, you probably haven't even noticed most of the time. The best thing to do would be to just stay upgraded and pay attention to deprecation messages in your logs and you'll be fine. This is all thanks to posts like this which highlighted the problems to the community earlier on. Now it should be much, much smoother.
