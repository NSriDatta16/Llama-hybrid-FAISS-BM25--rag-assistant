There are several ways to do this, depending on the framework you are using. If you intend to do this with "Xlib", the most basic way to program for X11, you should take a look at Xlib manual , with special attention to XInput. More elaborated information can be retrieved by using XI2 (XInput version 2) Other higher level frameworks can make things easier. Take a look at Qt . It's one of the best GUI API's I've ever seen. Tell us if you need more.
