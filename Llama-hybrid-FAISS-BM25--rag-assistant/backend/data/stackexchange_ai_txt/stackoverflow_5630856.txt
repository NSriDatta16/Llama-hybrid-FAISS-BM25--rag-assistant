You can't. .sln files store completely different data than .suo files. From MSDN : .sln : Organizes projects, project items and solution items into the solution by providing the environment with references to their locations on disk. .suo : Records all of the options that you might associate with your solution so that each time you open it, it includes customizations that you have made.
