The reason calling System.gc() is a bad idea is most likely because it does not guarantee anything. If you really want to be certain that the JVM does garbage collection, you must tell it to. One way is the same way as JConsole, namely through JMX. See http://java.sun.com/j2se/1.5.0/docs/guide/management/agent.html#local
