Moving averages will give you a smoother time series so that a trend is easier to see by eye. This approach makes sense when you’re exploring the data. The next step is to try to comment on where the time series will go next. Based on the tags you have chosen for your question, you are comfortable writing python. You might consider Facebook’s open source time series forecasting package . This will allow you to analyse the seasonality of your sales (e.g. how does the day of the week or the month of the year have an effect?) and allow you to understand what the trend is doing when the seasonal effects are removed. There tutorial for the python API is here .
