I personally find the C# port of YUI Compressor to be the best of the best when it comes to CSS and JavaScript minification. In the end, though, you're going to have to do the leg work to get a perfect YSLOW score. There's no library that you can just drag and drop and it will magically make your website fast. Look into making a controller for your content and doing the following: GZIP your response Far Future Expire: HttpResponse.ExpiresAbsolute = DateTime.Now.AddYears(5);
