My experience on JWS is not very extensive so far, but I could note that if your software depends on libraries that are platform dependent and/or have thoughts of dynamically passing arguments to the application, it might not be as straight forward as one would hope. (that's the situation I'm in at least) If I were in your shoes I would really take my time and weigh the options, read into the strengths and weaknesses of JWS. After all not everything you want/need might be possible when using JWS. Here's a good place to start reading: http://download.oracle.com/javase/6/docs/technotes/guides/javaws/developersguide/contents.html Otherwise there are a number of good resources available on JNLP syntax and example code at Sun's Java tutorials, here's the article on Deployment .
