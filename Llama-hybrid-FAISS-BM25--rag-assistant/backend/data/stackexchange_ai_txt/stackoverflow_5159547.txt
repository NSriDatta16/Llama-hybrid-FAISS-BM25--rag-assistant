Measuring similarities between text documents you can start with older techniques of document vetor similarity ( check vector space model ). You can use latent semantic indexing for the same. Here is one paper on document similarities. Text summarization is more difficult then similarity measures as you have to produce something meaningful to humans. OpenNLP is good library for all the basic related to text processing. More papers related to text summarization are here , may be good to start with.
