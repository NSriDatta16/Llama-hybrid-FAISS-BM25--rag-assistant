Interface builder includes the MKMapView (Map View). Drag the element into your XIB, add a referencing outlet in your controller, link them up. Then, set the region. Lots of good examples: http://developer.apple.com/iphone/library/samplecode/WorldCities/Introduction/Intro.html#//apple_ref/doc/uid/DTS40009466
