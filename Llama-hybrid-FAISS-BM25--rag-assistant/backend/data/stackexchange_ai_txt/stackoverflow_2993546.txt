With HttpClient 4.0, the following worked for me: import org.apache.http.params.HttpProtocolParams; HttpClient httpclient = new HttpClient(); HttpProtocolParams.setUserAgent(httpclient.getParams(), "My fancy UA"); HttpProtocolParams resides in the httpcore JAR file: http://hc.apache.org/httpcomponents-core/download.html
