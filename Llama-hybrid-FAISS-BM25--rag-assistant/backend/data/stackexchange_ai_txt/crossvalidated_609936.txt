Square them. Log them. Multiply them. Multiply their logs. Log their products. Take Fourier or wavelet transforms of time series data. Any function of your set of features is a possible additional feature to include instead of or along with the original features. Some such functions will be more useful than others, yes, but pretty much any function of your features included as an additional feature will increase the dimension of your feature space.
