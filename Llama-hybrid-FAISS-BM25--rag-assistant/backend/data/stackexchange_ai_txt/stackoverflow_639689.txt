You need to add a custom cursor as a resource to your application, get the resource handle, and then use SetCursor(...) The drag/copy/move cursors aren't part of the standard library - your application will need it's own. The standard cursors are all listed here: http://msdn.microsoft.com/en-us/library/ms648391(VS.85).aspx That being said, IDC_HAND does exist on newer operating systems, which may be what you are looking for...
