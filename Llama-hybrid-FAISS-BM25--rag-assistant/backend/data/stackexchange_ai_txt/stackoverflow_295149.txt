In GCC you can declare your function with the attribute deprecated like this: void myfunc() __attribute__ ((deprecated)); This will trigger a compile-time warning when that function is used in a .c file. You can find more info under "Diagnostic pragmas" at http://gcc.gnu.org/onlinedocs/gcc/Pragmas.html
