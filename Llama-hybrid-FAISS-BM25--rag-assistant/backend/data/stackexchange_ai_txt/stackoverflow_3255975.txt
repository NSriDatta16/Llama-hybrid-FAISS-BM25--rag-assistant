You can publish the topic dnd/cancel , i.e. dojo.publish('/dnd/cancel') , then call dojo.dnd.manager().stopDrag() to cancel the drop. But the recommended way is to customize the checkAcceptance() of dojo.dnd.Source to return false when current node is not droppable. Find more details in dojo dnd's doc .
