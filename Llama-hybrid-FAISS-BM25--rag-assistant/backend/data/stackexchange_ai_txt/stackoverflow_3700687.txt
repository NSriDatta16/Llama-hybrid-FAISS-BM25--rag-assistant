Every element is drawn with a different ZIndex by Silverlight. If you don't specify the ZIndex , Silverlight assumes that the most recent elements added have a higher implied ZIndex . The same goes for XAML - elements below other elements have a higher implied ZIndex . If you want to control the drawing of your Rect elements in your example, you'll need to modify the ZIndex manually. First, make sure your Layoutroot element is a Canvas . Next, in your mouse move event handlers, change the ZIndex dependency property to be higher. For instance, this example will bring the selected rectangle to the forefront while its being dragged.
