To get iterate over a reader you'd do: >>> import csv >>> for row in csv.DictReader(open(fname), delimiter='\t'): print(row) {'Name': 'Bob-Smith.local', 'UID': 'bobs'} {'Name': 'Carmen-Jackson.local', 'UID': 'carmenj'} {'Name': 'David-Kathman.local', 'UID': 'davidk'} {'Name': 'Jenn-Roberts.local', 'UID': 'jennr'} But since you want to associate Name with UID : >>> reader = csv.reader(open("masterlist.txt"), delimiter='\t') >>> _ = next(reader) # just discarding header >>> d = dict(reader) >>> d['Carmen-Jackson.local'] 'carmenj'
