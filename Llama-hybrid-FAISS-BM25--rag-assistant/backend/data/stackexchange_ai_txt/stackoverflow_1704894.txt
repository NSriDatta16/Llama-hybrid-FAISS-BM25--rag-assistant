Yeah, if you want to do this with no downtime you should look into HA (High Availability) techniques. Check out a book by Paul Bertucci - I think it's called SQL Server High Availability or some such. Otherwise, put up your "maintenance" page, take all your app servers down, do the DB and one app server first, then go live and do the other two offline.
