You should use GetROProperty in order to get the text and then parse it for the value. Looking at a Google results page I see that the result is in a paragraph with id=resultStats in the 3rd bold tag. Results 1 - 10 of about 2,920,000 for qtp . ( 0.22 seconds) So the following script gets the number (as a string with commas). Browser("micclass:=Browser") .Page("micclass:=Page") .WebElement("html id:=resultStats") .WebElement("html tag:=b","index:=2").GetROProperty("innertext")
