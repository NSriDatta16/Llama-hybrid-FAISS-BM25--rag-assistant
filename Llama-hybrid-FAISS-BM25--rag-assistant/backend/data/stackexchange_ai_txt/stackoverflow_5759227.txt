is linux kernel preemptive or not?

Is the Linux kernel preemptive or not? There is a big difference between Understanding the Linux Kernel 2nd edition and 3rd edition. In the 2nd it is written that the Linux kernel is not preemptive but in the 3rd in the same paragraph nothing is said.
