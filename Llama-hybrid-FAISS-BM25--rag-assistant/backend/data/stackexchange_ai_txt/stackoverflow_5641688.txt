To get around most rate-limiting or bot-detection from the likes of Google or Wikipedia or Yahoo, spoof your user-agent. This will make your script's requests appear to be from the latest version of Google Chrome. headers = {'User-Agent' : "Mozilla/5.0 (Windows NT 6.0; WOW64) AppleWebKit/534.24 (KHTML, like Gecko) Chrome/11.0.696.16 Safari/534.24"} req = urllib2.Request(url,None,headers) content = urllib2.urlopen(req).read()
