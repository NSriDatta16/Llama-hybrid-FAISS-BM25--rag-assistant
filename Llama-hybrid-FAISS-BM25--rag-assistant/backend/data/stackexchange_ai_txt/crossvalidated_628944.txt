Is there a discrete time-homogeneous Markov chain $(X_n)_{n \geq 0}$ in which one transient state $i$ satisfies $ \sum_{n=1}^{\infty}nf^{(n)}_{ii} ? Case 2. Is there a discrete time-homogeneous Markov chain $(X_n)_{n \geq 0}$ in which one transient state $j$ satisfies $ \sum_{n=1}^{\infty}nf^{(n)}_{jj}=\infty $ ? Case 3.
