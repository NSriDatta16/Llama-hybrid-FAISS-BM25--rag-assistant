Did you look at Areas ? "Areas let you organize a large project into multiple smaller sections in order to manage the complexity of a large Web application. Each section (“area”) typically represents a separate section of a large Web site and is used to group related sets of controllers and views." In a nutshell, an area is a folder in your project that has its own subfolders for controllers, models, and views.
