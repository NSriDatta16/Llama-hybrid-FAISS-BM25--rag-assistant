Here is an extension that adds full-fledged looping, conditional execution, and callable functions to Selenium IDE: SelBlocks You setup a script/endScript section in your test, and then call it with parameters. For example: call|fillform|name="dilbert",phone="555-1212" call|fillform|name="wally",phone='unlisted" script|fillform type|name|${name} type|phone|${phone} endScript (String values are quoted because parameters are regular javascript expressions.)
