There is no API for this in the Android SDK. You can purchase a Qualcomm MDP device with the Trepn power profiler . This costs 1,000 USD, last I looked. You can also try PowerTutor . I have not experimented with this yet but plan to in the coming months. Or, you can examine the output from the Settings battery screen, if your app is showing up there.
