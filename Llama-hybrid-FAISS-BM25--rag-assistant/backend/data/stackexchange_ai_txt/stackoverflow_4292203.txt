It's in Google Chrome as well. It's in version 9 (currently only on the dev channel, but soon on the beta channel, and a couple months away from everyone getting the update). Use window.webkitIndexedDB to access it. http://trac.webkit.org/browser/trunk/LayoutTests/storage/indexeddb/tutorial.html is a tutorial that explains a lot of the current gotchas in the comments. Hopefully we'll have something to post on the Chromium blog and/or html5rocks.com soon.
