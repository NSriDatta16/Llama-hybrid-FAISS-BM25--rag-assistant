Allow me optimize your rules: RewriteEngine On RewriteCond %{HTTP_USER_AGENT} (iphone|blackberry|palm|android) [NC] RewriteRule ^(.*)$ http://mobile.yourdomain.com [R=301,L] RewriteCond %{REQUEST_FILENAME} !-d RewriteCond %{REQUEST_FILENAME} !-f RewriteRule ^(.*)$ publicprofile.php?username=$1 [QSA,L] [NC] stand for 'case insensitive'. [QSA] is to append the query string. You can test your rules at mod_rewrite tester .
