Robert C. Martin (Uncle Bob) recommends 3 as a maximum in Clean Code: A Handbook of Agile Software Craftsmanship I don't have the book with me at the moment but his reasoning has to do with one, two and, to a lesser extent, three argument functions reading well and clearly showing the purpose of the function. This of course goes hand in hand with his recommendation of very short, well named functions that adhere to the Single Responsibility Principal .
