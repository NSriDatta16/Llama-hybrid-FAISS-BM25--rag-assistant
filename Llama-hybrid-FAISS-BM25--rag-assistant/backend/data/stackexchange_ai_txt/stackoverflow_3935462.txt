JMeter and most other load testing tools are designed to operate at the protocol-level (HTTP GET, HTTP POST, etc). Selenium, on the other hand, works at the user-level (click, type, drag and drop, etc). They are fundamentally not well-suited to work together. If you want to do Selenium load testing, check out http://browsermob.com . Disclaimer: I'm the founder of BrowserMob :)
