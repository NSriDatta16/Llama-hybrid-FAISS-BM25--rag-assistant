Quickest way that will allow you to retain any existing Images directories and organization, and keep them out of your XAP. string src = Application.Current.Host.Source.ToString(); string applicationRoot = src.Substring(0, src.IndexOf("ClientBin")); //Where CLientBin is the location of your XAP file string imageURL = applicationRoot + "Images/MyImage.jpg"
