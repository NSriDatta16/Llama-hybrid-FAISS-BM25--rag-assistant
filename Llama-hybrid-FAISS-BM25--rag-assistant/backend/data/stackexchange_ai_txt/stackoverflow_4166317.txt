I tend to use option 1 as well but also try to organize them logically. toString at the bottom simple stuff at the top and working down to more specialized methods. What is more important is you use proper JDoc comment format, then a decent IDE should be able to automatically generate the HTML jdoc's for you or you can use their app. After you have proper documentation your method order is not as much of an issue.
