The FastScroller widget is responsible for drawing the overlay. You should probably take a look at its source: https://android.googlesource.com/platform/frameworks/base/+/gingerbread-release/core/java/android/widget/FastScroller.java Search for comment: // If user is dragging the scroll bar, draw the alphabet overlay
