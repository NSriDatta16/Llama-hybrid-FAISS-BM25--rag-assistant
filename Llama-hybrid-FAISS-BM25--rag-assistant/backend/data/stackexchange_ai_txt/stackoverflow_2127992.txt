jNetPcap uses Sigar in some of its unit tests. Sigar advertizes itself as a "portable interface for gathering system information" including network interface detection, configuration info and metrics. Take a look at its NetInterfaceStat class. Note that Sigar also relies on platform specific JNIs, i.e. Rob H's remark still applies.
