It is well known that XOR cannot be solved by a linear classifier (e.g. logistic regression): http://www.ece.utep.edu/research/webfuzzy/docs/kk-thesis/kk-thesis-html/node19.html Try to use SVM with either RBF or polynomial kernels: http://scikit-learn.org/stable/modules/svm.html
