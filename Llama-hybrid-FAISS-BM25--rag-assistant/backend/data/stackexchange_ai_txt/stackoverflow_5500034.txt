To configure the maven-cobertura-plugin, you should add it in the section, not as a - I assume your project code does not need cobertura to compile. When running mvn cobertura:cobertura the section you quoted will not be referred to at all. For example: ... org.codehaus.mojo cobertura-maven-plugin 2.4 ...
