You may want to consider testing them through a unit testing framework instead of through the browsers. E.g. NUnit or - depending on your Visual Studio edition - MS Test might be options. You may be able to avoid the WCF protocol overhead and talk straight to the service implementations, unless you specifically want to test the protocol. Automating the test will help you to test more often and increase the test coverage, so you can move faster.
