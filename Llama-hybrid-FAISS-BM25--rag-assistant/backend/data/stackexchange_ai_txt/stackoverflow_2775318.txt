You can, yes. You'll just have to install from source instead of RPM or DEB. Visit http://archive.cloudera.com/docs/ and click on one of the "Tarball" releases (either CDH2 or CDH3) in the top-right corner. Once you get the tarball, you'll have to create a hadoop user, set some environment variables, etc. I encourage you to ask Cloudera questions in Get Satisfaction, where we're more likely to answer your questions. getsatisfaction.com/cloudera Thanks, and good luck.
