You might find this article , (part of a larger series ) (self-link) more helpful, and it goes more into the why of what you're doing, which will enable you to better debug things yourself in the future. As for your specific error Controller file was loaded but class does not exist This means magento was able to correctly require/include the file you placed your controller in but the controller class was misnamed. Controllers should be named Packagename_Module_ControllernameController and located in the folder app/code/local/Packagename/Modulename/controllers/ControllernameController.php
