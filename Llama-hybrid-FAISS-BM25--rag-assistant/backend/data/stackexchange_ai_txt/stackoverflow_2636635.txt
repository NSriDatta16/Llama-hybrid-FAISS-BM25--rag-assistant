Thanks for the ideas, I found a simple way I think is the most efficient. I initially thought occlusion queries could only be used with geometry but they can also be used with textures. Turn on occlusion queries Render the image using a fragment shader and discard texels below required color intensity Retrieve number of texels passing drawing test from query
