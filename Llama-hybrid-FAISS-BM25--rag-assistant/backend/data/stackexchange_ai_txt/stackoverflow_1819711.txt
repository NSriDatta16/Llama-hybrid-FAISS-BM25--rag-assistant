You are using cross joins. Try inner joins. select poo.Person_Organization_id from Person_Organization po INNER JOIN Person_Organization_other poo ON poo.Person_Organization_id=po.Person_Organization_Id INNER JOIN Person_Organization_other poo1 ON poo1.Person_Organization_id=po.Person_Organization_Id AND poo.company_name=poo1.company_name where po.person_id=117 AND po.delete_flag=0 group by poo.Person_Organization_id
