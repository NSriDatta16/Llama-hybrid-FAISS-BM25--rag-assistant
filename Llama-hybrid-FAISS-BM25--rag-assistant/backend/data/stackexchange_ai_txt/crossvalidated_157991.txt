You use logistic regression. All these forms of regression/ANOVA depend only on the nature of the dependent variable. ANOVA is the same thing as linear regression. So, here are starting places for various types of DV Continuous, unbounded response with normal errors: Linear regression/ANOVA Binary, categorical or ordinal response: Logistic regression of one type or another Count response: Poisson or negative binomial regression Time to event response: Survival methods, probably Cox model to start
