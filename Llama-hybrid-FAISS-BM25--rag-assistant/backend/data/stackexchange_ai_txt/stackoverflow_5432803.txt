Another idea is to use the It.IsAny() method to avoid having to look for a hardcoded value at all: repositoryMock.Setup(x => x.GetById(It.IsAny ())).Returns(GetSampleArticle()); Which is safe to do since you're not concerned with the actual value of the articleId so much as the mechanics of its retrieval.
