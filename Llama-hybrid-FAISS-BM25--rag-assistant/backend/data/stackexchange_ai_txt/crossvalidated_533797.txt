You should not ‘run’ your autoencoder on them separately. Fit it to your training set, then apply the learned transformation to all three sets. This is exactly the same as what you should do with PCA. In fact, PCA is related to a simple linear autoencoder . But the advice is more general. Always fit everything to your training set.
