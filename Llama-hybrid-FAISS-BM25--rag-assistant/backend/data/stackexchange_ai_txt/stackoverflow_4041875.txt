I suppose it's cache. Try adding this before the request: XMLHttpReq.setRequestHeader("Cache-Control", "no-store, no-cache, must-revalidate"); XMLHttpReq.setRequestHeader("Cache-Control", "post-check=0, pre-check=0"); XMLHttpReq.setRequestHeader("Pragma", "no-cache"); If it doesn't work, try adding an additional parameter to your url, making it unique and therefore, not caching. var url="query?option=person&userName="+person.innerHTML + "&d=" + new Date().getTime() I really don't like this solution, but it helps you to know if the problem is related to cache.
