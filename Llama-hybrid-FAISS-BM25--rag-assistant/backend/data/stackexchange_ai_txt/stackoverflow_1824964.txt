Yes, quite easily. Rails very strongly encourages a model-view-controller structure for your application. It's possible to put business code into your views, but it's pretty unnatural. Try working through one of the tutorials on the Rails site. They only take maybe 15 minutes and will give you a good idea of how things are structured in Rails.
