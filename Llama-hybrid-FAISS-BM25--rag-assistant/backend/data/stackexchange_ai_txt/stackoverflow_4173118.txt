Assuming you have gone through all of the relevant Railscasts and the official RailsGuides , you should check out the Scaling Rails series. Your public pages will likely not have any dynamic (user dependent) context, so page caching is good there. The user dependent pages get a little tricky and require fragment caching or a mixture of page/action caching with ajax calls to get the user dependent content. Here is a basic example.
