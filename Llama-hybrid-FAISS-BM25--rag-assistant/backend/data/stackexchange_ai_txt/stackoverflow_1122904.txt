A portion of your app could possibly run as a system service and its own user. That way it could also have its own secure store away from your human-users. This would also remove the overarching need to have the user running a program with permissions out of their regular scope. The actual user-space portion of your app could connect to the service and perform simple storage/retrieval operations.
