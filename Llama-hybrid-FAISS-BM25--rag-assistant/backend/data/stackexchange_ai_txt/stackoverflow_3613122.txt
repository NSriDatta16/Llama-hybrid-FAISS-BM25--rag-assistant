In addition to the =begin comment multi-paragraph comments here =end comment =cut form in other answers, you can also do this: =for comment this is a single pod paragraph comment do not put extra blank lines after =for. the comment ends after the first blank line and regular pod continues until =cut Hello! C =cut the comment paragraph will not appear in the pod output, but the Hello "Yay!" will.
