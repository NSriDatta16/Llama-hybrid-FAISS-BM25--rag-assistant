I thought the lexer rules have a priority of how they are listed. So if you want token TYPE to actually be created, move it above all the other lexer rules. grammar Test; fragment TYPE: ('text' | 'number' | 'bool'); IDHEAD: ('a'..'z' | 'A'..'Z' | '_'); IDTAIL: (IDHEAD | '0'..'9'); ID: (IDHEAD IDTAIL*); define: 'define' ID 'as' TYPE;
