Some aspects of Scala mixins can be satisfied using multiple (virtual) inheritance. Unfortunately, this often introduces more problems than it solves. Also, you can't mix and match superclasses on the fly a la : val me = new Human with Coder with Musician If you really, really want true mixins, you almost have to go with something like the template solution proposed in the answer by @ Logan Capaldo .
