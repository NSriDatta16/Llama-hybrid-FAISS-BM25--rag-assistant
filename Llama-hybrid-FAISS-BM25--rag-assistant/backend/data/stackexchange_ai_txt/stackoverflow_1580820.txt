There are lots of tools that do this. But afaik none of them are language independent (which in turn would be mostly impossible e.g. some languages might not even have functions). Generally you will find those tools under the categories of "code coverage tools" or "profilers". For .Net you can use Visual Studio or Clrprofiler.
