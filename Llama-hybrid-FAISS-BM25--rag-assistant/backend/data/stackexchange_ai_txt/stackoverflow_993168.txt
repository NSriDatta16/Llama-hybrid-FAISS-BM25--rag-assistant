Google published Web Authoring Statistics a few years ago. They found then that the script tag was found in roughly half the pages they surveyed in a sample of over a half-billion documents. Since the web has grown a bit since they did the survey, it's probably safe to invoke the spirit of Carl Sagan, and answer "Billions and Billions!"
