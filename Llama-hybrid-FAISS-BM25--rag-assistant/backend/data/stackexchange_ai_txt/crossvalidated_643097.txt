If you set $\pi = F(\eta)=(1+\exp(-\eta))^{-1}$ and solve for $\eta$ , you obtain $\eta=\log(\frac{\pi}{1-\pi})=F^{-1}(\pi)$ . In the terminology of generalised linear models (logistic regression is an example of a GLM), $F$ is the mean function and $F^{-1}$ is the link function.
