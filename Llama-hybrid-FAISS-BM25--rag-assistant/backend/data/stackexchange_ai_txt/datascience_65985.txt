This is actually much more about feature engineering than just finding any representation. Therefore, I'd think through which variables might help your algorithm. Here are some more ideas for features based on your time ranges which might be helpful: duration start time end time morning, day time, evening or night activity (categorical, i.e. not for LDA) For the start and end time you might want to have a look at cyclic feature design .
