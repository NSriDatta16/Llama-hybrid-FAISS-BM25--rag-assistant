Yes, you could use Google App Engine , which has a great database system, and store everything in there. Depending on how big your application is, you will have no storage limit, and it's a service that's always available and 100%* (if your application is not huge and uses loads of bandwidth) free. You can use Java, Python or Ruby in there. so there's loads of nice options.
