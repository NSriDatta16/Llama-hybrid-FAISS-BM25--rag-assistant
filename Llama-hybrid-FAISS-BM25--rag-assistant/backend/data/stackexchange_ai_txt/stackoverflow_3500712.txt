The standard pattern would appear to be: ev = model.Session.query(model.EnumerationValue).filter(model.EnumerationValue.key==key_level_2).filter(model.EnumerationValue.code==level_2).count() if not ev: ev = model.EnumerationValue(key=key_level_2, code=level_2) ev.keyvalues[key_parent] = level_1 model.Session.add(ev) Not terribly elegant (and I may have syntax errors - apologies) but does the job.
