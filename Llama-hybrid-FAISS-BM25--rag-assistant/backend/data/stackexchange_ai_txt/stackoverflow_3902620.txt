On most any system the main execution time will be i/o-bound. And how to achieve fastest i/o depends very much on the system. So there's no single answer to "fastest". Most "elegant" depends on the eyes that see. So in summary, neither question has a definitive answer; it sounds like fishing for solutions to a homework assignment. Is it homework?
