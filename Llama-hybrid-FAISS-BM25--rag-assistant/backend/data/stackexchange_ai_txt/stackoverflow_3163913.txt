Normally StorageBase would implement INotifyPropertyChanged. The setter on Slices would then raise the INotifyPropertyChanged.PropertyChanged event. Example: http://msdn.microsoft.com/en-us/library/system.componentmodel.inotifypropertychanged.propertychanged.aspx EDIT: You may also want to make Slices an ObservableCollection instead of a Queue. http://msdn.microsoft.com/en-us/library/ms668604.aspx
