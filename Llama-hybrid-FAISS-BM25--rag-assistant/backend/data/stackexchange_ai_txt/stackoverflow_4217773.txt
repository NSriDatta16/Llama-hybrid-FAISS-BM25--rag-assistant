The .OutlineLevel property of a Word document can apply to the Paragraph, ParagraphFormat or Paragraphs Collection objects. It is an enumeration that can take the values wdOutlineLevel1 - 9, or wdOutlineLevelBodyText. To find the OutlineLevel of the first paragraph in the document, use: Dim currOutlineLevel With ActiveDocument currOutlineLevel = .Paragraphs(1).OutlineLevel End With
