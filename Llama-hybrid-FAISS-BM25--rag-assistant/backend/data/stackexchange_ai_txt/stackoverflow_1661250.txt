I use David Allen's ultra famous Getting Things Done approach to organize myself and GTDInbox for the implementation (imagine Gmail on steroids). GTDInbox transforms Gmail into a unique task manager to effectively manage your inbox, reduce email overload and maintain inbox zero. GTD is not specific to software development but can be used for any personal projects like writing a book, developing software, creating a company, etc...
