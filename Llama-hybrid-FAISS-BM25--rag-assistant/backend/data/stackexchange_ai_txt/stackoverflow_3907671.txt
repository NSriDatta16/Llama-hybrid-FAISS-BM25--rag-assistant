The method you are calling returns a HTTPResponse object, so you need to leverage that object's methods to get what you want. maybe something like: require 'net/http' require 'uri' res = Net::HTTP.post_form(URI.parse('http://example.com'),{'post'=>'1'}) puts res.body.split("Date") Notice the body method. Or, if you want to see all the data returned: require 'net/http' require 'uri' res = Net::HTTP.post_form(URI.parse('http://example.com'),{'post'=>'1'}) puts res.inspect Hope this helps!
