Depends on what your 'storage and analytics services' are exactly. For most cloud SaaS services they handle the technology under the hood. For example, Dropbox and Gmail probably have Hadoop and Spark underneath, which users never have to touch. However, read the price structure of the products carefully to see whether the service provider absorbs the cost. Practically speaking, once you have an objective, just starting using the picked services until you hit a wall. This is the moment you know what you lack.
