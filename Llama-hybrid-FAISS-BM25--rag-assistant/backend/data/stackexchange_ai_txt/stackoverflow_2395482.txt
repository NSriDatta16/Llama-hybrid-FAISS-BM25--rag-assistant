You need to register your class in the container, and make sure NOT to use AsA_Publisher with the built-in profiles, also don't use MsmqSubscriptionStorage or DbSubscriptionStorage . In order to register your class in the container do: NServiceBus.Configure.Instance.Configurer.ConfigureComponent (ComponentCallModelEnum.Singleton);
