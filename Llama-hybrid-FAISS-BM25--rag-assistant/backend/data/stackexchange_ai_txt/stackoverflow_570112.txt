I'm not 100% sure that I understand the question. If you actually want what you are calling individual divs to have a picture on the left and text on the right, you can accomplish this by floating both the photo and the lat_art div left . If you actually want the text to be on the right and to flow under the picture if it is longer, do not float the 'lat_art' at all . There is no need to specify a height on the individual divs , or to clear floats on them as they will fall one below the other by default (divs are blocks). You are falling victim to divitis here as well - ROCK ON could likely be something more meaningful like ROCK ON or a paragraph or something else styled accordingly.
