I'd use ren-regexp , which is a Perl script that lets you mass-rename files very easily. 21:25:11 $ ls transform.php?dappName=Test&transformer=YAML&v_id=12345 21:25:12 $ ren-regexp 's/transform.php.*v_id=(\d+)/$1.txt/' transform.php* transform.php?dappName=Test&transformer=YAML&v_id=12345 1 12345.txt 21:26:33 $ ls 12345.txt
