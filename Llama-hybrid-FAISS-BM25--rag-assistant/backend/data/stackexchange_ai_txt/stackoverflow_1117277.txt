Unix developers have leveraged the command line for decades. PowerShell with its deep integration to the Windows platform and products (Excel et al.) enable automation of many of our daily chores. Andy White highlighted several key areas where PowerShell is more than handy. Check out PowerBoots and some of its uses similar to Tcl/Tk and inspired by Ruby Shoes.
