It looks like this will do it... Add this to your prefix: #define __IPHONE_OS_VERSION_MAX_ALLOWED __IPHONE_3_1 Do not ship without removing this, though. In fact, you'll probably have to disable huge chunks of code to get your app to build this way. And even so, the coverage is nowhere near complete. But it's better than nothing.
