Here are the benefits to standards mode: 1) Correct and accurate code is easier to maintain among a group of developers. 2) Standards mode produces a more reliable rendering of the content, which is important for cross user-agent compliance with regard to assistive technologies. 3) Code that is uniform and valid is easier for accessibility concerns. 4) Your code will have a far longer lifespan without conflict to future changes in the specification.
