vegan package has a vegdist function that can calculate, among other things, the Jaccard index. Assuming that's what you're after. It's use is pretty straightforward. library(vegan) data(varespec) vare.dist Other available methods are method Dissimilarity index, partial match to "manhattan", "euclidean", "canberra", "bray", "kulczynski", "jaccard", "gower", "altGower", "morisita", "horn", "mountford", "raup" , "binomial" or "chao"
