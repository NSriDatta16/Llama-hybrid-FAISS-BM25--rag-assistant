On page 37, this document ( http://www.nohau.se/products/starteam/StarTeam-2008-Best-Practices.pdf ) recommends going to a previous view/configuration and then sharing the file with your tip view. You can share a file by having both views open in your graphical client, and Ctrl-Clicking the deleted file from your old view and dragging it to your tip view (it will prompt you about sharing the file).
