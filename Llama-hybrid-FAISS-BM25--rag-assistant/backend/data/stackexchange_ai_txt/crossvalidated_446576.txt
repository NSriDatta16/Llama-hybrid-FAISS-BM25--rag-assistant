You can use peaks and troughs (maximum and minimum) values to identify breaking points. This is used, for example, to identify business cycles (booms and recessions). Here judging by the x-axis label on the second figure, they decomposed the time series into a seasonal portion and trend. You can get the trendline for your time series and then identify the minimum as the breaking point. There are many ways to do it in R, for example, using "forecast": library(forecast) dependent
