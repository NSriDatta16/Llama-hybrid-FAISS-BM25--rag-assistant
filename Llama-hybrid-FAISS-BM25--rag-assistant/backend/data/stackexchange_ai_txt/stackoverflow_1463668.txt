It was 80% about storage capacity, pure and simple. People don't realize that the capacity of their laptop hard drive today would have cost millions in 1980. You think saving two bytes is silly? Not when you have a 100,000 customer records and a hard drive the size of a refrigerator held 20 megabytes and required a special room to keep cool.
