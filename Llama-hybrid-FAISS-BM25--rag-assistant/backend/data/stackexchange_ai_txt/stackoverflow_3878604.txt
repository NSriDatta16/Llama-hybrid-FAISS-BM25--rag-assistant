Here are a few services you could use: Amazon S3 / Amazon CloudFront Rackspace CloudFiles OpSource Cloud Files Box.net The terms you are looking for is "cloud storage" and optionally "content delivery network" or CDN for short. For example on Amazon, S3 is the cloud storage and CloudFront the CDN.
