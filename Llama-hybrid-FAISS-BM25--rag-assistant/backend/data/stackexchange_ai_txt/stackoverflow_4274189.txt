Of course you can compute the average of a list of numbers and the predicate would look like this: average(List, Result) :- length(List, Len), sum(List, Sum), Result is Sum / Len. sum([], 0). sum([H|T], Sum) :- sum(T, Temp), Sum is Temp + H. Then you get: ?- average([1, 2, 3], X). X = 2.
