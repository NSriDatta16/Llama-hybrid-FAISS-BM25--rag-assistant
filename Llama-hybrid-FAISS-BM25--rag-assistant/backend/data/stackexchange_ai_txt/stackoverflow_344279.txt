LINQ to the rescue: DataTable dt = WhateverCreatesDataTable(); DataRow dr = dt.Rows[0]; int sum = dt.Columns.Cast ().Sum(dc=>(int)dr[dc]); For those still dragging their knuckles in the stone ages (aka pre-.Net 3.5 and LINQ): DataTable dt = WhateverCreatesDataTable(); DataRow dr = dt.Rows[0]; int sum = 0; foreach(DataColumn dc in dt.Columns) sum += (int)dr[dc];
