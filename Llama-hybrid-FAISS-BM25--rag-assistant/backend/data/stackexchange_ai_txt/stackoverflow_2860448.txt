A List Apart has a great article on that topic: Flash Satay: Embedding Flash While Supporting Standards . Read that to understand the general difficulties with embedding flash and to read about different approaches to embed it in valid HTML. And then go and use SWFobject to embed Flash as that is the best way to do it while having both standards support and browser compiliance. In addition to that you may use the normal Flash Satay method to embed it for people without JavaScript.
