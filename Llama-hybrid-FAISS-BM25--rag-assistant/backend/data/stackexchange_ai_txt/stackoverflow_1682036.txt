There are a slew of tools out there: Cacti Ganglia Zabbix Hyperic Monit Reconnoiter Graphite Each focus on different aspects of usability. Reconnoiter and Graphite were born out of specialized needs and wanting greater resolution than RRD can provide. I suggest you do take another look at Cacti before building yourself. Create templates in cacti for your hosts and your graphs. Then use the built in CLI tools to automate. This way for each host, you do not need to click through the GUI.
