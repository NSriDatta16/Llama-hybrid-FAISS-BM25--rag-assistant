It would be more usual to compute some measure of the treatment effect and then use the other three moderator variables. You have the choice between odds ratios, risk ratios, risk differences, and so on. Note that for average age and percent female you are doing an ecological analysis. You are not looking at whether being a woman affects your outcome but on whether being enrolled in a study which has a high proportion of women affects your outcome an similarly for average age. Country is fine as that is identical for every patient in each trial.
