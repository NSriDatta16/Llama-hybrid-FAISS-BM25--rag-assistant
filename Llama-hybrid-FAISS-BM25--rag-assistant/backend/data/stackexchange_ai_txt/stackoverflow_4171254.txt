If you aren't doing resource-file-per-page, use App_GlobalResources instead. You can organize your files into a folder structure using this method. IIRC, your file names have to be unique in the whole tree , not just in each branch. I've had good success naming the files by site section , but YMMV of course. Here's an MSDN article that should help you: http://msdn.microsoft.com/en-us/library/ms227427.aspx
