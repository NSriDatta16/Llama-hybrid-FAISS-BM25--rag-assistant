In the view, not the window, override - [(void) mouseDown: (NSEvent *) theEvent] Then in the override: get your point this way: - (void) mouseDown: (NSEvent *) theEvent { myPoint = [self convertPoint: [theEvent locationInWindow] fromView: nil] } ...similar for mouseDragged, mouseUp, rightMouse, etc.
