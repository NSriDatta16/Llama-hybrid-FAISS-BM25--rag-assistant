It could be that the algorithm they use for encryption doesn't work well with large passwords or that they only have limited storage to store it. Both are very poor reasons, I know, but it's possible. If I were to make password rules, it would only be things to protect users, like forcing them to use at least one special character and number or mixing lower and upper case.
