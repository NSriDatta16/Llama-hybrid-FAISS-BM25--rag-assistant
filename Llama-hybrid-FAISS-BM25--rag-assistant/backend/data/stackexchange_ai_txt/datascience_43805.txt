You can now use Pandas DataFrames directly with XGBoost. Definitely works with xgboost 0.81. For example where X_train, X_val, y_train, and y_val are DataFrames: import xgboost as xgb mod = xgb.XGBRegressor( gamma=1, learning_rate=0.01, max_depth=3, n_estimators=10000, subsample=0.8, random_state=34 ) mod.fit(X_train, y_train) predictions = mod.predict(X_val) rmse = sqrt(mean_squared_error(y_val, predictions)) print("score: {0:,.0f}".format(rmse))
