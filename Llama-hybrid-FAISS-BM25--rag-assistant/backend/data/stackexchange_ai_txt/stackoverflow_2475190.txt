You need the uniqueness if you might need to integrate your code with third party software, or provide it to someone else for integration. If you don't follow the rules, you increase the risk that at some point you will have a class naming collision, and that you will need to rename lots of your classes to address it. Or worse still, that your customers will have to do the code renaming. This also applies when code is produces as part of different projects in an organization.
