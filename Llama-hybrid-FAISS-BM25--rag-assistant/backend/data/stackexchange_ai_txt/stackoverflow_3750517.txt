The Samsung Galaxy Tab has a 1024×600 resolution. You can easily Google resolutions for other devices that have been announced and create AVDs with matching specs. You might also want to read up on Supporting Multiple Screens . Configuration examples: 320dp: a typical phone screen (240x320 ldpi, 320x480 mdpi, 480x800 hdpi, etc). 480dp: a tweener tablet like the Streak (480x800 mdpi). 600dp: a 7” tablet (600x1024 mdpi). 720dp: a 10” tablet (720x1280 mdpi, 800x1280 mdpi, etc).
