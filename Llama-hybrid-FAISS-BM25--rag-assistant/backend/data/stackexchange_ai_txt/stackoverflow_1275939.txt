Is shadowImage nil? UIImage *shadowImage = [[UIImage imageNamed:@"drop_shadow_4_pix.png"] stretchableImageWithLeftCapWidth:4 topCapHeight:4]; That method could return nil if the base image is less than 5 pixels wide or 5 pixels tall since it needs the 4 pixels for the caps + 1 pixel to stretch.
