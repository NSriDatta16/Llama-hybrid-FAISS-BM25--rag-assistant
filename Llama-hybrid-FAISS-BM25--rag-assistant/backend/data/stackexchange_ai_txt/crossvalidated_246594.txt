How to choose between different forecasting methods

If we have 2 ways of predicting a variable y: Regression: y(t) = ax(t) + b + e; and a time series AR model: y(t) = c.y(t - 1) + d.y(t - 2) + e. And both give similar results in out of sample testing, which method should we choose?
