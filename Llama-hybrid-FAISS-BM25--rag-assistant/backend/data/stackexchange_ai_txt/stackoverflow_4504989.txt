If the site generates content with JavaScript, then you are out of luck. You need a full JavaScript engine usable in C# so that you can actually execute the scripts and capture the output they generate. Take a look at this question: Embedding JavaScript engine into .NET -- but know that it will take "serious" effort to do what you need.
