it looks like you're trying to use the jQueryUI framework. .draggable() is not natively jQuery --- the jQueryUI (plugin) adds that functionality in. jQueryUI Home Alternatively, you can also link directly to the jQueryUI instance in GoogleAPIs. But personally, I prefer to build my own download package from their home page, especially if all you need is .draggable().
