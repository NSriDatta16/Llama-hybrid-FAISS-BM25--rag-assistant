Using a navigation controller is a very common way of structuring this. And as you note, the nav bar itself can be hidden on some view controllers. Be careful about presenting view controllers modally -- Apple's advice in the HIG is to use modals when you are focusing the user's attention on a certain piece of information or a crucial task -- don't just use them willy-nilly.
