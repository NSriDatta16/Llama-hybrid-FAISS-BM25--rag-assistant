Comparing your formulas, we have \begin{align} J(h) & \approx \hat{J(h)} & \implies \\ \int\!\!\hat{f_n}(x)f(x)dx & \approx \tfrac{1}{n}\sum_i\hat{f}_{\!\!-i}(x_i) & \implies \\ \mathbb{E}\left[\hat{f_n}(x)\right] &\approx \overline{\hat{f}_{\!\!-i}(x_i)} \end{align} which says that the expected value of the full kernel estimate is approximately equal to the sample average of the "leave the evaluation point out" kernel estimate.
