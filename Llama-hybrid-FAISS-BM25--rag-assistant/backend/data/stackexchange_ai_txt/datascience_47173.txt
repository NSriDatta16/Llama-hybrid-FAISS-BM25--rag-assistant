Generally, the first step in modeling is merging all separate datasets into a single dataset. It looks like the data can be joined on order as a key. Then, sort the data by date. Next, visualize the data to see general trends and outliers. The prophet package can estimate forecasts for time series data. A quick-start notebook is here .
