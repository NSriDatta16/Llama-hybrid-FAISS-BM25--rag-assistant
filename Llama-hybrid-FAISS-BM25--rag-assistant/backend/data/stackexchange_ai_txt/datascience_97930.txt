The goal of language modeling is to build a statistical model of how language is used in a specific context. One of the important components of that is token frequency. Bias can mean many things in machine learning. I think you bias in the sense of high chance of prediction. That kind of bias is useful in language modeling. If appears frequently, a useful language model will capture that property.
