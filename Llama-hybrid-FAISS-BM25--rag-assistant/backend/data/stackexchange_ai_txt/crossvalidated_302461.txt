This strikes me as a good candidate for a multilevel model . From the first paragraph of the Wikipedia entry: Multilevel models are statistical models of parameters that vary at more than one level. An example could be a model of student performance that contains measures for individual students as well as measures for classrooms within which the students are grouped.
