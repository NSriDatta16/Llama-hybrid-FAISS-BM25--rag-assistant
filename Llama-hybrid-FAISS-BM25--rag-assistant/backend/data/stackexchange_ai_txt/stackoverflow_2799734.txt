The store is created by the addPersistentStoreWithType:configuration:URL:options:error: method. Use NSLog(@"store path: %@", storeUrl); to see the file path of the store in the applicationDocumentsDirectory . To download and browse the whole applicationDocumentsDirectory of your device, use the Xcode Organizer : choose your device, click on the disclosure triangle of your application and then on the arrow on the right of the application bundle.
