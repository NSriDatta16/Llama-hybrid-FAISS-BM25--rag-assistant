Here's a quick inelegant approach: DECLARE @MyInt INT = 5003 PRINT CAST(SUBSTRING(CAST(@MyInt AS VARCHAR(4)), 1, 1) + 1 AS CHAR(1)) + SUBSTRING(CAST(@MyInt AS VARCHAR(4)), 2, LEN(@MyInt) - 1) Convert to an update as required. Tested on SQL08.
