You can map to Calendar, Date, or XMLGregorianCalendar: http://bdoughan.blogspot.com/2011/01/jaxb-and-datetime-properties.html You can leverage the @XmlSchemaType annotation to choose the XML format: xsd:date, xsd:dateTime, xsd:time, etc.
