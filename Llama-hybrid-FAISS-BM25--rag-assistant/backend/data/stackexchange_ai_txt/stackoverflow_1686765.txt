You Regex may be falling over due to the ")" on item 3. Add a ) to the regex and it seems to go on fine: (\n|\r|\a|\f)(\s|\d|\.|\))*?(? (Client|Customer|Role|Organi(s|z)ation|Vendor|Company|Employer))(\s|\S)*?(?=(\n|\r|\a|\f)(\s|\d|\.)*?(\k'id'))*?
