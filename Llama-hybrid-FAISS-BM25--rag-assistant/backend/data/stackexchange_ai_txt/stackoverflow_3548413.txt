In Blend 4, on the 'Data' tab > New sample Data.. > name data source as you like, f.e. 'MySampleDataSource'. Then your 'MySampleDataSource' will have a '+' button (the same Data tab on the right) with 3 options. Choose 'Add simple property' and name it 'FirstName'.
