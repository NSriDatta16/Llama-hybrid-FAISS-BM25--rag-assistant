Not much. MP = message passing. Those algorythms are optimized for high parallel cluster systems (2000 computers working on the same thing), NOT on "in one process, small fragments many times per second". Naturally this only works efficiently if the prolblem requires significant calculation. Examples: 3d rendering for movies, where a machine may calculate a frame in some minutes, you need many tens of thousand frames calculated.
