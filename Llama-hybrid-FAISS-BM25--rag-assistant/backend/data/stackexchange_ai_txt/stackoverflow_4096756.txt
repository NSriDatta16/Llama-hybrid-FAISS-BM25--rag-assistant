You may look into compressed bitmaps. A common strategy is to use word-aligned run-length encoding. C++ implementation: https://github.com/lemire/EWAHBoolArray Java implementation: https://github.com/lemire/javaewah Reference: Daniel Lemire, Owen Kaser, Kamel Aouiche, Sorting improves word-aligned bitmap indexes. Data & Knowledge Engineering 69 (1), pages 3-28, 2010. http://arxiv.org/abs/0901.3751
