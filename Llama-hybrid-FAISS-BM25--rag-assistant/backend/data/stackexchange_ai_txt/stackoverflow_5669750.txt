One can use replacements to mess with the labels directly, bypassing Option / AbsoluteOptions : ListLogLogPlot[Range[20]^3, Frame -> True] /. (FrameTicks -> x_) :> (FrameTicks -> (x /. {a_?NumericQ, b_Integer, s___} :> {a, Superscript[10, Log10@b], s} )) Thanks to Alexey Popkov this is now improved and less fragile.
