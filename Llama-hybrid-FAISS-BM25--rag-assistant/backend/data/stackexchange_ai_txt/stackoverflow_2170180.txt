Difficult to tell exactly what you want from your question, but the following takes the average of the values of S4 rows (months) starting from D4 (the first month): AVERAGE(OFFSET(D4,0,0,S4,1)) If you want to copy that around, be careful with your absolute references ($) (e.g. you would want at least D$4).
