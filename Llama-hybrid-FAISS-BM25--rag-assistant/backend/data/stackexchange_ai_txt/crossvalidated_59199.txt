It's probably not quite what you have in mind, but Dan Navarro has written a textbook for his own undergraduate statistics subject in psychology. I have read it all, but it seems to use the approach you mention on occasion. It uses a fair bit of R and generally seems to be more sophisticated than your average intro psych stats textbook. The PDF of the chapters are available online for free. Daniel Navarro: Learning Statistics with R
