It looks like that changed. The vendors I see support for are MSXML, Xerces, and ADOM v4. You can still use Open XML; just create the XML document yourself instead of dragging TXMLDocument on to a form. I use OmniXML for most of the stuff I do, and it works fine in D2007, D2009, and D2010.
