Write an InputFormat that reads PCAP files, returning something like LongWritable for the key (the nth packet in the file) and PacketWritable as the value (containing the PCAP data). For the InputSplit you can use FileSplit, or MultiFileSplit for better performance, as an individual PCAP file can be read surprisingly quickly. Unless your blocksize is larger than the size of your pcap files, you will experience lots of network IO...
