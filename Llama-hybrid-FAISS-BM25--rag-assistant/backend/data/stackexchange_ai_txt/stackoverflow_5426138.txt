As far as I know, # is not allowed in a fragment identifier. You could encode it as %23 and do something with that maybe. If you're doing on-page stuff that you would like to be able to scroll to, you could just hijack links by adding a certain class to them or something. Also, consider reading up on history.pushState and such on: https://developer.mozilla.org/en/DOM/Manipulating_the_browser_history
