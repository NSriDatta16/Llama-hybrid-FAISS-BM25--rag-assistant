Look at gwt-dnd, which has great support for dragging & dropping GWT widgets. You'll have to save the position of the widgets if you want them to stay in the same place, but that's all! http://allen-sauer.com/com.allen_sauer.gwt.dnd.demo.DragDropDemo/DragDropDemo.html#InsertPanelExample - drag the green boxes around.
