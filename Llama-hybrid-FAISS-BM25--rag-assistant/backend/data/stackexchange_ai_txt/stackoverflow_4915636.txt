The problem is that the system misses the library you promised it, in your case the C ancelButtonPlugin.framework one. Luckily, this is easy to solve, go to Xcode, and add a Copy Files build phase to your project. Destination is Frameworks and then drag'n'drop all your third party Frameworks into it. Recompile everything and you are done.
