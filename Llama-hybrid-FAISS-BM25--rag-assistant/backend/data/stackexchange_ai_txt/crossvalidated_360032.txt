There are actually a few generalizations of PCA to higher-order tensors: The Tucker decomposition used in " higher-order singular value decomposition ". PARAFAC aka CANDECOMP , is in some ways a special case of Tucker decompositions. Another, more recent variant uses the tensor train decomposition .
