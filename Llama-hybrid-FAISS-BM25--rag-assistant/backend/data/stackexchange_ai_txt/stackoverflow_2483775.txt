There is one elegant way to run IE6, IE7 and IE8 on the same machine, called virtual PC. First download virtual PC from Microsoft website here: http://www.microsoft.com/downloadS/details.aspx?FamilyID=04d26402-3199-48a3-afa2-2dc0b40a73b6&displaylang=en Then download 3 EXE files with IE6, IE7 and IE8 here: http://www.microsoft.com/downloads/details.aspx?FamilyId=21EABB90-958F-4B64-B5F1-73D0A413C8EF&displaylang=en Install them on your PC and test your web applications. Saved me days of looking for similar solutions.
