Ignore everybody telling you to use elements or Pragma . They are very unreliable. You need to set the appropriate HTTP headers. A good tutorial on how to decide which HTTP headers are appropriate for you is available here . Cache-Control: no-cache is probably all you need, but read the tutorial as there are many project-specific reasons why you might want something different.
