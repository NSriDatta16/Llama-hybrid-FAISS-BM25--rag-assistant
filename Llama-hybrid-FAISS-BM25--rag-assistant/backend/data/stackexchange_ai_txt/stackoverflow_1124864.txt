I'd second the reservations of the previous posters regarding MDX in the context of WPF. One shot into the dark though: Have you tried targeting your control for .NET Framework 3.5 SP1 already? There has been significant work regarding DirectX/Direct3D interoperability and performance, see for example: Paragraph 'Graphics Enhancements' in What's New in .NET Framework 3.5 Service Pack 1 Paragraph 'WPF Interoperability with Direct3D' in What's New in .NET Framework 3.5 Service Pack 1 Some of those enhancements might eventually yield positive side effects regarding your problem.
