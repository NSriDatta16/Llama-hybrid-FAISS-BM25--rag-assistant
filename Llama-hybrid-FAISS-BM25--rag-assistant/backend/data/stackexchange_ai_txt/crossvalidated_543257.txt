With only three years for each occupation, I would start out with visualization. Make a lineplot with 250 lines, years along the x-axis. Plotting ratios directly, that will confound levels with slope, but if there are groups of occupations with rather different slopes, that should stand More formally, you could try some kind of regression clustering , see for instance linear regression inappropriate when there are multiple underlying groups , but with only three points per line maybe a bayesian variant ...
