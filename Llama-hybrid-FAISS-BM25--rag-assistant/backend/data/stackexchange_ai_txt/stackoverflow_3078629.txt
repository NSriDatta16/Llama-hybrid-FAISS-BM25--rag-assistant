The commands are pull (to fetch the remote changes), and update to update your working directory to the most recent changeset. Since you don't seem very familiar with the way Mercurial works, it would be a very good idea to read some introductory material like: http://mercurial.aragost.com/kick-start/ http://hginit.com/ https://www.mercurial-scm.org/quickstart http://hgbook.red-bean.com/read/a-tour-of-mercurial-the-basics.html
