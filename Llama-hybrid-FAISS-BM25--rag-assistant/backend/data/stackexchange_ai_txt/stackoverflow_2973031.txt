The correct method for overriding a controller is specified here : http://www.magentocommerce.com/boards/viewthread/16224/ The method of copying files from the core to local is not good. Doing so in this way means that you can only be sure that your custom module will work with the version of Magento that YOU are using. If you want the best chance of your module being compatible with other versions, it needs to be loosely coupled - extending and overriding the functions necessary for your purposes
