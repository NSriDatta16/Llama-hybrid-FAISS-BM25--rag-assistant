Simple average works best. It's been shown empirically, but you can see it yourself. This phenomenon even has a name: forecast combination puzzle . All the weight optimization schemes are a waste of time. Here is one recent paper explaining this puzzle: Gerda Claeskens, Jan R. Magnus, Andrey L. Vasnev, Wendun Wang, The forecast combination puzzle: A simple theoretical explanation, In International Journal of Forecasting, Volume 32, Issue 3, 2016, Pages 754-762, ISSN 0169-2070,
