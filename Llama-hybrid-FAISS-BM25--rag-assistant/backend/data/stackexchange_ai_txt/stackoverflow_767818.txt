Assuming your code looks like . The alt attribute is to be used when something can't be displayed. "For user agents that cannot display images, forms, or applets, this attribute specifies alternate text." W3C HTML 4.01 Use the title attribute to get your desired result on IE/Firefox/Chrome . "Values of the title attribute may be rendered by user agents in a variety of ways. For instance, visual browsers frequently display the title as a "tool tip"..." WC3 HTML 4.01 For even more info read http://www.456bereastreet.com/archive/200412/the_alt_and_title_attributes/
