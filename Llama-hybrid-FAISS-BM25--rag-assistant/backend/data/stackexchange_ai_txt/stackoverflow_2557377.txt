Another option is to use data brush . Click brush icon on the figure toolbar and make a selection. Then select in menu Tools-Brushing-Create new variable. You can leave default variable name ans . This variable will contain X and Y coordinates of all selected points. Then just run mean(ans(:,2)) to get average of Ys.
