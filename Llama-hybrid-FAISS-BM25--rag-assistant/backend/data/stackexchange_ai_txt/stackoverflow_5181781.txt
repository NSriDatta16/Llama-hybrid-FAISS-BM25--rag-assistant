You can limit number of returned columns by using Projection and Transformers.AliasToBean and DTO here how it looks in Criteria API: .SetProjection(Projections.ProjectionList() .Add(Projections.Property("Id"), "Id") .Add(Projections.Property("PackageName"), "Caption")) .SetResultTransformer(Transformers.AliasToBean(typeof(PackageNameDTO)));
