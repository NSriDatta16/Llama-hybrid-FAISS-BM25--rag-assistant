Favor simplicity... Properness will be needed when you are writing a specification, a distributed system, and one that will be used in many different circumstances outside of your control. Being Pragmatic , it is oftentimes better to use the "power of the language" and just write code to handle each case. Don't abstract unless you need to . Keep it simple. Keep it quick. Keep it clean. You do not know what it may need to evolve into (features or performance), and the more abstraction you have, the harder it will be to re-factor.
