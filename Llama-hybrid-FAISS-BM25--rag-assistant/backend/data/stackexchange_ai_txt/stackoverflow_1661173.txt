This should work: NSDictionary *headerFields = [(NSHTTPURLResponse*)response allHeaderFields]; NSURL *url = [NSURL URLWithString:@"http://www.mywebserver.com/login.php"]; NSArray *cookies = [NSHTTPCookie cookiesWithResponseHeaderFields:headerFields forURL:url]; Then you can know if the cookies array contains the cookie you want. You could also call this after receiving the response: NSArray *cookies = [[NSHTTPCookieStorage sharedHTTPCookieStorage] cookiesForURL:url];
