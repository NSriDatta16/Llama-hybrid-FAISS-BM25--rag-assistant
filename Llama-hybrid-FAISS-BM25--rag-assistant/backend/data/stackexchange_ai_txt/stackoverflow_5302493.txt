You can have a "standard settings" and can import this settings on settings.py of your project, how: On your standard settings: # settings_standard.py TEMPLATE_CONTEXT_PROCESSORS = ( 'context_one', 'context_two' ) And on your project settings: # project settings.py import settings_standard.py TEMPLATE_CONTEXT_PROCESSORS += ( 'context_three', 'context_four' ) Attention for += on second TEMPLATE_CONTEXT_PROCESSORS. :)
