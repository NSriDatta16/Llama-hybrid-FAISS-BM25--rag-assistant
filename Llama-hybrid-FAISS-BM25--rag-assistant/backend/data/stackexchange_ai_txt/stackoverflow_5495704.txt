Also consider that free versions of your app (wherever they are) can point to the paid version on a select few markets (or just the main market) to consolidate your sales rather than fragment them over a bunch of markets you'll never meet the minimum for. My paid app is on SlideMe, but I won't reach the payout threshold for a while so I can't comment on it yet.
