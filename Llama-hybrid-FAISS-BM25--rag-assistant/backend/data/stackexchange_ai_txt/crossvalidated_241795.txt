"Regression" versus "regression to the mean"

Are The two concepts really two sides of the same coin ? The latter is often referred to simply as a regression, but surely this is just an unfortunate coincidence? The former is about predicting values of a dependent variable from a weighted combination of values of independent variables , whereas the second is Simply the observation that, with time, the average of all so far observed values of a random variable will tend to approach its true mean.
