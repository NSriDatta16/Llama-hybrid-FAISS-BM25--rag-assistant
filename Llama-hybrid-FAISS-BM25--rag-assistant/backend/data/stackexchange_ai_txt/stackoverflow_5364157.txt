Try this: transform(D8, PL8.lag = c(PL8[-1], NA)) It would be a bit easier if you used a time series class. In that case you could use lag : library(zoo) z In the other direction we would have: transform(D8, PL8.lag = c(NA, head(PL8, -1))) and lag(z, 0:-1)
