If you want to determine the relevance of any individual teacher you should look at the Chi-Squared test for independence . This will tell you likelihood of your questions being statistically significantly related to your outcomes. In terms of looking at group effects there are lots of techniques to do this. The simplest (in my opinion) to understand is the decision tree . There are lots of other models like logistic regression that also model this problem.
