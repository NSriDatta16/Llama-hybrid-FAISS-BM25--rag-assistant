One approach could be to regress a contineous value, and apply a rounding afterwards. Then, you are looking for a neural network that maps an input to a value y >=0. There are many activation functions which you could use, for example linear and quadratic functions. According to the Universal Approximation Theorem, you should use non-linear activation functions.
