This may not be the most pragmatic solution, but it is a solution. Use a Bayesian comparison. library(tidyverse) library(brms) library(tidybayes) nyapples % recover_types(apples) %>% spread_draws(b_Intercept,b_placeNY) %>% mutate(ratio = (b_placeNY+b_Intercept)/b_Intercept) Here is a KDE of the resulting distribution of ratio The model estimates that the price of New York apples exceeds the price of LA apples by least 20% with probability ~62%.
