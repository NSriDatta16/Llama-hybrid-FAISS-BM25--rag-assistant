Variance of bivariate normal distribution plus normal distribution

Problem: $W = -27 + 0.3X + 0.45Y + E$ The pair $\begin{bmatrix} X \\ Y \end{bmatrix}$ behaves like a bivariate normal with vector of averages $\begin{bmatrix} 156 \\ 86 \end{bmatrix}$ and covariance matrix $\begin{bmatrix} 16 & 23 \\ 23 & 164 \\ \end{bmatrix}$ and $E \sim N(0, 3^2)$ . What is the variance of W? My Answer: $var(W) = 0.3^2.var(x) + 0.45^2.var(y) + (2).(0.3).(0.45).cov(X,Y) + var(E)$ $var(W) = (0.3^2)(16) + (0.45^2)(164) + (2)(0.3)(0.45)(23) + 3^2$ $var(W) = 49.86$ Question: Why textbook says $var(W) = 89.4916$ ?
