I think all you need to do is call: [[NSHTTPCookieStorage sharedHTTPCookieStorage] setCookieAcceptPolicy:NSHTTPCookieAcceptPolicyAlways]; and, assuming you're using NSURLConnection , it will save any cookies the server sets for you, and regular cookie-based authentication will just work.
