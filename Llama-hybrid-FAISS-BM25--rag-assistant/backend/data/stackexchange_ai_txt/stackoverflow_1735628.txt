Neural nets are treated as objects in MATLAB. To access any parameter before (or after) training, you need to access the network's properties using the . operator. In addition to mtrw's and Amro's answers, make MATLAB's Neural Network Toolbox documentation your new best friend. It will usually explain things in much better detail.
