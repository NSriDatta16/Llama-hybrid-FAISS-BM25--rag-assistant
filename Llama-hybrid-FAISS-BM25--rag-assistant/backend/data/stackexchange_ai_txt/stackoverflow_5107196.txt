If you really want to choose background images based on resolution, you could combine density (-hdpi, -mdpi...) and screen size (-small, -large...) qualifiers, but it won't give you much precision, as they are not standardized, especially screen size qualifiers. See Resource directory qualifiers for screen size and density .
