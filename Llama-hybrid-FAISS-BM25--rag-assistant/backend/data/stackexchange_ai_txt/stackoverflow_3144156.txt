What you're running into is the Same origin policy , not firewall issues. If the machines share a top-level domain, you can set their document.domain properties to match : Two sites sharing a common top-level domain may opt to communicate despite failing the "same host" check by mutually setting their respective document.domain DOM property to the same qualified, right-hand fragment of their current host name. Otherwise, you may need to setup your first host to proxy the request to the other host .
