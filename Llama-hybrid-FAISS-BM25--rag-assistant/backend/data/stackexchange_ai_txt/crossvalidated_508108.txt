This is an interesting paper one may want to read before doing model averaging: On Model Averaging Regression Coefficients . Even though it is counter intuitive to average the coefficients as the interpretation is no longer Effect of X1 on Y in presence of X2, X3, ...., there are some ongoing investigations on how and when to do averaging of coefficients.
