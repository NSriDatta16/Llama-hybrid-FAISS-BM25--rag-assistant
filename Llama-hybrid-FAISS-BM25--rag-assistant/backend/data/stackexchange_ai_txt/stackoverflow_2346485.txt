To answer your follow up comment - check the values you have got set in your binding configuration on the server side. Pay attention to attributes like maxBufferSize and maxReceivedMessageSize on the binding, and attributes like maxStringContentLength and maxBytesPerRead on the readerQuotas node of the binding.
