As Joey suggested, the solution is to use Word's built in styles. I resolved this by applying the following to my Word._Application object: oWord.ActiveWindow.Selection.ParagraphFormat.LineSpacingRule = Word.WdLineSpacing.wdLineSpaceSingle; oWord.ActiveWindow.Selection.ParagraphFormat.SpaceAfter = 0.0F;
