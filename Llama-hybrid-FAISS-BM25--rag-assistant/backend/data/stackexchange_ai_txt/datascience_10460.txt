Firstly, cohorts are used mostly for descriptive and inferential analytics, rather than for predictive analytics. Upon a close look at your dataset, I see a clear seasonal periodicity and there might be trends which can be observed. So, my advise would be to go with the time series analysis (Start with the basic seasonal naive ( snaive in R) algorithm). The snaive line would give you a nice idea whether to go forward with time series or whether to use a simpler estimation like the regression approach.
