It is very project dependent. If you know the exact specs of your page with no chance of major modifications, then check IE often (all other browser fixes are usually minor) If you re unsure about the final outcome of the project, then it s a waste of time fixing every inconsistency that IE has if you`re planning to make revisions. In general however, it is wise to check browser compatibility at any large point in a project, such as installing an applet, embedding a video, flash or iframe.
