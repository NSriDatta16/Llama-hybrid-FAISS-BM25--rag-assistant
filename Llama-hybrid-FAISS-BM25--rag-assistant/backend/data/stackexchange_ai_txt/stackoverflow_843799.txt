You need to inject your own code into the process that owns the windows you wish to intercept messages from. Fortunately, SetWindowsHookEx() makes this fairly easy, although you may have a bit of trouble at first if you've only used it for in-process hooking up to now. I can recommend two excellent articles on the subject: Joseph Newcomber's Hooks and DLLs Robert Kuster's Three Ways to Inject Your Code into Another Process
