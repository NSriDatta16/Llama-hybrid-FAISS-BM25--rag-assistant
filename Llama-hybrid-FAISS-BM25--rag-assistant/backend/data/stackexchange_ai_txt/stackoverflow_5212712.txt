One pattern you can consider is the Service Locator Pattern. An explanation of Service Locator can be found here . Basically, a service locator is a registry + cache combined to find the resource once and keep it in memory for object retrieval during the lifecycle of the application. Service Locator is mainly implemented using the Singleton pattern .
