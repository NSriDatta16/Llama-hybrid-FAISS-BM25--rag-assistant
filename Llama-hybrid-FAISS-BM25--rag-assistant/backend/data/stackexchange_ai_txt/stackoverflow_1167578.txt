App_Data is safe because by default you can't download file from there by typing in a URL to your browser. IsolatedStorage is good if you need to isolate users from each other. IsolatedStorage requires less collaboration with your IIS admin when it comes to granting NTFS rights to folders outside of your virtual directory. Blobs in the database are possibilities, too but the extra effort involved with blobs in databases makes it a last resort choice.
