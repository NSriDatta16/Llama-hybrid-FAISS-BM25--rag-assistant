Most likely what you want is to sub-divide the plane with a space-filling-curve like a z-curve or a hilbert-curve and thus reducing the complexity of a 2D problem to a 1D problem. Look for quadtree. Link: http://dmytry.com/texts/collision_detection_using_z_order_curve_aka_Morton_order.html
