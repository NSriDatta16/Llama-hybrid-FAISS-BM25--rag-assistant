So, you want a report that groups by Group and counts the Students in each Group? Insert a group in Crystal Reports on group_name. Drag the student_id field to the Details section. While the field is selected, right-click it and select Insert | Summary. Choose Count, then Group #1. Right click in the margin to the left of the Details section and choose Hide. Run the report.
