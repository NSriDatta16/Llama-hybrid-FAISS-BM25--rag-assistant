I think the F# MailboxProcessor is what you're looking for Alexey. Using the MailboxProcessor you can define tens of thousands of agents within one .NET process much like you can spawn tens of thousands of lightweight processes in Erlang. This MSDN post by Don Syme is a great introduction. If you're coming to .NET from an Erlang background keep in mind you're going to be missing lots of OTP goodies (supervisors, location transparency, mnesia, ...).
