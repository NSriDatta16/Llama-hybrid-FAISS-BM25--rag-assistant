Building on @honk's example, i think you'd want this: \documentclass{article} \begin{document} \begin{table} \centering \begin{tabular}{>{\raggedright}l|>{\raggedright}p{2cm}|>{\raggedright}p{2cm}|} \multicolumn{1}{c}{A} & \multicolumn{1}{c}{B} & \multicolumn{1}{c}{C} \\ 1 & Some longer text & Some other text\\ 2 & Some longer text & Some other text\\ 3 & Some longer text & Some other text\\ 4 & Some longer text & Some other text \\ \end{tabular} \end{table} \end{document} Words still won't be hyphenated, but this should solve the blank line problem you're having.
