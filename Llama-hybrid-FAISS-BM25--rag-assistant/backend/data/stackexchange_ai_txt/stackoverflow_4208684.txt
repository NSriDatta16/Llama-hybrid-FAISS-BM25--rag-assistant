You can't force it. And the crash logs are not uploaded to Apple by the device. They are uploaded by iTunes when the user syncs the device. You know that you can grab the crash logs directly from the device by using Xcode? Connect your device and open the Organizer window in Xcode. It will show all crash logs available on the device.
