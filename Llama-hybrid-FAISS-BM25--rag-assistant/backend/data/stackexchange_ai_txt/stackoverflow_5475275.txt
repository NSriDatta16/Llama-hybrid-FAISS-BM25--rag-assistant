If you're using VS 2010 - you can take a look at SparkSense . It's also available within Visual Studio's Extension Manager for an easy install. It has its limitations - but you can try it out and see if you like it. You may want to check out Robert Greyling's blog post about SparkSense functionality here: http://blog.robertgreyling.com/2010/08/sparksense-gets-its-first-outing.html
