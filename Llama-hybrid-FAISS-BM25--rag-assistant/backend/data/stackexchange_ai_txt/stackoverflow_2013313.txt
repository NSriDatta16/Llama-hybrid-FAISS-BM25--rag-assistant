Whether it's an unwanted copy or not is for you to decide. The reason to synthesize accessors with the copy qualifier is to be sure of the ownership of those objects. Keep in mind though, that immutable objects like an NSNumber or an NSString won't actually duplicate their storage when they're sent a -copy message, they'll just increase their retain counts.
