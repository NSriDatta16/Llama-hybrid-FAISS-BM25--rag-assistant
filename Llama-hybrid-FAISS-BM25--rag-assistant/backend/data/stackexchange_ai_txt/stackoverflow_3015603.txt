Please check: if the font outline isn't erroneous. There are many fonts the metrics of which is utterly messed up. Try another font and see if it works; if you're dealing with the embedded font. If so, ensure that the characters that are missing are actually embedded. Over the years, Flash has had countless issues with font embedding and text layout engine in general. I've described one of such matters in Embedding fonts in Flash CS3 .
