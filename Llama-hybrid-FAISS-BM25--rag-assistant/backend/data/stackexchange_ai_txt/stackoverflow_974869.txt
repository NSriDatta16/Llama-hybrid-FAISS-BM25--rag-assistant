What you're trying to do is fragile and difficult to maintain. You should attempt to create a csv feed to fetch the data. All it takes is for someone to come along and modify the HTML and your scraper will throw up on it (probably years after anyone remembers how your program works). Try to get CSV and image data from the original source (ie, database or whatever) and build the Excel file from that.
