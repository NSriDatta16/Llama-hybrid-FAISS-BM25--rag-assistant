You could eliminate the keyboard entirely by just adding support for a left/right swipe or drag gesture so the user can essentially treat the app like a photo gallery. No onscreen controls are needed-- it's a device-native navigation paradigm, easy to explain on an app startup splash screen. If an external keyboard is required in order to support other app data-gathering functionality however, users will experience the "gorilla arm" problem...
