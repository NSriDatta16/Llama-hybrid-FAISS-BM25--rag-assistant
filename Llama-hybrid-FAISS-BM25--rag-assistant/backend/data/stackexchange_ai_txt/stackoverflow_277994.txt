The answers by @Bill the Lizard and @Robert Gamble are correct for the question as asked, but I do wonder if you're asking what you think you are... If you're looking for overlapping events then you need to take into account events longer than your search range. Monday Tuesday Wednesday Thursday Search: |-----------| Shopping |-----| Found OK Eating |--------| Found OK Stack Overflow |---------------------------------| Not found! If you wanted to include SO, you'd do: SELECT * FROM table WHERE (start_date start_of_range)
