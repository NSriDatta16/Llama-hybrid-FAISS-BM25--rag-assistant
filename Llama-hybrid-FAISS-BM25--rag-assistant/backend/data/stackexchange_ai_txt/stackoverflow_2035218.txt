This seems to be first implemented here: http://www.thomasfrank.se/sessionvars.html . However, if this technique takes off, I would bet money that the browsers would eventually crack down on it due to its inherit vulnerability to XSS. A more long term solution is likely to be: http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/DOM_storage which grew out of the HTML 5 specification (the biggest drawback being the lack of backwards compatibility for browsers before IE 8).
