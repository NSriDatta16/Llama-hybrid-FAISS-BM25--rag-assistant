You could use an anchor tag by the paragraph you want to be visible, whenever the situation arises where you want to be sure that this section is visible you can link to the anchor through javascript. Example follows Create an anchor by the desired paragraph The javascript to move to your anchor function moveToAnchor () { window.location.hash="FOO"; } What remains is to call this script when you desire. you could do it with an onload in the body tag or through some other means you feel appropriate
