Apart from progressive enhancement as already discussed (don't make your site require JavaScript until it really has to), a with onsubmit would be necessary to reliably catch an Enter keypress submission. (Sure, you can try trapping keypresses on separate form fields, but it's fiddly, fragile and will never 100% reproduce the browser's native behaviour over what constitutes a form submission.)
