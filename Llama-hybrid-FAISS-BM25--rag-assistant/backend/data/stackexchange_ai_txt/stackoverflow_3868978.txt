The main difference is that the Visual Studio agent licencing has changed. 2008 has a per cpu licence and 2010 is licences per 1000 virtual users. This means that 2010 is significantly more flexible than 2008 for load testing with multiple machines. As you are licenced for the Test Edition of 2008, you may have to pay more (double?) to get a licence for 2010 Ultimate , other than that there is no reason to use VS2008.
