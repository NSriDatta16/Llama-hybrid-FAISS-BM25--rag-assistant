stretchableImageWithLeftCapWidth:topCapHeight: returns a new UIImage. You need to do something like UIImage *image = [UIImage imageNamed:@"balloon_2.png"]; UIImage *strImage = [image stretchableImageWithLeftCapWidth:15 topCapHeight:13]; cell.image = strImage; ... or just assign the result of the message to cell.image right away. See also: UIImage reference
