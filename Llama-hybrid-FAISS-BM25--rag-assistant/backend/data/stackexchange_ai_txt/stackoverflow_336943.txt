The rel attribute isn't typically used by many UAs (user agents) however it specifies what relation the linked page is to the current page. Certain pseudo-standards have popped up around the place for example Mozilla uses the prefetch relation to preload pages. Google sets [used to set?] its first few results to prefetch in this way so those pages are supposed to load quicker.
