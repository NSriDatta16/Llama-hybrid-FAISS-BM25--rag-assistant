These headers will force the browser, and proxies if any, not to cache the page and force a new request to the server for that page: header("Cache-Control: private, must-revalidate, max-age=0"); header("Pragma: no-cache"); header("Expires: Sat, 26 Jul 1997 05:00:00 GMT"); // A date in the past
