I do not believe classic SVM is the right tool in the situation simply because you have no negatives. I'd try to use one of the following: Semi-supervised approach: if you data can be clustered well enough, then you can determine which of the clusters corresponds to the positive examples using your data and use this as your classifier Use 1-class SVM
