It depends on how far you're looking to take it. You can use GDI+ and mix in some unmanaged code into your C# to get a better hold on the graphics capabilities. Learn to use the Bitmap class and pay close attention to your references (I've had numerous encounters with dangling references to bitmaps.) If you're looking for high-performance graphics in .NET check out Managed DirectX .
