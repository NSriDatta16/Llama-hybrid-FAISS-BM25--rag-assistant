To answer your second question... To create a formula field in CR 8.5: Insert Fields->Formula tab->New in CR 10 and up: go to the "Field Explorer", right click "Formula Fields", click "New". or Report->Formula Workshop->Formula Fields->New Then you can drag the formula field onto the report.
