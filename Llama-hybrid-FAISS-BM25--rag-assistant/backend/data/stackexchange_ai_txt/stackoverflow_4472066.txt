First of all, you should not empty the tab page collection, just remove the tab you are dragging and insert it at the next position. Second, while you remove and insert the tabpage, you can suspend the drawing of the TabControl using a little bit of interop, like described in the answer here , by calling SuspendDrawing on your tabcontrol before removing the tabpage and ResumeDrawing after you insert the tabpage at the new position. This should produce a flicker free tab reordering.
