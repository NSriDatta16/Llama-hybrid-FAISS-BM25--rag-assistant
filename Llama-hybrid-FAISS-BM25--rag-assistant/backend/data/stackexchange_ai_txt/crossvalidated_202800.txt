Transfer Functions can be very useful in analyzing this kind of time series data. Take a look at http://www.autobox.com/cms/index.php/news/48-autobox-software-review-in-orms , particularly the material on the furnace data analysis (p68). Process control is a very important application of time series causal analytics and this review is very informative in this regard. For a general introduction to transfer function analysis, see https://web.archive.org/web/20151025004937/https://onlinecourses.science.psu.edu/stat510/node/75 .
