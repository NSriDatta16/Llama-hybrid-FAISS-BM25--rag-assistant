In your ResolverFund class, 'EmployeeCode' and 'OrganId' are not nullable - they are just normal ints. To make them nullable that need to be defined as public int? EmployeeCode; public int? OrganId; or public Nullable EmployeeCode; public Nullable OrganId; Once you have done that you must refresh your web references from your other project, then it should work
