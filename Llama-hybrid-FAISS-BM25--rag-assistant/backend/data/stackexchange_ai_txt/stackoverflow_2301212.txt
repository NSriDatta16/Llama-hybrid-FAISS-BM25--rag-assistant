* System storage limits may preclude the limits specified here. * A limit of storage means that the limit is dependent on the amount of storage available. * A limit of statement means that the limit is dependent on the limit for the maximum length of a statement. But if you want theoretical maximums then the following data maybe useful for you Maximum number of rows in a non-partitioned table 4294967288 Maximum number of rows in a data partition 4294967288 You can check out IBM's reference page .
