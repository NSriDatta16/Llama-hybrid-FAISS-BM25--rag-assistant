I'm fairly sure this is a classic case of multiple membership, and there are models that have been proposed to account for things like this: https://arxiv.org/abs/1907.04148#:~:text=Multiple%20membership%20multilevel%20models%20are,that%20have%20multiple%20membership%20structures . A popular package for Bayesian models in R called brms has a helper function for setting up these kinds of models: http://paul-buerkner.github.io/brms/reference/mm.html
