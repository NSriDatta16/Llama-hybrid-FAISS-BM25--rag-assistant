Try SNNS . It is a great software package for simulating neural networks. A JNNS version of the toolsuite is also available. It is called JavaNNS . I've used the SNNS and JavaNNS for neural networks simulations and it was very useful. It has a steep learning curve, but it's certainly worth trying!
