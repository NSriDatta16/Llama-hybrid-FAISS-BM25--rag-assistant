The setting is stored in: site.RootWeb.AllProperties["SRCH_TRAGET_RESULTS_PAGE"] If doesn't exist or is null then '/_layouts/osssearchresults.aspx' is used The Search Center URL is in site.RootWeb.AllProperties["SRCH_ENH_FTR_URL"] And the drop down mode in site.RootWeb.AllProperties["SRCH_SITE_DROPDOWN_MODE"]
