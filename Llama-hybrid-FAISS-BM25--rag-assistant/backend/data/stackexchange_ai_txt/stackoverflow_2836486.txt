you define you layout and put it under res/layout-Qualifier/my_layout.xml where Qualifier could be one or more of the following size : small, normal, large density: ldpi,mdpi, hdpi. For example layout for large screen with high density would be res/layout-hdpi-large/my_layout.xml For full list of attributes see answer above
