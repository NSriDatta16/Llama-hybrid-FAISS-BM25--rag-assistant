There are three local storage mechanisms related URL requests that have an effect to how authentication challenges are handled: (obviously) NSHTTPCookieStorage , NSURLCredentialStorage and NSURLCache . NSURLCredentialStorage is probably the next place you should be looking at, but clearing all the caches is generally considered the surest way to have the effect that you want. This snippet is handy: https://gist.github.com/559071 (clears all the caches).
