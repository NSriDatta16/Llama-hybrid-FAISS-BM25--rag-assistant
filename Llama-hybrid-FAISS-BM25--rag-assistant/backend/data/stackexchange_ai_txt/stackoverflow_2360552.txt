For those trying to reposition the dropped item. Take a look here. Jquery drag /drop and clone . I actually had to use code that looks like $(item).css('position', 'absolute'); $(item).css('top', ui.position.top - $(this).position().top); $(item).css('left', ui.position.left - $(this).position().left); to do it.
