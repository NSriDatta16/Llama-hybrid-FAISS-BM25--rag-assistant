If you want to see some more advanced AIM implementations, you can check out any of the major PHP-based e-commerce systems, like Magento, osCommerce, or zencart. They all support AIM out of the box, and can be useful. Of course, as theIV said, Authorize.net's developer guides are pretty good, too.
