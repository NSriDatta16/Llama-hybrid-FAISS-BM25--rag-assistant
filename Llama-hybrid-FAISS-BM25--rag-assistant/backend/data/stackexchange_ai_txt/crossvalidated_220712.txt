I understand the question is, what is the number of coefficients of a $k$-dimensional VAR($p$) model? The number of coefficients in a VAR($p$) model for $k$ time series is $k+pk^2$. $k$ is the number of intercepts; each lag $p$ adds a $k \times k$ matrix of coefficients.
