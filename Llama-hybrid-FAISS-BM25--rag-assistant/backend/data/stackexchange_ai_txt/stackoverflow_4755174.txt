I am a Vmware ESX user, I have 30 production VM's with the largest being 232GB. I backup my VM instances onto an internal SATA drive and then copy these off once a week to an external eSata. I use teracopy (free), it runs on average at 45MB/s on an XP machine with 3GB. Hope that helps Sailen
