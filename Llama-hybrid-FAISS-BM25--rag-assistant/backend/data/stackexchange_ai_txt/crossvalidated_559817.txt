In your question there is an implicit assumption about the regressor being linear. In case it is linear your assertion is correct. But for the case of non linear regressor you may think on the dimensionality reduction step as a feature extraction. In that case it has a very important role in order to get good results. It might reduce the noise, it might assist with the learning, etc...
