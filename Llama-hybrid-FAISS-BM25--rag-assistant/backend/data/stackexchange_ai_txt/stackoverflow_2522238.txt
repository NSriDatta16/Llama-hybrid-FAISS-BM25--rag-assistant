Nokogiri supports some options for handling bad XML. These might help: http://rubyforge.org/pipermail/nokogiri-talk/2009-February/000066.html http://nokogiri.org/tutorials/ensuring_well_formed_markup.html I just messed around with the broken fragment and Nokogiri handles it very nicely: #!/usr/bin/ruby require 'rubygems' require 'nokogiri' doc = Nokogiri::XML(' Article 1 ') doc.to_s # => " \n Article 1 \n" doc.errors # => [#
