The answer is simple: change your application logic. GPS is not perfect. Signal attenuation and reflections (amongst other things) in urban or indoors environment will cause more inaccuracies including the jumping around motion that you are experiencing. A robust application must learn to deal with this. You could try to write a filter to essentially average the previous location results to smooth the output, but don't expect perfection.
