Remark: The common notation for sample average of $X$ is $\bar{X}$. Notice that since $\sum_{i=1}^n (\bar{X}-X_i)=0$, we have $\sum_{i=1}^n \bar{X}(\bar{X}-X_i)=0$ \begin{align}\sum_{i=1}^n (\bar{X}X_i-X_i^2)&=\sum_{i=1}^n X_i(\bar{X}-X_i) \\ &=\sum_{i=1}^n X_i(\bar{X}-X_i) -\sum_{i=1}^n \bar{X}(\bar{X}-X_i) \\ &=\sum_{i=1}^n (X_i-\bar{X})(\bar{X}-X_i)\\ &=- \sum_{i=1}^n (X_i-\bar{X})^2\end{align} Similar simplification can be performed for the numerator.
