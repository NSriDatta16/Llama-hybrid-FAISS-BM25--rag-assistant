This will select all the logins on the 18th of March per hour and then calculates the average. SELECT AVG(count) FROM ( SELECT COUNT(*) count FROM EVENTS_ALL_RPT_V1 WHERE EVENT_NAME = 'Login' AND EVENT_DATETIME >= CONVERT(DATETIME,'2010-03-18 00:00:00', 120) AND EVENT_DATETIME
