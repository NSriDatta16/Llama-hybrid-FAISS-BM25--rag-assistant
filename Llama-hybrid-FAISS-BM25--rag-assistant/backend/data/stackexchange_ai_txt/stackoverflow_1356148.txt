I like enumitem for this sort of thing: \documentclass{article} \usepackage{enumitem} \begin{document} \begin{enumerate} \item List item \item Another list item \end{enumerate} Paragraph of comments on list items 1 and 2. \begin{enumerate}[resume] \item Further item \item Final item \end{enumerate} \end{document}
