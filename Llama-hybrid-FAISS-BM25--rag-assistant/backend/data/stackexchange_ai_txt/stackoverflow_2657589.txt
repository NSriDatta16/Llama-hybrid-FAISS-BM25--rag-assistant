You are logged in as far as you are logged in facebook. You will have to create custom PHP's session handling. However, if you want to remove the user from application's cookies, you can do this: //this will clear cookies for your application and redirect them to a login prompt $facebook->set_user(null, null); $facebook->redirect($appcallbackurl);
