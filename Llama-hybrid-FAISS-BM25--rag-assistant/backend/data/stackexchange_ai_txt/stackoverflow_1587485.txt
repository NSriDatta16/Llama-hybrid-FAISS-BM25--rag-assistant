Check APC's links out, they are what you are looking for : the mapping is quite straightforward according to the name of the enumeration. But as you began to notice, there is something tricky about integers. Here is my mapping : Int16 : NUMBER(5) . Int32 : NUMBER(10) . Int64 : NUMBER(19) . The thing is that if you call GetInt64 on a NUMBER(38) column, you will get an exception even if the value is in the correct range...
