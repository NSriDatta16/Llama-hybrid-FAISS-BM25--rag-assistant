Store numbers in numeric data types, strings in character data types and dates in date datatypes. If you do it any other way you are doing in wrong and you will be working against the database, which in turn will seem to work against you. (which I believe just happened). As for your question, you just order by the date column. If the rows come out in the "wrong" order, read the previous paragraph again. select * from dateTable order by date;
