Technet: Filtered Index Design Guidelines There are really no downsides when compared to having no index at all. The upsides are all the upsides you get by having an index on the recent data, and the downsides are the same for having an index (except it's a percentage of the full downside), such as maintenance (insert/update) cost, storage cost etc.
