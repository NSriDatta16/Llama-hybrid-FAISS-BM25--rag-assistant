You'll want to look into the User-Agent strings, a list of which can be found here: http://www.zytrax.com/tech/web/mobile_ids.html . Keep in mind that it's not 100% reliable, because many mobile browsers pretend to be desktop browsers (I know Skyfire encourages this just in case there are more videos in the desktop version).
