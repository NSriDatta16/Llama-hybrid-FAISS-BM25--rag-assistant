You have to understand that dismax is not designed to handle such queries. Dismax supports a simplified version of the Lucene QueryParser syntax. When using it, it allows the users only to provide keywords and not to bother with syntax. You can find more details about dismax here . Pay attention to the part about the mm parameter that allows to simulate boolean queries with dismax.
