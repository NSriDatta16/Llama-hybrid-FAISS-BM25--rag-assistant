Are you on Vista or Windows7? Are you on 32 or 64bit? Right click the process in Task Manager and select 'Create Dump File'. You can then drag the DMP file into Visual Studio (as long as its a .NET4 process) and view the information you require. see here for more information. shameless plug See also my post on all the issues you might encounter obtaining and analyzing a dump file
