It might not be an elegant solution, but at least it should work: According to the Thrift Wiki there are C#-bindings, but they really should be called .NET-bindings: there is nothing C#-specific about them. So you could implement your communication-library in a C++/CLI dll-library and reference that .dll from your unmanaged code.
