A rule based classifier is generally suited more for this problem where most of your features are going to contain discrete values. So, Decision trees, Boosting, Random forests should do the job for you. One thing you should always keep in mind is how you are going to evaluate your model. For fraud detection, make sure that False negative is eliminated completely. A false positive is fine, but the opposite is dangerous.
