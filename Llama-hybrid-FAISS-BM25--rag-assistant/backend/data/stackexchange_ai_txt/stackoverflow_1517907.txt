My understanding was that this feature is support in FF, Safari, and IE8 Nope, it's only in WebKit (Safari/Chrome), and also via Gears. You may be thinking of the simpler Local/Session Storage feature, which just stores unstructured data. This is supported by Firefox 3.5, IE8 and Safari, but not Chrome.
