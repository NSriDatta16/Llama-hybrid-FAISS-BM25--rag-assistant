Are you on Mac? I suspect GMail gives you LF line endings seeing basing in your browser's User-Agent. In any case, proper solution to the problem is to convert text using universal_newline converter. See documentation for ruby built-in converters here: http://ruby-doc.org/core-1.9/classes/Encoding/Converter.html
