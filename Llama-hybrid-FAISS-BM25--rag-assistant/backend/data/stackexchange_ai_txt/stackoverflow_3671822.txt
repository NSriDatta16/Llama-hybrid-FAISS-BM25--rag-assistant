I think the simplest way is to use Google Gears or HTML5 storage capabilities and add an offline mode to this application. Therefore, you just need to open the application once in the browser and enable the offline access. Then, it just works simply using the offline capabilities. http://www.w3.org/TR/offline-webapps/ http://www.devcomments.com/HTML5-Offline-GWT-APP-at246553.htm
