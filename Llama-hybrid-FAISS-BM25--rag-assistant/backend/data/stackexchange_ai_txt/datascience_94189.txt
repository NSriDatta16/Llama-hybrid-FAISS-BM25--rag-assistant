Another method is Bayesian Blocks from Studies in Astronomical Time Series Analysis. VI. Bayesian Block Representations by Scargle et al. Bayesian Blocks is a dynamic histogramming method which optimizes one of several possible fitness functions to determine an optimal binning for data, where the bins are not necessarily uniform width.
