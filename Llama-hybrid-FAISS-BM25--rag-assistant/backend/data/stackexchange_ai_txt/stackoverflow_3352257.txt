There are two approaches you could take, depending on your requirements: Store the co-ordinates yourself (as you had planned). You should check out the Geokit gem which supports a MySQL backend and provides helpers for finding nearby locations. Store your co-ords with with Simplegeo for which you can use this gem . Again, this takes care of your storage and finding nearby.
