You might be interested in this advice that I found on a discussion board for marketing web apps . Unlike sponsorship, conference registration is inexpensive and success can be ensured if you plan ahead. To maximize your success utilize tools like EventVue to prearrange meetings with attendees, schedule meetings with organizations in the region, and meet with your passionate users who are local. Executing these three items will make any conference a success.
