You should check out the kSoap2 lib for Android. I found this video tutorial especially useful: http://www.vimeo.com/9633556 . Apart from that, using SOAP in Android apps is discouraged, and not supported by the SDK. Consider using REST, if that's possible, it's more lightweight.
