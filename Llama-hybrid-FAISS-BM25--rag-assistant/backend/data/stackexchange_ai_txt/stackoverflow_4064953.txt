Also, look on this fragment: print(self.queue) while self.count you do print(self.queue) only before in first while iteration, so, self.queue.pop() can make many iterations (and fetch many links) and raise "cannot pop from empty list" only when queue is really empty! try this: while self.count for detect moment when you take exception.
