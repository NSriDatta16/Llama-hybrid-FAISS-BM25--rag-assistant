At Google PageSpeed, they highly discourage you from using this technique, because it makes things slower. Apart from the sequential loading of your script.js before all the others, there's another catch: Modern browsers use speculative parsers to more efficiently discover external resources [...] Thus, using JavaScript's document.write() to fetch external resources makes it impossible for the speculative parser to discover those resources, which can delay the download, parsing, and rendering of those resources.
