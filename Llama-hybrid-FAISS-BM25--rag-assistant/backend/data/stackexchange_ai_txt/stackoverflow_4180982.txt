BOOLEAN is an alias for TINYINT(1) and is stored as one byte of data. ENUM('y','n') is also stored as 1 byte of data. So from a storage size point of view, neither is better. However you can store 9 in a BOOLEAN field and it will accept it. So if you want to force two states only, go for ENUM.
