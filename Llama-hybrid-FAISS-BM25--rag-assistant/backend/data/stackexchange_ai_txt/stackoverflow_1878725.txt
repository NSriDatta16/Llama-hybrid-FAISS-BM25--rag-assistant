It's worth noting that this is an area that's had a lot of attention as part of .NET 4 and EF4. They now have support for POCOs, as well as Self-Tracking-Entities, both of which can be sent back and forth between layers and tiers (including WCF boundaries). Take a look at this blog entry about Self-Tracking-Entities for more information.
