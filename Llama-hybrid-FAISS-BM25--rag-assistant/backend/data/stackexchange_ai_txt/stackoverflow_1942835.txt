In SSRS, put an extra detail row in your table. Then use the Scope parameter on aggregate functions, and do the average from first principles. Eg: (Sum(Fields!Sales.Value, "table1") - Fields!Sales.Value) / (Sum(Fields!NumSales.Value, "table1") - Fields!NumSales.Value)
