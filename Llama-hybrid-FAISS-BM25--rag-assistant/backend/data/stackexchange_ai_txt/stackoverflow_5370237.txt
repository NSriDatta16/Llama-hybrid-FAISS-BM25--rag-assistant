You should deselect the row in the didSelectRowAtIndextPath method of the delegate of your UITableView. It should look something like this. - (void)tableView:(UITableView *)tableView didSelectRowAtIndexPath:(NSIndexPath *)indexPath { [tableView deselectRowAtIndexPath:indexPath animated:YES]; /* initialize your view controller here v and then push it */ SomeViewController *v = [[[SomeViewController alloc] init] autorelease]; [self.navigationController pushViewController:v animated:YES]; } Dot confuse it with didDeselectRowAtIndextPath method - it happens as people do not pay much attention when selecting methods from intelisense.
