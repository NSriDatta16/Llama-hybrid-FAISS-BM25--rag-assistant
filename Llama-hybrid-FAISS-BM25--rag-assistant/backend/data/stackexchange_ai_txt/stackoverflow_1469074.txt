If your SQL is predefined and cannot be changed your best option is to load your CSV into a database and run queries against it. Apache Derby is a viable option, so are MySQL , which even has a CSV storage engine or PostgreSQL . Does your SQL use any proprietary functions / extensions? If so, that may limit your choices.
