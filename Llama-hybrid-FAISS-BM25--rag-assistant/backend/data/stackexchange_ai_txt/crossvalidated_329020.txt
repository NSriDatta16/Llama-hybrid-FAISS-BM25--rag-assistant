For the ROC and AUC, you should use the probability predictions: roc1 Also make sure that you train a random forest for classification rf.model The performance is quite similar, (AUC: 90.9 vs 90.78). Depending on the data RF sometimes cannot get much better performance than logistic regression. See here for an interesting comparison: https://epub.ub.uni-muenchen.de/39955/1/TR.pdf
