How to adjust for known prognostic clinical or molecular confounders when using survival analysis methods that are not based on Cox regression?

When using penalized Cox or Coxnet regression for survival analysis, it is possible to account and adjust for known prognostic clinical or molecular confounders by including them in your model as unpenalized features (via setting a penalty factor option or similar to 0 for those features). But for survival analysis methods not based on Cox regression, such as survival SVM, survival gradient boosting, or survival random forest, how does one do the same?
