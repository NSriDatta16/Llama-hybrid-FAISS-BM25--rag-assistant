options("HTTPUserAgent") or getOption("HTTPUserAgent") prints your current settings, and options(HTTPUserAgent="My settings") is the way to change it. To temporary change this option use: withr::with_options : withr::with_options(list(HTTPUserAgent="My settings"), download.file(..something..)) or Droplet answer if you use download.file .
