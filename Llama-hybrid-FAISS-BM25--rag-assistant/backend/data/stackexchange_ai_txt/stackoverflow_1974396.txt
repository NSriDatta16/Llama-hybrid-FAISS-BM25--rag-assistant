Predefined exceptions are declared in package SYS.STANDARD, there you'll surely find them all. On my 9.2.0.7 I found one not in the list: USERENV_COMMITSCN_ERROR exception; pragma EXCEPTION_INIT(USERENV_COMMITSCN_ERROR, '-1725');
