Yes, this is possible. E.g. I have a Huawei E220 USB stick for UMTS (3G mobile) connectivity which has four interfaces: Two are vendor specific (kernel driver eventually maps them as /dev/ttyUSB{0,1} ) and two are mass storage. I know you can use the two /dev/ttyUSB{0,1} simultanously.
