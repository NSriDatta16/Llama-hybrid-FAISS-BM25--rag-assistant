Take one step back from the code: What does abstract mean? Abstract The gist of it is "not real, but capturing a property of real things" You need to know this for OOP because you will be designing object universes, which requires you to think about how those objects are related. Abstraction allows you to group some of those objects, thus organizing 1) Your thinking process 2) Your code
