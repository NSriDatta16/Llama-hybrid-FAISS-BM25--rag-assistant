Question 2) You should use the data of one year ago. Question 1) You can do that via the following steps: A) gather data for abandoned and healthy projects and store them in a simple csv file for example. B) fit a classification model (you can use gradient boosting or random forest for instance) on part of your database (training set) C) test the model on the other part of your database (test set - about 20% usually) D) predict the outcome of your new project! You will also have the accuracy of the model.
