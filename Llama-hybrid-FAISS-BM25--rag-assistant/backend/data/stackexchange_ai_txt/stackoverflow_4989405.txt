You should change your addTarget call to this: [new_button addTarget:self action:@selector(newMethod:) forControlEvents:UIControlEventTouchUpInside]; Pay attention to semicolon after the newMethod: name This is because the signature of your selector should be as following: - (void) newMethod:(id) sender This way it will be called. Good luck
