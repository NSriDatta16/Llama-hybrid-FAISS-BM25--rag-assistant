You can download your app data onto your computer using Xcode's Organizer. Launch Xcode List item Connect your handset In Xcode, go to Window > Organizer Select your device, under "Devices" In the window to the Right, find "Applications" window Find your app and tap on the arrow next to the name to expand the list An item should appear underneath the app name, titled "Application Data" Click the arrow pointing down, located at the far right side of the window Save the folder anywhere onto your drive Now you will be able to find your sqlite in the "Documents" folder inside that folder you just saved.
