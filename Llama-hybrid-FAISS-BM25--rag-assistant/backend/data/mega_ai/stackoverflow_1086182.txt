10,000 threads: each will have a (default) 1MB stack space allocated. Therefore they will need 10GB RAM, which is impossible on a 32bit process (and likely to break total available RAM/Page on 64bit). Nothing to do with tracing. Additional: Great new article on thread (and process) limits on Windows, by Mark Russinovich. Please note the final paragraph. " Pushing the Limits of Windows: Processes and Threads "
