There are a few ways to implement movement in an iPhone app. You can use the accelerometer (tilt the device around various axis. Tap and drag. You can monitor the direction of a drag and move your sprite accordingly. You can use a simple tap mechanism where tapping on the left side of the screen moves the unit left, and tapping on the right moves the unit right. Hope this gives you some ideas.
