The idea is to work around properties files. They are easy to work with and you can extend this system easily. You can find a nice answer here . Netbeans have also a great sample tutorial . After you get your languages right, you will need software to switch locales, localize dates and amounts etc. Kai Toedter' tools are very reliable. You can add these beans to your IDE and drag and drop them to your JPanels.
