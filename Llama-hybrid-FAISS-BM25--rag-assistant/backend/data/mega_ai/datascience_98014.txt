One option is start with pattern matching with established tools. For each statement, find the subject and sentiment. Here are off-the-shelf tools (no training or machine learning): WordNet a large lexical database of English words. Synset is a way to find instances are the groupings of synonymous words that express the same concept. SentiWordNet assigns to each synset of WordNet.
