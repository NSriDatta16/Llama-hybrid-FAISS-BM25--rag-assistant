DoBusyWork (as @RBerteig has suggested, the name is a red flag) may be a way to force the compiler to not optimize a busy-loop. These darned compilers are getting so smart that sometimes they decide, "Oh! You don't need a loop there! I see what you're trying to calculate!" despite your real interest as a programmer.
