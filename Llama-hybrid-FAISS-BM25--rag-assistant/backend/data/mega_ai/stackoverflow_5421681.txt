In touchesMoved , before applying newcenter to your view, round it to your grid step size: float step = 10.0; // Grid step size. newcenter.x = step * floor((newcenter.x / step) + 0.5); newcenter.y = step * floor((newcenter.y / step) + 0.5); This will cause your view to "snap" as you drag it.
