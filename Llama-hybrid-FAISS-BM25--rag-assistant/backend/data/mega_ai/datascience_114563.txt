You have several choices: The trend can be calculated as $\frac{last - first}{first}$ , e.g. $\frac{3.9 - 3.6}{3.6}$ You can perform a linear regression including the 4 points and use the slope as trend. Or any variant, e.g. average difference: $\frac{(3.7-3.6)+(3.8-3.7)+(3.9-3.8)}{3}$ .
