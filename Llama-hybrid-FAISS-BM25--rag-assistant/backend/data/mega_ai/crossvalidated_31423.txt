"10 'experts' forecast some pdf of risk (or an event related to risk) - how do you combine these to make a decision? Assuming the experts come up with their pdfs using independent pieces of information, the unique correct way to combine the evidence is using the pointwise product of the density functions, just as we do when doing Bayesian estimation.
