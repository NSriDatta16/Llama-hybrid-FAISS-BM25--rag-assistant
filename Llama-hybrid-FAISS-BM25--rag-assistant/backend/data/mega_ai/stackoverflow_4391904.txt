see http://www.omegahat.org/RCurl/FAQ.html for a thorough explanation and particularly (depending on your preference for security): If you don't have a certificate from an appropriate signing agent, you can suppress verifying the certificate with the ssl.verifypeer option: x = getURLContent("https://www.google.com", ssl.verifypeer = FALSE)
