SD cards are FAT formatted, i.e. all content is available to all. Your only real option is encryption. You can probably adapt the code here to encrypt and decrypt a file: http://www.androidsnippets.org/snippets/39/ Obviously you'd have your app generate a random key on first run, and store it in your preferences (which is private to your app on internal storage).
