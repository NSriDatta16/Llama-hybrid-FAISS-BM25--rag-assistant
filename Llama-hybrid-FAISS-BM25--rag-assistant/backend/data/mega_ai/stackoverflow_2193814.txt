So long as you're in the moreNavigationController 's delegate, this should do the trick: [viewController isEqual:[navigationController.viewControllers objectAtIndex:0]] By comparison, topViewController will give you the opposite of what you want. Using objectAtIndex:0 should help avoid any private shenanigans.
