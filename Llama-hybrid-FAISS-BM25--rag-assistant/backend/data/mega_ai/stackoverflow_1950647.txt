If you are trying to address a particular situation, you are looking for the abstract factory pattern . A factory determines how to instantiate a family of types based on runtime information. If you are wondering how to address this situation in the abstract, you are looking for an Inversion of Control container , which manages the organization and fulfillment of dependencies. The registration of the strategy would include the conditional logic.
