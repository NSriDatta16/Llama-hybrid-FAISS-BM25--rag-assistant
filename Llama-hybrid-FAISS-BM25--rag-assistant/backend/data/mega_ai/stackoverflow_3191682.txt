If the video is not playing in full-screen, you can: Detect moving parts (simple optical flows Perform template matching for the video controls :D (much simpler than YouTube logos, ...) Morphological operations on the flow fields. If the video is playing in full-screen, an SVM trained on the histogram of optical flow should tell you that it is full-screen.
