In logistic regression the data generating process is: $$ \begin{aligned} y_i | x_i &\sim \text{Bernoulli}(p_i(x_i;\beta)) \\ p_i(x_i; \beta) &= \frac{e^{x_i^T\beta}}{1 + e^{x_i^T\beta}} \end{aligned} $$ Notice $P(y_i=1)$ depends on $x_i$ and $\beta$. Currently you are just setting $p(x_i;\beta)$ to some value. You need to fix a true $\beta$, calculate $x_i^T\beta$, and then get $p_i(x_i;\beta)$. For example bet will give you data for $\beta = 2$.
