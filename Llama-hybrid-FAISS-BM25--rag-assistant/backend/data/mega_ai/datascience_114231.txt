Some popular methods for POS-tagging are Hidden Markov Model (HMM) and Conditional Random Field (CRF) book . But you could use any recurrent networks (RNN, LSTM, bi-LSTM), CNNs or Transformers, to process your sequence of embeddings. One could also use BERT (or other pre-trained models) to perform Name Entity Recognition blog .
