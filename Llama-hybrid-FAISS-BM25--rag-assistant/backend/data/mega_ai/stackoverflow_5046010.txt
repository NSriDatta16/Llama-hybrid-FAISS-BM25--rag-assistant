It depends on the size and complexity of your project. For small websites with minimal functionality, a base page might be overkill. That said, I would typically use it for site-wide functionality, such as security. I tend to keep functionality in the master pages to a minimum since their primary purpose is to organize your layout and factor out common display areas from you content pages to avoid duplication and ease maintenance.
