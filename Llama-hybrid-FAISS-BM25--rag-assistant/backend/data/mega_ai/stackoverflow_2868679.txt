First off, you should be writing unit tests ;) Say you end up needing to change the underlying database. If your data access code is tightly coupled to your business logic, this could prove to be a huge effort. With loosely coupled code, your business logic will remain unaffected. What if you decide you want to write some command line utilities that leverage your backend components? Providing multiple entry points to your system is much more easily accomplished with loosely coupled code.
