What harm are they doing where they are? They will take up 'space' wherever you put them. If are confident you will never need them again then there is a case for archival to less expensive storage (eg tape?) - otherwise whatever you do will 'overly complicate the retrieval process' You could consider compressing them in-place if you are not already doing so
