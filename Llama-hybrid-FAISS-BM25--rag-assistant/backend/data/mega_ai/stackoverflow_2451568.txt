The "drag it over to my toolbox" part cannot work. Right-click the toolbox and select "Choose Items", use the Browse tab. You are much better off just adding the UserControl to your project instead of putting it in a DLL by itself. When you do, it automatically appears at the top of the toolbox after you compile.
