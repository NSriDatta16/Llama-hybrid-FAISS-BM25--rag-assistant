First of all, MySQL is both ubiquitous and modern. ANSI SQL is more or less the same in all RDBMs, so you can learn any of them and you'll be good. Once you've mastered ANSI SQL, then all you've got left is the localized solutions for each one of them, which won't be portable to other systems - and so, totally discouraged to use, unless they simplify your tasks in a way justifying it.
