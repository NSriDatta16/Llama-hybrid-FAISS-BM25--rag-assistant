I have changed the strategy of form loading, this will make a great change on form load timing, now it's taking an average of 37 ms instead of 466 ms. Method: On First time Click on Top-tab/Icon, application load all form under that tab/icon and on click on Form Icon it will only switch visibility. And again visit on Top-tab will not load the form under that tab.
