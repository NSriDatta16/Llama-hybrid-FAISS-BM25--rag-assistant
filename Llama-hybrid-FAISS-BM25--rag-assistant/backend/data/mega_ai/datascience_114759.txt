In general this could be achieved with the snippet below where you have to replace x with your observations. However I'm not sure whether the Caonva-Hansen-Test is suitable for weekly observations (m=52) since these sort of tests are usually designed with monthly or quarterly time series in mind. Therefore it might be better to aggregate your observations to a monthly level (m=12). from pmdarima.arima import CHTest import numpy as np x=np.random.normal(size=1000) CHTest(m=52).estimate_seasonal_differencing_term(x)
