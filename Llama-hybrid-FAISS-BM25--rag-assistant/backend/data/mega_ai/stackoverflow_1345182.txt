As your buttons subclass from UIControl, you can use UIControl's implementation of recognition of gestures. Look at UIControl's - (void)addTarget:(id)target action:(SEL)action forControlEvents:(UIControlEvents)controlEvents . This will let you specify a method on an object to be called whenever certain events occur. The possible events are, among others: UIControlEventTouchDragInside = 1 A complete list is available in Apple's documentation for Control Events . When UIControlEventDragOutside fires, you may want to reevaluate which view is currently playing.
