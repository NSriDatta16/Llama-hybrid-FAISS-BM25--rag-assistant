STEP 1: Open the map you want to embed. It could be a map you created under my maps tab, or a search for address, local business or driving directions. STEP 3 : Paste the HTML code into your website or your blog editor. or else See this site http://www.higheredblogcon.com/library/deweese/presentation.html
