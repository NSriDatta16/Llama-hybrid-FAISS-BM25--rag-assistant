Hosting your own Mail Transfer Agent (MTA) has some benefits with easier mailbox creation and less latency but it requires significantly more maintenance. There's also the problem with spam filtering that would require some constant tweaking. An outside firm will likely have a more advanced filtering system in place that you can leverage the larger quantity of traffic passing through it. Never underestimate the increased effort for uptime as more components are integrated.
