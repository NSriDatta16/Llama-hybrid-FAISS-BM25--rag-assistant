Depends on what your goals are. If you want all newline characters to look the same, you'd better be thorough . According to the linked article, there are at least five patterns to look for: Windows (CR LF) Mac (CR) Unix (LF) Unicode Line Separator Unicode Paragraph Separator I'd think looking for byte patterns (by treating it as a binary file rather than a text file) would be the way to go.
