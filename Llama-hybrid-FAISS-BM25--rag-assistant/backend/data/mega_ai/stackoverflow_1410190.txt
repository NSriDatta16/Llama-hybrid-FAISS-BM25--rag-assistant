It's not just maintenance. The more specific is the interface between Observables and Observers the more coupled they become. The Gang Of Four book has a section for this very problem, and they advise against both the push and the pull models. The pull model may be inefficient, the push model may not be reusable enough. So, it highly depends on the situation. I tend to go slightly above the pull model.
