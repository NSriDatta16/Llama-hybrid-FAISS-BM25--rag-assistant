If you want to have a structured way to store and retrieve information, a database is most definitely the way to go. It makes your application flexible and more powerful, and lets you focus on the actual application rather than incidentals like trying to write your own storage system. For individual applications, SQLite is great. It fits right in an app as a file; no need for a whole DRBMS juggernaut.
