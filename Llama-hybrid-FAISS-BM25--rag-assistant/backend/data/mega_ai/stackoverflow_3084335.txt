Since scriptlets (those things) are discouraged since a decade, here's the EL solution: Your IP address is: ${pageContext.request.remoteAddr} If you actually intend to use this for some business purposes rather than displaying purposes, then you should be using a servlet. It's then available by HttpServletRequest#getRemoteAddr() .
