Of course, the emulator mimics a mobile device but I wouldn't deploy anything without testing it on at least 1 real phone. With all the fragmentation that exists with Android I'm sure most top-quality apps are tested on a wide variety of devices. When considering google accounts, keep in mind that many people have multiple gmail/google accounts on a single phone.
