I figured out what the issue was. It's an IE bug dealing with IE, HTTPS and addons. (See here ) It was a caching issue. When I set: header("Cache-Control: max-age=1"); header("Pragma: public"); (see here ), the PDF was in cache long enough for the adobe reader add-on to grab it.
