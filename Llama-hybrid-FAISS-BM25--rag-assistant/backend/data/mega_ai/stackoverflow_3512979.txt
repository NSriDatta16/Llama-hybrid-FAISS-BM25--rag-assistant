This helped a lot and and motivated me to search even more. And I found a solution which gives a huge boost: PRAGMA synchronous=OFF INSERT/UPDATE Queries run 4 times faster, now. It has however a small disadvantage. More details here: http://web.utk.edu/~jplyon/sqlite/SQLite_optimization_FAQ.html#pragmas
