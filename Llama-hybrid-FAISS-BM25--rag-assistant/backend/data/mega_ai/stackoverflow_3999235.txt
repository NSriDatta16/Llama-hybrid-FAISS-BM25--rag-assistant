Child1Entity has a collection of child entities ( IList ), which, being the third layer, causes the Child1Entity collection to have duplicates. My question is how can I apply the de-duplication process to the child collection. The reason I have duplicates is because I am using LeftOuterJoin on the child collections. I cannot remove the LeftOuterJoin.
