Have a look at Christian's 2010 24ways post under the heading Caching a full interface (near the end). He basically does: localStorage.setItem('state',f.innerHTML); Followed by: if('state' in localStorage){ f.innerHTML = localStorage.getItem('state'); } Where f is the element he wants to cache.
