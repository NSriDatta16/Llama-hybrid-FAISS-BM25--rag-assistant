In addition to undersampling the majority class (i.e. taking only a few NEW), you may consider oversampling the minority class (in essence, duplicating your OLDs, but there are other smarter ways to do that) Note that oversampling may lead to overfitting, so pay special attention to testing your classifiers Check also this answer on CV: https://stats.stackexchange.com/a/108325/49130
