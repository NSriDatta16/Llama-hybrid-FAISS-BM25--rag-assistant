In theory, you shouldn't have to change anything - just copy your EARs and WARs across, and off you go. In practice, it may not be that simple, there are a bunch of minor differences in how they interpret the JavaEE spec (JBoss5 is generally stricter). However, all I can advise is that you give it a try, and address each problem as it arises.
