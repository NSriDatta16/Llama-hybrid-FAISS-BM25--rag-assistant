Blackberry has several mobile options you can springboard from; Personally, I'd lean towards PDF export, since you've stated you don't need all the "bells and whistles". You can set up subscriptions in Report Manager, and have those subscriptions email PDF's out to your Blackberry users. This way, they get the data wherever they happen to be, on the appropriate email address. Unfortunately, there is no way (that I've heard of) to go about stripping it down for a specific user agent...
