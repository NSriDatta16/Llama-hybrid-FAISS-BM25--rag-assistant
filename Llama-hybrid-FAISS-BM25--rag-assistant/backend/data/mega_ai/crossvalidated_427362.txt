This notations means the average of $h(x)$ over the distribution $p_{\beta}$ . Written out in terms of an integral $$\langle h(x)\rangle_{p_{\beta}}=\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}h(x)p_{\beta}(x)dx$$ In general, an average of a function over a distribution is the integral of the function and the distribution over the domain of the distribution.
