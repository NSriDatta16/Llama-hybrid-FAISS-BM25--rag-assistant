You may need to take a look at this work submitted and accepted for CVPR 2018 : Learning to Segment Every Thing In this work, they try to segment everything, even objects not known to the network. Mask R-CNN has been used, combined with a transfer learning sub-network, they get very good results in segmenting almost everything.
