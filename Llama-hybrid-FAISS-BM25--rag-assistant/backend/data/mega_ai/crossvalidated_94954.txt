There's a lot of literature on financial time series, e.g. Tsay's book on subject. also Carol Alexander book called "market risk analysis" where she has step by step excel examples with popular methods like stochastic volatility, GARCH and exponential smoothing. For a beginner ARCH is the good place to start.
