The best thing to do here is to start to be honest about the strain and show, in writing, what exactly is happening and who all has demands on your team. You need to show the hours that all of your tasks are taking and who is doing them and be able to show that level of status. This merely give you some sort of proof about what's happening. At that point you can start to do the re-organization you need to do in order to start to support growth.
