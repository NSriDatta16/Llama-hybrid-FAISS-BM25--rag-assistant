Ok, I figured out how to use System.Windows.Media.Imaging.JpegBitmapEncoder in the method now: JpegBitmapDecoder decoder = new JpegBitmapDecoder(pixelStream, BitmapCreateOptions.None, BitmapCacheOption.None); BitmapFrame frame = decoder.Frames[0]; frame.CopyPixels(pixelBuffer, stride, 0); I measured the performance and it seems to have 100% improvement. Nice job from WIC and WPF. Decoding 1024x768 image is down to 9 to 10 msec now.
