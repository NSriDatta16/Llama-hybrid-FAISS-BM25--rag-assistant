It may work as desired, or it may not. The problem is, by introducing a lot of binary features, you also introduce bias, if you blindly use e.g. Euclidean distance. You will need to pay extra attention to normalizing your data and weighting your attributes appropriately. One way or another, first make sure you can measure similarity reasonably before continuing with clustering.
