Once you start talking about 'outcome' variables or dependent variables or the like, I think you are into the modeling world. Chi-square tests for association and does not posit that one variable is dependent. Similarly, the various kinds of correlation are tests of association. Since your outcome is binary, the usual method is logistic regression
