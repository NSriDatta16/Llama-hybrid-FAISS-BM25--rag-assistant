cs231n is a Convolutional Neural Networks for Visual Recognition course offered at Stanford University. This course is taught by professor Fei-Fei Li and covers state of the art practices for computer vision. Topics covered in the course include neural networks (CNNs, RNNs,...), deep learning, visualization, ethics, and more.
