Pay your $99 to Apple, log into the developer portal, provision your device (can also be done through organizer). Install that provisioning profile on your iPad and then try and build & run again in xcode. I highly recommend the organizer route, it simplifies all this for you. But if you opt to do it through the web, there are instructions in the portal to tell you what to do.
