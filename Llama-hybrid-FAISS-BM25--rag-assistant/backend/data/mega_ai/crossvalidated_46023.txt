If you have a categorical dependent variable with two levels, you do not want ANOVA or GLM (in SAS terminology), you want logistic regression (which, confusingly, is part of glm in R terminology. (that confusion comes from the difference between general linear model and generalized linear model, which unfortunately have the same acronym).
