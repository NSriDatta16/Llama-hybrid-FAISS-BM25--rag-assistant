Legal trademark and copyright issues sometimes restrict the usage based on domain when embedding fonts. Edit: That's why you should always look at the tiny letters on the font license that you might buy. If you created it, then you have full ownership and no problems at all. P.S: I am not a lawyer, but can confirm the case with several customers of mine.
