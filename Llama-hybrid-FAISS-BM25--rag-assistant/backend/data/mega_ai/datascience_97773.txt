The most appropriate variation of regression is ordinal regression . Ordinal regression tends to not perform well because of the complexity and sparseness of most ranking problems. Almost all supervised learning algorithms have a learning to rank variation . Typically, learning to rank problems involve text so algorithms that work well with text. Ranking support vector machine s (SVM) was a popular choice before the deep learning revolution.
