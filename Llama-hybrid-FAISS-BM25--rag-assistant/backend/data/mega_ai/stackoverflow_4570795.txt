While inelegant, archiving the entire eclipse folder is also insufficient. I think you want to archive the following: The entire eclipse installation folder. The .metadata folder of every workspace. By doing this, you will not only preserve the plugins and their settings, but you will also preserve an eclipse installation in which you know your plugins work.
