If the dim of your data is more than 2, you may want to transform your data with PCA, t-SNE or Custom linear projections. t-SNE is recommended for clustering result. For example: from sklearn.manifold import TSNE tsne = TSNE(n_components=2, random_state=0) data = tsne.fit_transform(data) This may lose some information, but useful for visualization.
