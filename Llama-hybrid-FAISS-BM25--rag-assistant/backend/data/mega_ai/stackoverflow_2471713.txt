You can set fill-columnn to a really large number, and fill. C-u 10000 C-x f M-x fill-individual-paragraphs Or you can use a little custom function: (defun refill-paragraphs-to-be-one-line () "fill individual paragraphs with large fill column" (interactive) (let ((fill-column 100000)) (fill-individual-paragraphs (point-min) (point-max))))
