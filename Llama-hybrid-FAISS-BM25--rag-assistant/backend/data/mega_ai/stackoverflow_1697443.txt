FoFou is my favorite forum software because it makes it easy for people to post questions and answers. A visitor shouldn't have to register, fill out a bunch of preferences and check their email before they can leave a comment. FoFou doesn't make them do any of that. Fo rums Fo r Yo u was designed for websites that host open source software so the whole point is to encourage users who may only visit once or twice to submit feedback and answer each other's questions. FoFou provides all the functionality of a slick forum, without the confusing bells and whistles. FoFou is open source, easy to set up and uses Google's App engine thus you can either host it yourself or use Google's free cloud hosting. Read more from their manifesto .
