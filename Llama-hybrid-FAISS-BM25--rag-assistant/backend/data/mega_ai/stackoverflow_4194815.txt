You need to use linker flags to tell the linker to place that segment in sharable memory. See http://msdn.microsoft.com/en-us/library/ms933104.aspx I belive you need to add something like #pragma comment(linker, "/SECTION:.testseg,RWS") to your program. I'm not sure, this may only work in a DLL...
