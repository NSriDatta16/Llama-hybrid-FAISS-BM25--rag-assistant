Section 7.1 from Bergmeir & Benítez Sánchez (2012) is the reference for RSNNS and provides a short overview on neuralnet and nnet. Package nnet is the simplest one and restricted to a single layer; RSNNS and neuralnet have more options. Bergmeir, C. N., & Benítez Sánchez, J. M. (2012). Neural networks in R using the Stuttgart neural network simulator: RSNNS. American Statistical Association. https://www.jstatsoft.org/article/view/v046i07/v46i07.pdf
