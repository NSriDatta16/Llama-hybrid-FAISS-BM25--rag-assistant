The cascade (the the 'C' in CSS) is well defined to allow a clear definition of what rules will take precedence (including allowing for important, user and agent rules). But the rules are also not simple (thing of complex matches with large hierarchies specified). The last step of the cascade is document order of the declaration, with last winning.
