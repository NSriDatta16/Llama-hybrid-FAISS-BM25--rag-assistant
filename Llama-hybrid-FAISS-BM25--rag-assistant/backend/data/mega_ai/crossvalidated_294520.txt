It is possible to incorporate this information by weighting points differently in random forests. How this works is that in the bootstrapping process, observations will be sampled with a probability proportional to their weights. In R , this can be done in the ranger package. However, see also this useful answer and comment about how to treat the OOB error estimates if you plan on weighting your data.
