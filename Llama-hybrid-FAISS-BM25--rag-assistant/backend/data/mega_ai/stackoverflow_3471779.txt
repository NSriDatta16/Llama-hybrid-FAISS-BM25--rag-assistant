One file per class. Folders for related classes. Use modules/packages/assemblies/namespaces if your language supports them. In general, keep many levels of abstraction, and try to keep them separate through whatever mechanism you can in your language/ide/platform of choice. Read Domain Driven Design , which discusses these issues (design, documentation, organization and communication).
