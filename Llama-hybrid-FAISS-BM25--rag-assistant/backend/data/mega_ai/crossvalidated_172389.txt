Yes, some really nice actionable insights can be derived from the data set. Some which I can think of are: Personalized recommendations: You can have a look at this thread for a fair idea about the concept. This thread for the algorithmic intuition behind recommender systems. Depending on the time the user is browsing a particular website and his most preferred items, the products can be ranked(in terms of placement) on the website. Time Series analysis of sales performance: The sales performance can be analyzed through a time series and insights can be developed from the exploratory analytics and plots. Dynamic personalized offers and recommendations: The browsing behaviour and click analytics of the user can be overlapped with the time of the day and the corresponding recommendations for the users to offer customized real-time offers for the user.
