In your question, you specify no cache on both the client and server. To me this means no caching anywhere. This will prevent any caching from happening anywhere. Response.Cache.SetExpires(DateTime.UtcNow.AddMinutes(-1)); Response.Cache.SetCacheability(HttpCacheability.NoCache); Response.Cache.SetNoStore(); Either put this in the load of the page(s) you don't want cached or create a base page class. from http://skysanders.net/subtext/archive/2010/03/23/preventing-caching-of-content-in-asp.net-pages-and-other-httphandlers.aspx
