Other answers have pointed to the world of machine learning, and that is certainly one place where this problem has been addressed. However, another approach that may be better suited to your needs is the use of the QR factorization with with low rank updates. Approaches to doing this and using it to solve least squares problems are given in: Updating the QR factorization and the least squares problem by Hammerling and Lucas.
