If the word "From: " is unique, >> s => "Come Find me please. This is paragraph one.\n\nThis is paragraph two. \nCapture everything before me as this is the last sentence.\n\n\n\n\nFrom: XXX XXX \nDate: Mon, 17 May 2010 10:59:40 -0700\n\nTo: \"xxx, xxx\" \nSubject: Re: XXXXXXXX\n\ndone" >> s.split(/From:\s+/).first.strip => "Come Find me please. This is paragraph one.\n\nThis is paragraph two. \nCapture everything before me as this is the last sentence."
