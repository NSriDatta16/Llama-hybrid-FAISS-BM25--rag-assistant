Using the standard QR-algorithm, the order of the resulting eigenvalues depends on the decomposed matrix and the inital values for the eigenvalues. In my implementation of the QR-decomposition I sort the eigenvalues and the corresponding eigenvectors in the postprocessing of QR like this: sort eigenvalues foreach eigenvalue e: e.eigenvector.new_position = e.new_position However, the negative eigenvalue of -3.6 indicates there is something wrong with the calculation of the covariance matrix since all eigenvalues of a PCA should be non-negative.
