I ended up making my picturbox look like a button by giving it a raised border style. Then on the mouseclick event I simulate the look of a button press by changing the border style for a few hundred miliseconds. Private Sub simulateButtonPress(ByRef pictureBox As Infragistics.Win.UltraWinEditors.UltraPictureBox) pictureBox.BorderStyle = Infragistics.Win.UIElementBorderStyle.Inset Application.DoEvents() System.Threading.Thread.Sleep(400) pictureBox.BorderStyle = Infragistics.Win.UIElementBorderStyle.Raised Application.DoEvents() End Sub
