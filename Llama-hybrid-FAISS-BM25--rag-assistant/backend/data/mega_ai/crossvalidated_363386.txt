Maybe, “performing PCA on a single time series” means application of singular spectrum analysis (SSA), which is sometimes called PCA of time series. In SSA, multivariate data are constructed from lagged (moving) subseries of the initial time series. Then PCA (usually, SVD, which is PCA without centering/standardizing) is applied to the obtained multivariate data. Example in R: library(Rssa) s
