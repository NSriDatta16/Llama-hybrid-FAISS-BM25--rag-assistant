Code coverage tools are almost certainly superior to any ad hoc method you could come up with. That's why they exist. Even developers who get TDD are far from immune to gaps in coverage. Often, code that fixes a bug breaks a lateral test or creates a branch that the original developer did not anticipate and the maintenance developer didn't realize was new.
