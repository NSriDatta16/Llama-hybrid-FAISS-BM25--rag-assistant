Exporting sources from ClearCase? The surest way is a snapshot view on a dedicated workstation: since a snapshot view has its storage (the equivalent of the .svn directories) outside the said view, that means the resulting directory is clean. Then you can take this tree, compress it and transfer it whenever you want, (like a workstation without any VCS)
