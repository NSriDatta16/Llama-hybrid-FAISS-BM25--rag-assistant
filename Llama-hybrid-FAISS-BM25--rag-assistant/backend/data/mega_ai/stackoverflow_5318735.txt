Python PEP 8 encourages 4 spaces, no tabs. Many editors support this, including: Vim , Emacs , Komodo , WingWare , and PyCharm to name just a few. I should point out that I'm not specifically discouraging you from using tabs, but addressing your question regarding indentation standards.
