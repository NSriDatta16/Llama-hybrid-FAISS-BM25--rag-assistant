You can't automatically do that, since this is the expected behaviour. It's up to the interpreter what to do with the entities. The average webbrowser for example will interpret and display them flawlessly. If you're allowed to join a 3rd party library, then I'd suggest to use the Apache Commons Lang StringEscapeUtils#unescapeXml() for this.
