Recall that one of the assumptions of linear regression is linearity, which is expressed as $$\mu_{y|x_1, \dots, x_k} = \beta_0 + \beta_1x_1 + \dots + \beta_kx_k$$ The logistic regression assumption that you just stated may be interpreted in a similar way.
