Coherence can cache non-Serializable but POF-aware (either PortableObject or one having a registered PofSerializer) objects starting with 3.4. Before that POF (or any other custom Serializer for that matter) was not usable as a storage (within the backing map) and tranport format within the cluster (on the TCMP protocol), it was only used as a transport protocol in TCP*Extend.
