It really depends on the specific function $f_w(x)$. But as Marc Claesen points out, one example of this is the SVM setting. Here, the norm $\|w\|^2$ has a natural interpretation as the reciprocal of the margin of the classifier. Minimizing this allows you to find a large-margin classifier.
