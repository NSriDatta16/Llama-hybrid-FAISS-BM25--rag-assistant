What do you think about delegating the responsibility of creating users to a third party source (like Facebook, Twitter, OpenId...)? Doing that will not solve your problem, but it will be more work for a user to create additional accounts - which (assuming that the users are lazy, since most are) should discourage the creation of additional "dummy" users.
