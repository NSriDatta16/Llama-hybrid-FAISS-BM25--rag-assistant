David Cantrell gives a good approximation of Γ -1 (n) on this page : k = the positive zero of the digamma function, approximately 1.461632 c = Sqrt(2*pi)/e - Γ(k), approximately 0.036534 L(x) = ln((x+c)/Sqrt(2*pi)) W(x) = Lambert W function ApproxInvGamma(x) = L(x) / W(L(x) / e) + 1/2
