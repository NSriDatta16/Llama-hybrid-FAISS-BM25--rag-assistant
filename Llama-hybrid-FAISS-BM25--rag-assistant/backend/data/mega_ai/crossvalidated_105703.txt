It's not a "worse" estimator; it's not an estimator at all because they are two completely different concepts. The mean of the ratios is not equal to the ratio of the means. If this does not intuitively make sense to you, think of the case where X=0 in your example. The ratio Y/X would not even exist in this case. This question has already been answered here in greater detail: https://math.stackexchange.com/questions/80583/simple-question-regarding-ratio-and-average
