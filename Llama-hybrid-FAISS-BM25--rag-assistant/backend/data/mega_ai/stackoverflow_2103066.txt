I just posted a rebuttal of Jeffrey Palermos post . In short, we should not let concrete implementation details influence our design. That would be violating the Liskov Substitution Principle on the architectural scale. A more elegant solution lets us keep the design by introducing a Lazy-loading OrderShipper.
