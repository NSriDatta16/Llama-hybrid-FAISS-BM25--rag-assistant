Check out Embedded.com as well as Jack Ganssle's collection of articles . Well worth the read. EmbeddedRelated.com is also a good resource with access to various newsgroups archives. To get familiar with a microcontroller it is always helpful to grab a datasheet, e.g. the Freescale S12X offers a wide variety of functionality (from CAN, SCI etc.). Search Freescale's website for more information.
