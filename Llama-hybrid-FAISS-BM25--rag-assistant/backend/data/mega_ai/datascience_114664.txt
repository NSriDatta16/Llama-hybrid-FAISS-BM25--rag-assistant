The scikit-learn is a library that is used most often when working with the more traditional non neural network models, whereas the other three are more focused on neural networks. Between pytorch , tensorflow , and keras is sort of the odd one out because it is a library built on top of tensorflow meant as an interface to more easily create and train neural networks.
