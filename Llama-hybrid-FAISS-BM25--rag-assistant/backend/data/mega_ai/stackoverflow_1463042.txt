You can also use DisplayObject.hitTestObject() movieClipOnStage.hitTestObject(movieClipYouAreDragging) If you do this on a loop while you are dragging (using an Event.ENTER_FRAME , Event.MOUSE_MOVE or a Timer event) you should be able to get the result you are after.
