There is a little Mac App called LatexIt that makes it very easy to convert LaTeX equations to PDF, PNGs etc. (I use it to create equations for my slides in Keynote or PowerPoint. It's very nice, with drag 'n drop support, so you can just 'drag' the equations anywhere to insert them.)
