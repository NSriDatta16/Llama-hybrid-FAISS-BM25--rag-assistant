They've redirected http://twitter.com/ceejayoz to http://twitter.com/#!/ceejayoz , which is actually just http://twitter.com/ to a search engine. There are indexable pages behind the scenes (URLs like http://twitter.com/?_escaped_fragment_=/ceejayoz ), but you don't see them. The intent isn't to game PageRank, that's just a side effect of the AJAX URLs they're using.
