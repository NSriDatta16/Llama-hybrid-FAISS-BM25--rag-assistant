Unfortunately, this is not possible. You may only add controls to custom groups in Office 2007/2010. The built-in groups are really a different thing than the custom groups that you may add. For example, you will see for example that the built-in groups such as the ones for font and paragraph formatting behave differently with respect to resizing the application window.
