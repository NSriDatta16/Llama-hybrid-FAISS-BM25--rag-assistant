The main advantage of using different assemblies is for breaking down large scale projects - different teams or departments can work on separate components. Yes. Your business logic should be seperate from your DAL. It's useful for architectural reasons as well as Unit & Integration Testing. This is very subjective and depends on the size of your project, team, organisational structure, business requirements, etc.
