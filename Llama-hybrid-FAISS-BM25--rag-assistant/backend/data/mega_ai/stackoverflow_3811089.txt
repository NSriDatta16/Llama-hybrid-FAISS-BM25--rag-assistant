For TortoiseSVN client: Select the files you wish to move, Right click and drag the files to the folder you wish to move them to, A window will popup after you release the drag and drop on a folder. Click "SVN Move versioned item(s) here" , After you click the above the commit window message box will appear. Enter a message and submit. Now you are done.
