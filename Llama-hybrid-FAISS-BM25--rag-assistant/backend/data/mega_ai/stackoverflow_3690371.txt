Margins aren't supposed to collapse if there is a border between them. So you can add a hidden border to prevent margin collapse. The following worked for me in my tested versions of FF, Chrome & IE. Paragraph 1 in blue box Paragraph 2 in blue box Paragraph 1 in green box Paragraph 2 in green box
