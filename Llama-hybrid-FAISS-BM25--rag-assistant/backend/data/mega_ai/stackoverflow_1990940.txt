OCR is very, very difficult! What approach to use to attempt OCR will be based on what you are trying to accomplish (hand writing recongnition, computer generated text reading, etc.) However, to get you started, read up on Neural Networks and OCR. Here are a few jump-right-in articles on the subject: http://www.codeproject.com/KB/cs/neural_network_ocr.aspx http://www.codeproject.com/KB/dotnet/simple_ocr.aspx Use your favorite search engine to find information. Have fun!
