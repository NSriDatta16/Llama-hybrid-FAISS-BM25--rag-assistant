That depends on the image. In general the answer is 100%, but in different strengths, depending on both the image and the specific network. In fact, encouraging full-network usage is one of the objectives of the "dropout" technique. This has been written about extensively in many places, but this blog post is a nice quick introduction. I'm not aware of in-depth results on "network utilization", but it might be enlightening to fit a simple network of your own (or download a pre-trained one like AlexNet) and see which neurons activate, and how strongly they activate, for various input samples.
