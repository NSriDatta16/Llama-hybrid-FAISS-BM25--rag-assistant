This is a very broad and very basic question so I will recommend a very broad and basic book - but one that addresses your question thoroughly: Predictive Analytics For Dummies by Anasse Bari, Mohamed Chaouchi, Tommy Jung See especially Chapter 14: Predictive Modeling with R Hope that helps to get you started... Addendum Concerning your comment the following two resources a also well worth a look: An introduction to data science by Jeff Stanton (free ebook) Rattle - sophisticated data mining user interface with lots of additional material (mostly free)
