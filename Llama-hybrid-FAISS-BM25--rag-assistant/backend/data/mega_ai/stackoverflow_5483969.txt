You need to invite the developer to your account in the apple developer site. Member Center > People > Invitations > Invite a Person to Your Team This should automatically install their developer profile and then you can sign the app with the "Team Provisioning Profile". Also check out Organizer > Provisioning Profiles > Automatic Device Provisioning in Xcode 4; this simplifies installing profiles.
