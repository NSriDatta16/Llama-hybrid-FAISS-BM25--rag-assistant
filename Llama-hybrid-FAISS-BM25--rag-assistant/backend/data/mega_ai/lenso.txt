Lenso.ai is a Polish technology company specializing in reverse image search. Overview Lenso.ai was established in 2024 by Polish software engineer Lukasz Kowalczyk. The company developed a platform for reverse image search and face search. The platform allows users to find the source of an image or locate visually similar images online. In 2024, Lenso.ai partnered with Eyematch.ai to integrate facial recognition-based search in selected regions. Technology Lenso.ai utilizes artificial intelligence to enable reverse image search. Users can upload images to search for similar visuals, locate landmarks, or perform face recognition searches. The platform can detect altered or modified images and includes copyright protection features to identify duplicates. According to the company, uploaded images are not stored permanently, emphasizing user privacy. == References ==
