If your word document lists all the text files like this: {tab} {newline} {tab} {newline} {tab} {newline} Then all the lines are available in the Paragraphs collection . You can loop through that with a simple For Each loop: Dim p As Paragraph For Each p In ActiveDocument.Paragraphs Debug.Print p.Range.Text Next p
