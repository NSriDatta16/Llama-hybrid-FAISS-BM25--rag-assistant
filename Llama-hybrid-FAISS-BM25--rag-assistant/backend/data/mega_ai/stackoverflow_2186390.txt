As a low level ITERATION deals with the CX registry to count loops, and of course data registries. RECURSION not only deals with that it also adds references to the stack pointer to keep references of the previous calls and then how to go back.- My University teacher told me that whatever you do with recursion can be done with Iterations and viceversa, however sometimes is simpler to do it by recursion than Iteration (more elegant) but at a performance level is better to use Iterations.-
