In that case there is a chance of resources getting freed up from the planned work. First, I'd start to talk about people or team members, not resources ("respect people"). It may get difficult to allocate new tasks to them during sprint You shouldn't allocate tasks to people (this sounds like micro-management). Tasks are in the queue (the sprint) and the team should self-organize to get items DONE, focusing on most important items first. What is the best way to plan resources in these situations? The best way is to not do it.
