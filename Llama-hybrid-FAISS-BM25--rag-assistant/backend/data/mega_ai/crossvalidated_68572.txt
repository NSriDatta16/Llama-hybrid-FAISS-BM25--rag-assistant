You should be able to analyse GxE using any mixed effects model package in R, but selecting one really depends upon your data: lme4 , ASreml-R , MCMCglmm are some of your options. Here are some papers that might help: Bolker et al (2009) TREE is great, and be sure to look at the supplementary materials. Wilson et al (2009) JAE is generally useful, but especially so if you are using a pedigree.
