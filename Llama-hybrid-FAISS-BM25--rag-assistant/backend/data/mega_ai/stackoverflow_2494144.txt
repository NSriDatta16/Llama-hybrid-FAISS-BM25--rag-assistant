You can do two things: You can disable items in the List that are not draggable, based on a property on that selectedItem's data object. This will cause them to appear visually disabled, which you may not want, so you can also try... You can set the dragEnabled property of the list to "true" when valid items (based on the selectedItem's data object) are selected, and to "false" when the items are invalid.
