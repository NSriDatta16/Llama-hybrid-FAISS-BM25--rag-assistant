It really depends, for anything that involves user acquisition I would steer clear of it unless your trying to do something very interactive and different. The install silverlight wall would lose you a lot of users. On the other hand if you are developing an application for a known group such as a internal type app of rolling out something new to an established community then I think it is a great choice. Most tech savvy people should have silverlight these days, it's just the average person who may still be using IE 6/7 that will have no idea about it.
