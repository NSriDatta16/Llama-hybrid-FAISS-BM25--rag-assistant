The same (similar) time series will have the same (similar) marginal distributions, but the converse does not follow. What's wrong with a plot of the time series? If you want a numerical comparison, interpolate both to the same grid of equally spaced points: there are choices of grid and of interpolation method, but you can calculate any measures of similarity or dissimilarity you like.
