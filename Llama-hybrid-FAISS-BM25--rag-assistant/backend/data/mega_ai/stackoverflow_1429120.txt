Though you've not asked for books, and because the answers above are so good, I'm only going to suggest you get your hands on copies of these two tomes: UNIX Network Programming, Volume 2, Second Edition: Interprocess Communications, W. Richard Stevens Advanced Programming in the UNIX Environment, Second Edition, W. Richard Stevens and Stephen A. Rago There are inevitable ins & outs with this kind of coding, these two books will help you through whatever confusion you encounter.
