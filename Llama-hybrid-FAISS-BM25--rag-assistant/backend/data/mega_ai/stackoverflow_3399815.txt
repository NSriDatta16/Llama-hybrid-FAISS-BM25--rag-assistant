Try the mean function for starters. Passing a matrix to it will result in all the columns being averaged and returns a row vector. Next, you need to subtract off the mean. To do that, the matrices must be the same size, so use repmat on your mean row vector. a=rand(10); abar=mean(a); abar=repmat(abar,size(a,1),1); anorm=a-abar; or the one-liner: anorm=a-repmat(mean(a),size(a,1),1);
