Aside from suggestions about bandwidth limits, you could easily be running into storage account limits, as each table partition is limited to roughly 500 transactions per second. Further: there's an optimization deployed (Nagle's algorithm) that could actually slow things down for small reads (such as your 1K data reads). Here's a blog post about disabling Nagling , which could potentially speed up your reads considerably, especially if you're running directly in an Azure service without Internet latency in the way.
