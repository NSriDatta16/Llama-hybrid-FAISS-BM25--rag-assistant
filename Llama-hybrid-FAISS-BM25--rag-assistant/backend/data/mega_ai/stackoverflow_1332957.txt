You would just grant the INSERT and SELECT privileges on the table in question (this prevents the possibility of a row being changed) GRANT INSERT,SELECT ON mydb.mytable TO secureduser@localhost IDENTIFIED BY 'password'; From this you would go on to add wider permissions to the other tables in the database for this user. Also, check out the Archive Storage Engine , which is custom designed for this kind of audit-trail application.
