One (of the many) things to consider with service API's is the ease in which they can be consumed by your end user. REST is gaining a very strong tooling presence. By far the largest dev group out there is the .NET dev group, and with ADO.NET for services (Astoria) consuming REST using Linq is very simple and very elegant.
