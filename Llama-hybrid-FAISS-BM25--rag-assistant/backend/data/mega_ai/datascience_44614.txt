Widely adopted method to measure similarity between two images is structural similarity: Wikipedia Useful links Implementation However, if you aim on training a network that should produce images of desired properties, I would consider GANs framework. In this case, the similarity between the generated and target image is defined by a special discriminator network. Depending on what is your task, there are many GAN sub-types.
