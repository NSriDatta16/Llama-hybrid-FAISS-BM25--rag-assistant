Yes, Devel::Cover is the way to go. If you develop a module, and use Module::Build to manage the installation, you even have a testcover target: perl Build.PL ./Build testcover That runs the whole test suite, and makes a combined coverage report in nice HTML, where you can browse through your modules and watch their coverage.
