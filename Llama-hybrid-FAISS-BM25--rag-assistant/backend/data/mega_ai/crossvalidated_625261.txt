One possibility is not enough digits, or, alternatively, wrong choice of units. B isn't 0, it's just Suppose you wanted to look at whether a person's weight was related to their having a heart attack. So, you gather data and do a logistic regression. But you measure weight in metric tons. You could then easily get a significant result with B = 0.000. So, change your units. Another possibility is that your N is really, really big. That seems less likely in this case.
