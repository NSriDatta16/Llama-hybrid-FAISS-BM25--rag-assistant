Start with the equation $$\Lambda (\alpha + \beta \cdot x) = \bar{y}$$ You have the logit coefficients $\alpha$ and $\beta$ and you can calculate $\bar y = \frac{1}{N}\sum_i^N y_i $ . Now you can solve that to get $x$ . Then you can calculate the marginal effect at that value of $x$ by taking the derivative.
