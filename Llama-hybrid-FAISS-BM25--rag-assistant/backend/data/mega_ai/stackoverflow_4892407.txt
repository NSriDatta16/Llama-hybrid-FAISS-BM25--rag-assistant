Should be trivial with a little bit of math. You can use getLatitudeSpan() and getLongitudeSpan() to get the extents of the visible map, and getMapCenter() to see the center point. Just map that to the coordinates of the touch event. EDIT: Mark, as always, has an even more elegant solution.
