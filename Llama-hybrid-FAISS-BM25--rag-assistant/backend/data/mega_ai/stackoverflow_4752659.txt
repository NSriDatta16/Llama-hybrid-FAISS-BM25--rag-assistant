You don't need the OCSP responder cert. Your OCSP request worked and you got back your answer: the cert ( amazon0.crt ) is good, not revoked. The line "no nonce in response" is due to the fact that VeriSign's OCSP responder doesn't send back nonces (due to the large volume of certs it must handle, VeriSign pre-signs the responses and therefore cannot include nonces - it adheres to RFC 5019). You can use -no_nonce to avoid sending a nonce. To get rid of the "Response Verify Failure" error message, I added the intermediate CA to my CAfile (in your case it's called veri-ssp-intermediate-ca.crt ).
