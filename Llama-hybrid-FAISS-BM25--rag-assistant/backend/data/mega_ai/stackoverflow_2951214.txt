Flex/Flash: Don't show 'bar' cursor when dragging over a TextField/TextArea?

For example, consider this interaction: alt text http://img.skitch.com/20100601-dhsqsgfsjphfdf3eambshww72m.png I'd like to prevent the cursor changing to the "bar" in step "2". How can I do that?
