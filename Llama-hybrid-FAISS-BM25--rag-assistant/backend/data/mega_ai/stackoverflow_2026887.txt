No, each database can only have one owner. As stated previously you can have more than one superuser, or you can grant permissions specifically to group roles that are then inherited. You might want to look at http://blog.hagander.net/archives/70-Faking-the-dbo-role.html , for a way to fake something similar to what you're asking for. It's not perfect, but it might be good enough for you. It should be able to solve the object-ownership problem at least.
