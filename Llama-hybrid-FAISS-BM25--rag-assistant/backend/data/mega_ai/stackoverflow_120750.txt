You could also take a look at the Hibernate Shards project: http://www.hibernate.org/414.html ... which is focused on adding support for horizontal partitioning to the Hibernate Core. It does not yet cover the full Hibernate API, but does support a large portion of it (which may or may not be sufficient for your needs). Of course, they are working towards complete coverage.
