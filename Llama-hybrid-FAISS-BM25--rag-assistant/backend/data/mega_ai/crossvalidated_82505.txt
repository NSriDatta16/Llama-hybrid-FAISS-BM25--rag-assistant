Should be something like this: library(MCMCglmm) m Afterwards, you should check to make sure assumptions of low autocorrelation between intervals are met, and that the chain has converged, using something like: plot(m) autocorr(m$Sol) There are some examples also given in the MCMCglmm vignette: vignette('Overview',package='MCMCglmm')
