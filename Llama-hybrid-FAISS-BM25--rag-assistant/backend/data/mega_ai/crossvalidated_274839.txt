You don't need to centre the variables for varpart() (either the response or the predictors). If you have your variables coded as factors and not dummy variables, then vegan will create the correct contrasts for you. So for a variable colour with three levels red , green , and blue , you'd have this as a single factor variable in R with those levels: levs rather than three hand-coded dummy variables env
