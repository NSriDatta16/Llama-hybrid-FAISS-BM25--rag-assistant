Although it may be possible to run a multiple logistic regression on your data, you should be very wary of the results. A major danger that you face is that your model, however well it might fit your present data set, will not generalize to your population of interest. The issues in having such a low number of cases to predictors is covered on this Cross Validated page , with several useful links from that page.
