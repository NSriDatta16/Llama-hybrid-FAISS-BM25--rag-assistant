They are the same. The answer was that you had to invoke the parser in a way that would cause tokenization of the input. Some examples are: LexicalizedParser lp = LexicalizedParser.loadModel("edu/stanford/nlp/models/lexparser/englishPCFG.ser.gz"); String sent = "This is one last test!"; lp.apply(sent).pennPrint(); or in a more complex scenario: TreebankLanguagePack tlp = lp.getOp().langpack(); Tokenizer toke = tlp.getTokenizerFactory().getTokenizer(new StringReader(sent)); List sentence = toke.tokenize(); lp.apply(sentence);
