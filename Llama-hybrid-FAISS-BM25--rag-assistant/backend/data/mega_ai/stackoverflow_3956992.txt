The error is because you are appending a numeric value to a string. Here are the alternatives cnn1.execute("insert into tags values(" & cint(rst1.fields(0).value) & ")") OR cnn1.execute("insert into tags values("+ rst1.fields(0).value +")") Use & when you want something to appear as part of the string.
