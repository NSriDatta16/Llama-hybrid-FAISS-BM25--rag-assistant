Figured it out. This is what I found to work: Cache-Control:no-cache, no-store Connection:Close Content-Length:7683 Content-Type:text/html; charset=utf-8 Date:Wed, 09 Jun 2010 03:37:38 GMT Expires:-1 Pragma:no-cache Server:ASP.NET Development Server/9.0.0.0 X-AspNet-Version:2.0.50727 X-AspNetMvc-Version:2.0 achieved with the following ASP.NET code: Response.Cache.SetCacheability(HttpCacheability.NoCache); Response.Cache.SetAllowResponseInBrowserHistory(false); Response.Cache.SetMaxAge(new TimeSpan(0)); Response.Cache.SetNoStore(); Response.Cache.SetExpires(new DateTime(1940, 1, 1));
