This is one reason why using a separate ViewModel for every page is recommended as a best practice, and using your Entity classes directly in the ViewModel is discouraged. With separate ViewModels, you can tailor the attribute-based validation as needed. It will complicate things in that you will then need to find a way to map between the ViewModels and your Entities.
