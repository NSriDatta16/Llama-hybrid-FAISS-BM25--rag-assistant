You can use the JPA annotations @PostLoad, @PostPersist, @PostRemove, @PostUpdate, @PrePersist, @PreRemove, @PreUpdate in void methods without parameters inside your entity class. They will be called when the event is performed for a specific entity. http://download.oracle.com/javaee/5/api/index.html?javax/persistence/package-summary.html
