You could create your own faces tag library to make it less verbose, something like: Then create the taglib in your view dir: /components/ph.taglib.xml http://peterhilton.com/core i18n i18n.xhtml create /components/i18n.xhtml You can probably find an elegant way of passing the arguments with a little research. Now register your new taglib in web.xml facelets.LIBRARIES /components/ph.taglib.xml Just add xmlns:ph="http://peterhilton.com/core" to your views and you're all set!
