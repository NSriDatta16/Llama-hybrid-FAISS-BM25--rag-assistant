Use jslint to check your code. If you do this and get it passing with regards to semicolons, it should pack correctly. In JavaScript, semicolons are implicitly added at newlines. This introduces situations that can be ambiguous. This blog post: http://robertnyman.com/2008/10/16/beware-of-javascript-semicolon-insertion/ describes the problem succinctly and gives an example.
