You can use the window function to extract a subset of a time series. For example : R> myts myts Qtr1 Qtr2 Qtr3 Qtr4 2001 1 2 3 4 2002 5 6 7 8 2003 9 10 11 12 2004 13 14 15 16 2005 17 18 19 20 2006 21 22 23 24 2007 25 26 27 28 2008 29 30 31 32 2009 33 34 35 36 2010 37 38 39 40 And then : R> subts subts Qtr1 Qtr2 Qtr3 Qtr4 2005 18 19 20 2006 21 22 23 24 2007 25 26 27 28 2008 29 30 31 The result is still a ts object : R> is.ts(subts) [1] TRUE
