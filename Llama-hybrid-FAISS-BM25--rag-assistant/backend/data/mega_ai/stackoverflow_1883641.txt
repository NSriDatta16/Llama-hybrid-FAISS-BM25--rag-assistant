I personally don't like dashes and dots in the entry because they look untidy. On the other hand, they draw attention. A question of preference and philosphy. Anyway, it has to be a clearly understandable call to action (= you need to click here, otherwise you won't be able to continue). My favourites in descending order: Click to select Select one Please select one An empty field is o.k. only if the selection can stay empty and is not required for the form to be complete.
