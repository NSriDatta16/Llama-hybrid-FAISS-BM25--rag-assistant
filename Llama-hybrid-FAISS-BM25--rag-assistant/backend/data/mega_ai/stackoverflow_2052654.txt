There's a simple HTTP server embedded in the Sun 1.6 JRE. It's not JavaEE or servlet-compliant, it's very lightweight, but it might be good enough for your requirements. No need to download any 3rd party stuff if this is all you need. The javadocs, rather bizarrely, are out on their own, here .
