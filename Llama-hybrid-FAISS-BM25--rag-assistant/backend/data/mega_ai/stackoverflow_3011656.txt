There's always the good ol' trick: console.log("\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n"); or a shorter variation of the above: console.log('\n'.repeat('25')); Not the most elegant solution, I know :) ... but works. For me, I usually just print a long "-----" separator line to help make the logs easier to read.
