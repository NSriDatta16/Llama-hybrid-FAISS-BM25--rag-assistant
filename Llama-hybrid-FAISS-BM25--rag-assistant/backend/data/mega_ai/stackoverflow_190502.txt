The culture info is in thread-local storage, so if the background worker runs processes on different threads, this may be expected. http://msdn.microsoft.com/en-us/library/system.globalization.cultureinfo.currentculture.aspx http://msdn.microsoft.com/en-us/library/system.threading.thread.currentculture.aspx I am not sure what the recommended practice for transfering culture info across threads, though.
