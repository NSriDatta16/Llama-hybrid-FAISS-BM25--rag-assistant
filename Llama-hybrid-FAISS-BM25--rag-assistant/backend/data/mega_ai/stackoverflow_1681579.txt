Even if you could technically accomplish this -- and a few solutions come to mind, all involving some variation of screen-scraping -- you would be violating the Facebook terms of service and US privacy law . You can accomplish this with Facebook Connect, and it does not require redirection to Facebook. It's just a simple pop-up window where the user enters their Facebook username/password. It's very easy, very elegant, and has the advantage of not landing you in prison. :-)
