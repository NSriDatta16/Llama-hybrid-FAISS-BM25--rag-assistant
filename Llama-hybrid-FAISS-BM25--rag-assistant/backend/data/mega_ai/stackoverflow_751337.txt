I'm not sure about Tkabber, but there are various clients that'll let you play with PubSub. None of them exposes innovative user interfaces that leverages the unique concepts of PubSub : they are mostly developer tools for basic CRUD in the service discovery node tree. But maybe this is just what you need... Instant messaging clients with PubSub service discovery : Miranda Psi Specialized PubSub administration clients : PubSubClient x60br
