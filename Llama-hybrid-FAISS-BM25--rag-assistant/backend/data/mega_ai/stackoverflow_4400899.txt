You need to use a NSDateFormatter . I encourage you to read the class reference as linked by Kay . Here's how you'd do it. I'm assuming the date is originating as a NSString . NSDateFormatter *formatter = [[NSDateFormatter alloc] init]; [formatter setDateFormat:@"MM-dd-yyyy HH:mm:ss"]; NSDate *myDate = [df dateFromString:yourNSString]; NSString *myNewDate = [formatter stringFromDate:myDate]; [formatter release]; [myDate release];
