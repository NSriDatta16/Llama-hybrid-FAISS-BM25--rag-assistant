There is also the YUI 2 Storage Utility which abstracts the storage for you (HTML 5, Google Gears, SWF) depending on what the browser supports: The Storage Utility provides a mechanism for storing significant amounts of textual data, client-side, whether or not your browsers supports the proposed HTML 5 Storage specification.
