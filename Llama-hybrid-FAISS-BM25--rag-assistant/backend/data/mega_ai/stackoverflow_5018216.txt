There is no equivalent to COM for Mozilla that's available to non plugin code. Plugins can use XPCOM . If you want something like ADODB.Connection, take a look at the new HTML5 local storage APIs. There's a good tutorial at http://paperkilledrock.com/2010/05/html5-localstorage-part-one/
