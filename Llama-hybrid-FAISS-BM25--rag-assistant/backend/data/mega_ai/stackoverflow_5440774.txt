I had a similar requirement and you just require one more pig statement to remove the redundancies in 5 (1,5) (2,5). a = LOAD 'edgelist' USING PigStorage('\t') AS (user:int,following:int); b = GROUP a BY user; x = FOREACH b GENERATE group.user, a.following; store x INTO 'following-list';
