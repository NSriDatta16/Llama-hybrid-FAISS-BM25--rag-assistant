It is probably best to perform multiple imputations of your missing data, rather than simple interpolation. That way you can capture inter-relations of variables beyond time alone, and can estimate the uncertainties introduced by the imputation process. The Amelia package in R was designed to address this issue for time series; its rationale is presented here .
