To get immediate processing of mails without polling, this should be integrated into your mail delivery process (MDA, Mail Delivery Agent). On most Unix boxes, this can be easily done via procmail and formail (part of procmail). If you have more control over your mailer, tighter integration might be possible for more performance || better error handling.
