Don't do this. You are deviating from what people expect to see when they read a paper about algorithms. You should follow expected practices; your ideas are more likely to get the attention that they deserve. When in Rome, do as the Romans do. Formatting code (or pseudocode as it may be) in a LaTeXed paper is very easy. See, for example, Formatting code in LaTeX .
