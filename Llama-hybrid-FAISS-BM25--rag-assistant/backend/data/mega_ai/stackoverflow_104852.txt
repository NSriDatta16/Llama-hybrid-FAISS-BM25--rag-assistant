Simple. Put your map and the clickable buttons into a new MovieClip, you could call it interactiveMapContainer or something similar, then call your startDrag method on interactiveMapContainer and you'll still be able to click the buttons once you've dragged it about. Jakub Kotrla's method will also work very well, although it is slightly more complicated.
