All machine learning algorithm operates only on numerical dependent variables. Ordinal dependent variable could be either implicitly treated as nominal by R 'factor' or Py Panda 'categorical' or you will need to convert/encode them into nominal. Refer to "encoding categorical data" section in https://www.datacamp.com/community/tutorials/categorical-data
