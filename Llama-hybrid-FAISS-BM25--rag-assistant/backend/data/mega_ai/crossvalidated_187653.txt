Convolution is a function-valued operation on two functions $(f*g)(t) = \int _{-\infty }^{\infty }f(\tau )g(t-\tau )d\tau$. A common statistical application is obtaining the density of a sum of independent random variables. DO NOT use this tag for convolutional neural networks. See https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Convolution and https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Deconvolution
