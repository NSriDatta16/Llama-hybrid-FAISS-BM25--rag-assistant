It's not relative to PHP only. What those words mean are "design patterns" concept, that is, a method of organizing code that achieves a series of properties, such as loose dependency, ease of reuse and test. I suggest you a book colloquially called Gang of four . It explain basic design patterns and it's a classic in its topic.
