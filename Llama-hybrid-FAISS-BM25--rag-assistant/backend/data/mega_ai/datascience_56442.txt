Make sure to increase the value of n_estimators when you decrease the learning_rate, otherwise the model will stop too early to find the optimal values. This should be done after tuning the other parameters. Here are a couple of good tutorials regarding the tuning of the hyperparameters: https://blog.cambridgespark.com/hyperparameter-tuning-in-xgboost-4ff9100a3b2f https://www.analyticsvidhya.com/blog/2016/03/complete-guide-parameter-tuning-xgboost-with-codes-python/
