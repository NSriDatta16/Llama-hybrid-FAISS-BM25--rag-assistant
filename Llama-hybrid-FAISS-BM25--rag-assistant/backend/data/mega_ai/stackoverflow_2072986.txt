You can download "slowmo" from http://mschrag.github.com/ to re-enable the slow motion feature, allowing you to toggle between modes. I tested it on the iPhone Simulator 3.1 (139.1) and it still works. Run the slowmo.sh script in the download, then press shift 3 times in quick succession.
