In distributed systems you often see a broker or scheduler allocating work to workers or agents; the scheduler might be implemented with some knowledge of the workload, and be able to split it up, or might just manage allocating tasks from a queue to workers. I've yet to find a shared description or name for this pattern -- but take a look here for a starting point: Master-Worker pattern in distributed computing .
