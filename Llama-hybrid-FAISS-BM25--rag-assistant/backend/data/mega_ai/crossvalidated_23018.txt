whuber is right -- particularly given your background in computer science -- but ... The DATEVALUE function will convert many types of text strings into an Excel numeric date. Otherwise, you may have to play around with the FIND and MID functions to get the substrings you need. I can definitely see that the FIND and MID approach will work on the snippet you sent. Then, your best bet is to use a pivot table to organize the data into some meaningful chunks to graph.
