There are quite a few implementations of fixed-point arithmetic. However, we often run out of decimal places very, very quickly with fixed-point storage. It's ideal for monetary transactions, where we know that we aren't going to store/care about any irrational numbers. Additionally, for a lot of other things, fixed-point arithmetic just isn't worth the overhead. Floating point is just a lot faster. Things to read: Floats Fixed-point arithmetic Decimal vs Double
