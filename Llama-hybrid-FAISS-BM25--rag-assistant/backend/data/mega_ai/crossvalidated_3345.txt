By the tone of your question, it may be handy to read a little bit about generating draws from a posterior. Two introductory springer books (full of R codes) comes to mind (in order of preference): a) Bayesian computation with R. Springer, 2007. J. Albert. b) Introducing Monte Carlo Methods with R. Springer 2010. C. P. Robert, G. Casella You will find more detailed explanations and ways to circumvent important captcha's in these that you could possibly get from a 15 lines answer on here.
