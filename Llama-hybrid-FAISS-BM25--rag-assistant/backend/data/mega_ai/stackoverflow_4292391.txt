/Wall is very pedantic. /W4 is probably all you really need. To answer your question, you can disable specific warnings around your headers with: #pragma warning(disable:xxxx) #include #pragma warning(default:xxxx) Or change the warning level with: #pragma warning(push,3) #include #pragma warning(pop) See the MSDN documentation: http://msdn.microsoft.com/en-us/library/2c8f766e.aspx
