Unless you really want the printout to skip all the text which happens to be on a logical line wider than the physical line on paper, you'll be better off to use something other than : Take the string from the textbox, wrap its paragraphs into ... and then send that to the browser wrapped into a .. . Or, even simpler: Send a ... where ... is your sting with the line feeds just replaced by (or , respectively).
