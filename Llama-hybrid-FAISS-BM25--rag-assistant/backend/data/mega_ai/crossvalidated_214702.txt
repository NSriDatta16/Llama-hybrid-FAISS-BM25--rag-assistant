The alternative hypothesis is β1≠0 OR β2≠0 OR β3≠0, via De Morgan's Laws. Proving any of those three conditions would disprove the null hypothesis. The breakdown β1≠β2 OR β2≠β3 OR β3≠0 is mathematically equivalent but might be easier to show significance or design an experiment for.
