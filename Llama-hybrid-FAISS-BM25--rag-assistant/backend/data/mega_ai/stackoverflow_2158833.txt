assuming that you can't develop the feature in a way that prevents or at least warns on misuse. have examples of usage of your new features. use these in your presentation. make the examples available to everyone - via the means most appropriate to your organisation. also add links/reference to the examples from the documentation of the class. if possible, examples should be from where the feature is really used. this removes the need to create additional examples that are not maintained with the live codebase.
