Featured Items and Player of the Week will depend (usually) on your project specific Player / Item models. It shouldn't take long to program these small pieces. As far as news, there are quite a few different solutions out there. Here's one that I've created (because I can't think of a better one off hand): http://github.com/monokrome/django-news/ The best bet with django is to find the smaller apps you might be able to leverage, and then write your own project with your custom wants/needs in mind.
