Try adding the jar to your Lotus Notes classpath in notes.ini : JavaUserClasses=...;yourAgent.jar or copy your jar to the default Notes lib directory, which should be something like: C:\lotus\notes\jvm\lib\ext Another thing to try is to put your dll in C:\lotus\notes.
