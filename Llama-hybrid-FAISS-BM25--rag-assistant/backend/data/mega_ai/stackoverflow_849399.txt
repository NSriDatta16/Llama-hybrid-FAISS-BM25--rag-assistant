Check out Bertrand LeRoy's "Using ScriptManager with other frameworks" http://weblogs.asp.net/bleroy/archive/2008/07/07/using-scriptmanager-with-other-frameworks.aspx Contains a control that inherits from ScriptManager which undoes some of its built-in script additions and allows you to replace it with whatever framework you like.
