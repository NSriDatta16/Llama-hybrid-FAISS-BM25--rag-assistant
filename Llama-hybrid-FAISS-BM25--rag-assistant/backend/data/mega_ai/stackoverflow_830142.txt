Take a look at the UIScrollViewDelegate protocol. You can set your view controller as the delegate for the scroll view and implement some of the methods there, like: – scrollViewDidScroll: – scrollViewWillBeginDragging: – scrollViewDidEndDragging:willDecelerate: – scrollViewShouldScrollToTop: – scrollViewDidScrollToTop: – scrollViewWillBeginDecelerating: – scrollViewDidEndDecelerating:
