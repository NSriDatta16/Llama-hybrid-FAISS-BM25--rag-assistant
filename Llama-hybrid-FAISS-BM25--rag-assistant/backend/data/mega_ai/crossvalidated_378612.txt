The second thing would be the intent. While you could definitely regard it as just a kind of shorthand, it can also be seen as intended somewhat more formally if you regard it as specifying the cdf in terms of component cdfs, since the cdf of a mixture with mixing weights $\mathbf{w}$ is the weighted average of the cdfs with the same mixing weights.
