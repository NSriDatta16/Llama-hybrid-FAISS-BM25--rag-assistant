Different variance estimator for multilevel ordinal logistic regression in Stata and R

Among other data, I use this Stata datafile (from Stata help for meologit): http://www.stata-press.com/data/r13/tvsfpors.dta I estimate (among others) this model: # R formula syntax library(ordinal) library(readstata13) mydata The columns in the variance-covariance-matrix for the variance of the latent higher-level variable seem to differ systematically. The covariances differ by an identical constant, and the variances (of the variance) themselves by the squared term of the constant. The constant differ dependent on the data and model structure.
