You first need to get the index to the min value: [minVal, minInd] = min( MATRIX(:,2) ); And then access the 1st row at that index: MATRIX(minInd,1); A little less elegant syntax would be: MATRIX(find(MATRIX(:,2)==min(MATRIX(:,2)),1));
