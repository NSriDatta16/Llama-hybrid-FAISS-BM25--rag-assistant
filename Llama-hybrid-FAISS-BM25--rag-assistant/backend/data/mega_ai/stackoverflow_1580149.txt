It's not exactly clear how your various EmplId grouping requirements relate to counting employees, but this should get you started: query = From j In db.table1s _ Join a In db.table3s On j.EMPLID Equals a.emplid _ Join d In db.table2s On d.ORG Equals j.Org _ Where a.appcode = "neo" _ Where a.success = True _ Group By j.Org, d.ORGANIZATION_DESCRIPTION Into Count() VB Group By on MSDN
