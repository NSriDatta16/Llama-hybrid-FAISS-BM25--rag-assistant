Precise meaning of and comparison between influential point, high leverage point, and outlier?

From Wikipedia Influential observations are those observations that have a relatively large effect on the regression model's predictions. From Wikipedia Leverage points are those observations, if any, made at extreme or outlying values of the independent variables such that the lack of neighboring observations means that the fitted regression model will pass close to that particular observation.
