The solution for my own question is this : Add a TextBlock to your template Use Template Binding > Content for its Text property (And other properties if needed) Align the TextBlock ( Strech for Horizontal and Center for Vertical) Use Center for TextBlock's Text Alignment property under Paragraph tab. Remove Content Presnter You're done!
