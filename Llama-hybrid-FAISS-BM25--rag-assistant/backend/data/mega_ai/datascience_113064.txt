One way to go about it: You identify the positions in the time series where the outliers have occurred and predict a yes / no (binary classification) for the future i.e. whether a particular observation in the future will be an outlier or not. The feature engineering involved with this approach might be slightly tricky and will be heavily dependent on what data you have.
