Actually a three layer neural network can model arbitrary function with the linear and logistic functions, which was proved by Kolmogorov in 1957 (Kolmogorov, Andrei Nikolaevich. "On the representation of continuous functions of many variables by superposition of continuous functions of one variable and addition." Dokl. Akad. Nauk SSSR . Vol. 114. No. 5. 1957.).
