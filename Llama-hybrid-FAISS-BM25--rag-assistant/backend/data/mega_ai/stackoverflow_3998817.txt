Say, you have 2 blocks of scores: 1st: n scores with average = a1 2nd: m scores with average = a2 then average of total scores equals: a1*(1.0*n/(m+n))+a2*(1.0*m/(m+n)) In case you want just add 1 score (a2) to existing set formula becomes a1*(n/(n+1))+ a2/(n+1)
