This is what you have posted. I suspect the cross joins are where your problem lies. SELECT DISTINCT a1.[CreateDate], a2.[UserName], c1.[name], c1.[organisation], c1.[address], a1.[IsApproved], a1.[UserId] FROM [aspnet_Membership] CROSS JOIN [RolesAndAllUsers] a2 CROSS JOIN [clients] c1 INNER JOIN [aspnet_Membership] a1 ON c1.userid = a1.UserId INNER JOIN [clients] c2 ON c2.userid = a2.UserId WHERE a1.IsApproved = 'False'
