Adapter or Wrapper (I've never heard anyone call it Verneer) is one of the Gang of Four Design Patterns. Here's what it's for: Convert the interface of a class into another interface clients expect. Adapter lets classes work together that couldn't otherwise because of incompatible interfaces Have a look at this . The definitive reference, now 15 years old, is the Gang of Four "Design Patterns" .
