If this is going to be data for JavaScript's purposes, why even try to put it in the HTML at all? Here's a simple example of what I mean var nodeData = { foo: {/* Data to associate with div#foo */} , bar: {/* Data to associate with div#bar */} }; Foo! Bar! It's simple and elegant since the principle of IDs being unique per document nicely matches with javascript's dictionaries requiring unique keys per entry.
