Any chance this is implemented in .NET? - if so take a look at the Reactive Framework . It provides a very elegant way of handling streams and especially cancelling them on the fly. On top of this, you get a very extensible library of Linq extension for all sorts of stuff, like Buffering, Memoization, Zip ect.. We use it a lot for transforming (and parsing), modelling of streamed data. Jeff from the Reative team has a couble of nice blogs about Streaming and Reative here :
