Consider using a portal framework like uPortal . You could aggregate all those webapps -- no matter what technologies they're written in -- by leveraging a web proxy solution . This suggestion might be overkill if your needs are really & truly simple; but uPortal (and others) provide scores of additional functions -- like administration, statistics, SSO, and integrations with other platforms and services -- that quickly add to their attractiveness.
