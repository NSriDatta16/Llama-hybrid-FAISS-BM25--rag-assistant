M-q is bound to fill-paragraph, what it is doing is trying to do is intelligently turn the text into a paragraph. It has features that try to guess the 'fill-prefix, which is what appears to be happening to you. You can unbind M-q if you don't like it. (global-unset-key (kbd "M-q"))
