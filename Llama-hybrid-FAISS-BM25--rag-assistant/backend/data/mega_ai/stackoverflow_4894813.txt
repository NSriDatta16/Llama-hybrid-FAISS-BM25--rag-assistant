You need to set your expires into the future. Try changing your Expires, Cache-Control, and Pragma to look something like this: Cache-Control:max-age=300 Date:Fri, 04 Feb 2011 04:52:58 GMT Expires:Fri, 04 Feb 2011 04:57:58 GMT ... (remove Pragma. You can do this by editing you .htaccess file)
