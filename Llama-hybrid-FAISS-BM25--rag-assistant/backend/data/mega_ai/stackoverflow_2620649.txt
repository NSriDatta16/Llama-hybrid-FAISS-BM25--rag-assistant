var cars = session .CreateCriteria () .CreateAlias("InteriorParts", "parts") .CreateAlias("parts.Producer", "producer") .Add(Expression.Eq("producer.Id", id)) .List(); Depending on your case you might also want to filter duplicate cars that will be fetched due to the SQL joins: .SetResultTransformer(Transformers.DistinctRootEntity)
