You want take a weighted average over all the vertices. So say your vertices are v1, v2, v3 .... vn with masses m1, m2 ...mn and have x and y coordinates v1x, v1y, v2x, v2y etc then to get the center of mass (cx, cy) you want: cx = (v1x*m1 + v2x*m2 + ... vnx*mn) / (m1 + m2 .... mn) cy = (v1y*m1 + v2y*m2 + ... vny*mn) / (m1 + m2 .... mn) It's essentially the same principle as when you do it for a line.
