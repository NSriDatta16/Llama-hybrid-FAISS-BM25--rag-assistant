There are some articles that are worth reading, to understand why some people who are far smarter than I are wary of exceptions: Joel on Software - 13 - Exceptions Joel on Software - Making Wrong code look Wrong The Old New Thing - Cleaner, more elegant, and harder to recognize My favorite example is: if you use exceptions, this code is very likely wrong - and its impossible to tell just by looking at it: void SomeMethod(){ m_i++; SomeOtherMethod(); m_j++; }
