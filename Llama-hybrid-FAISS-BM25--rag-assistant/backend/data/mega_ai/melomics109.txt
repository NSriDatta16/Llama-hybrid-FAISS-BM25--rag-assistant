Melomics109 is a computer cluster located at Universidad de MÃ¡laga used to create digital music. It is part of the Spanish Supercomputing Network, and has been designed to increase the computational power provided by Iamus. Powered by Melomics' technology, the composing module of Melomics109 is able to create and synthesize music in a variety of musical styles. This music has been made freely accessible to everyone. The cluster consists of three cabinets with customized front panels. Album 0music is the first album composed and interpreted by Melomics109. It was launched in July 2014, and was released in audio (MP3) and editable format (MIDI), under CC0 (public domain) licensing.
