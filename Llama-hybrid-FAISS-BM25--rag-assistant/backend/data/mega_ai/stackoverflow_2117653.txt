This is called hungarian notation and considered bad practice. According to Robert C. Martin, author of "Clean Code", type encodings "make it harder to change the name or type of a variable, function, or class. They make it harder to read the code. And they create the possibility that the encoding system will mislead the reader." Don't use them. Choose your variable names wisely.
