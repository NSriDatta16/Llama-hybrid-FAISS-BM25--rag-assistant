You can fit the time series to an AR(1) process. The regression coefficients and autocorrelation function will give you an indication of its mean reversion properties. The regression parameters of the AR(1) model can be mapped into an Ornstein Uhlenbeck process: the appendix of the Avellaneda and Lee paper has an example of the AR(1) $\rightarrow$ OU mapping
