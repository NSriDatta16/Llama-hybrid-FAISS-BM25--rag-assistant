It's not clear what you mean by 'percentage', but I'll show you how to group by month to get the max price. The percentage is probably an aggragation too, which you can add later. select Area, HouseType, max(PriceSold) as MaxPriceSold from YourTable group by Area, HouseType, Year(DateSold), Month(DateSold)
