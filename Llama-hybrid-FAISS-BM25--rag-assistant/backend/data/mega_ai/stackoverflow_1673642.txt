There are several Flex frameworks that can help you: I personally like Mate . There's also Swiz Cairngorm is very established Spring Actionscript , which used to be called Prana, is a port of some of Spring to Actionscript. PureMVC Each of these frameworks will help you better organize your models and services. Take a look at each of them and choose one that you're comfortable with.
