Starting with Visual Studio 2010 and C# 4.0, you can avoid these large dependencies on the office SDK by taking advantage of a feature called NoPia or Interop Type Embedding. This feature will essentially embed all COM interop types used from a given PIA into your application. This frees you from the burden of having to deploy it as part of your application. Here's a quick link on the subject. http://blogs.msdn.com/mshneer/archive/2008/10/28/advances-in-net-type-system-type-equivalence-demo.aspx
