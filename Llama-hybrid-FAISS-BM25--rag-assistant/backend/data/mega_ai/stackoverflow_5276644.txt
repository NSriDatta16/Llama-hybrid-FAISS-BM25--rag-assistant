This isn't really an answer to your question, but sIFR isn't really used anymore. Font-embedding with CSS @font-face is the preferred way to deliver custom fonts. Check out: http://code.google.com/apis/webfonts/ http://www.fontsquirrel.com/ For some free fonts to embed.
