svm_problem() takes two parameters: the first parameter of a vector of labels, and the second is a matrix of features. You get this assertion error because you are only specifying 2 labels, [1, -1] , as the first parameter in your call to svm_problem . Example: y = [1,-1,1,1] x = [[1,0,1], [-1,0,-1], [1,2,3], [4,5,6]] prob = svm_problem(y, x)
