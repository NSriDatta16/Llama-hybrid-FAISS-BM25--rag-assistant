Better results that DTW? For the problems of time series classification, there is nothing significantly better than DTW, see https://arxiv.org/abs/1602.01711 As for "better execution time", DTW with a warping constraint and LB_keogh is fast enough for virtually any task. See http://www.cs.unm.edu/~mueen/DTW.pdf
