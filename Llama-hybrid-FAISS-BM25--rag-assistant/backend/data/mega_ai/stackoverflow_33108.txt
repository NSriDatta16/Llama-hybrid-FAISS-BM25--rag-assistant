You'd probably need to chain an actual virus scanner to the upload process (the same way many virus scanners ensure that a file you download in your browser is safe). In order to do this yourself, you'd have to keep it up to date, which means keeping libraries of virus definitions around, which is likely beyond the scope of your application (and may not even be feasible depending on the size of your organization).
