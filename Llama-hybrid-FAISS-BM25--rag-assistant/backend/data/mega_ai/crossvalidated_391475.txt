Dropout as been shown to be an approximate variational inference, and variational inference is an approximation of the Bayesian posterior. See Gal & Ghahramani for more details or Yarin Gal's thesis. Gal, Y., & Ghahramani, Z. (2016, June). Dropout as a Bayesian approximation: Representing model uncertainty in deep learning. In international conference on machine learning (pp. 1050-1059). Gal, Y. (2016) . Uncertainty in deep learning. University of Cambridge.
