"Lines of code" is probably not the best metric to optimize. This looks like a classic example of outgrowing a "dictionaries and lists" solution. I've been there many times. Use a class, it will let you write understandable maintainable code, with named methods for manipulating your data structure. This will give you flexibility to hide and change the underlying storage without changing the semantics, or the caller's view of the data.
