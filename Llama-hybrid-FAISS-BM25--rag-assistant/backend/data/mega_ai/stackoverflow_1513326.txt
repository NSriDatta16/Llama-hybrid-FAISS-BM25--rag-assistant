Several Apple examples use the following formula to convert the decibels into a linear range (from 0.0 to 1.0): double percentage = pow (10, (0.05 * power)); where power is the value you get from one of the various level meter methods or functions, such as AVAudioPlayer's averagePowerForChannel:
