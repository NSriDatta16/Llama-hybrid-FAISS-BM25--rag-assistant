It's not a book and not addressing optimization, but one of the best resources to self-learn calculus are the lectures by Gilbert Strang that were recorded and are available on YouTube. He also wrote a great handbook. If you would find Strang a little bit too hard, I'll recommend starting with the Khan Academy lectures.
