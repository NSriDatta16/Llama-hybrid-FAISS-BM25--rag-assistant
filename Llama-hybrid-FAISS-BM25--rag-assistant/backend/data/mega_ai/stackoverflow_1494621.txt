You could grab the current height of the Div being dragged, save it in a variable and assign a generic height to the DIV being dragged. Once it's placed, use the call back to reassign the height within the variable. var curHeight = $(div).height(); $(div).css({height: 20px;}); You may also want to consider forceHelperSize
