You should always use the right tag for the job. is used to divide the page into logical sections. is for paragraphs of text. through are used for headings. and represent lists of data. and it's children are for tabular data. is for forms and so on. Working this way has benefits for SEO because the search engines know what sort of data they're reading and can rank it's importance accordingly. It also gets you a good way towards improving accessibility (e.g. screen readers will often build a page outline based on heading structure). More importantly for you, it makes it a lot easier to style your website consistently.
