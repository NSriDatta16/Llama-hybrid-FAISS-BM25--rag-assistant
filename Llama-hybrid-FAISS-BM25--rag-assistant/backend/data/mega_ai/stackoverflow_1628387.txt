"Efficiency" could mean many things. For Ruby green threads mean that you won't get as much concurrency as you might think out of threads, so using multiple script instances would be the best way to minimize total real time from start to finish. As to creating them, here is t he Pickaxe book example of downloading pages "in parallel": require 'net/http' pages = %w( www.rubycentral.com www.awl.com www.pragmaticprogrammer.com ) threads = [] for page in pages threads
