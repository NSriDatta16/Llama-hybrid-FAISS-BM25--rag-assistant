ADO is the mechanism/code that retrieves data from the database. Virtually all database send and retrieval operations use ADO. DataSets and Datatables are mediums( or classes) to transfer data between the database to the point where it's consumed in the application. Unless it's a throw away applications I avoid DataSets/DataTables. Explanation why.
