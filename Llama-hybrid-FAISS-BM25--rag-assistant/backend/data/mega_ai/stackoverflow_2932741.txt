I'd prefer to use mirroring or log shipping for something like this, but those options are off the table if your second PC is running Express. The distribution agent will retry, however if the disconnect continues for an extended amount of time, your subscription may be deactivated or expired/dropped. Your publisher must be at least Workgroup edition (which limits the number of subscribers). See these links for the specifics on which editions support which features.
