It isn't handled by Windows messages, even though a message loop is required to make it work. Classic COM requirement. Start reading at RegisterDragDrop() to see the plumbing. Notable is that the UIPI aspect of UAC gets in the way, you cannot D+D from a non-elevated process to an elevated one. ChangeWindowsMessageFilter() is the usual workaround, it doesn't work for D+D. No know workaround.
