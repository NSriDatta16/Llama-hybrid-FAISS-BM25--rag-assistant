A service-based solution will mostly help you if you have a system that is consumed by multiple clients, which need to share common data or functionality. Otherwise, I don't see its benefit to you as it pertains to the question. You'll probably want to leverage user controls and different classes that resolve different business objectives. Look into separation of concerns design pattern.
