You can "buy speed" by sacrificing storage space or onserve storage space but got worse performance. As your problem is speed, you'll need some precalculations. And yes, some profiling of the query. BTW, the "big ones" used to have different configs (different hardware and settings) for OLTP (serving the actual transactions in real time) and DW (analysing large amounts of data).
