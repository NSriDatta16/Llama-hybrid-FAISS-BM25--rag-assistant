This paper answers your question: Hunt, C.; Taplin, R. Aggregation of Incidence and Intensity Risk Variables to Achieve Reconciliation. Risks 2019, 7, 107 pdf download free from: https://www.mdpi.com/2227-9091/7/4/107 In brief, EAD is aggregated by simple summation, whereas PD and LGD are aggregated per a new kind of "mean" that we have proposed, such that the product EAD * PD * LGD of these aggregates does exactly reconcile to the correct total EL - or, interpreted using your words: the total EL is decomposed as the product of these three aggregates.
