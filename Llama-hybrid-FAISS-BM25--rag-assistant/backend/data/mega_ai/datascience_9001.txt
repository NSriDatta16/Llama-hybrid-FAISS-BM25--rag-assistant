In Data Skeptic they talk about different aspects of data science: Data Skeptic is a podcast that alternates between short mini episodes with the host explaining concepts from data science to his non-data scientist wife, and longer interviews featuring practitioners and experts on interesting topics related to data, all through the eye of scientific skepticism. This is a good intro to Data Science which also explains the basic concepts in a not too technical way. A bit more technical is Partially Derivative : Partially Derivative is a podcast about the data of everything. Each week we look at a new way that data has changed how we understand and experience the world. From ancient warfare to modern love and everything in between.
