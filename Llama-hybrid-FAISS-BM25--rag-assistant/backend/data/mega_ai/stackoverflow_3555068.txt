You could put the sessionId (key) and timestamp (value) in a static HashMap on creation (indicated by session.isNew() ). And check during polling in your servlet whether you want to time out the session, which could be performed by invoking session.invalidate() http://download-llnw.oracle.com/javaee/1.2.1/api/javax/servlet/http/HttpSession.html#invalidate%28%29
