Why CIFAR 10 Vs. CIFAR 100 is the most popular for OOD benchmark?

CIFAR 10 Vs. CIFAR 100 is the most popular dataset for the task of Out-of-Distribution performance evaluation. On the infamous "Papers-with-code" [1] CIFAR 10Vs.100 is the most used Benchmark for OOD. Also on Google's blog announcing their new state-of-the-art pre-trained embedding model pixel, CIFAR 10Vs.100 is used for benchmarking OOD [2]. [1] https://paperswithcode.com/datasets?task=out-of-distribution-detection [2] https://ai.googleblog.com/2022/07/towards-reliability-in-deep-learning.html
