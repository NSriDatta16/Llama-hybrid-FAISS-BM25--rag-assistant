This works as expected: System.out.printf("%30s|%30s%n","Organization","Number of users"); System.out.printf("%30s|%30s%n","Arcot","100"); results in Organization| Number of users Arcot| 100 on my machine. You didn't add line feeds. %n is the preferred notation in format Strings.
