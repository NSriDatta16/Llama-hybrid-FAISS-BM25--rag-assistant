It's not a bug. Unlike Ephemeral, EBS based instances don't give you any free storage. You can however create a new EBS volume and attach it to your instance. Cheers Ray EDIT: I believe I led you astray as this seems to have changed. You still cannot attach local disk to an existing running instance but this does seem to be possible at launch time.
