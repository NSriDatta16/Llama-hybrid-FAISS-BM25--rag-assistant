I also tried to get the field1 = 'a' OR field2 = 'b' Your code didn't work for me. Here is my solution $results = Mage::getModel('xyz/abc')->getCollection(); $results->addFieldToSelect('name'); $results->addFieldToSelect('keywords'); $results->addOrder('name','ASC'); $results->setPageSize(5); $results->getSelect()->where("keywords like '%foo%' or additional_keywords like '%bar%'"); $results->load(); echo json_encode($results->toArray()); It gives me SELECT name, keywords FROM abc WHERE keywords like '%foo%' OR additional_keywords like '%bar%' . It is maybe not the "magento's way" but I was stuck 5 hours on that. Hope it will help
