Unfortunately, embedding one form in another is nearly impossible in Drupal. That is due to its form-handling and node-handling system. If you want such relationships, you often need to write a simple, small module that maintains relationships in the database, offers forms to insert/update them and ways to extend nodes with your relational behaviour.
