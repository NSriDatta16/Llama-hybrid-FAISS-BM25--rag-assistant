No, a UIButton over your tableview is going to obstruct touches to the table, and views with alpha less than something like 0.1. One method would be to subclass UITableView and override touchesBegan to detect a touch. From there, you have many options for how to deal with resigning first responder, notification, delegate method, reference to the text field.
