What your looking for software historian. These are databases where all data is organised temporally, really their core function. Once of the uses of the software historian, is to enable a playback function, where time series data can be played back in the order it was entered, which is often critical in those industries that require it, like real time applications in finance, gas, oil and electric utilities. Some of the companies that offer software historians, are Wonderware FactoryTalk OSI PI
