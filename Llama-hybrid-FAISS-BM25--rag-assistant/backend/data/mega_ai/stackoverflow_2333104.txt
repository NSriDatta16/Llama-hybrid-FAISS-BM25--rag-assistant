as greg notes, gears indeed will be phased out by google, html5 is supposed to replace gears for offline webapps. most important specs: " appCache " (to store files locally) " localStorage " (to store strings) " webDB " (to store structured data, will not be official part of html5 though). and some examples; sticky notes (appCache+webDB): http://ajaxian.com/archives/view-source-tutorial-sticky-notes-with-html5-and-css3 todo list manager (appCache+localStorage for simple strings +sync while online): http://hacks.mozilla.org/2010/01/offline-web-applications/ trappist db (appCache + localStorage for structured data, disclosure: blatant self-promotion): http://futtta.be/NoWebDB/
