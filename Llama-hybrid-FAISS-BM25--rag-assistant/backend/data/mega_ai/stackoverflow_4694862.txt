Consider URL encoding and decoding your movie titles. You'd end up with foo.com/bar/Bob%58The%20Return As an alternative, consider leveraging an HTML helper to remove URL unfriendly characters in URLs (method is URLFriendly() ). The SEO benefits between a colon and a placeholder (e.g. a dash) would likely be negligable.
