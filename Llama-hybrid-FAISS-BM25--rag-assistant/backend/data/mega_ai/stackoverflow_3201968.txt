the value of the Bundle Identifier Key within your info.plist file must match the App-ID that you have assigned to your provisioning profile in the portal. another reason could be, that said provisioning profile is indeed not installed on the computer you're developing on. In that case you simply download your profile and open it. it will then appear in XCODE's Organizer Window. hope I could help sam
