Unless you have hundreds of thousands of keys, you won't see any noticeable size increase. On modern systems text is trivial. You could store tens of thousands of keys in a couple of megs. The use of long highly unique keys, constants, class names, ivars etc is actively encourage because of Objective-C's open name space.
