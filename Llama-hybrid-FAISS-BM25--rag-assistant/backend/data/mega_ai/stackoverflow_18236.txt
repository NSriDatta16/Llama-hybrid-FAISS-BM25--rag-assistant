Don't collapse by concatenation for storage of related records in your database. Its not exactly best practices. What you're describing is a pivot table. Pivot tables are hard . I'd suggest avoiding them if at all possible. Why not just read in your related rows and process them in memory? It doesn't sound like you're going to spend too many milliseconds doing this...
