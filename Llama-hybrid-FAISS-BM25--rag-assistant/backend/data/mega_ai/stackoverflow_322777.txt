In our case, we produce extremely customized software for the specific circumstances of a state office. Because of that, our software has no utility for anyone else. Being a state office, we aren't at liberty to "donate" time or money, either. In theory, we could open-source some of our documentation, but again a lack of demand would make it nothing more than an empty gesture.
