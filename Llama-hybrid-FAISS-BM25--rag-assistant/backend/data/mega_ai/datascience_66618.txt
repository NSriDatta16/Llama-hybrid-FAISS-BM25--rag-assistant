The interest is to predict $y$ . If we know the real distribution $P$ of $(x,y)$ , there is no need to build any more machine learning model. Given $x$ , we can directly consult $P$ to know the probability of $P(y|x)$ . For the case of discrete $Y$ , we have $P(y|x)=\frac{P(x,y)}{\sum_z P(x,z)}$ .
