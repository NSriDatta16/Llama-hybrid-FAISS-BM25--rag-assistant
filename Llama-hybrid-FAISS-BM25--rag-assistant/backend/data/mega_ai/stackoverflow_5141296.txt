If your site uses the local storage then in chrome, open your site and right click it. Go to inspect element. A tab will open up after the footer of your website. On that tab please click on storage. You will be able to view the database and tables . You can also write custom query there. Please let me know if it works for you , I can help you sending the snapshot. In firefox you can download the addons called SQlite manager from the link below: https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/sqlite-manager/ May this helps you
