Perhaps you could have the organization embedded on the URL, for example if your route looks like this /{organization}/{controller}/{action} then you'll get URLs like /bigorg/products/list /smallorg/products/list and you'll receive the organization in your controller either a parameter to your method or via the RouteData object.
