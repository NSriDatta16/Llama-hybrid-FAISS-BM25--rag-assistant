If both documents live on the same domain and are under your control, you will need to have the newly loaded document emit script which notifies the parent window it has just been loaded: window.opener.notifyOpened(window.location.href + ' just loaded'); Otherwise, no. The act of navigating in the browser is outside the scope of the JavaScript sandbox. Obviously there are much more elegant, robust ways of doing what I demonstrated above, but that gets the general relationship across.
