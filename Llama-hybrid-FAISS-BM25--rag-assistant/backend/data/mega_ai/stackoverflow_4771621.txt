There's a lot of online examples for httpd SSL virtual host configuration. Just specify something similar to the following, and put your mod_jk JKMount references inside of it. NameVirtualHost 192.168.100.10:443 ServerName domain.com:443 UseCanonicalName Off CustomLog /var/log/httpd/domain.log combined ErrorLog /var/log/httpd/domain-error.log SSLEngine on SSLVerifyClient none SSLCertificateFile /etc/httpd/conf/ssl.crt/domain.com.crt SSLCertificateKeyFile /etc/httpd/conf/ssl.key/domain.com.key SSLProxyEngine on SetEnvIf User-Agent ".*MSIE.*" \ nokeepalive ssl-unclean-shutdown \ downgrade-1.0 force-response-1.0
