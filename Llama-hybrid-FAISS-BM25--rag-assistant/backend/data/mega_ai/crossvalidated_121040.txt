mixed results for stationarity tests and structural breaks

Following situation: I want to forecast a time series of the number of trucks on the motorway in some country. Here how the regular week looks like: I have data for 4 years and divide the huge time series in 672 part time series… so we have now time series for example like a measurement of the number of vehicles each Monday at 11 o’clock: Then I test the time series for if it is season-stationary or trend-stationary with R-function nsdiffs() and ndiffs() respectively. Both tests (OCSB and CH) for season-stationary deliver the same result (ts is season stationary). With SLT-decomposition I adjust the time series for seasonality and test it for trend-stationary with adf-, kpss- and pp-test (R-function ndiffs()). Here I have mixed results: adf and pp shows stationary but kpss not! I figured out that kpss is very sensitive to structural breaks and there are some of them (R-function breakpoints()): Thinking logically the trend should be deterministic because the population of trucks is growing but still the kpss-test causes headache.
