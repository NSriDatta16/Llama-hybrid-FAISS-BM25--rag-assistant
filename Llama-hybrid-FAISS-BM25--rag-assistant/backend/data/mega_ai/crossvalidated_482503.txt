Normal Distribution Probability Question

A recent study by the EPA has determined that the amount of contaminants in Minnesota Lakes in parts per million averages 64 ppm with a variance of 17.6. Suppose 35 lakes are randomly selected and sampled. What is the probability that the sample average amount of contaminants is between 64 and 72 ppm?
