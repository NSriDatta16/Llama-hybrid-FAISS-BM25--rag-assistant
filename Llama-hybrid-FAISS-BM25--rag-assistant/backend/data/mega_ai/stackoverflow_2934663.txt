RIA Services is the fastest way to develop database apps in Silverlight. http://www.silverlight.net/getstarted/riaservices/ IF your app is RIA Services enabled, it's as easy as: Create Entity Framework Model for your database Add new Domain Service for the model Drag and drop Data Sources onto your Silverlight UI from VS "Data Sources" You're done.
