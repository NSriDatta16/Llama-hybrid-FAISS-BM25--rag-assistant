Video player creators are calling this "fall-forward", which may help in your search. I'd encourage you to look at some of the existing solutions, which often wrap the flash player in an HTML5-like API for better compatibility. The Kaltura HTML5 beta has a fall-forward example at http://www.kaltura.org/advanced-examples .
