Your Expires header should be the date in the future at which time the content will expire and caches will be forced to fetch it again. Get rid of the Pragma header For Cache-Control you can add: public max-age=2592000 Assuming you want it cached for 30 seconds. For greater control you should follow hafichuk's advice and use ETags. For references on cache headers check out Headers
