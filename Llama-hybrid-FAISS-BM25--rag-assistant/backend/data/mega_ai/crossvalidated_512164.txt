Coefficients are just random variables, with variances and covariance, and there are rules for calculating functions of random variables that you learn in Stats 101. They all involve either calculating linear combinations of the coefficients or else non-linear combinations and using the Delta Method to get the approximate variance of the output. Here are two (of many) ways you might use to do that in Stata.
