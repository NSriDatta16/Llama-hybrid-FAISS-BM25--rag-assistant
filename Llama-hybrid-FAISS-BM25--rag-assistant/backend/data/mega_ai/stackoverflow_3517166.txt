Yes, you must can create an Ad Hoc distribution provisioning profile and a matching certificate. You can add up to 100 devices on the iphone developer portal. To add an iPad/iPhone/iPod to the list, you must know its UDID. Then you send the binary of the App and the Ad Hoc provisioning profile to the user (your boss). He will drag both files (the binary is actually a folder) to iTunes and when he syncs his device - he will get the application.
