On my tests, it only changes the hash tag: E.g. the real URL is: http://www.facebook.com/photo.php?fbid=x&set=z and clicking next results in: http://www.facebook.com/photo.php?fbid=x&set=z#!/photo.php?fbid=y&set=z&pid=pid&id=id The part after the hash is setup for Google AJAX crawl . But for the purpose of the browser, it's just a hash (fragment identifier).
