The first thing you have to do is to drag your view object (from the library) into the collection of objects on the XIB window (where files owner and such is). You main window is locked at a give size but supplemental views (not shown on the primary window) can be any size and controlled as the Answer 1 indicates. At run time, you will have to associate the scroll view/supplemental views.
