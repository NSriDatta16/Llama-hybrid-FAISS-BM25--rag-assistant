They are not the same, and your theme will not work without modification in both editions. Aside from bonus features (which you won't have in community), many of the standard libraries in Magento are not in sync between the two versions, so there is a marked difference in the way that some functions are handled. Agree w/ Jon that you need to find an EE instance to test on.
