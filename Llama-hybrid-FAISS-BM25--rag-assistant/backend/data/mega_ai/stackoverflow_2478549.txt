Use the following from my .emacs: (defun unfill-paragraph () (interactive) (let ((fill-column (point-max))) (fill-paragraph nil))) (defun unfill-region () (interactive) (let ((fill-column (point-max))) (fill-region (region-beginning) (region-end) nil))) I can't take credit, I googled this years ago.
