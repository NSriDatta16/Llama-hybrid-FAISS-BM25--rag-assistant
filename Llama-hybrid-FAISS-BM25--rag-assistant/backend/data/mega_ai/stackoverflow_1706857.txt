The number of new rows should not be able to generate that level of fragmentation, but really need some more information to not just be taking guesses, primarly what is the clustered index for the table. Would also check whether you have a reindex followed by a shrink in your maintenance or auto_shrink set on since that can easily cause high levels of fragmentation.
