Interpretability of Random Forest Decisions

Decision trees as we know can be easily converted into rules which increase human interpretability of the results and explain why a decision was made. But in case of the random forest when we have many decision trees to get the decision from, will the end results be interpretable i.e. how it came to a conclusion?
