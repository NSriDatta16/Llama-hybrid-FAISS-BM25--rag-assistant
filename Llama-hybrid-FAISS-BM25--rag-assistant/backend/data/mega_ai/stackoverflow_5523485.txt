I don't know of a more elegant way. Rather than using SingleOrDefault , I think you want something like: bool isUniqueUserName = !context.UserSet.Any(u => u.Username == i_UserName); for performance. Any() stops at the first match and doesn't have to enumerate the entire sequence.
