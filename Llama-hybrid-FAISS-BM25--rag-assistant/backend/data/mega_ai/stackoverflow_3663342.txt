You'll need \usepackage{array} to do this. From what I can gather, you're looking at the m{} alignment, which wraps a paragraph at the specified point value, and vertically aligns the text to the middle of the field. For example, something like this: \begin{tabular}{|m{2cm}|m{1ex}|m{1ex}|m{1ex}} \hline & x1 & x2 & x3 \\\hline Long Label & 1 & 0 & 1 \\\hline Long Label & 0 & 1 & 1 \\\hline \end{tabular}
