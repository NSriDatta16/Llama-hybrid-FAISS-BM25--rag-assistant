Well you can easily get the top left and bottom right lat/lon coordinates using GeoPoint tlGpt; // Top left GeoPoint brGpt; // Bottom right tlGpt = mapView.getProjection().fromPixels(0, 0); brGpt = mapView.getProjection().fromPixels(mapView.getWidth(), mapView.getHeight()); At any zoom level where you could actually see the bus stations on the map, then the top left longitude could be considered the same as the bottom left longitude, similarly bottom left long would be approx = bottom right long. Thus you could consider the bounding box as a rectangle, rather than an isosceles trapezium (trapezoid in US English)
