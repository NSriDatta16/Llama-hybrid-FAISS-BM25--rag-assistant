Open the form in design mode. Locate the PrintDocument control in the toolbox. Drag and drop it onto your form. That adds a field to your form class named "printDocument1". Next, double-click the printDocument1 image displayed below the form. That adds the PrintPage event handler. Use the code from your book. Review the instructions in the book, it should have mentioned this. Best to use its step-by-step instructions rather than mine.
