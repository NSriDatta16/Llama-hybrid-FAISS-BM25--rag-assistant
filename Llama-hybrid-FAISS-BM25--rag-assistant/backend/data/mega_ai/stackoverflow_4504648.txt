Take this one with a grain of salt (and be sure to load Alexandria beforehand): (defun positions (item sequence &key (test #'eql)) (mapcar #'car (remove item (map 'list #'cons (alexandria:iota (length sequence)) sequence) :test-not test :key #'cdr))) That said, it does have the advantage of working on arbitrary sequences: CL-USER> (positions 'x #(x x y y x x y y)) (0 1 4 5) CL-USER> (positions 5 (list 5.0 -1 5 5.0 -1) :test #'=) (0 2 3) CL-USER> (positions #\l "Hello") (2 3)
