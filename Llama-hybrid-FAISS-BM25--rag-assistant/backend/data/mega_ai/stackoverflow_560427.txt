An alternative to flood-fill is the connnected-components algorithm. So, Cheaply classify your pixels. e.g. divide pixels in colour space. Run the cc to find the blobs Retain the blobs of significant size This approach is widely used in early vision approaches. For example in the seminal paper " Blobworld: A System for Region-Based Image Indexing and Retrieval ".
