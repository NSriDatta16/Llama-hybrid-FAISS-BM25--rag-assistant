... a move to IPv6 ? A contentious subject since the Service Providers have some incentives to wait for a crisis to have a reason to charge additional fees... Note that Mobile Networks are the early adopters of IPv6 (they use 4to6 gateways): since the major growth in connected devices come from the Mobile side, the fixed side will be dragged to IPv6 by force anyways.
