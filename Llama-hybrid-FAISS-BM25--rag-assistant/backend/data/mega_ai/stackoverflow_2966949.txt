Where should these values persist? Are they only needed in the Silverlight Client or also on the Server? If they are only needed within the Silverlight Client in the browser, you can use Silverlight's Isolated Storage: http://blogs.silverlight.net/blogs/msnow/archive/2008/07/16/tip-of-the-day-19-using-isolated-storage.aspx http://msdn.microsoft.com/en-us/library/system.io.isolatedstorage%28VS.95%29.aspx If that does not work, you can just add the two methods to your web service and update the service reference (proxy) in your Silverlight project: http://support.microsoft.com/kb/308359
