You want: $query = mysqli_query($db, "SELECT online FROM tbl_system WHERE property = 'extranet'"); $row = mysqli_fetch_array($query); $online = $row[0]; mysqli_fetch_field() is for retrieving column definitions , not data, and it's working perfectly well: giving you back a column definition object. mysqli_fetch_array() , mysqli_fetch_assoc() , and mysqli_fetch_object() are for data retrieval.
