The AIC and standard error measure different things, and if you are trying to minimize standard error, a cross-validation approach may be better to use. Another alternative is the Bayesian information criterion (BIC), which is more parsimonious than the AIC. Also, here's a good article comparing the relations between various evaluation metric for supervised machine learning: Data mining in metric space: an empirical analysis of supervised learning performance criteria .
