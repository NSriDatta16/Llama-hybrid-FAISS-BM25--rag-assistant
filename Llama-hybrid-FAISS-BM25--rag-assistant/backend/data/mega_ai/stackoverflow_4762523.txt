To force ie and other browsers to download you have to specify specific headers like in this example : header("Content-disposition: attachment; filename=\"".$fileName . ".csv\""); header("Content-Type: application/force-download"); header("Content-Transfer-Encoding: binary"); header("Pragma: no-cache"); header("Expires: 0"); Other wise if you use text/xml, ie thinks he knows how to display it and does so.
