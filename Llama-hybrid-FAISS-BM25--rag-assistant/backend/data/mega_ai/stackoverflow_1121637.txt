You shouldn't set the .Text value of the textbox... set the value of the property it's binding to. :) I'd encourage you to read more about the Model-View-ViewModel method for designing your views. It keeps a clear separation of concerns when doing this kind of work. The reason you are seeing this "bug" regarding focus causing the binding to refresh is because most of the time this kind of thing is not appropriate. Here's a pretty good video introduction to MVVM: MVVM on Channel 9
