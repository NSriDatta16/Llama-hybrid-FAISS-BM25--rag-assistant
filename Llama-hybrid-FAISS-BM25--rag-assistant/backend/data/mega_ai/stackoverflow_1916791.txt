I would go ahead and say that no, O3D probably will not slay Flash, at least in its current incarnation. The API is far less complete when compared to the breadth of ActionScript 3; it's still a third party plugin that needs to be installed; and Flash isn't just a video playback donkey. That said, this is a pretty open-ended question. Take a look at the Papervision 3 project that will be leveraging Flash Player 10's 3D functionality.
