Assuming your goal is to infer whether a sentence is: positive already happened, positive likely to happen, negative already happened or negative likely to happen; you end up with a 4-classes classification problem, which you need to label in advance (this would be, if feasible, the tedious-human work). After that, you can also apply word embedding layers to your model, to capture the meaning you would like to get out of your vocabulary. If you want a reference with an example from the Keras documentation, you can have a look at it: NLP approaches to infer Processes from Text
