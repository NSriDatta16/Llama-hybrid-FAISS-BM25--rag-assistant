Your issue is the subject of two bugs filled in the MediaWiki bug tracker in late 2004 and 2005 : Bug 1115 - Newline as list item terminator is troublesome Bug 1584 - Need method for multiparagraph list items, continuing numbered lists, and assigning specific numbers to list items By reading them, you will find the solution is to not use the MediaWiki syntax but to rely on "pure" HTML.
