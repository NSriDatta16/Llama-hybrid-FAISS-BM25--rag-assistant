Transformers are increasingly being used in the area of temporal forecasting, which is autoregressive by nature and thus a good fit. Lim et al. (2021) recently announced "temporal fusion transformers" in which they perform quantile regression https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S0169207021000637 . I have not experienced any issues with normalization, although I normalize my data before feeding it into the transformer.
