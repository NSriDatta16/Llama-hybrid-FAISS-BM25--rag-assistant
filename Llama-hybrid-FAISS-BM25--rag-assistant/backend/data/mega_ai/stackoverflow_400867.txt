As mentioned above, Dragon Naturally Speaking is the best speech recognition software out there, however Microsoft Speech Recognition isn't far behind and comes bundled with Vista. Vocola has recently been ported to MSR, and has a .Net integration feature. A few tips Learning to dictate takes some time. Just because you can speak doesn't mean you know how to use speech recognition software Getting proficient with a mix of SR and keyboard/mouse is much easier than full hands-free operation. Use CodeRush or equivalent to type less.
