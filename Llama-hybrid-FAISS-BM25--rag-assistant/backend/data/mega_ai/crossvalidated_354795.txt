If the statistical model you choose provides sufficient accuracy, a multivariate model provides more insight and statistical power. An example of insight is obtaining estimates of correlations between outcomes. For Bayesian modeling you can compute posterior probabilities of compound statements involving multiple outcome tendencies. Power comes from combining information. For accuracy consider Bayesian models.
