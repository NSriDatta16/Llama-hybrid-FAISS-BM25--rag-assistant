Open up Xcode, then to Organizer (Window -> Organizer) . From your desktop, take the certificate and drop it onto Organizer, and you should see the list of certificates you have successfully installed. . You should now be able to select the profile in your drop-down menu. Note: All of the certificates you see on this screen will appear in your drop-down.
