I suspect there are a couple of problems here. Firstly, CamelCase naming for Namespaces and Modules will often fail silently in Magento. I assume you actually meant that the path is app/code/local/myCompanyname/ moduleName/Block /Adminhtml/Catalog/Product? I suggest you recreate your module using all lowercase names. Secondly, you need to update the layout xml for the adminhtml_catalog_product_edit and adminhtml_catalog_product_new to insert your Block. For a great example of how to do this, look at googleoptimizer.xml in app/design/adminhtml/default/default/layout . Pasting for reference: googleoptimizer tab_googleoptimizer ...
