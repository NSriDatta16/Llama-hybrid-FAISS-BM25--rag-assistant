Assigning Integer (value * 2) + 1 and non-integers (x * 2) is analogous to Hilbert's paradox of the Grand Hotel , which describes how to assign infinitely more guests to an infinite hotel. With regards to finding objects by their ID, there's ObjectSpace._id2ref(object_id) . Unless your implementation doesn't have ObjectSpace.
