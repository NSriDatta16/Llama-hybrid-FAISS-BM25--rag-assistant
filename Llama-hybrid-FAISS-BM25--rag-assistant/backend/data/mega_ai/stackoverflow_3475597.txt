Attach a MouseListener to the button Attach a MouseMotionListener to the button On mousePressed you register the location of the MouseEvent On mouseDragged you register the new location of the MouseEvent you take the delta of the two events and add that to the size of the button using setSize/setPreferredSize/whatever they are called
