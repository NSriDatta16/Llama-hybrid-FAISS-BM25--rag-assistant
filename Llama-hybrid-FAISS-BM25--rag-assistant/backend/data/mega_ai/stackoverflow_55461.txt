You would have to consider the target application that will host the control. If it is a line of business application I've heard that WPF doesn't offer great advantages over Forms. According to this blog entry however, the author believes that the killer WPF is a LOB application that leverages the graphical power afforded by WPF for data visualisation. In the end I guess it is a cost/benefit analysis. Do you go down the WPF route and pay the cost of the learning curve for the future benefit of graphical data visualisation or do you stick with the tried and true method and risk developing an outdated application.
