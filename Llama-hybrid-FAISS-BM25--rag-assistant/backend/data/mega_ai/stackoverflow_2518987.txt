The order of the variables should never change, but as others have said, the byte packing will vary. Another thing to consider is the endianness of the platform. To get around the byte alignment/packing problem, most compilers offer some way to guide the process. In gcc you could use __attribute__((__packed__)) and in msvc #pragma pack .
