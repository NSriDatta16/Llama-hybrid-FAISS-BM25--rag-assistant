Figured this one out. It was due to the browser caching. header("Expires: Mon, 01 Jul 2003 00:00:00 GMT"); // Past date header("Last-Modified: " . gmdate("D, d M Y H:i:s") . " GMT"); // Consitnuously modified header("Cache-Control: no-cache, must-revalidate"); // HTTP/1.1 header("Pragma: no-cache"); // NO CACHE header("location: http://www.domainname.co.uk"); did the trick.
