On the inappropriateness of stepwise regression analysis for model building and testing. European Journal of Applied Physiology , 101(2):263–264. Steyerberg, E. W., Eijkemans, M. J., and Habbema, J. D. F. (1999). Stepwise selection in small data sets: a simulation study of bias in logistic regression analysis. Journal of clinical epidemiology , 52(10):935–942. Whittingham, M., Stephens, P., Bradbury, R., and Freckleton, R. (2006). Why do we still use stepwise modelling in ecology and behaviour? Journal of Animal Ecology , 75(5):1182–1189.
