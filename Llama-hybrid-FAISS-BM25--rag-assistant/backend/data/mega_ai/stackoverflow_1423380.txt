The answer is - it depends. It is usually preferred to keep them separate, but there may be situations where keeping them together is just as good option. When you keep them separate you can reference and use your data contract without dragging your operation contract with it, which gives you more flexibility in how you structure your application. You can also version your contracts independently. But it all depends on your actual application.
