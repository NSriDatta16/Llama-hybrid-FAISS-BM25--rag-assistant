Making a custom PDF browser control could be an extremely challenging task. You could instead use the real Adobe Reader in your application as it is provided as a COM control which could be hosted in any Windows application. Here are the steps: Start a new WinForms application Make the Acrobat control available in the toolbox (Tools -> Add/Remove Toolbox Items: turn on "Adobe Acrobat 7.0 Browser Document" in COM Components tab). Don't forget to grab a cup of coffee before clicking on the COM tab. Drag the control to the form Manipulate it: axAcroPDF1.LoadFile("mypdf.pdf"); axAcroPDF1.Show();
