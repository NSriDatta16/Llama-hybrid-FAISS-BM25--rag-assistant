You could try folding your array: [true,true,false,true].inject(:&) #=> false (AND-ed) [true,true,false,true].inject(:|) #=> true (OR-ed) [1,2,3,4].inject(:+) #=> 10 (summed) [1,2,3,4].inject(:*) #=> 24 (multiplied) You get the clue. Back to your example: [1,2,3,4].map {|x| f(x) }.inject(:&) However it won't shortcircuit - you probably better look into "any?" or "all?" except your "f()" has side-effects (like modifying a persistent storage).
