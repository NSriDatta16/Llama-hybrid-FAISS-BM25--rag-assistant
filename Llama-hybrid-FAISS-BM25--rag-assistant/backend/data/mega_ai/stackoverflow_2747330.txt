You must serialize the Object anyway, so you could serialize to XML or JSON aswell. A human readable storage form is what I would prefer. Have a look at Xstream for example. A great, threadsafe tool for marshalling/unmarshalling. I assume of course, that your Object is a Bean/POJO.
