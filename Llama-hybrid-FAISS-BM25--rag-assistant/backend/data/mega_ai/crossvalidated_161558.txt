Generally, if the dummy variables themselves are not of interest, but are just needed as controls to better estimate other coefficients, then you can simply make note of the fact that they where included in the model(s) and do not need to report their estimates and standard errors. However, if you are trying to publish, the guidelines for reporting results may vary between journals and organizations, so perhaps it would best to check with them.
