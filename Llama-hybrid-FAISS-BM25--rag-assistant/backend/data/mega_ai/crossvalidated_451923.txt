I suggest you a very simple online update formulas: At each time $t$ you provide me a point of a time series $x$ of size $N$ , then (1) $\hat\mu=\hat\mu+\frac{1}{1+t}(x-\hat\mu)$ (2) $\hat\Sigma=\frac{t}{1+t}\hat\Sigma+\frac{t}{(1+t)^2}(x-\hat\mu)^\prime (x-\hat\mu)$ See for instance this paper .
