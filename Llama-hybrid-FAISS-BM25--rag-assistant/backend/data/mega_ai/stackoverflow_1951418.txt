What you probably want to do is this... myButton = [[UIButton buttonWithType:UIButtonTypeCustom] retain]; myButton.frame = CGRectMake(20.0, 13.0, 116.0, 138.0); UIImage *tImage = [[UIImage alloc] initWithContentsOfFile:[[NSBundle mainBundle] pathForResource:[self.visue1 lpath] ofType:@"jpg"]]; [myButton setBackgroundImage:[tImage stretchableImageWithLeftCapWidth:11 topCapHeight:0] forState:UIControlStateNormal]; [tImage release]; Of course the cap widths will depend on your image. It just repeats the next line of pixels over and over again until your image stretches to the proper width
