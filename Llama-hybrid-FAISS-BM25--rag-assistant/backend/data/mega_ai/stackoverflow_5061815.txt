You are discussing the field of Natural Language Processing (NLP). It is a very complex issue, and an area of active research. It is safe to say there will never be a simple way of parsing a general English sentence, let alone establishing meaning. The School of Informatics at the Univeristy of Sussex has a set of NLP lectures online that may help you to understand some of the issues which make this such a hard problem.
