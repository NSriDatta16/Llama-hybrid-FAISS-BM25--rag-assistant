"Can it be open source and not free? Why open source not free software?" Yes. An organization can charge you a fee to provide, support and debug open source products. See http://www.fsf.org/ for more information. For other opinions, see http://www.gnu.org/philosophy/open-source-misses-the-point.html and http://www.opensource.org/docs/osd
