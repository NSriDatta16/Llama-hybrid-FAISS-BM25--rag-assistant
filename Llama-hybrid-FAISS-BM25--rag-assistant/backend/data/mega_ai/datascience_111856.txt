According to Forecasting: Principles and Practice by Rob J Hyndman and George Athanasopoulos , time series with trends, or seasonality, are not stationary. On the other hand, a time series with no trend or seasonality might show cyclic behavior. This would be the case if cycles do not have a fixed length and one cannot be sure where for example the peaks will be.
