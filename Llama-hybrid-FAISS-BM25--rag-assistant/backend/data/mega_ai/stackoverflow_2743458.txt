According to the RFC 2616 , here is the convention used by client to send their User-Agent. User-Agent = "User-Agent" ":" 1*( product | comment ) Example: User-Agent: CERN-LineMode/2.15 libwww/2.17b3 The header should contains a product token , and additional informations about version, used library by the client, etc. But as you may know, User-Agent can totally be faked, so you can't really rely on it. user-agents.org is a searchable database of user-agents
