"NotFound" is a generic error that Silverlight uses and it could mean anything. I would encourage you to use a tool called Microsoft Service Trace Viewer to diagnose your issue, i have found this absolutely invaluable in the past. You can find details on how to use it in the post by Yi-Lun Luo here .
