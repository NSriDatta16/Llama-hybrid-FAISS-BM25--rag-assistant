You can't read OLE objects so easily. In fact, it is bad practice to keep pictures as OLE objects in database. It is preferred to have em as BLOB objects or path and filename at some storage. AccessImagine can handle both scenarios for MS Access and C#. You can download it here - http://access.bukrek.net
