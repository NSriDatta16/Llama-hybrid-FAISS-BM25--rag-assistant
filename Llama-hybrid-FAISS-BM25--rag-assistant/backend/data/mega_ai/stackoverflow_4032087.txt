You can add your own interpolations to Paperclip. To give a simple example: Paperclip.interpolates :year do |attachment, style| attachment.instance.created_at.year end Now you can use :year in the :path option like this: has_attached_file :bookblock, :path => "#{CONF['storage_path']}bookblock/:year/:id/:style.:content_type_ehas_attached_filextension" You could define three interpolations: :year , :month and :day , or just one that returns the whole year/month/day string.
