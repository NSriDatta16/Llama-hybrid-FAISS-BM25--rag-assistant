You may find useful the following paper, which presents the design and implementation of a highly available key-value storage system that some of Amazon’s core services use to provide an “always-on” experience: Giuseppe DeCandia, Deniz Hastorun, Madan Jampani, Gunavardhan Kakulapati, Avinash Lakshman, Alex Pilchin, Swami Sivasubramanian, Peter Vosshall and Werner Vogels , “ Dynamo: Amazon's Highly Available Key-Value Store ”, in the Proceedings of the 21st ACM Symposium on Operating Systems Principles, Stevenson, WA, October 2007.
