You may want to take a look at Flixel . What is flixel? flixel is a completely free collection of Actionscript 3 files that helps organize, automate, and optimize Flash games; an object-oriented framework that lets anyone create original and complex games with thousands of objects on screen in just a few hours. Flixel is licensed under the MIT license and the site has quite a few tutorials to get you started.
