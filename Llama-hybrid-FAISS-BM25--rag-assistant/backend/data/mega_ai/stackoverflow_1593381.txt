I guess with the following code you can get your results in a Hashtable: var hashTable = (Hashtable)Session.CreateSQLQuery("SELECT * FROM MyTable") .SetResultTransformer(Transformers.AliasToEntityMap) .UniqueResult(); Obviously all your data will be detached from the session...
