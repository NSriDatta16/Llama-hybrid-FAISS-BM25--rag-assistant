It depends on taste, and the sizes of your classes. The separation is purely for organization. So, if you think it would be easier in one file, or one class per file, depends on you. Advantages: easier to find what you want. Less scrolling! Disadvantages: constant switching between files. May be annoying when making new classes on the fly.
