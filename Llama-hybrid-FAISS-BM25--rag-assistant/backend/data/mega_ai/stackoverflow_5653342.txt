It looks like your best bet is to use wx.PyDropTarget and wx.DataObjectComposite , from which you can call .GetReceivedFormat().GetType() . There's an example app on the wxPython wiki: http://wiki.wxpython.org/DragAndDrop#wxDataObjectComposite
