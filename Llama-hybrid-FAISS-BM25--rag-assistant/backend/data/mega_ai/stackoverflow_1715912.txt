Other than the obvious question of why you would want to do this, I think you're going to have a very difficult time keeping information out of a browser from the server side of things. You might want to to take a look at this page about HTML Meta Tags and look into the CACHE-CONTROL and PRAGMA NO-CACHE options of the meta tag. Good luck, and hope this helps.
