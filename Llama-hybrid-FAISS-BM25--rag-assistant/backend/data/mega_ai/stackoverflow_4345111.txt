That behavior is quite well documenten. See Chapter 11.11 of the ECMAScript Specification: http://www.ecma-international.org/publications/files/ECMA-ST/ECMA-262.pdf Unfortunately it's not very easy to understand, however the last paragraph states: The value produced by a && or || operator is not necessarily of type Boolean. The value produced will always be the value of one of the two operand expressions.
