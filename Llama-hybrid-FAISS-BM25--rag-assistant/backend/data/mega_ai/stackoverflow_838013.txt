You could put this in the Page_Init of your Master: Response.Cache.SetNoServerCaching(); Response.Cache.SetCacheability(HttpCacheability.NoCache); Response.Cache.SetNoStore(); Response.Cache.SetExpires(new DateTime(1900, 01, 01, 00, 00, 00, 00)); This is, e.g., what most bank websites do so you can't effectively use the back button.
