I would say, if you are in the business of making money, do what the client wants if they are willing to pay for it. Discourage it by any means possible: Don't offer any discount, and overestimate the time. Most clients will decide that 5% of the clients don't deserve that kind of cost. But if they still want it, at least you'll be making a profit by doing it.
