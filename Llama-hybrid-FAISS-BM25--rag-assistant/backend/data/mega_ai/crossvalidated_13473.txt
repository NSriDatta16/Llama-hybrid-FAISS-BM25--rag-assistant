The first thing you need to do is determine (or at least think about) whether the poor return rate skewed your data. Ie, is there a statistical difference between the "average" person you attempted to survey (who was presumably representative of the population you wanted to study) and the "average" person who chose to respond? You don't want to end up electing Alf Landon .
