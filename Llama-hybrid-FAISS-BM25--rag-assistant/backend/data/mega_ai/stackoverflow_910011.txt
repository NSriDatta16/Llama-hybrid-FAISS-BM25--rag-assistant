Actually, the standard pattern for this functionality (Gang of Four, even) is Memento . Also, while most programs use Undo/Redo stacks, afficionados of certain text editors prefer Undo/Redo trees so that they don't lose their entire history if they undo a few commands, try a new one, and change their minds.
