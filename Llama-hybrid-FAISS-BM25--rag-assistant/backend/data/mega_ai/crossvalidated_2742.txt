On the use of oblique rotation after PCA

Several statistical packages, such as SAS, SPSS, and R, allow you to perform some kind of factor rotation following a PCA. Why is a rotation necessary after a PCA? Why would you apply an oblique rotation after a PCA given that the aim of PCA is to produce orthogonal dimensions?
