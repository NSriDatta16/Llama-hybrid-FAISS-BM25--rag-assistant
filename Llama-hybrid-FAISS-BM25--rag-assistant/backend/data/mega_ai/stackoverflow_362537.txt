Pragmatism and People Skills. Being pragmatic about a situation. Yes, it may be ideal, but is it really necessary. Something may be the coolest feature in the world, but will it really generate any more revenue. Asking these questions and being realistic will allow you to filter out a lot of chaff from the wheat. I am still learning People Skills. It is not all about knowing all the answers, but rather being able to relay these to the Management and the Clients in a way they will understand and embrace. Being an Autistic with OCD and Bi-Polar makes things a little tricky sometimes.
