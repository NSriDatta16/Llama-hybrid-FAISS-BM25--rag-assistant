The problem may be the values you are trying to insert are beyond the storage capacity of the MySql numeric type [TINYINT] (which ranges from -127 to 127 signed, or 0 to 255 signed). You may need to change the table structure such that the column(s) which are TINYINT are INT(#) instead.
