In your background view (you might have to drag an IBOutlet to it) say like: imageView.bounds = view.bounds; imageView.hidden = YES; [view addSubview:imageView]; Then when your button is pressed (possibly an IBAction) do this to turn the background on and off. if (imageView.hidden){ imageView.hidden = NO; }else{ imageView.hidden = YES; }
