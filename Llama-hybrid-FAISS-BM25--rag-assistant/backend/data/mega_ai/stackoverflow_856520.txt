And if you care to do it correct (which means, don't allow wordsplitting to cut your filenames apart or unexpected globbing to expand to random filenames), use an array: files=(*.py) packages=("${files[@]/%.py/}") nosetests --with-coverage "${packages[@]/#/--coverage-package=}"
