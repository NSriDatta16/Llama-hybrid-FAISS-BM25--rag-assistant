Magento has the very powerful ability to host multiple Stores and Websites on a single installation. The Configuration scope refers to which Website or Store that you are currently managing the settings for. So that drop-down is actually very important. You manage the entries in the drop-down under System>Manage Stores, but you can read more on multi-store setups on the Magento wiki and these two blogs .
