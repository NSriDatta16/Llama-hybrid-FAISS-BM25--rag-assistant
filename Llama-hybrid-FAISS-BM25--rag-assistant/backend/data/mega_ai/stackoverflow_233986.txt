Are you using Apache or nginx in front of your mongrel(s)? Are you logging the user_agent? This is from my nginx.conf: log_format main '$remote_addr - $remote_user [$time_local] $request ' '"$status" $body_bytes_sent "$http_referer" ' '"$http_user_agent" "http_x_forwarded_for"';
