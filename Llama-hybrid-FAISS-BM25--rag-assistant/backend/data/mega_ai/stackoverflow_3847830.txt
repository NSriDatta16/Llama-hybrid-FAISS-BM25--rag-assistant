You don't, for the reasons I gave you in response to your previous question today : because HoltWinters needs ts , you cannot (easily) use it on irregular time series. You can approximate it by, say, sampling every Wednesday and creating 52-week years from that. But there is no way around the basic fact that "business day"-based series are irregular.
