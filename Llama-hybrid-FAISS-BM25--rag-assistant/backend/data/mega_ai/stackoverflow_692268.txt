You should read the lines from the file, CL-PPCRE:SPLIT them to get the list, and step through this list: (loop for (key value) on (cl-ppcre:split " " "a value1 b value2 c value3") by #'cddr appending (list (intern (string-upcase key) (find-package :keyword)) value))
