The only way you can control caching from the content end of the stick is to prevent caching in the first place. context.Response.Cache.SetExpires(DateTime.UtcNow.AddMinutes(-1)); context.Response.Cache.SetCacheability(HttpCacheability.NoCache); context.Response.Cache.SetNoStore(); You cannot invoke methods on the client browser.
