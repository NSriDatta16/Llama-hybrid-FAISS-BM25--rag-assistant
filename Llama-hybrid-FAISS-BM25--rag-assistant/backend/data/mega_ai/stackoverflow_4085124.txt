There is another school of related thought termed SOAD (Service Orientated Application Design) exists were every component of the system is a service. This is to leverage the benefits offered by the environment that they are built with (EJB, WCF) i.e. you get lots of free plumbing. Some more resource on this at Building a SOA SOA Design Pattern Achieving integrity in a SOA Achieving Flexibility/Maintainability in a SOA
