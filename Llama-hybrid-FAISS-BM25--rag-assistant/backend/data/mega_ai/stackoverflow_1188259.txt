There is no way to force it to be visible (as noted above, and rightly so), however if you show a balloon notification (like Windows Live Messenger does when you first try to close the window), your icon will appear while the balloon is visible. You could also use this to draw attention to your app to say "look at me, I'm down here!". Please, for the sake of your users, make it only do this once, at most, though!
