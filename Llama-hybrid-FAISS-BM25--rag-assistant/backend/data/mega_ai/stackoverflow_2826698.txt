Those settings need to be added to your existing HTTP connector element, i.e.: compression="force" compressionMinSize="512" noCompressionUserAgents="gozilla, traviata" compressableMimeType="text/html,text/xml,image/png,text/css,text/javascript" /> Just adding them to a new with no other settings won't do you any good.
