First, I strongly encourage you to avoid scriptlet blocks in your JSP pages. It's been deprecated for a really long time. Using the JSP EL/JSTL (or OGNL in Struts2) is a much better approach. If your action exposes Department via a getRoot() method, then you can pass it to a JSP tag as: Note: You cannot pass OGNL expressions to JSP simple tags the way you can to a Struts2 tag. Then, assuming 'tree' in your tag refers to Department: ${tree.someProperty}
