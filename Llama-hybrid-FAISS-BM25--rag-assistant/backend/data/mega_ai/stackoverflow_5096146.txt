For Ubuntu edit this php.ini for CLI: /usr/local/lib/php.ini add /usr/local/lib/php/ to your include line include_path= "/opt/zend/library/:/var/www/library/:/usr/local/bin/pear/:/usr/local/lib/php/" Took me a day to figure it out.
