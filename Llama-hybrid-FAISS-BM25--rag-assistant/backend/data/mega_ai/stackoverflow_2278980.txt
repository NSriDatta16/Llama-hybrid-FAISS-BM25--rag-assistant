May be you can look at how Eclipse does something similar in its org.eclipse.jdt.internal.ui.javaeditor.EditorUtility class. You can see ( line 222 and following ) it: final IEditorInput input= editor.getEditorInput(); marker= ((IFileEditorInput)input).getFile().createMarker(IMarker.TEXT); marker.setAttribute(IMarker.CHAR_START, offset); marker.setAttribute(IMarker.CHAR_END, offset + length); With offset and length something you should be able to infer from your TextSelection .
