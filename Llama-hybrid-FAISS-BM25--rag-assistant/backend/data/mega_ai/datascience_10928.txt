Stanford NLP is natural language processing research group at Stanford University. It is comprised of faculty, post-docs, programers, and students that all work towards helping computers better understand human language. This group provides and maintains a NLP toolkit called CoreNLP that allows for natural language processing in Java. The group also works across multiple departments at Stanford including the Linguistics and Computer Science departments. It is also a part of the Stanford AI Lab.
