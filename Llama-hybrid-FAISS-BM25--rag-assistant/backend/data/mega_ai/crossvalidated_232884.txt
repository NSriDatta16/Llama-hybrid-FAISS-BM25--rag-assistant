If the professor scored significantly below average, than that would be worth reporting. Therefore you are interested in both ends of the tails and need to perform a two tailed test. Usually you compare your p with some alpha. That alpha is agreed upon to be 0.05 just as testing is agreed to be two tailed. These are two reasons for two tails.
