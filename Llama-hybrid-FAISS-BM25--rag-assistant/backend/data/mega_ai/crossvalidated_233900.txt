If you have a true time series, specifying it as such gives you access to a litany of mathematical analyses not (generally) otherwise possible. Seasonal decomposition. FFT. Spectral analyses. Marking modeling. Etc etc etc. If modeling is an attempt to explain variability, having knowledge of the time component takes care of a large chunk of it.
