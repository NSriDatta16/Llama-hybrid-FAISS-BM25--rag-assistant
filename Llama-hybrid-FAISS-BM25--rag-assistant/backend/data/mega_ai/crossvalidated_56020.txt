Just note that by De Morgan's law, $\overline{\bigcap A_i} = \bigcup \overline{A_i} $ where $\overline{A_i}$ means "not $A_i$ or equivalently the complement $A_i^c$". Now note that $P\left(A\cup B\right) = P(A)+P(B)-P(A\cap B) \le P(A)+P(B)$ So finally $$ P\left(\bigcap A_i\right) = 1-P\left(\overline{\bigcap A_i}\right) = 1-P\left(\bigcup A_i^c\right) \ge 1-\sum P\left(A_i^c\right) $$
