A $2\times m$-MA is not the same as a $2m$-MA. See my introductory book: https://www.otexts.org/fpp2/moving-averages.html If $m$ is not even, then a $m$-MA would be used. For most seasonal data, $m$ is even ($m=12$ for monthly, $m=4$ for quarterly).
