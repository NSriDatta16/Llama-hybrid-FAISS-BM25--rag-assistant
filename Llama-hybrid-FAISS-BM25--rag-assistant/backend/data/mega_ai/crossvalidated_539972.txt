Panel Data Regression, ADF Stationarity Test in R

I am conducting a fixed effects regression with balanced quarterly paneldata. plm(MOD.WITHIN, data=pdata, model = "within", effect= "individual") The dependent variable is gov. bond yield of country i. Independent variables are several country-specific variables and country invariant variables (global/cross-sectional invariant) like VIX index, US term premium and US expected future policy rate, which do not differ across individuals but across time. yield_it ~ X_it + vix_t + ustermp_t + usexp_t I already tested for the appropriate model (fixed effects) and tested for cross-sectional dependence (Pesaran/Breusch-Pagan) pcdtest(MOD.WITHIN, test = c("lm")); pcdtest(MOD.WITHIN, test = c("cd")) and serial correlation (Breusch-Godfrey) which both seem to apply. pbgtest(MOD.WITHIN) Hence, I tested for stationarity i.e. unit-roots for all my variables.
