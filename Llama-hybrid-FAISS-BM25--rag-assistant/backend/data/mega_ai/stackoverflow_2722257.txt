The specific examples you give happen to be DLLs that are usually linked through manifests and the side-by-side, at least when building applications (with the correct project settings) from Visual Studio. Why are you trying to instruct the compiler to link them by code? The most often-used way to link to a particular DLL is when you have the lib for the DLL available, and then to use the pragma #pragma comment(lib, " ")
