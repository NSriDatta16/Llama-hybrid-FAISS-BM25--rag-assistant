The method of storage depends at least partially on what you're going to do with the information. If it's simply to read it in and then print it out again, you could process it strictly as text. However, network programs often make use of this type of data. See the structures in the system header files netinet/in.h , arpa/inet.h , and sys/socket.h Or see the man page for inet_aton()
