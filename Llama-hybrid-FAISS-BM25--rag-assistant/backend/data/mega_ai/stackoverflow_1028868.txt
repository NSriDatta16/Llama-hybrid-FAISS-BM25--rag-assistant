I don't think they are evil or bad. Maybe they are not widely used, but they do have many uses,callbacks being one of them. A special advantage is that they can extend from a different class than the outer class, so you could have multiple inheritance. I would say that one of the issues with inner classes is that their syntax is somewhat "ugly". That's something that discourages some people. Here at work there are many of them.
