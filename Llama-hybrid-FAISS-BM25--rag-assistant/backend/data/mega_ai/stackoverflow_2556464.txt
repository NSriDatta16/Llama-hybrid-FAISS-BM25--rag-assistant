The 'advanced' for-loop doesn't expose the counter to you, and hence, you cannot write the result of counter++ to the specific array slot. Your case is the case where the 'advanced' for-loop isn't made for. See: http://java.sun.com/j2se/1.5.0/docs/guide/language/foreach.html Take a look at the last paragraph.
