Busy sharing cars

Given a pool of C cars available to use by P people and that the average person uses a car t percent of the time, how often will there be zero cars available for use? For example, if there are 500 people and 50 cars, and the average percent of time a person uses a car is 10%, how likely will there be zero cars available for use by the remaining 450 people?
