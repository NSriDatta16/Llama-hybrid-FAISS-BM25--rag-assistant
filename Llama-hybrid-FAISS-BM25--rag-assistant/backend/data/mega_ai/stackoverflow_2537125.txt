Not very elegant, but you could try $find = 'abc(.*)'; $replace = 'def\\1'; Note that if your $find contains more capturing groups, you need to adjust your $replace . Also, this will replace the first abc in every line. If your input contains several lines, use [\d\D] instead of . .
