Your question should yield some good answers. Here are some starting points. An ability to work with the tradeoffs between precision and the demands placed on computing power. Facility with data mining techniques that can be used as preliminary screening tools before conducting regression. E.g., chaid, cart, or neural networks. A deep understanding of the relationship between statistical significance and practical significance. A wide repertoire of methods for variable selection. The instinct to crossvalidate.
