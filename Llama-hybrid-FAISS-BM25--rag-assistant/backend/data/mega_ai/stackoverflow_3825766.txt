Kranu is correct, you're not allowed to read responseText when readyState is 3. See http://www.davidflanagan.com/2005/08/xmlhttprequestreadystate-3.html The solution is to send a message at a time. When you receive one message, just make another XHR. That's how google does (did?) server push.
