It depends on how your views are set up. Generally leveraging the responder chain is the best way to go. It allows you to play tricks, though it may be too specific to address your particular needs. You can also play tricks with forward events by override hit testing: http://developer.apple.com/library/ios/#documentation/EventHandling/Conceptual/EventHandlingiPhoneOS/MultitouchEvents/MultitouchEvents.html%23//apple_ref/doc/uid/TP40009541-CH3-SW3 Your particular case sounds pretty exotic, so you may have to play tricks like having a parent view whose frame is large enough to contain both views in question.
