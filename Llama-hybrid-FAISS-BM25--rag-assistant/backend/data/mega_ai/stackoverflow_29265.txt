High-level discussions about purpose, scope, limitations of operating environment, size, etc Audition a single paragraph description of the system, hammer it out Mock up UI Formalize known requirements Now iterate between 3 and 4 with more and more functional prototypes and more specs with more details. Write tests as you go. Do this until you have functional software and a complete, objective, testable requirements spec. That's the dream. The reality is usually after a couple iterations everybody goes head-down and codes until there's a month left to test.
