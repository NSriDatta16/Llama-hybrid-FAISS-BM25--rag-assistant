One argument often heard from DBA's in this context is that "if RI is used, then unloading/loading to reorganise is harder for us to do". This is of course true, per se, but the vilain is that the 'harder' is usually suggested to mean 'so much harder that it becomes nigh impossible for us DBAs to do our jobs'. And THAT is bullocks.
