You can use a couple of different tricks to keep state on the client, you can keep a top level frame and store a javascript variable there. You can use Flash local "SharedObject", Silverlights' IsolatedStorage or the equivelant in Google Gears. but.. Don't follow this line of thinking. You need SSL. You are not going to build something secure, you are going to build something that shoots you or someone using your app in the foot.
