MS-Office makes crappy HTML, period. You're better of spending time rebuilding the HTML from the original text than trying to walk through that minefield. I made a few macros that do some search/replace functions on Word to do basic things like wrap tags around paragraphs and stuff like that, then re-markup the whole thing from scratch.
