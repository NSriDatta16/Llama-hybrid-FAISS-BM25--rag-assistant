You may be interested in a particular kind of neural network designed for sequential tasks: rnn s come in several flavors, including lstm and gru networks which have been used to model long sequences such as natural language. This can be used to generate $n$ -step ahead predictions; in the natural language setting, this can be used to do stuff like writing novel tweets.
