Try setting the Precedence: bulk -header (or junk ) in your notifications. Usually OOO-notifiers like Unix vacation do not send notifications for incoming mails with that header. As this header is slightly discouraged, you could try setting an empty Return-Path , as suggested in Precedence: header in email .
