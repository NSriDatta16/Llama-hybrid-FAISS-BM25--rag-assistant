COUNT(*) facts and myths: MYTH : "InnoDB doesn't handle count(*) queries well": Most count(*) queries are executed same way by all storage engines if you have a WHERE clause, otherwise you InnoDB will have to perform a full table scan. FACT : InnoDB doesn't optimize count(*) queries without the where clause
