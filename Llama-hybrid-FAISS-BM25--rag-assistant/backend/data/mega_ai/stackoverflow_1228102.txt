You take your old baggage with you when you go. Meaning that any project management bad practices you had before will still linger. However, I will say that things improved greatly when we began to close the loop between us and the customer. Greater and more frequent feedback and prototyping with the customer means far fewer moments of the customer saying, "This is not what I wanted."
