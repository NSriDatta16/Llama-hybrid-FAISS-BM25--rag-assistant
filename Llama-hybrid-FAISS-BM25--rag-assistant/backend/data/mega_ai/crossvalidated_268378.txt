This class of problem is known as learning to rank ; it's one of the core tasks of information retrieval, e.g. what a search engine does, though your case is slightly different in terms of the relative numbers of objects and so on. There are many methods, overviewed e.g. in the Approaches section of the linked Wikipedia article, most of which can probably apply to this kind of problem.
