Instead of passing this when you initiate the drag/drop, create one of the standard containers for that purpose. Specifically: DragDrop.DoDragDrop(this, new DataObject("myFormat", this), DragDropEffects.Move); and then now you know to expect a specific kind of data: var control =(BaseDragableUserControl)e.Data.GetData("myFormat");
