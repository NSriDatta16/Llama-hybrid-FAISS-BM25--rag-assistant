I'd say choreography is well suited internally for highly decentralized organizations. You won't need a central business process executor. This facilitates independent growth and development by each of organization sub-units. (I subscribe to this interpretation of orchestration vs. choreography question: http://geekexplains.blogspot.com/2008/07/ways-of-combining-web-services.html )
