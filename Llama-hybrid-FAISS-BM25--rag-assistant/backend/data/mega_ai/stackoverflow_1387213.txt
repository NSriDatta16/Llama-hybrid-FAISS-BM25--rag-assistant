This appears to be hardcoded in URI::_query::query_form(). I'd conditionally modify that based on a global as is done with $URI::DEFAULT_QUERY_FORM_DELIMITER and submit your change to the URI maintainer. Other than that, perhaps you could use a LWP::UserAgent request_prepare callback handler?
