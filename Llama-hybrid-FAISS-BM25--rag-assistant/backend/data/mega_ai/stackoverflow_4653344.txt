Try this instead... $('.remove').removeAttr('href'); Setting the href to a blank fragment # is bad practice, hacky and ugly. It often makes the browser jump to the top of the page. Alternatively, with your click handlers you could do... $('a').click(function(event) { event.preventDefault(); }); You can also return false .
