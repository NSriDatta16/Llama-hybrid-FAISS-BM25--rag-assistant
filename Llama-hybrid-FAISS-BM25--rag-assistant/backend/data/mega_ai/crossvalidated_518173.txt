If your categorical variable is binary, you can use (binomial) logistic regression, if it has more than two levels, multinomial logistic regression. Maybe you should spline the continuous independent variable. That, and other decisions, depend on context that you did not tell us. In all cases, plot your data! This answer assumes you have enough data, at least aroun 100 for logistic regression.
