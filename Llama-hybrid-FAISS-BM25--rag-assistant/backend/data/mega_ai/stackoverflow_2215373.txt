One data structure you could use is a one-dimensional R-tree . These are designed to deal with ranges and to provide efficient retrieval. You will also learn about Allen's Operators ; there are a dozen other relationships between time intervals than just 'overlaps'. There are other questions on SO that impinge on this area, including: Determine Whether Two Date Ranges Overlap Data structure for non-overlapping ranges within a single dimension
