How does Random Number Generation work?

I'm using the set.seed() function in R to achieve reproducability of my results. I compare different regression methods (e.g. RandomForest, SVM, GAM) by their MSE derived from a cross-validation procedure. To my surprise, I realized that results differ whether I place 'set.seed(123)' at the beginning of my code (and then running the whole script) or whether I place 'set.seed(123)' just before calling each method in the script.
