Since connectWith accepts a selector, you can provide it a selector that selects both the other connected lists and your trash can. $("#sortable1, #sortable2").sortable({ connectWith: '.connectedSortable, #trash' }).disableSelection(); $("#trash").droppable({ accept: ".connectedSortable li", hoverClass: "ui-state-hover", drop: function(ev, ui) { ui.draggable.remove(); } }); Example: http://jsfiddle.net/petersendidit/YDZJs/1/
