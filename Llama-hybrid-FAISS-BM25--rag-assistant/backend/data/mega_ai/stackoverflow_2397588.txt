You want the OMP_SET_LOCK / OMP_UNSET_LOCK functions: https://hpc.llnl.gov/tuts/openMP/#OMP_SET_LOCK Basically: omp_lock_t writelock; omp_init_lock(&writelock); #pragma omp parallel for for ( i = 0; i Most locking routines such as pthreads semaphores and sysv semaphores work on that sort of logic, although the specific API calls are different.
