I was suddenly enlightened when I looked at the problem again at home. Why not get the last order increment id from the sales/order collection? $orders = Mage::getModel('sales/order')->getCollection() ->setOrder('increment_id','DESC') ->setPageSize(1) ->setCurPage(1); echo $orders->getFirstItem()->getIncrementId(); Tested and working on Magento 1.3.2.3
