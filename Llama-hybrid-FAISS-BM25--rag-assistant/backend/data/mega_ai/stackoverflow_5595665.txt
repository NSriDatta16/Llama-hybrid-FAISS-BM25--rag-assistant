This depends on how you want your application to work. I would venture to guess that for a DragEvent, you would need to hold down a longer click on the ball to enter into a DragEvent. Where a MotionEvent would be picked up as soon as you touch the screen and move your finger. It is really all about the user experience you want for your users.
