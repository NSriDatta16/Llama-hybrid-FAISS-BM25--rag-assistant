The line skipping part is actually easy. To read a specific line, just seek to the beginning of the file and use a loop with fgets to get each line until you reach the one you are interested in. This is the brute force approach, and might offend your sense of aesthetics, but computers were invented to do this type of work. There is a more elegant way to do the line skipping, but that might also be one of the problems your professor wants you to solve, so I won't mention it here.
