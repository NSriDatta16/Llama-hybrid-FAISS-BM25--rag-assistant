You may want to transform your independent variable, but none of the assumptions of logistic regression require that your independent variables be Normal, even approximately. When dealing with prices, which are often right skewed, a useful transformation is the log. Dropping cases over a certain value can't be recommended, unless there is some other reason to do this.
