I would strongly discourage passing object instances between an executable and a regular DLL. Mainly for the exact reasons you are are encountering. What happens if the DLL is rebuilt and you've changed the object in some incompatible subtle way? As Mason points out, packages are the preferred way to partition your application into modules.
