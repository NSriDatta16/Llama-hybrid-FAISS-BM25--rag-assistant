It appears that your professor is testing your understanding of what space DLLs are loaded into, and how this relates to threads. Without doing your homework for you, I encourage you to consider what happens if two threads each call LoadLibrary() on a particular DLL. Is the DLL loaded into the process twice? Given the result of the above, what implications does this have regarding the two threads making calls into that DLL?
