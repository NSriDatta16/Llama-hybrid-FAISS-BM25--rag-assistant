Should you do this? No. Two reasons: Overloading the global new operator will inevitably cause you pain, especially when external libraries take dependency on the stock versions. Modern OS implementation of the heap already take fragmentation into consideration. If you're on Windows, you can look into " Low Fragmentation Heap " if you have a special need. To summarize, don't mess with it unless you can prove (by profiling) that it is a problem to begin with. Don't optimize pre-maturely.
