If you really need it, you could encourage your users to use a log off function, but in general most people will ignore it. If you really really need it, you could use the onUnload javascript event to capture the event of when some of your users navigate to another site. Some general information on onUnload and in which scenarios it doesn't work can be found in this question Alternative for JavaScript onUnload . Ugly hacks for excluding links to your own domain http://www.dynamicdrive.com/forums/archive/index.php/t-4947.html . Again, unless you really really need it, stick with configuring the session handling of the app servers.
