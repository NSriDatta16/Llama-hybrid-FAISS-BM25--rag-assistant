You can use .setResultTransformer(CriteriaSpecification.DISTINCT_ROOT_ENTITY) on the criteria. List ordersAndProducts = session.createCriteria(Order.class) .setResultTransformer(CriteriaSpecification.DISTINCT_ROOT_ENTITY) .setFetchMode(“products”,FetchMode.JOIN) .add(Restrictions.eq(“id”,”1234”)) .list(); Now you retrieve all orders with eagerly loaded products.
