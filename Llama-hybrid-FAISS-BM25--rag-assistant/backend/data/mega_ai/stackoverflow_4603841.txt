In terms of associating the keywords with dictionary entries: One general method of getting what you need would be to look at this as a multi-class classification problem: http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Multiclass_classification One implementation that I believe should work for your particular need would be SVM Light: http://svmlight.joachims.org/svm_struct.html You will still need to train the classifier with sample data, of course, so you will need to have a representative training set. In terms of actually organizing the results in a database, it seems relatively simple: create a dictionary table (with the create a keyword table, with foreign keys to the dictionary table the natural (logical) primary key of the keyword table would be (keyword, dictionary_table_id)
