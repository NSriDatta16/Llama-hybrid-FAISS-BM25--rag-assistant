You could also consider using an NSDictionary as your internal storage mechanism and then write them out to property list files using [NSDictionary writeToFile:atomically:]. A friend of mine likes to refer to dictionaries as God's data structure (tongue in cheek). If your data size is modest there are several advantages to this: human readable, human writable, changeable.
