This solution works correctly: (defun lat-p (lst) (labels ((lat-p* (lst) (cond ((null lst) t) ((atom (car lst)) (lat-p* (cdr lst))) (t nil)))) (if lst (lat-p* lst) nil))) However a much more elegant solution(with no recursion) would be: (defun lat-p (lst) (and lst (every #'atom lst)))
