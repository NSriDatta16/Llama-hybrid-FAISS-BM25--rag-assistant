I would take a look at getting your IDE to automatically generate everything. In Netbeans, the steps are: Right Click on your project, click "add Web Service Client", enter the WDSL url and click Finish. This will auto-magically create the Java proxy for you. To implement the client in your code, drag and drog the required method (located in Web Services References in your project), into your code.
