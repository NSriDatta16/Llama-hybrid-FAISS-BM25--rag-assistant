LATE is a subset of TOT....I quote from Mastering 'Metrics by Angrist and Pischke: "Researchers and policy makers are sometimes interested in average causal effects for the entire treated population, as well as in LATE . This average causal effect is called the treatment effect on the treated (TOT for short)" (Chapter 3 Section 1)
