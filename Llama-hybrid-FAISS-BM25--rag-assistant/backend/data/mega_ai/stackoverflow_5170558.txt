Native Cassandra secondary indexes have some limitations. They are not supposed to be used for columns with high cardinality (too many unique values), according to datastax documentation. It seems like the create_date column you are indexing on will have high cardinality. Also, there is no such thing as compound index in native Cassandra index support. For more in depth coverage, you can visit my blog post http://pkghosh.wordpress.com/2011/03/02/cassandra-secondary-index-patterns/ Pranab
