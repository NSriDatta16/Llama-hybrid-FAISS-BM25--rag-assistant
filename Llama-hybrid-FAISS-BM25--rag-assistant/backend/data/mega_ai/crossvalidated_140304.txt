Take $(X_n)_n$ an arbitrary ergodic Markov chain and define $T_n$ as $$T_n=\min\{t>T_{n-1}\,;X_t\le 2X_{T_{n-1}}\,,X_t\ge X_{T_{n-2}}\}\,.$$(Let us assume that the conditions are met for $T_n$ to be almost surely finite!) Then $X_{T_n}$ is not independent of $X_{T_{n-2}}$ given $X_{T_{n-1}}$.
