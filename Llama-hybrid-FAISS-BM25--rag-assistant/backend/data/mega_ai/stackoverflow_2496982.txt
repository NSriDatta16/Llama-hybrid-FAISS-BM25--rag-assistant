Some websites block requests that dont have certain headers in the request. In particular one i have found in the past is the "User-Agent" header, try copying a header from a browser request and add it into your WebClient WebClient.Headers.Add("User-Agent", "Mozilla/4.0 (compatible; MSIE 8.0)");
