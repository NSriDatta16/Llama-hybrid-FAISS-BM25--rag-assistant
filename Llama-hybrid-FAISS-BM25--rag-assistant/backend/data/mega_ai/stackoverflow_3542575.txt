Do they fully understand what it will take to get someone in their organisation trained to be able to maintain the application? Make this clear to them. What happens when an employee of the company who's had access to this information leaves? This is a more likely source of data leakage. Offer to sign a non-disclosure agreement which is standard in this sort of case.
