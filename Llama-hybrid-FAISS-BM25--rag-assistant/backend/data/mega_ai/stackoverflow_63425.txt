After you have learned the basics, study the "Gang of four" design patterns book. http://www.amazon.com/Design-Patterns-Object-Oriented-Addison-Wesley-Professional/dp/0201633612/ref=pd_bbs_sr_1?ie=UTF8&s=books&qid=1221488916&sr=8-1 This is a classic, and a must read for any coder who wants to understand how to use OO to design elegant solutions to common coding problems.
