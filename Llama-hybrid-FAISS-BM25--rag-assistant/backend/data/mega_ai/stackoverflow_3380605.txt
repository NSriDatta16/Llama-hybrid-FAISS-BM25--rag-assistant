Make an outlet for the button and connect it. in the .h, between the curly braces UIButton* button; after the curly @property(retain, nonatomic) IBOutlet UIButton* button; and in the .m, after the @implementation @synthesize button; Then connect in IB by dragging a referencing outlet circle in the connections inspector to File's Owner and choosing button. Now you can change the button text with [self.button setTitle: @"hello" forState: UIControlStateNormal]; // or any string, like from your table
