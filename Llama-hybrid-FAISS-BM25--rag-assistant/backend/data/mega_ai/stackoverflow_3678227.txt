From your application, call ChangeWindowMessageFilter with the following values to allow dragging and dropping to/from your elevated application and non-elevated applications like Explorer: ChangeWindowMessageFilter (WM_DROPFILES, MSGFLT_ADD); ChangeWindowMessageFilter (WM_COPYDATA, MSGFLT_ADD); ChangeWindowMessageFilter (0x0049, MSGFLT_ADD);
