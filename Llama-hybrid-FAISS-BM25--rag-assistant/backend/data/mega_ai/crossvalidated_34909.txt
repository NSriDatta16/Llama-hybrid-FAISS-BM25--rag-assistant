Maybe you want to take a look in Statistics and Finance: An Introduction by David Ruppert. It covers a wide range of topics but none in detail. The author actually written this book with engineers in mind and it's very application-oriented. Table of Content: 1 Introduction 2 Probability and Statistical Models 3 Returns 4 Time Series Models 5 Portfolio Theory 6 Regression 7 The Capital Asset Pricing Model 8 Options Pricing 9 Fixed Income Securities 10 Resampling 11 Value-At-Risk 12 GARCH Models 13 Nonparametric Regression and Splines 14 Behavioral Finance
