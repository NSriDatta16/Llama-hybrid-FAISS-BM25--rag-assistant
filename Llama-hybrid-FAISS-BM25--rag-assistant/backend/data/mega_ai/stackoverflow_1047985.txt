Stay away from ACRONYMS (including those you've listed) and Methodologies(tm). At least in the beginning. Read good books. Start with this one: Pragmatic Programmer . Learn algorithms and data structures, possibly from Introduction to algorithms by Cormen et al . Write a lot of code. Practice is more important than anything else.
