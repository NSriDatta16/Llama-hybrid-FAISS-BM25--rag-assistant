I use this command: sudo /Developer/Library/uninstall-devtools --mode=all Edit (1 year later): If you've downloaded Xcode from the App Store, it's self-contained, as @mediaslave suggests. You can just drag it to the trash or use AppZapper (or a similar utility) to remove the developer tools. If you're looking to update it, you can also do that straight through the App Store.
