Managing Gigabytes - Witten, Moffat, and Bell : a quite detailed look at some of the technologies behind information retrieval, text and image compression. (Disclaimer: my university supervisor is the second author.) You should also know about ACM's SIGIR , which organises an annual conference on information retrieval, and has a mailing list as well.
