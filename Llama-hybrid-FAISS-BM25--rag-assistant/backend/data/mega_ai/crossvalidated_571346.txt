Determine expected goals from two normal distributions

Assume Team A scores an average of 3 goals per game with a standard deviation of 1.0, and assuming Team B allows an average of 2 goals per game with a standard deviation of 0.5. How would you determine the expected goals of Team A scoring on Team B?
