Take a look at https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/216/ (Javascript Debugger) or FireBug for Firefox. Both are open source and can give you the details. Another option is the Web Inspector for Chrome and Safari, Dragonfly for Opera, or F12 Dev Tools for Internet Explorer. All of them can give you ideas on how to implement your own debugging tools.
