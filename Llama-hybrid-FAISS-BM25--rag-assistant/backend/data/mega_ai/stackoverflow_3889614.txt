Ok, solved the problem. In my case it was an error with Clientmapping configuration. Teamcity default was "//depot/... //team-city-agent/.." I changed it to "//depot/... //team-city-agent/..." with 3 dots on the end and it worked. Thats the normal way of perforce with 3 dots.
