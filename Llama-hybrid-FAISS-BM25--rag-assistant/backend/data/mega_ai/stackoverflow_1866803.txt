Just a guess: isn't it possible that when you send the Garage instance, the X process is only receiving a stub for it? If so, when you invoke the equals method, it could be in fact executing a remote call to it, actually calling those methods in the original JVM (not in the X process). You should be able to confirm that by adding breakpoints in both JVMs and invoking equals .
