Besides all the dotty mess here, you can use UIView's bringSubviewToFront: and sendSubviewToBack: to organize your subviews. Basically this should help, although if you have more subviews you will need to play around with it a little bit: [self.tabBarController.moreNavigationController.topViewController.view addSubview:imageView]; [self.tabBarController.moreNavigationController.topViewController.view pushSubviewToBack:imageView]; //or [self.tabBarController.moreNavigationController.topViewController.view bringSubviewToFront:tableView];
