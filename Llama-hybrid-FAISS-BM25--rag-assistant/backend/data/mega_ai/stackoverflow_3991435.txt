There is also the option to make a symbolic link for the /images folder to be under the DocumentRoot, or alternatively under the /app_name folder. foo@bar:/var/www/app_name$ ls -la drwxr-xr-x 2 foo foo 4096 2010-10-21 21:55 . drwxr-xr-x 80 foo foo 4096 2010-10-21 21:54 .. lrwxrwxrwx 1 foo foo 10 2010-10-21 21:55 images -> /home/foo/images/ This way, you can link to your images just with : This way, the images don't need to be stored in the same folder, or even on the same partition, and you don't reveal how you file system is organized.
