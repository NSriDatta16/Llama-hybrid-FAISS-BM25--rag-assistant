They often are used to mean the same thing. However, "build" may also mean the full process of compiling and linking a whole application (in the case of e.g. C and C++), or even more, including, among others packaging automatic (unit and/or integration) testing installer generation installation/deployment documentation/site generation report generation (e.g. test results, coverage). There are systems like Maven , which generalize this with the concept of lifecycle , which consists of several stages, producing different artifacts, possibly using results and artifacts from previous stages.
