I would show the shortcuts for the most common operations such as: Save: ctrl-x-s Save as: ctrl-x-w Open/find file: Ctrl-f Undo: ctrl-_ Kill buffer: ctrl-x-k Switch to buffer: ctrl-b Fill paragraph: M-q And copy/cut and paste: Set marker: ctrl-space Cut: ctrl-w Copy: M-w Cut line: ctrl-k Paste: ctrl-y
