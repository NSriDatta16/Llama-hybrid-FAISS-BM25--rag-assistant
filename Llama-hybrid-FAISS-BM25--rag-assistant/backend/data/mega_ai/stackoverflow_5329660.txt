I found a solution by doing an applescript that "Clicks" the "Always Allow" button after having no luck with security or certtool for adding the ACL privileges. #!/usr/bin/osascript tell application "System Events" tell window 1 of process "SecurityAgent" click button "Always Allow" of group 1 end tell end tell
