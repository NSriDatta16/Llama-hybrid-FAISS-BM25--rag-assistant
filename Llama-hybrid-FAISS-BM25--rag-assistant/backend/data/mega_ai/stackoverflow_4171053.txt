It's up to individual teams whether or not they open up their code. On the Contest Rules page under section 8, Ownership and Open Source: Hey, we’re just running a competition here. What you do with your source once the competition is over is up to you. We encourage participants to open source the codebase of their applications for the benefit of the community. However, if you choose not to open source your application, well that’s up to you.
