Often these glasses will include a magnification feature, though this varies from model to model. While not specifically designed as an accessibility too, the Meta Ray-Ban smart glasses are compatible with accessibility app Be My Eyes and can be used to make hands-free phone calls to sighted volunteers. They also have OCR capacity which can be useful for reading signs and text in the same as specifically designed accessibility smart glasses.
