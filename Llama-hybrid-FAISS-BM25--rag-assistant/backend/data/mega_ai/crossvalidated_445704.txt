This would be called ties, according to wiki " https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wilcoxon_signed-rank_test Ties receive a rank equal to the average of the ranks they span." So for your case 1,1,1,1,3,3,3,3,4,4,4,4 will give ranks 2.5, 2.5, 2.5, 2.5, 6.5, 6.5, 6.5, 6.5, 10.5, 10.5, 10.5, 10.5 Notice that the sum of ranks 1, 2, ... 12, is the same as the sum of these tied ranks
