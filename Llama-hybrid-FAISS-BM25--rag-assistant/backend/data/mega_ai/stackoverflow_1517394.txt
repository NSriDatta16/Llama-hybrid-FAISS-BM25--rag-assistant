There is more to JNLP than just Sun's implementation. The OpenJDK packages shipped by Debain, for instance, bundle netx , which stores its files in ~/.netx/cache/ . The Wikipedia entry has a list of known implementations other than Sun's. You really shouldn't rely on this path being well-known in your application's code.
