Typically the connection is closed after use; freeing it back into the pool of available connections. If there are a high number of transactions taking place on a single client it makes sense to leverage a single connection instead of creating multiple connections only to immediately close them. It is somewhat circumstantial however the typical best practice is to close it after use so that it becomes available within the pool again.
