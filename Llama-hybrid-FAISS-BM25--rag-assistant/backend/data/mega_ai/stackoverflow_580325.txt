Two thoughts: Consider organizing the file as a data flow graph and doing some canonicalization on that represention. Since you know the instruction set, this may be feasible, maybe just strapping up a disassembler and doing some text processing. A trainable classifier such as CRM114 might come in handy for giving you a compact representation that gives you some idea whether binaries have much in common.
