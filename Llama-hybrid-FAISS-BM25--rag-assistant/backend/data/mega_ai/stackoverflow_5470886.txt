You are comparing apples and oranges. FastMM memory is netto usage of memory allocated through FastMM. This does not include at least these: FastMM overhead Windows overhead of blocks allocated by FastMM on your behalf Windows overhead of things not allocated by FastMM (like the space occupied by DLL's in your process space) for GUI apps: overhead of GDI, GDI+, DirectX, OpenGL and other storage for visual objects allocated on your behalf. --jeroen
