If you know which observations are incorrect, then you should remove them unless you want capture that effect in your model. Your problem sounds like an outlier detection problem. Depending on your assumptions, you can use a variety of methods out there. One simple example: If you plan on using linear regression, there is a lot of well cited literature on outlier detection in a linear model. Methods such as Studentized-residuals, Leverage using the hat matrix or simply looking at the residual plot.
