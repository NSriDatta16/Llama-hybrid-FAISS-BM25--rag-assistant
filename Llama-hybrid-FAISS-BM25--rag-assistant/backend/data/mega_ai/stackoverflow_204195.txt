You need a smoothing filter, the simplest would be a "moving average": just calculate the average of the last n points. The question here is, how to determine n, can you tell us more about your application? (There are other, more complicated filters. They vary on how they preserve the input data. A good list is in Wikipedia ) Edit!: For 10Hz, average the last 100 values.
