We recently ran into this problem ourselves. See this MSKB article These are the headers we ended up having to use to get it to work over SSL. header("Expires: Sat, 01 Jan 2000 00:00:00 GMT"); header("Last-Modified: " . gmdate("D, d M Y H:i:s") . " GMT"); header("Pragma: public"); header("Expires: 0"); header("Cache-Control: must-revalidate, post-check=0, pre-check=0"); header("Cache-Control: public"); header("Content-Type: application/octet-stream"); header("Content-Disposition: attachment; filename=\"$file_name\";"); header("Content-length: " . strlen($csv_string));
