No need for regex: $parts = parse_url($url); $params = array(); parse_str($parts['query'], $params); If you have PECL pecl_http installed: $url = http_build_url($url, array('query' => 'v='. $params['v']), HTTP_URL_REPLACE | HTTP_URL_STRIP_FRAGMENT); Or without pecl_http: $url = $parts['scheme'] . $parts['host'] . $parts['path'] . '?v=' . $params['v']; This is more robust against changes of the order of the query parameters. Reference: parse_url , parse_str , http_build_url
