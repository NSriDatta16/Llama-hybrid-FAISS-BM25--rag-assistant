Look at Robert Martin's SOLID principles . Specifically, look at the Single responsibility Principle . The complex chain of dependencies in your example looks like it breaks the SRP. Encapsulation -- itself -- isn't a principle. It's part of achieving the various principles. Along with inheritance, polymorphism and other more obscure OO features.
