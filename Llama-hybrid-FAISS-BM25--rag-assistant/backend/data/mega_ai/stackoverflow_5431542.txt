You can either use marshaller.setProperty(Marshaller.JAXB_FRAGMENT, true); or marshaller.setProperty("com.sun.xml.bind.xmlDeclaration", false) to disable the default XML declaration, and then add your custom XML declaration, by marshaller.setProperty("com.sun.xml.bind.xmlHeaders", " "); to the generated xml, thus avoiding the standalone="yes" property.
