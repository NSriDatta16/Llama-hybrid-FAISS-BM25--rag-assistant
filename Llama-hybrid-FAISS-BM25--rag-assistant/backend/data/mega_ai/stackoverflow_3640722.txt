If you use Nginx instead of lighttpd then you can take advantage of Nginx's embedded Perl support to leverage the Perl module JavaScript-Minifier to minify and cache JS server-side. Here are the details on how to achieve this: wiki.nginx.org/NginxEmbeddedPerlMinifyJS
