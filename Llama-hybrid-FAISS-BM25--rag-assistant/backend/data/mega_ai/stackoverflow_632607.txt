While I suppose there must be some way, perhaps kludgy, to do what you want, I am not aware of any way to do it. The standard XML parsers expect well-formed XML, as you're discovering. If you want to store your XML as a number of separate fragments in different files, then probably the best way to do this is to create your own Reader or InputStream that actually (behind the scenes) reads all of the fragments in order, and then provide that wrapped Reader or InputStream to the transformer. That way, the XML parser sees a single XML document but you can store it however you want.
