Compared to EJB, RMI is a "small" specification pertaining to remote invocation. EJB is much larger and actually includes RMI. The Wikipedia article about Enterprise JavaBean is a must read. I would recommend you also read Mastering Enterprise JavaBeans 3.0 by Sriganesh et al.
