Generally we can find many definitions on Coding standards by a simple search. You can easily use a one of them as your guidelines. But honestly it is a convension not a strict standard. So you may have a freedom to use whatever notation. But you should not deviate a lot from current standards because it can make some hidden or unnecessary problems (Ex : In future changes/While refactoring). But as far as you stick to a single standard that would be ok. And the most important thing, you MUST adhere to your company\organization standards. Everyone is supposed to follow such standards, if not you are in trouble. However this is a very good article I found on the web. Coding Standards
