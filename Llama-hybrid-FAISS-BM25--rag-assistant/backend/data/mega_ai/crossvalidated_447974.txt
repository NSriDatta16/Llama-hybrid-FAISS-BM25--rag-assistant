As you say PCA is based on the equation $$X=UV^T$$ To rearrange we premuliply by the inverse of the scores matrix, $U^{-1}$ $$ U^{-1}X = V^T $$ The inverse of U is simply the U matrix times the reciprocal of the squared eigenvalues (norm of the U matrix).
