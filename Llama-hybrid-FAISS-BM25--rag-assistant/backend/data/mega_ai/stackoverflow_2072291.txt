You could use Amazon's Simple Storage platform: http://aws.amazon.com/s3/ You could store your files in a SQL database as blob fields. Bandwidth of 300 GB signifies the amount of data they will let you transfer per month before they will cut your site off or start charging you per GB (depending on who the provider is)
