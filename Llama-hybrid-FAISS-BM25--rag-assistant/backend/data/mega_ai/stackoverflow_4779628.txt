No, basically. Compilation symbols are applied on a per-project basis in the project settings, and on a per-file basis depending on #define pragmas. There's no way of making the project you're compiling against determine your compilation symbols - they vanish after compilation, effectively. It would be simplest to create appropriate solution-wide configurations, and project configurations within them which define the appropriate symbols.
