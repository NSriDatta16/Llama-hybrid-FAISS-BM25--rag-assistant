Besides the issues strager mentioned, you might be missing initialization for adjacent , e.g. like this: Node::Node(std::string name, unsigned adj_num) : name(name) , adjacent((adj_num > 0) ? new Node*[adj_num] : 0) , adjNum(adj_num) {} Note the unsigned parameter, a negative adj_num is most likely meaningless in this context. If you don't initialize adjacent , it contains some garbage value and dereferencing it or passing it to delete[] leads to undefined behaviour.
