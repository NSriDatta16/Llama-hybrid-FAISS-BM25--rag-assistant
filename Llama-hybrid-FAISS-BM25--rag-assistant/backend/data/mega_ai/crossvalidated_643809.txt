You will have to define a class of data generating processes or the problem is not well-defined. If you are willing to restrict attention to invertible ARIMA processes for example, then Piccolo distance might do what you want. See https://www.jstatsoft.org/article/view/v062i01 for a general discussion of distances between time series.
