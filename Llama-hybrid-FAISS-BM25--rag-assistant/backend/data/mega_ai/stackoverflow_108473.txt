It can help with performance. moving certain tables/elemnts to distinct file areas/portions of the disk. this can reduce to a certain extent the amount of external fragmentation impacting the daabase. I would also look at other factors such as tracesql to determine why queries etc are slowing down - there can be other factors such as query statistics, SP recompiles etc that are easier to fix and can give you greater gains in performance.
