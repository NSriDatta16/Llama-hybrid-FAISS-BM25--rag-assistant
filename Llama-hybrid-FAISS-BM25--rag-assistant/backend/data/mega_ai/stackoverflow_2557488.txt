I think (and hope) that this is very likely not possible, and the reasons are that car manufacturers would be in a lot of legal trouble if they allowed "non-core" gadgets like a JVM built into the entertainment/navigation system to interfere with the motor or steering controls. That is a much worse security risk than your average browser exploit. Fly-by-wire cars are scary enough as it is without end-user/hacker accessible parts.
