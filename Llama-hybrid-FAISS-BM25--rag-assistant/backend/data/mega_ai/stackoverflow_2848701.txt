Computer science cherry picks mathematics for its needs, at least where you aren't using computing for an area that is heavily mathematical, for example games programming. This includes logic, graph theory, statistics, vectors and trigonometry, set theory. For the average job your daily task will involve logic and not much more. If you get an above average job then this may differ.
