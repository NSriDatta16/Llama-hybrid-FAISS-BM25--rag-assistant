You should use SHA1 to hash your passwords for storage in the database. It's the simplest, yet most effective way to store passwords: $password = sha1($password); It's also exceptionally safe. Though the integrity of it is beginning to creep, it's rather easy to upgrade this function to SHA-256 (which is incredibly secure).
