I would suggest that you read http://research.microsoft.com/en-us/um/people/cormac/papers/2009/SoLongAndNoThanks.pdf This paper discusses part of the reason it is hard to get users to follwo good security advice; in short the costs lie with the users and they experience little or no benefit. Increasing the password length and forcing more complex passwords can reduce seciryt by leading to one or both of; reused passwords between sites/applications and writing down of passwords.
