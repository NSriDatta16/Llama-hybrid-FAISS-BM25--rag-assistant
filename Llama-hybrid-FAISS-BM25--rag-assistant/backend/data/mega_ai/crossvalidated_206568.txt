In the hope of giving you something other than just a "go and read a book" answer. Assuming that you are using [R] there are a bunch of useful automated feature selection libraries. Boruta. TLDR; Uses Random Forests and predictor randomization to determine importance. Best place to get your head around it is the JStatSoft paper: https://www.jstatsoft.org/article/view/v036i11/v36i11.pdf and for an excellent worked example try this Kaggle script. https://www.kaggle.com/jimthompson/bnp-paribas-cardif-claims-management/using-the-boruta-package-to-determine-fe Max Kuhn's Caret package has a bunch of useful tools including wrapping several algorithm with feature selection or at least ranking capability. http://topepo.github.io/caret/featureselection.html VarSelRF provides both forward and backward elimination and importance rank filtering. https://cran.r-project.org/web/packages/varSelRF/index.html HTH
