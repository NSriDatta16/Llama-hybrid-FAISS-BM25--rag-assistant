For your ordinal responses, look into ordinal logistic regression. See for instance How to understand output from R's polr function (ordered logistic regression)? . Use group membership as predictor. For the nominal (not ordered) responses a chisquare test could be fine. But maybe multinomial logistic regression is better, see for instance Logistic regression for multiclass .
