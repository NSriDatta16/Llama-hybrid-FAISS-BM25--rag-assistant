If you want to get your hands dirty, just leverage objective-C's dynamic behavior, and implement it yourself. You could create a category on UIView with a recursive method that prints out info about its children. Then in GDB just call it on the window (since window is a subclass of UIView. Something like: @interface UIView ( DebugExras ) - (void)debugPrintViewTree; @end And in GDB: gdb: po [[[UIApplication sharedApplication] keyWindow] debugPrintViewTree]
