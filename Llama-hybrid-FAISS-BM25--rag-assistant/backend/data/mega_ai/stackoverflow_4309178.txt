The other answers have explained why this is a bad idea. Nonetheless, one way to do it is by preprocessing a makefile fragment: In foo.mk.in : #ifdef FOO FOO_DEFINED := y #else FOO_DEFINED := n #endif In Makefile : foo.mk: foo.mk.in $(CPP) $(CPPFLAGS) -o $@ $
