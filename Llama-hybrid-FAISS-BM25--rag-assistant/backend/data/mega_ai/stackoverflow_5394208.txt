The major reasons you would likely pick Gradle over Gant: The functionality of Gant is a subset of Gradle. (basically a groovy wrapper around ANT) Gant is built with Gradle Beyond that Gradle has a plugin architecture, is DSL based, integrates with Maven and Ivy and has true incremental builds. Gant was a valuable innovation in its time, the lessons of which are in Gradle.
