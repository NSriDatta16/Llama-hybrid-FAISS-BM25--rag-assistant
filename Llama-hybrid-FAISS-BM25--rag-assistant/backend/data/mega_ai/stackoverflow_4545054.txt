This question doesn't work very well. Selecting frameworks is difficult, and very context specific. For each selection process you might end up with a simple shortlist and a simple list of questions to answer, but those lists do not transfer well to other selections. The number of parameters and the parameter sensitivity influencing a decision is very large, and at enterprise level a lot of them are not technical. Currently, there are no frameworks available that are ready to support these near-term enterprise needs: the switch for most of the workforce from pc to tablet and phone; the switch from web client and rdbms to p2p/disconnected based storage and distribution
