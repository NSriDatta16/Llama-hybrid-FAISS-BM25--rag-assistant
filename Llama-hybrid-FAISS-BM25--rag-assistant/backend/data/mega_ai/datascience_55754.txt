The purpose of smoothing is to prevent a language model from assigning zero probability to unseen events. That is needed because in some cases, words can appear in the same context, but they didn't in your train set. Smoothing is a quite rough trick to make your model more generalizable and realistic. You can also see it as a tool to prevent overfitting.
