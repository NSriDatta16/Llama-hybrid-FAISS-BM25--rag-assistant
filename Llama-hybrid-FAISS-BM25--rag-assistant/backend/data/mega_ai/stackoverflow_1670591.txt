I'm sure everyone knows this already, but what the heck... fragmentation is evil. It's surprising how many developer systems have badly fragmented NTFS drives. Adding a nightly scheduled defrag task is an easy way to mitigate this, at least on XP systems. Also, when purchasing a drive, another reason to get a really big one is to avoid filling the drive up to the point where defragging starts to fail.
