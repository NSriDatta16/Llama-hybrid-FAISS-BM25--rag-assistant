Here's a quick and dirty way: "hello %%one_boy%%'s something %%four_girl%%'s something more".gsub(/(%%.*?%%)/) do |x| x[2 .. (x.length-3)].upcase end The x[2 .. (x.length-3)] bit slices out the middle of the match (i.e. strips off the leading and trailing two characters).
