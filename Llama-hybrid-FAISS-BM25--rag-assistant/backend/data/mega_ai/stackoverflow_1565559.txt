Another way would be to use a do block: my @files = do { opendir my $d, '/your/dir/'; readdir $d; }; Its more elegant because my $d is local to block (unlike your DIR global) and closedir isn't needed because the filehandle is automatically closed when $d went out of scope. /I3az/
