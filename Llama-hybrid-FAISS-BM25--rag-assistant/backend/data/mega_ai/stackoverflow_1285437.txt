Your versioning scheme depends of your business and technical needs. Some companies release "major" upgrade every year to get attention and some income for upgrades. Some of them still release betas, until satisfied with the software quality. Prepare your own scheme and let your customers know it. Typically first letter is a main version numer for major changes, then for improvements, and then for builds and patches.
