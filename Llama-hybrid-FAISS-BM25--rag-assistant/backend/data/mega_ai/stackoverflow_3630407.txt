save the output line with squeezing all whitespace characters: LINE=$(top -b -n 1 | tail -n +8 | head -n 1 | tr -s ' ') And then use cut to get the part you want: echo " Parameter is $(echo $LINE | cut -d' ' -f2) $(echo $LINE | cut -d' ' -f3)" etc. granted, not the most elegant way, but the fastest I can come up with
