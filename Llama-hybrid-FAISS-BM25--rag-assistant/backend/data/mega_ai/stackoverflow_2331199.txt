Functions are just a way of grouping statements of code together in a way that makes them easier to call and reuse. In your example doit() only contains one statement and contains no parameters so the value of it is questionable, you may as well just call makeWhizzyWig directly. However, in most cases functions are a vital part of structuring and organising code.
