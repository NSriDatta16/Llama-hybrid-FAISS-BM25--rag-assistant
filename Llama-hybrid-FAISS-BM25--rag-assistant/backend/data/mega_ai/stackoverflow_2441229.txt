Use a time series package. The xts package has functions designed specifically to do that. Or look at the aggregate and rollapply functions in the zoo package. The rmetrics ebook has a useful discussion, including a performance comparison of the various packages: https://www.rmetrics.org/files/freepdf/TimeSeriesFAQ.pdf Edit : Look at my answer to this question . Basically you need to truncate every timestamp into a specific interval and then do the aggregation using those new truncated timestamps as your grouping vector.
