I had this same scenario - I fixed it by converting the Category columns to 'object' instead of the 'category' type. X[categorical_features]=X[categorical_features].astype("object") Edit: Another possible cause, SMOTENC wants indices instead of the names of categorical attributes but other transformers may be reordering the dataframe columns (numeric scalers for example) so the indices are wrong when the pipeline gets to SMOTENC
