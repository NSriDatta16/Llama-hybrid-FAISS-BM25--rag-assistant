To give a practical example, still quite relevant. In the field of flow cytometry , a recent (2015) clustering algorithm, FlowSom , makes use of self-organizing maps. Even more recently (2016, see here ), it has been shown to be amongst the top performing clustering algorithms in the field of flow and mass cytometry.
