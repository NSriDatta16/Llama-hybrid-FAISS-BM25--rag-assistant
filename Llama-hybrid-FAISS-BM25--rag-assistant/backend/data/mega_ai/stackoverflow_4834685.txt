Calculate how much space [pixels] one item, including its padding, will take in the display area. Multiply that by the amount of items you are showing at any given time and this gives you your fluid width for your display area. Look at this Pagination example for the Carousel by Yahoo Pay special attention to numItems and the following CSS excerpt to get your fluidity: /* Always be sure to give your carousel items a width and a height */ .yui-carousel-element li { height: 75px; width: 75px; }
