I ended up embedding the video as binary data: [Embed(source="1004.flv", mimeType="application/octet-stream")] Then using NetStream.appendBytes() to feed the video to a NetStream that's hooked up to a Video object. This works beautifully, and the NetStream can be paused and replayed.
