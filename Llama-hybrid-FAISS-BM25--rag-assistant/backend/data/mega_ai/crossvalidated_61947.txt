If your data happens to be multivariate time series (or something that behaves similarly), maybe Multivariate Curve Resolution could help. Literature: A. de Juan, M. Mäder, M. Martínez, and R. Tauler: Combining hard- and soft-modelling to solve kinetic problems, Chemometrics and Intelligent Laboratory Systems, 2000, 54, 123-141. DOI: 10.1016/S0169-7439(00)00112-X In R, package ALS gives an implementation, and it is also discussed in the UseR! Chemometrics book .
