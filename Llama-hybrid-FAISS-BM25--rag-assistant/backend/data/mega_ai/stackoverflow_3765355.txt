figure out which columns are giving you the most problems, then change \begin{longtable}{cccccccccc} to \begin{longtable}{>{\raggedright}p{width-of-column}ccccccccc} the >{\raggedright} will tell latex to make the cell content with ragged right edges instead of justified and the p{width-of-column} will allow you to specify the width of your column.
