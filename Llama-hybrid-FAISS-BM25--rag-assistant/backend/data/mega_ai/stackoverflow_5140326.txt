You don't even need to mention them that word. Just tell them you are working on the task at hand. As refactoring is normally an integral part of the development cycle ( test - code- refactor ), this is indeed (a part of the) truth. If (s)he insists on getting more details, you can tell you are "finalizing the solution" , "ensuring long term maintainability of the code" , or - if (s)he fancies management speak - "leveraging the synergies within the product architecture" :-)
