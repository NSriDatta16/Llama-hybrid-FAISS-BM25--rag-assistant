This depends on the rest of your code, but it should be ok for you to do: p{ margin-bottom:0; } You may think this will affect all of the rest of your paragraphs, but due to CSS's "margin-collapsing" feature it actually shouldn't - provided you're in standards mode with a valid DOCTYPE .
