Just to save someone the misery I had to go through. What is required is a media scan of the SDCARD to make these properties available through the content provider and android does this when the media is mounted: sendBroadcast(new Intent(Intent.ACTION_MEDIA_MOUNTED, Uri.parse("file://" + Environment.getExternalStorageDirectory().toString())));
