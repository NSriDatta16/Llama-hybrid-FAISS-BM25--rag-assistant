I'd recommend the Crystal Clear method The Seven Properties Frequent delivery/integration using time-boxed iterations Reflect and improve, criticise and fix Osmotic (passive) knowledge acquisition and communication through office organisation and open channels Personal Safety, safe to be honest, confidence to court criticism Stay focused, clear tasks, priorities on work, limit the workload Access to expert users, fast, quality feedback The usual agile stuff: automated testing, CM, continuous integration
