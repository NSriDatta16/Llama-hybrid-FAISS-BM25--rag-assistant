$_SERVER['HTTP_REFERER'] is the full URL that led to the page that checks it. It's not guaranteed to be set, though it's the only way to know what you're looking for. Also, you may want to check $_SERVER['HTTP_ACCEPT_LANGUAGE'] instead of $_SERVER['HTTP_USER_AGENT'] for the language of the user. Here is the full reference to the $_SERVER superglobal array.
