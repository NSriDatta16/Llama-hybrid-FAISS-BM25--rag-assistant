This kind of expert system is often built by using Bayesian Networks which model the probabilities of questions, answers and diagnoses and their dependencies. As a basic first read whether this is helpful for you, you can check out the Wikipedia article . If you want to learn these techniques in depth, I recommend the superb book by Koller and Friedman, "Probabilistic Graphical Models".
