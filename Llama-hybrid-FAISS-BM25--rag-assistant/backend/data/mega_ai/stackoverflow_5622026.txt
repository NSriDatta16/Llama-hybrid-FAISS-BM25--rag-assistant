You have the xhdpi and xlarge qualifiers for the resources. Also, the usual rule to know the size of an hdpi icon corresponding to a mdpi icon is to multiply by 1.5 the dimensions (because resolution is 1.5 times greater: 240dpi vs 160dpi). For tablets width xhdpi screens, you could apply the same calculation: multiply by 2 the dimensions of mdpi images (320dpi vs 160dpi).
