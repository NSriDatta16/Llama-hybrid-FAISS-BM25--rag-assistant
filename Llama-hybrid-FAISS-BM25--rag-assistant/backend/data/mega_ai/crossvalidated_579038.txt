De Morgan: $$\Pr(\cap_{i=1}^n A_i)=1-\Pr((\cap_{i=1}^n A_i)^c)=1-\Pr(\cup_{i=1}^n A_i^c)$$ Subadditivity: $$\Pr(\cup_{i=1}^n A_i^c)\leq\sum_{i=1}^n \Pr(A_i^c) \Leftrightarrow 1-\Pr(\cup_{i=1}^n A_i^c)\geq 1-\sum_{i=1}^n \Pr(A_i^c)=\sum_{i=1}^n \Pr(A_i)-(n-1)$$ The result follows: $$\Pr(\cap_{i=1}^n A_i)\geq \sum_{i=1}^n \Pr(A_i)-(n-1)$$
