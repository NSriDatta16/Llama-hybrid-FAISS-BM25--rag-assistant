Depending on your other requirements, a min-heap might be what you are looking for. It gives you constant time retrieval of the minimum element. However you cannot do some other operations with the same ease as with a simple binary search tree, like determining if a value is in the tree or not. You can take a look at splay trees , a kind of self-balancing binary tree that provides improved access time to recently accessed elements.
