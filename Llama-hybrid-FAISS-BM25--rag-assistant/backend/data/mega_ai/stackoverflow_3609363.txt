Your best bet is to add the following headers to the top of your page that is to return the content you wish not to be cached header('Last-Modified: Mon, 01 Jan 1970 05:00:00 GMT'); header('Cache-Control: no-store, no-cache, must-revalidate'); header('Pragma: no-cache'); This will allow you to control the cache on the server side rather then adding the "random" number hack at the end of the request.
