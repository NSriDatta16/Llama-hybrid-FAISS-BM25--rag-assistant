PC Lint is generally considered the most pragmatically useful of the reasonably-priced tools with Misra checking capability. It is pretty straightforward to semi-integrate any command-line checking tool into Eclipse, either as an 'External Tool' or (usually better) calling it from your project makefile under a dedicated target .
