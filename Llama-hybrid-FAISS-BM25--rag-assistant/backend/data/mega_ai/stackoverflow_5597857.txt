Order by the date, and select the first row only: SELECT DATE(`created_at`) as date, account_id, changed_by_id, status FROM statuses WHERE `changed_to` = 'entered' AND account_id = 49794 ORDER BY 1 LIMIT 1; This may not be the most elegant option, but it's both clear to understand and efficient.
