you can use an advise for this. For your .emacs: (defadvice fill-paragraph (around disable-for-visual-line-mode activate) (unless visual-line-mode ad-do-it)) This will change fill-paragraph to do nothing when visual-line-mode is on. You can also add an error if you prefer that.
