Yes, you are right that a normal distribution can't be assumed. You need to account for many things in time series data. One of them is trend. If you don't account for outliers, changes in variance, parameters, seasonality, level(ie intercept) and outliers then you won't find the change in trend. You should refer to this paper " Outliers, Level Shifts, and Variance Changes in Time Series " by Tsay. Post an example dataset.
