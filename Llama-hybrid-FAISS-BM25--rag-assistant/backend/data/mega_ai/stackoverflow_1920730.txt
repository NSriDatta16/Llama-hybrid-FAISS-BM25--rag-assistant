No, conditional comments are entirely an Internet Explorer thing AFAIK. However, you can create a conditional comment that IE will not parse and thus is for non-IE browsers only: this is however not valid HTML. There seem to be workarounds to make it valid. Check out the "Downlevel-Revealed conditional comment" paragraph in the Wikipedia article .
