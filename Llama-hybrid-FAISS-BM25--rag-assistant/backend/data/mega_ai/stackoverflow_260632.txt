You might consider partitioning the functionality to reflect the organization of the stakeholders. Usually if you have two distinct organizational groups, then development and administration requirements are easier to manage if the functionality is similarly partioned. And vise versa. Most of us don't spend that much time writing applications that explore the outer boundaries of hardware and software capabilities.
