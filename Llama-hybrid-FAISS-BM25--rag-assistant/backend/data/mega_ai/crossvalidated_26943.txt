You should add the Bayesian network to your question since that implies a further independence assumption: E and F are independent give D. There might still be something missing. Do you know P(E)? In that case, you could simply marginalize over E: $P(D|F) = \sum_i P(E=i)P(D|E=i \cap F)$.
