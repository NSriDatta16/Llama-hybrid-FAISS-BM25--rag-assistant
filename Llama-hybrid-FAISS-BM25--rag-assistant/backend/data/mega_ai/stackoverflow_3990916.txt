Windows has an option to disable re-drawing of windows until you've finished moving them. Perhaps it is enabled on one machine but not the other? When you drag any window around does it render as a dotted outline, or is the whole window re-drawn as you move it? On Windows 2003, the option is under Display Properties , Appearance , Effects , Show window contents while dragging :
