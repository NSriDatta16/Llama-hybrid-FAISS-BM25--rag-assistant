Simulation of a time series using the unconditional moments

$y_{t}$ follows a covariance stationary AR(1) process $ y_t = \phi y_{t-1} + \varepsilon_t \hspace{1cm} \varepsilon \sim \mathcal{N}(0,\sigma^2) $ I want to simulate a time series $y_{1:T}$ . Can I use the unconditional moments to simulate the time series, namely draw from $y_t \sim \mathcal{N}\left(0, \frac{\sigma^2}{1-\phi^2}\right) \hspace{1cm} t=1, \ldots, T $ to obtain $y_{1:T}$ ?
