Its just a style guideline. Programming languages have started to recommend and push formatting conventions so that code is more readable. Also, symbols that get substituted by the preprocessor deserve special attention-- they live outside/before the type system and may not be what they appear to be. Constants are just constants, they won't change at compile or runtime.
