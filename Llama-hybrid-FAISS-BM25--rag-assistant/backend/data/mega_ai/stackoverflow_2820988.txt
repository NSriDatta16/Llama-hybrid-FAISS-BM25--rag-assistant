If you are looking for a couple of examples: Facebook moved to Cassandra only after it was storing over 7 Terabytes of inbox data. (Source: Lakshman, Malik: Cassandra - A Decentralized Structured Storage System .) (... Even though they were having quite a few issues at that stage.) Wikipedia also handles hundreds of Gigabytes of text data in MySQL.
