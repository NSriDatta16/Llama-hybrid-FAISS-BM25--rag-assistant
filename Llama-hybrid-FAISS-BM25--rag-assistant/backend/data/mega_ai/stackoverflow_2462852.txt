Put your cursor on the first line, count how many lines should be indented, in the above example it's 5, then for hash (#) type :.,.+5%s/^\([ ]*\)/#\1/ or for a tab indentation, :.,.+5%s/^\([ ]*\)/ \1/ , and are the tab and enter keys. There are probably more elegant ways of doing this, but something like this is a quick-n-dirty thing.
