As a process of simplification use De Morgan's Law to move the NOT operator into the parenthesis. So it would be !((A+B) * (A+!B)) = !(A+B) + !(A+!B) The next step (again use De Morgan's Law) after this would lead to your answer. I hope this helps. cheers
