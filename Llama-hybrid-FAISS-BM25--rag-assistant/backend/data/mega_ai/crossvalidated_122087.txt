A good approach to reduce the dimension of the feature space in regression is partial least-square regression, which finds factors which are both good at explaining the variance in the feature space, but also at predicting the variable of interest. With a few tweaks, this approach can be used for logistic regression too. For a discussion, see this paper , or this one .
