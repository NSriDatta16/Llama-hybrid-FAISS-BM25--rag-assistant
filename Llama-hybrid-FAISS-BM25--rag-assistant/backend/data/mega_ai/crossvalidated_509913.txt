Research has been targeted at RL with subgoals. It is sometimes referred to as hierarchical reinforcement learning [0]. Most works formalize this using the options framework [1]. For example environments, see these [2] [3]. A thing that is relatively recent is enforcing the subgoals somehow. RS Sutton, D Precup, S Singh. 1999 [2] Building Portable Options: Skill Transfer in Reinforcement Learning. GD Konidaris, AG Barto. 2007 [3] Modular multitask reinforcement learning with policy sketches. J Andreas, D Klein, S Levine. 2017
