Enabling implicit flush as blueyed said should do the trick since it calls flush after every echo however some browsers also require no-cache headers to be set. Here is what I use. Your mileage may vary depending on the browser. header('Cache-Control: no-cache, no-store, max-age=0, must-revalidate'); header('Expires: Mon, 26 Jul 1997 05:00:00 GMT'); // Date in the past header('Pragma: no-cache');
