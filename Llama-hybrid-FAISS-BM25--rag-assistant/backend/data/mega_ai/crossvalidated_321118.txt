Using videos Khan Academy (statistics and probability) : These videos explain all the basic statistics, distributions, probability, CLT, ANOVA, Goodness of Fit. Books The Statistical Sleuth - Ramsey and Schafer Applied Linear Regression - S Weisberg The above books are common texts used in graduate level mathematics/statistics. The authors explain the concepts by using examples and they are my go to books . I have used these books during my graduate school. For Advanced Topics For advanced topics of Markov Chain, MCMC, Time series modelling, I recommend picking up any textbook which deals with NLP (natural language processing) and read their first few chapters.
