When you start a new job, is a new person born who is not you, and the old you killed? If your design is based on that scenario, as it appears, then the practice in your organisation is very strange. Instead, a person has an employment relationship with an employer. Don't use inheritance to model roles; allow the same person to have roles which represent their relationships with other objects.
