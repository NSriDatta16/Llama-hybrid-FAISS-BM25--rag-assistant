Found the answer: There are two protocols, pcast:// and itpc:// (the second may stand for "iTunes PC"). Feedburner uses the first of those, so that is what I'm going to use. Update: Firefox doesn't support pcast (WTF Mozilla?). itpc works in all browsers I tested.
