As long as you limit it to basic shapes, you could calculate an averaged 'direction' of the current stroke, and create a sequence of 'strokes' out of these. It's probably easier to recognize a shape based on that info: a circle has a quite constant second derivative a 'seven' has a stroke to the right, followed by a stroke to the lower left ...
