One major reason has nothing to do with technical reasons. A lot of "enterprise" systems are being sold to big stuffy "enterprise" clients. The client demands SOAP and that's what they get. What are their reasons? Sometimes it is very pragmatic: their team is familiar with SOAP and they have many existing SOAP services (and this team would maintain the product after it is delivered). Sometimes it's not: they read it in some article and they have their minds made up.
