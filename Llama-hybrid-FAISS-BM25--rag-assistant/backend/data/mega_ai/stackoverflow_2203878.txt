I would encourage you to take a look at Perl for tasks like this. Many of Perl's features are designed expressly with the goal of "processing text with concise syntax". It can of course jump over into cryptic, but so can Ruby. Perl also has extensive support for one line programs typed into the shell. echo "hello world" | perl -lpe 's/\b(.)/\U$1/g' prints Hello World
