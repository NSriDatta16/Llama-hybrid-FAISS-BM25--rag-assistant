I had this issue with the Magento admin session . The crux of the problem is that Magento's cookies obey PHP's session.gc_maxlifetime parameter. If you adjust that in your htaccess like the following, you should have better luck: php_value session.gc_maxlifetime 86400 Hope that helps! Thanks, Joe
