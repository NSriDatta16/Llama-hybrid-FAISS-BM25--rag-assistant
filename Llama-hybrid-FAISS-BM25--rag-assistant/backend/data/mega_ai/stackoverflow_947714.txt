I am pretty sure we do not have access to this (although I don't really see why). I know that flash only redraws the changes of the stage from frame to frame. You can see this by right clicking on the Flash Debug Player and selecting "Show Redraw Regions". You could try looking at what people are doing with the Convolution Filter and the Displacement Map Filter . Here are some examples: Water ripples - David Lenaerts Rain effect - Scott Morgan There examples seem to work fine, with good speeds, although they have quite small stage sizes.
