You should use \vphantom , it makes a vertical space equal to its argument and no horizontal space: \begin{equation} \begin{split} f = & \left( \frac{a}{b} + \right. \\ & \left. \vphantom{\frac{a}{b}} c \right) + d \end{split} \end{equation} (I recommend \vphantom over \phantom in this case because \phantom adds horizontal space that you don't need.)
