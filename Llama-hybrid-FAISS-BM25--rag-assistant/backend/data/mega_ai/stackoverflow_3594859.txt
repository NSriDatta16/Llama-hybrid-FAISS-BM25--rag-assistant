This can helps when there is lot of roles and when they are very complex. The more roles you have, the more complex it is to satisfy all of them. They have different needs, values, power, etc. Having the picture sounds a bit trivial, but it really helps too. Check this really nice video from Jeff Patton on the subject: http://www.infoq.com/presentations/pragmatic-personas His website: http://www.agileproductdesign.com/
