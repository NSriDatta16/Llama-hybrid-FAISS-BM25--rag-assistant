Your app cannot. It's done all down in CFNetwork - Communicating with HTTP Servers . I believe it just adds missing header values not supplied by NSURLRequest . The defaults are: USER-AGENT "AppName - Eng/1.0 CFNetwork/485.13.9 Darwin/10.7.0" ACCEPT "*/*" ACCEPT-LANGUAGE "en-us" ACCEPT-ENCODING "gzip, deflate" CONNECTION "keep-alive"
