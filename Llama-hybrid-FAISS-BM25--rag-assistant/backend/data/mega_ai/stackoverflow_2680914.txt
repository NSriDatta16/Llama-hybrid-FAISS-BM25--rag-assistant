It may be legal, and whoever you're working with might not mind (even more so if you're working alone) but if it were me it would make me crazy. Spelling out D10.roll() doesn't take up much of your time, and it makes your code that much easier to follow since the name is self-explanatory. The bottom line, though, is that elegance is in the eye of the beholder; if everyone who's working on your code agrees that that's a simple and clear identifier, nobody is going to stop you.
