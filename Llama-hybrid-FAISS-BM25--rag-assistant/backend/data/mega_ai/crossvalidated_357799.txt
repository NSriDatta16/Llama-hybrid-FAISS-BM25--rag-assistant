If your goal is just to complete descriptives with no standard error around the descriptives (i.e., no confidence interval around the sample mean or sample standard deviation), you can use complete() to recover the imputed data sets and then perform actions on them. For example, to compute the average sample mean and variance across the imputations, you might do the following: imp.data If you want inference, you can manually apply Rubin's rules to the estimates.
