What are you looking for? Or, more importantly, what are you going to do if you detect it? By definition, onPause() is when you should be starting to pause your worker threads and save your data back to persistent storage. In onDestroy() , you can call isFinishing() to see if you're on your way out or just destroying and then recreating yourself because of something like an orientation change.
