In this MWE y equals your time series. y1_1000 your partly time series which you want to forecast. arma11 equals your ARMA model which you fitted against your time series. interval_forecast equals your forecast and y1001_1100 equals the real values of the time series which you want to compare against your forecast. library(tseries) library(forecast) set.seed(1) y If one wants to know how many observations are not coverd by the interval.
