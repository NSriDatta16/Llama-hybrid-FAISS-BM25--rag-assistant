Placing output=AST; in the options section will not produce an actual AST, it only causes ANTLR to create CommonTree tokens instead of CommonToken s (or, in your case, the equivalent C structs). If you use output=AST; , the next step is to put tree operators, or rewrite rules inside your parser rules that give shape to your AST. See this previous Q&A to find out how to create a proper AST.
