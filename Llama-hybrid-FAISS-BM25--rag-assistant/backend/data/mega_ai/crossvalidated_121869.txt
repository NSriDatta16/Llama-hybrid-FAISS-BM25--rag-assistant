Spectral density function is an Fourier transform of autocovariance function and there exists a Rietz - Fisher Representation theorem which states that time domain and frequency domain representations for the time series are almost equal. There are actually methods for estimation on frequency domain which might take into account various frequency bands. http://www.jstor.org/discover/10.2307/2526316?uid=3737976&uid=2&uid=4&sid=21105062541343
