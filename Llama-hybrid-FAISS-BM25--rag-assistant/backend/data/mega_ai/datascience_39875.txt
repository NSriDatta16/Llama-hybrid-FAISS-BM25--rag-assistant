1) Could you upload sample images maybe? It would be easier to decide. 2) Your dataset is very small, training anything significant from scratch will most certainly overfit the model. Take an existing model, that knows what a bag is (e.g. Mask R-CNN) and finetune it to your problem by changing the loss function and some architecture. 3) Actual framework should not matter: work with whichever you find convenient.
