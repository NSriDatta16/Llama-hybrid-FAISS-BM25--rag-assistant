After vectorizing your custom text, you need to do one of the 2 things in spaCy: load those binary vectors to nlp.vocab with something like nlp.vocab.load_rep_vectors . Or simply replace the vec.bin file in "data/vocab/vec.bin". Detailed information here: https://stackoverflow.com/questions/43524301/update-spacy-vocabulary .
