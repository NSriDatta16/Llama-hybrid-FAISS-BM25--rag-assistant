Your best option is to make one large neural network and for the output layer you should use 20 neurons (number of possible outcomes) and you should use a softmax activation function. Your model can look something like this. input_dim = X_train.shape[1] model = Sequential() model.add(layers.Dense(10, activation='relu', input_dim=input_dim)) model.add(layers.Dense(20, activation='softmax')) model.compile(loss='categorical_crossentropy', optimizer='adam', metrics=['accuracy']) model.summary()
