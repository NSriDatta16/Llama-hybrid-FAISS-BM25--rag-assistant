Wikipedia is a good source: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/isotonic_regression . Two books that cover the subject are 1) Robertson, T., Wright, F. T. and Dykstra, R. L. (1988) Order Restricted Inference. Wiley. 2) Barlow, R. E., Bartholomew, D. J., Bremner, J. M. and Brunk, H. D.(1972). Statistical Inference Under Order Restrictions: The theory of Isotonic Regression. Wiley. You will find that this produces the closest monotonic function (increasing or decreasing) to the data.
