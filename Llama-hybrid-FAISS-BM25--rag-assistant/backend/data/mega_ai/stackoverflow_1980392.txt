Alexa estimates website traffic by extrapolating the data from the browsing sessions of the subset of the Internet population who use the Alexa toolbar or browser extensions. This isn't a truly random sample, so questions are raised over the accuracy of such data: http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Alexa_Internet#Accuracy_of_ranking_by_the_Alexa_Toolbar Installing the Alexa toolbar modifies the browser user-agent, so you can estimate the % of visitors to your site who are contributing data to Alexa by scanning your server logs for requests with the appropriate user-agent strings.
