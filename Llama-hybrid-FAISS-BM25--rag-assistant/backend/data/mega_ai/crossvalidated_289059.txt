If you write the autocorrelation of your time series at lag 1 : $$ \begin{align}\mathbb{E}\left[X_{t+1}X_{t}\right] &= \mathbb{E}\left[X_t\left(\rho X_t+\sqrt{1-\rho^2} \varphi\right)\right] \\&= \mathbb{E}\left[\rho X_t^2\right]+\sqrt{1-\rho^2}\mathbb{E}\left[X_t \varphi\right]\\ &=\rho \sigma^2+\sqrt{1-\rho^2}\mathbb{E}[X_t]\mathbb{E}[\varphi]\\ &=\rho\sigma^2 \end{align}$$ because $\mathbb{E[\varphi]=0}$ You can then have an estimation of $\sigma^2$ based on an estimation of $\mathbb{E}\left[X_{t+1}X_t\right]$
