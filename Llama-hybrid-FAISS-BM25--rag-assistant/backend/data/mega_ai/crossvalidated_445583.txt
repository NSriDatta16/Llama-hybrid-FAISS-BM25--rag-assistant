Detrending methods

Detrending is removing a trend from a time series; a trend usually refers to a change in the mean over time. When you detrend data, you remove an aspect from the data that you think is causing some kind of distortion. To detrend a time series you first need to estimate the trend and there is multiple ways to do that moving average method parametric regression non parametric regression my question is how do we know which method gives us the best estimation for the trend ? in other word , if we conduct all these methods what are the caractiristics that tells us which is the best estimator
