If you want to get crazy, mark the most 'active' emails in one of several ways: Last access Frequency of use Contacts with pending sales Direct bosses Etc Then, present the active emails at the top of the list. Pay attention to which "group" your user uses most. Switch to that sorting strategy exclusively after enough data is collected. It's a lot of work but kind of fun...
