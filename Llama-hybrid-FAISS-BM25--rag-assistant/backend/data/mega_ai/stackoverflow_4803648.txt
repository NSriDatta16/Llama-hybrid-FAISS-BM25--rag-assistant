First, make sure you have correctly installed the Installer, Developer and WWDR certs in your Keychain. In Xcode, edit your target build settings and under 'Code signing entity' and select your Developer certificate. Then build your app using 'Build and Archive'. Finally, open Organizer (via Window menu) to verify and submit your app. At least, that's the theory. I agree that it's tricky. The Apple Developer Forums have several instructive postings about the potential pitfalls. Good luck!
