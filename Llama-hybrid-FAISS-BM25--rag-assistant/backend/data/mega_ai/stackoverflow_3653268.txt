Since it's a LaunchAgent, it runs as part of your login session, and hence will be killed if you log out. On the other hand, if it's dying before you log out, and you can't find/fix whatever is causing it to crash/exit, or you can tell launchd to automatically restart it by adding KeepAlive to its .plist
