An example of deduplication using open source tools and vector embeddings is demonstrated in the blog below. The approach uses a high dimensional vector space with semantic understanding of the context of a field to help in disambiguation, and is capable of linking records across languages, or with very complex acronyms and misspellings. https://terminusdb.com/blog/entity-resolution-with-ai-dblp-and-acm-publication-benchmark/
