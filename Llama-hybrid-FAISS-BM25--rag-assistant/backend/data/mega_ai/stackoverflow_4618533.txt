Glassfish may be more firepower than you need, but I wouldn't give up on leveraging an existing library. Some options for sockets: SimpleJavaServer (GPL) QuickServer (LGPL) Netty (Apache). If you decide on going more towards web services than sockets, Jetty is the way to go for small footprint HTTP.
