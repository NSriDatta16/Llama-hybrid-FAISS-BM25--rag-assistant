Have you tried: Click Window > Preferences . A Preferences window appears. In the Preferences window, click Workbench > Editors . Select one of the following options: To turn off real-time syntax validation, click Structured Text Editor and uncheck the Analyze annotations while typing box. To control other annotation settings, click Annotations and select your annotation preferences. Click OK to save your preferences and close the page. From this Ganymede help page . Another method: Window > Preferences > Validation . Then uncheck the Manual and Build boxes next to HTML Syntax Validator and JSP Syntax Validator .
