Just in case anyone wants to hide the keyboard when you touch outside of the textview, it's pretty easy... - (void)touchesBegan:(NSSet *)touches withEvent:(UIEvent *)event { [super touchesBegan:touches withEvent:event]; [self.xFront resignFirstResponder]; } xFront is my outlet to my UITextView .
