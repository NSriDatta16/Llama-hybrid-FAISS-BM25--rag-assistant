var_filter validate or sanitize your var according to a list of predefined filters: FILTER_VALIDATE_EMAIL, FILTER_SANITIZE_EMAIL, FILTER_VALIDATE_URL, FILTER_SANITIZE_URL. trim and stripslashes are old school way to sanitize your vars mysql_escape_string sanitize your querys for mysql, therefore this function are deprecated, you must use mysql_real_escape_string, the syntax is identical. var_filter are most elegant and have many options from trim and stripslashes http://www.php.net/manual/en/filter.filters.php
