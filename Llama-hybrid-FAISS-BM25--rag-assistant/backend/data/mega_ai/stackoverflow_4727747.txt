I could be wrong (someone please correct me if I am), but I don't believe strings.xml is editable at runtime. Rather, it's a set of predefined resources that your app has access to, and it cannot be modified by the program--only read. If you're looking to make this information available to your app for subsequent uses, you should look at using SharedPreferences: http://developer.android.com/guide/topics/data/data-storage.html#pref
