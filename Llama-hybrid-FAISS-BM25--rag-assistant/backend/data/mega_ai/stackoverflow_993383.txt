Sugarsync is free with 2gb of storage. It's not got a source control system (but branching, labelling doesn't matter for solo projects of your own does it?). However your get a revision history for all your files and comes with a nice free tool that syncs your filesystem. Update: I use Bitbucket which has unlimited repositories, which I'd recommend
