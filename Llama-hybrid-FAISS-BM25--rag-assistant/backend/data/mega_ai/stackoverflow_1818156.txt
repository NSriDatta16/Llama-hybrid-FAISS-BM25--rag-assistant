I rewrote your query as: SELECT x.day_of_week, AVG(x.count) 'average_records_created' FROM (SELECT DAYOFWEEK(t.datetime_entry) 'day_of_week', COUNT(*) 'count' FROM VMS_LOGS t GROUP BY DAYOFWEEK(t.datetime_entry)) x GROUP BY x.day_of_week
