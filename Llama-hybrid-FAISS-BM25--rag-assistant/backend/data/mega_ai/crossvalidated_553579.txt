I believe your model selection could be the issue. You should look into VAR because the relationship between your predictor series and response series is potentially bidirectional. "VAR can be used for most multiple time series analysis, and is preferred when causal relationships may be bi-directional. It captures linear interdependencies among multiple time series simultaneously, with one equation computed per variable" Understanding and using time series analyses in addiction research (Beard et al., 2019).
