Innovation in an existing organisation will nearly always challenge the status quo. Unless you're incredibly lucky you'll need a senior person to get you through the political mine field that causes. Assuming you have that, your champion, a good approach to innovation is a reasonable incentive, how about Ipod/Cash Prize, to enter write up a single idea on 1 side of paper, you can enter as many times as you want. Also ask all your new hires for ideas, they're invaluable because they don't know what can't be done, they haven't been coloured by your culture.
