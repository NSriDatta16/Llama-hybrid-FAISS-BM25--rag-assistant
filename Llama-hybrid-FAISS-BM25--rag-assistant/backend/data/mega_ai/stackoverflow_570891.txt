Also, just before the call to sel.setVisible(true); pls invoke sel.pack(); Pls note that instead of setSize it is better to use setPreferredSize , which is leveraged during frame packing. Not directly relevant to your question, but still -- the use of a good layout manager is a huge time and frustration saviour when using Swing. MigLayout is simply an excellent one-stop layout manager.
