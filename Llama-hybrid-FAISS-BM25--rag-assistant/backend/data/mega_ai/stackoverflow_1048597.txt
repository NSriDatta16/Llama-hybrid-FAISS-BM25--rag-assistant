There are a number of solutions for determining mobile device, based on the UserAgent making the request. (Some solutions use other factors as well.) Try looking at: WURFL MDBF DeviceAtlas DetectRight Volantis MobileAware Movila UAProf The technology you are using to create your pages will also affect which of the above is the best solution for you.
