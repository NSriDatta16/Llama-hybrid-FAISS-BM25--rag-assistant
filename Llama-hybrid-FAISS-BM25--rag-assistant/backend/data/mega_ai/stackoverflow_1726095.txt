Generally, is an indication of poor semantic HTML. The most common case is using to declare paragraph separations, which there are much better ways to do it semantically. See Bed and BReakfast . There are occasions where it is the proper tag to use, but it is abused often enough that people adopt a "do not use" mentality as to force better semantic thinking.
