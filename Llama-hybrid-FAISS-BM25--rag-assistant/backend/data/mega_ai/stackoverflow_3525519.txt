You want to Enable Visual Styles by adding a manifest dependency to the common control 6 assembly to your applications manifest. If you use DevStudio it should be as simple as adding the #pragma directive from the linked page: #pragma comment(linker,"\"/manifestdependency:type='win32' name='Microsoft.Windows.Common-Controls' version='6.0.0.0' processorArchitecture='*' publicKeyToken='6595b64144ccf1df' language='*'\"")
