Take a look at theses functions for converting coordinates between views: - (CGPoint)convertPoint:(CGPoint)point fromView:(UIView *)view - (CGPoint)convertPoint:(CGPoint)point toView:(UIView *)view You could use -(BOOL)pointInside:(CGPoint)point withEvent:(UIEvent *)event on all your subviews (except the one you're dragging) to find out if there was a collision.
