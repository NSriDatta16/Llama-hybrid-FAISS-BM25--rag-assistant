I don't know if you have payed any attention to trends in hardware lately (last 5 years) but we are heading to a multicore world. A general wake-up call was this "The free lunch is over" article. On a dual core PC, a single-threaded app will only get half the CPU cycles. And CPUs are not getting faster anymore, that part of Moores law has died.
