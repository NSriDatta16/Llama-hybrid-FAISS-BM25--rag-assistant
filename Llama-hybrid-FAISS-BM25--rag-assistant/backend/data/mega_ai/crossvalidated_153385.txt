According to the article "High Level Versus Low Level Data Science" bucketization is The bucketization step (sometimes called multivariate binning) consists of identifying metrics (and combinations of 2-3 metrics) with high predictive power, combine and bin them appropriately, to reduce intra-bucket variance while keeping the buckets big enough. So my understanding is that you greedily bin the data according to the most predictive features, then analyze the subgroups.
