To execute actions when your application starts or stops you should use a ServletContextListener : http://download.oracle.com/javaee/6/api/javax/servlet/ServletContextListener.html In web.xml: ... com.example.Listener ... In contrast to Peter Knego's proposal this solution is portable to any servlet container and not confined to Tomcat.
