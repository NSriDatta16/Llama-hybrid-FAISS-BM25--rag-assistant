If you need a Sorted ConcurrentMap look at ConcurrentSkipListMap . Considering its complexity it is both non blocking and fast. To be more specific: This class implements a concurrent variant of SkipLists providing expected average log(n) time cost for the containsKey, get, put and remove operations and their variants.
