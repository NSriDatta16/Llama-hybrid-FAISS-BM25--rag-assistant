cc2: will be a prefix that is defined at the top of your .aspx file, within an statement. cc1 is the default prefix that is used when you drag an assembly onto the design surface. cc2 would be the next default prefix. You must have a .dll in your project that defines a class named WebToolkitGridView . Perhaps this link will explain the Register directive , and help you track it down.
