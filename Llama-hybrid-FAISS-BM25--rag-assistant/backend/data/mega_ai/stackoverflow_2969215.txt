Coldfusion requieres a server component to interpret it. That said, there are many ways to have it run, based on what your host provides... Such as the Adobe Coldfusion Server Product Line, BlueDragon, Railo. There are open source solutions, and paid solutions. Or depending on the complexity of the colfusion code, you could convert it to php or whatever language you are more familiar with.
