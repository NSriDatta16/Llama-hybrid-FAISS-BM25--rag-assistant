You just need to join all your tables: SELECT companies.id, companies.name FROM companies, tags, companies_vs_tags, paragraphs_vs_tags WHERE companies.id = companies_vs_tags.company_id AND tags.id = companies_vs_tags.tag_id AND tags.id = paragraphs_vs_tags.tag_id AND paragraphs.id = paragraphs_vs_tags.paragraph_id AND paragraphs.name = "Microsoft";
