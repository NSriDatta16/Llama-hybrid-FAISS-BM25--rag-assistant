As soon as you use the hashbang in a URL, Google will spot that you're following their protocol, and interpret your URLs in a special way - they'll take everything after the hashbang, and pass it to the site as a URL parameter instead. The name they use for the parameter is: _escaped_fragment_ reference: http://www.seomoz.org/blog/how-to-allow-google-to-crawl-ajax-content
