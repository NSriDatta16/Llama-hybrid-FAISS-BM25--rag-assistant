Learning either Java or C# will give you a grounding in the basic skills for a programming job. Once you know one of these languages, learnng the other one will be fairly simple, as there are a lot of similarities. If you want to know which is "best", then look at the job market and decide where you think the better opportunities or most interesting jobs lie.
