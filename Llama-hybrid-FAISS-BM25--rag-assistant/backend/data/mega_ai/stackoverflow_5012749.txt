No, there's no cleaner way to do what you've shown. I'm not really sure why you feel that variables are a "hack" approach. Setting a flag is a common technique used when writing UI code. The real hack would be some obscure way to prevent raising the event the first time, but not subsequent times. Future maintenance programmers are guaranteed to understand how setting a flag works; they're not guaranteed to appreciate the "elegance" of your alternative approach.
