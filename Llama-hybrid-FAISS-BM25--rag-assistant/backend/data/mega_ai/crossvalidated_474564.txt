Yes, this is the idea that the original paper promoted. Note, however, that it is a little bit tricky to use the term alignment. For purposes of the statistical machine translation, it was defined as a meaning correspondence of the source and target words. A highly cited 2017 study shows that the attention might learn very unintuitive alignments which seem totally wrong without any loss of translation quality.
