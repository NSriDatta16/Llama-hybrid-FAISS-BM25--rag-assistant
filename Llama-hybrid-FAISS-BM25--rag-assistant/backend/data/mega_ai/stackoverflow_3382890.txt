Links itself cannot have tooltips. Viewers always show the URL. But TCPDF can add "Annotations", which is the closest you can get to tooltips (but have to be assigned to a rectangle area instead of to text/paragraphs). http://www.tecnick.com/pagefiles/tcpdf/doc/com-tecnick-tcpdf/TCPDF.html#methodAnnotation You'll have to refer to the PDF specification. Section 8.4 and 8.4.5, and you are looking for "Popup". http://www.adobe.com/devnet/pdf/pdfs/PDFReference16.pdf
