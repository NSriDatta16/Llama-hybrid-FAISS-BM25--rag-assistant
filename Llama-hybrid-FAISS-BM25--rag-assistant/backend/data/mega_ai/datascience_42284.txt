After a lucky google search, I found the function na.interpolation from the imputeTS -package. After changing the dataset to a time series ( XTS ), I was able to impute the right data. library(xts) library(imputeTS) set.seed(1337) df % data.table() df[sample(seq_len(nrow(df)), size = nrow(df) * 0.3), x := NA] df$y x #> 1970-01-02 100 #> 1970-01-03 96 #> 1970-01-04 NA #> 1970-01-05 88 #> 1970-01-06 NA #> 1970-01-07 80 full
