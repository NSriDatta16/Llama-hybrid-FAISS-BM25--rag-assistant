Check the rules around the wifi prohibition. See if you can use an Airport Express wired to your development Mac inside a sealed Faraday cage room with no external network access. Your organization may already have access to such rooms. Or see if you can clone your QA servers (with non-confidential test data) at an outside test facility or test site.
