"Series" is: a group or a number of related or similar things ( http://dictionary.reference.com/browse/series ) a number of things or events that are arranged or happen one after the other ( http://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/series ) A number of objects or events arranged or coming one after the other in succession ( http://www.thefreedictionary.com/series ) A number of events, objects, or people of a similar or related kind coming one after another ( http://www.oxforddictionaries.com/definition/english/series ) Time series is a sequence of values "coming one after another". Series does not have to be a sum like in mathematics .
