What level of math is required for machine learning research

There are several levels of math understanding: Know the math Know the intuitions behind math concepts Know the intuitions and proofs of math concepts Know the intuitions, proofs of math concepts and be able to apply them to deduce new results My question is what level of understanding is required for machine learning research? Especially for publishing papers in conferences like CVPR or NeurIPS.
