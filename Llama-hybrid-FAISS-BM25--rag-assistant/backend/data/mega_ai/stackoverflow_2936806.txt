I solved this by implementing touchesBegan, touchesMoved and touchesEnded in QuartzView.m. I also added this in QuartzViewController.m: scrollView.maximumZoomScale = 1.0; scrollView.minimumZoomScale = 1.0; scrollView.scrollEnabled = NO; scrollView.pagingEnabled = NO; Works like I wanted it to :)
