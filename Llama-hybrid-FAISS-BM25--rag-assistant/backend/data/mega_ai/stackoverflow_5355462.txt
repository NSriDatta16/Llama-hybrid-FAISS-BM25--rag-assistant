I use something like this in a couple of initd scripts I've written. Replace the COMMAND with whatever you need PIDFILE=/var/run/service.pid COMMAND="java -jar start.jar" $COMMAND > /dev/null 2>&1 & echo $! > $PIDFILE Edited with @dogane 's suggestion, tested as well.
