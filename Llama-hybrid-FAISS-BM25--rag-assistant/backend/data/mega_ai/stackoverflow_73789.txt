One way would be to periodically recycle your storage media, i.e. read data off the decaying medium and write it to a fresh one. There exist programs to assist you with this, e.g. dvdisaster . In the end, nothing lasts forever. Just pick the least annoying solution. As for #2: you can store data in encrypted form to prevent data recovery experts from making sense of it.
