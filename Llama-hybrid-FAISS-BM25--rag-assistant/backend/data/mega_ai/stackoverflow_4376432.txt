For each point, calculate the distance D(n) from the origin, this is an O(n) operation. Use a O(n^2) algorithm to find matches where D(a-b) skipping D(a)-D(b) > delta. The result, on average, must be better than O(n^2) due to the (hopefully large) number skipped.
