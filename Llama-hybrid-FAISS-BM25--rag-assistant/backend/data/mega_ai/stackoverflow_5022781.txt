To force scan of entire SDCARD use sendBroadcast(new Intent(Intent.ACTION_MEDIA_MOUNTED, Uri.parse("file://" + Environment.getExternalStorageDirectory()))); or to only scan a particular folder use sendBroadcast(new Intent(Intent.ACTION_MEDIA_MOUNTED, Uri.parse("file://FolderName" + Environment.getExternalStorageDirectory())));
