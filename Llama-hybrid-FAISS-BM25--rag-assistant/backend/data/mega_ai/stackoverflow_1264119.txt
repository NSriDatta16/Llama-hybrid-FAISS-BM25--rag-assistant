Two of the most requested features for Adobe AIR have been the ability to launch native executables from an AIR application, and the ability to integrate native libraries into an AIR application. Unfortunately, neither feature will be included in Adobe AIR 1.0. But there is a workaround . It's a bit complicated , yet elegant, and should solve your problem. It relies on CommandProxy , a kit for proxying calls between applications and the operating system. You will find here a detailed article on how to implement this solution
