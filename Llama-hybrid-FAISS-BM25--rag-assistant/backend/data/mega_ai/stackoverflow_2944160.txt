For the case where the mouse is leaving your work area, try: MouseEvent.MOUSE_OUT You may be able to detect MOUSE_UP in the case where the mouse is dragged back in. The behavior of the mouse outside of your flash app is entirely operating system and browser dependent, and any access you have to it within flash will be inconsistent at best.
