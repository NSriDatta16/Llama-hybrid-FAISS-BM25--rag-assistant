Trick question: WordPress, a very simplistic blogging platform, should never be stretched into something as intricate as an e-commerce solution. An e-commerce platform offering a simple blogging module is another matter entirely. Since you don't know much about WordPress anyway, you may want to consider using a e-commerce platform like magentocommerce.com , zen-cart.com , or oscommerce.com . All of them are PHP/MySQL based, like WordPress.
