Try not to use ereg_replace as it is going to be discontinued. ereg_replace This function has been DEPRECATED as of PHP 5.3.0 and REMOVED as of PHP 6.0.0. Relying on this feature is highly discouraged. strip_tags That said do you want to change all those chars to empty or are you trying to strip the tags? You can also convert the chars to the html_entities . $desc = strip_tags($desc);
