All of the model data is available through this methods myObjectContext.MetadataWorkspace.GetEntityContainer(myObjectContext.DefaultContainerName, DataSpace.CSSpace); that should at least give you a start on how to do what you want. DataSpace.CSSpace specifies the mapping between the Conceptual names and the Store names. DataSpace.CSpace gives you the Conceptual Model, and DataSpace.SSpace gives you the storage model.
