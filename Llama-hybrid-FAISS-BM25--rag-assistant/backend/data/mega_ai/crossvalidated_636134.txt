As said in comments, arbitrary cutoffs are to be avoided. You seem to have a binary outcome variable sober/not , so logistic regression, and one ordinal predictor spiritual acceptance . As it does not seem obvious that this predictor acts linearly, you could represent it via a regression spline. Search this site for (many) examples. Also see advice in comments!
