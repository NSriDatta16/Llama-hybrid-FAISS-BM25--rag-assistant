I think this is not amenable to statistical analysis or prediction. For time series prediction you need an underlying stable generating mechanism. Profits, the decision to pay a dividend and the amount are affected by a host of factors hardly stable. Think only of the recent pandemics. No method that neglects those factors has much chance in my view.
