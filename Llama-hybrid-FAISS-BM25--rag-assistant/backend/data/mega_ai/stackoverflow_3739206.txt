And, of course, "fragmentation" is suspect: The file may be in pieces in the same cylinder. No seek overhead, just rotational latency. Or not as the pieces may be an optimal order (chances are near zero for this one). The file may be "contiguous" but across several cylinders. Even reading sequentially will result in seeks. The file may be on a stripe set and you have no idea where the boundaries are. You may skip to another controller, another spindle, or another partition on the same drive. Be careful about what conclusions you draw.
