If you’re producing HTML5, and you don’t mind working around Internet Explorer’s lack of support for the new elements in HTML5, then you should wrap your navigation in a element. (Just like you should wrap your paragraphs in a element — that’s what markup is, describing content by wrapping it with tags.) As for wrapping your navigation list in a , you don’t have to, but it’s often useful for styling purposes.
