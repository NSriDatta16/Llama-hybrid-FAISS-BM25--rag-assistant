This was the recommended text on the course I took: P.B.Kline, Principles and Practice of Structural Equation Modeling , The Guilford Press. It is an introductory text, and not heavily mathematical. For a more mathematical, Bayesian, treatment, you could try: S-Y. Lee, Structural Equation Modeling: A Bayesian Approach , Wiley.
