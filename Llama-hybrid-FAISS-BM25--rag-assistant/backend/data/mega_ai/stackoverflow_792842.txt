Some appointments: Save your personalized schemas and objectclasses definitions on your new server. You can look for your included files at slapd.conf to obtain it, for example (this is a part of my slapd.conf): include /etc/ldap/schema/core.schema Include your personalized schemas and objectclasses in your new openLDAP installation. Use slapcat command to export your full LDAP tree to a single/various ldif files. Use ldapadd to import the ldif files on to your new LDAP installation.
