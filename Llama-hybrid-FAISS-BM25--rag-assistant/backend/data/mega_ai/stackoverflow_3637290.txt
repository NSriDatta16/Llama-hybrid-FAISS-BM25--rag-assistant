That would seem difficult, unless the map were stationary. If the map won't be panning/zooming, you could do the "falling" via a TranslateAnimation , applied to an ImageView that floats above the map (e.g., later child in the RelativeLayout that holds the MapView ). When the animation completes, you'd make the ImageView be View.GONE , and you'd add the actual marker to the ItemizedOverlay .
