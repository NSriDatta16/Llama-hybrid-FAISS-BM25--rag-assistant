ChatGPT, being a generative model, generates sequences of tokens. There are no labels in the sense of a classification problem. Therefore, re-labeling using the reward signal does not make sense in the context of ChatGPT. On the other hand, RLHF is used to lean the model toward generating desirable sequences.
