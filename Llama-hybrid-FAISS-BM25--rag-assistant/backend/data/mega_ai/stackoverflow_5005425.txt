There's a control in the Silverlight Toolkit that addresses this problem called the LayoutTransformer . It ensures that when you change the transform, the bounds of the control are within the LayoutTransformer bounds. You can see this at work in the Silverlight Toolkit demo . Just scroll down the tree on the left to the Layout section.
