There are two main schools of thought is Statistics: frequentist and Bayesian . Bayes theorem is to do with the latter and can be seen as a way of understanding how the probability that a theory is true is affected by a new piece of evidence. This is known as conditional probability. You might want to look at this to get a handle on the math.
