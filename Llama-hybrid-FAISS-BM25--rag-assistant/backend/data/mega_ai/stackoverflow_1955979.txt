I think you cannot center-align images. You can center-align paragraphs. Perhaps something like this will help you: For Each oILShp In ActiveDocument.InlineShapes oILShp.Select Selection.ParagraphFormat.Alignment = wdAlignParagraphCenter Next For deletion, just call Delete for each Shape object satisfying your conditions.
