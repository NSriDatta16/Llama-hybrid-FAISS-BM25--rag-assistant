It can be done by creating two ListBoxes, as you described, one bound to an ObservableCollection and one bound to an ObservableCollection . EntityA contains an ObservableCollection as a property. The ListBox items of EntityA are templated to display the child collection of EntityB's as a ListBox. The ListBoxDragDropTarget is specified in this ItemTemplate, rather than the parent.
