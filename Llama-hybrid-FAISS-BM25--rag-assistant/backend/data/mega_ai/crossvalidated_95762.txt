One possibility you might consider is a form of regression model suitable for ordered categorical response (IV), with cosine similarity as a predictor. There are many such forms of potentially suitable model for that case Some extend logistic regression - such as ordered logistic regression (ordered logit, proportional odds) Another suitable model is the stereotype model which is related to multinomial logistic regression (which itself is suitable for nominal data but is also at least sometimes used with ordered data) There's a little more discussion (and links) here
