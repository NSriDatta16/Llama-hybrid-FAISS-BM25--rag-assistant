You can simulate the effect for your users by timing several runs, then having a progress bar advance at the average records / second rate. The only other ways will be 1 - Refer to the API of your database engine to see if it makes any provision for that or 2 - Break your INSERT into many smaller statements, and report on them as you go. But that will have a significant negative performance impact.
