I would recommend Wolfgang Ehrhardt's CRC/Hash. http://home.netsurf.de/wolfgang.ehrhardt/ It's fast and "can be compiled with most current Pascal (TP 5/5.5/6, BP 7, VP 2.1, FPC 1.0/2.0/2.2) and Delphi versions (tested with V1 up to V7/9/10)". I've used it with D11/D12 too.
