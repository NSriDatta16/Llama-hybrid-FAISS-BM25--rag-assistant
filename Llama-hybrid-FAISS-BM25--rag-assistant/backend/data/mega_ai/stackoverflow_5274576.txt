Well, it turns out not entirely the wrong tree :-) For reference, the !today function has a few other methods, and you can use them to gather individual sections of the date as necessary: !today (dd) - gives the day of the month, in numeric form !today (MM) - gives the month of the year, in numeric form !today (yyyy) - gives the year, in numeric form There are a few others, but all I ended up using were these. Combine them as necessary, and Robert is your mother's brother, as it were...
