Average day difference is a SUM of differences divided by COUNT(*) SUM of differences is in fact difference between MIN and MAX : SELECT id, ref, DATEDIFF(day, MIN(date), MAX(date)) / NULLIF(COUNT(*) - 1, 0) FROM mytable GROUP BY id, ref
