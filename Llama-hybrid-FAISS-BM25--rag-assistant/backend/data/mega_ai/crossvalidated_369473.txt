Prediction counts as a kind of statistical inference, and it's not restricted to machine learning. But when you're looking at mere associations rather than predictions, and you're interested in the statistical significance of these associations, there's indeed no reason to split the dataset into training, testing, and validation sets. (What would you even be testing or validating?)
