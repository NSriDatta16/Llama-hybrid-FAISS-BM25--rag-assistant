GHC implements what you want : Extensions to the deriving mecanism . These modifications are often shown for future standard language extension (As seen on haskell' wiki ) To Enable this extension, you must use the following pragma {-# GeneralizedNewtypeDeriving #-} and then use a deriving on your newtype declaration, as usual data SomeType a = SomeCons a deriving (Num)
