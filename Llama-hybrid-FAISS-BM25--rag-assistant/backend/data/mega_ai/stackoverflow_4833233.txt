Replace all newlines that are not followed by or preceded by a newline: text = "This should all be on one line since it's one sentence.\n\nThis is a new paragraph that should be separate. " Or, for Ruby 1.8 without lookarounds: txt.gsub! /([^\n])\n([^\n])/, '\1 \2'
