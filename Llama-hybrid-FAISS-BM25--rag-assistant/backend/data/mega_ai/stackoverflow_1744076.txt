Hi I also searched for it: dem_req_rate = num_call * curr_rate where curr_rate is the rate passed to httperf depending on the iteration number and the setting of rate_step , low_rate and high_rate params ( man autobench for more on those params) net_io = the average network throughput in kilobytes per second KB/s is the Net I/O from httperf from the Miscellaneous Section of its output and man page (not linked) errors is the Total errors from the Errors Section of httperf results and its man page (not linked)
