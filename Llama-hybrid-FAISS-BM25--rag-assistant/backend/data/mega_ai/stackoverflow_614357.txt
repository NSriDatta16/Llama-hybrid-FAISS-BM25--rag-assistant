Not easy to do have a look at OperatingSystemMXBean method getSystemLoadAverage() only available since java 6 you could keep a track of it and if it is the same for a while determine the pc is idle. This assumes idle in terms of idle CPU OperatingSystemMXBean operatingSystemMxBean = ManagementFactory.getOperatingSystemMXBean();
