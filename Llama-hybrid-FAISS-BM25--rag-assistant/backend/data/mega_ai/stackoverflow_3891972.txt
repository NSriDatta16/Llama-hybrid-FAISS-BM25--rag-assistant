The weak point in the session handling is the recognition by the server of a designated session, because it must be part of the informations, the client is giving to the server. Therefore, I would encourage some crypting of the key, and a time out of its validity. It is up to you to store the key then in the cookie, in the url, or wherever might suits your needs.
