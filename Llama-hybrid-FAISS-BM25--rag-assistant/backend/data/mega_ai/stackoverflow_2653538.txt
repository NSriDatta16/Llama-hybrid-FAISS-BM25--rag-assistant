Taking a clue from Leo's comment to Bozhidar: (defun my-isearch-buffers () "isearch multiple buffers." (interactive) (multi-isearch-buffers (delq nil (mapcar (lambda (buf) (set-buffer buf) (and (not (equal major-mode 'dired-mode)) (not (string-match "^[ *]" (buffer-name buf))) buf)) (buffer-list))))) You might have to tweak the conditions inside the and to filter whatever other kinds of buffers you want to ignore.
