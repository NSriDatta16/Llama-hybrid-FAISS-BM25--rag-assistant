A nicer way than just hacking into session storage mechanisms on both sides would be setting up OpenId provider and plugging OpenId consumers to both asp.net and php applications. There's lot of existing code to do it. It would be both more elegant and error prone than the low level solutions. As a bonus you could use integrated OpenId login in the rest of your company applications and become a company hero. See: Using OpenID for both .NET/Windows and PHP/Linux/Apache web sites
