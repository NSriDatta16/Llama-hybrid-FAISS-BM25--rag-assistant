An inelegant, but functional solution is to merge the static text fields together, and avoid having to synchronize Print When Expression and Text Field Expression in favour of just the latter. ($V{LAST_WEEK_TALLY_0}.add( $V{LAST_WEEK_TALLY_1} ).add( $V{LAST_WEEK_TALLY_2} ).add( $V{LAST_WEEK_TALLY_3} ).longValue() == $V{THIS_WEEK_TALLY_0}.add( $V{THIS_WEEK_TALLY_1} ).add( $V{THIS_WEEK_TALLY_2} ).add( $V{THIS_WEEK_TALLY_3} ).longValue()) ? "-" : ($V{LAST_WEEK_TALLY_0}.add( $V{LAST_WEEK_TALLY_1} ).add( $V{LAST_WEEK_TALLY_2} ).add( $V{LAST_WEEK_TALLY_3} ).longValue()
