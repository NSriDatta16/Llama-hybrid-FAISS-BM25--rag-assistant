BindingSource and PropertyDescriptor are more elegant techniques for performing manual data-binding, which is more-or-less what you're doing with the ListView when it's in VirtualMode . Although it generally uses reflection internally anyway, you can rely on it to work efficiently and seamlessly. I wrote a blog article recently which explains in detail how to use these mechanisms (although it's in a different context, the principles are the same) - http://www.brad-smith.info/blog/archives/104
