That depends on how you have configured storage-conf.xml on your two nodes. Hint. take a look at 7000 in storage-conf.xml. (TCP port 7000 is the standard/default port used by Cassandra for internal communication, i.e. address to bind to and tell other nodes to connect to). UDP port (7001 default) was previous used for gossip, was removed in 0.6.0.
