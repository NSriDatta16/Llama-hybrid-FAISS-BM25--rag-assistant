You can frame your problem as reinforcement learning (RL). Reinforcement learning (RL) is sequential decision-making under uncertainty. "The value of the loss" could be framed as maximizing rewards. The output is the sequence that maximizes reward for n-steps ahead. The "triangular' array of arrays" is the history. Reinforcement learning (RL) has the ability to make predicts with current best set of parameters, frequently called "exploitation"
