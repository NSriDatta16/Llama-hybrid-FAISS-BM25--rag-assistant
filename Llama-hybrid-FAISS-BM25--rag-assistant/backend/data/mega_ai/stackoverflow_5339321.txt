Assuming you've done a successful Product > Archive then, from Organizer (Shift Apple 2) click Archives. Select your Archive. Select Share. In the "Select the content and options for sharing:" pane set Contents to "iOS App Store Package (.ipa) and Identity to iPhone Distribution. Click Next, enter an App name and click Save. Full gory details with screenshots are here: Xcode4UserGuide
