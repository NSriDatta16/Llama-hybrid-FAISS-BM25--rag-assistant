The plain answer is no . Depending on the target complexity and the adequation of the proposal to the target, the number of required simulations varies from one to infinity. Some convergence assessment tools have been devised over the years, primarily in the early days of MCMC, but they are partial and far from fool-proof. We included a chapter on some of these in our book .
