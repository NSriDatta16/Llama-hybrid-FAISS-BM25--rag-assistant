There's no way to do this using just the drag source APIs. If you look at Interface Builder it does something similar to what you want. When you drag a button out of the library the button animates in the middle of the drag. According to most people the way Apple is doing this is by making the drag image a small transparent image. Then they create a Window and have it follow the mouse. You can then use the window to do any type of animation you want.
