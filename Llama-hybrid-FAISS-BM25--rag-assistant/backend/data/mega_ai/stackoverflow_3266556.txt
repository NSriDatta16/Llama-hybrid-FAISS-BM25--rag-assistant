ArrayObject implements ArrayAccess so you can simply access them with the regular Array Notation, e.g. echo $arrayObject['key1']; In addition, ArrayObjects are IteratorAggregates , so you can iterate over them with foreach and decorate them with any of the SPL iterators , including the RecursiveIteratorIterator
