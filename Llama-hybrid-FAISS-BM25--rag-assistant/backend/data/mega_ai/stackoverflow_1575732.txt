sys.dm_db_index_physical_stats (size and fragmentation) sys.dm_db_index_usage_stats (usage, number of scans/seeks/updates etc) sys.dm_db_index_operational_stats (current activity on index) Remember 'table' means the clustered index or the 'heap'.
