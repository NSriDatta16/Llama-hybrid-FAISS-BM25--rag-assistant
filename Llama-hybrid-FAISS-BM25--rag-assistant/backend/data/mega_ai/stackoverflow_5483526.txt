Other web applications that do this, that are not already mentioned: draw.io , is free and uses Google Drive or Dropbox for storage (including Google Drive Realtime). I co-founded this. Lucidchart is native JavaScript, like draw.io. Creately and Cacoo are Flash implementations, although Cacoo are moving away from Flash. Aside from my obvious bias, I would suggest Lucidchart or Gliffy (mentioned above).
