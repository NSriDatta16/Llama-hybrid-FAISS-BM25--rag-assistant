My last approach is: Convert all "strange letters" to "normal letters" -> à to a, ñ to n, etc. Convert all non-word characters to _ (i.e not a-zA-Z0-9) replace groups of underscores with a single underscore remove all tailing and leading underscores As for storage, I believe the friendly URL should go to the database, and be immutable, after all cool URIs don't change
