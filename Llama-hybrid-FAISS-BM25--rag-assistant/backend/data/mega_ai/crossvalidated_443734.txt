There is a substantial treatment of the problem in Learning nondeterministic classifiers by del Coz, Díez, and Bahamonde, in Journal of Machine Learning Research 10 (2009) 2273-2293. Their analysis compares three strategies: "a multiclass Support Vector Machine (SVM), a Logistic Regression, and a Naïve Bayes."
