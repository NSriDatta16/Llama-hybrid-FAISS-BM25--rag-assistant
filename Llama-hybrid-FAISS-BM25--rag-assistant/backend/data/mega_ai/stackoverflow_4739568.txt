Try socketpair() instead of pipe() / mknod(S_IFIFO) . A socket pair can be made in domain AF_UNIX with type SOCK_STREAM (stream-based, your usage will need framing/reassembly), SOCK_DGRAM (datagram-based, will not be fragmented or merged), or SOCK_SEQPACKET (record-based, may be fragmented but not merged).
