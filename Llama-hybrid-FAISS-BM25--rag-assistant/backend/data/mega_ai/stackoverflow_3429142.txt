This appears to be the de facto behavior in browsers (At least I couldn't find any written standard about anchors and scrolling). The browser tries its best to scroll all windows until the desired fragment is visible. (You'll notice this even when you click on the "got to top" link and also if you add "padding-bottom: 3000px;" to the div in your example.) Consider using jQuery's scrollTo plugin which actually manipulates scroll position of the appropriate container for you.
