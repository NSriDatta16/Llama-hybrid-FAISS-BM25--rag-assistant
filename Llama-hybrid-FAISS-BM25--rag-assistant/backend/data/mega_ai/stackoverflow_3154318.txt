It sounds like you have different header include directories listed for the two build directories. In XCode, right click on your project, and choose Get Info. Under the Build tab, type in 'paths' in the search box to filter the properties below. Pay attention to the Header search paths. Now, switch your 'build configuration' between debug and release - are they the same? Add any to release that aren't in debug.
