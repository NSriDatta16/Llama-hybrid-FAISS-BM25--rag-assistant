Make sure that there is plenty of spare room in UI controls. Text has a tendency to become a lot longer when translated from English to something like French or German. Although it is focused somewhat on the Windows side of i18n things, pay attention to Michael Kaplan's blog . He is very well versed in this field, and has posted many blogs posts containing general stuff that's useful.
