You can't. Multiple people can visit your site from the same IP (makes ip storage useless) Multiple people can visit your site from the same PC Multiple people can visit your site from the same browser (makes cookies useless) The closest you can get is to store the visitors IP in combination with a cookie to not count those in the future. Here is a tradeoff, if they clear the cookie, they are a new visitor. If you only store the IP, you count whole proxies as one visitor. Another option is to use user accounts and track exactly which user viewed what page, but this is not really a good option for public sites.
