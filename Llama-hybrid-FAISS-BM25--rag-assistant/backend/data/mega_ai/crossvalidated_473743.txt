Yes, absolutely. In fact, this is the case for which the Box-Pierce test, and its later refinement, the Ljung-Box test were designed. The application of the test to residuals - which, from a subject matter point of view, is often the more interesting hypothesis to test as there are not many time series which might conceivably be white noise - in turn is fraught with certain difficulties, which are described for example here .
