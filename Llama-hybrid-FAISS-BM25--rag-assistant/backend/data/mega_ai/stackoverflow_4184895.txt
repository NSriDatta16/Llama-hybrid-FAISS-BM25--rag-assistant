Use curl_setopt($ch, CURLOPT_USERAGENT, 'Mozilla/5.0 (Windows; U; Windows NT 6.1; en-US; rv:1.9.2.12) Gecko/20101026 Firefox/3.6.12'); You can replace the useragent with another one of course. However, "Bad Request" is most likely NOT related to a missing/bad useragent. It sounds like the webserver itself doesn't like your request.. not the application behind the requested URI.
