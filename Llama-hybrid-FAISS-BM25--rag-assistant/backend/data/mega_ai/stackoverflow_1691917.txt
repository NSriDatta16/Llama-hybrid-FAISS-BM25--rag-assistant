Finding specific numbers is difficult and I would strongly encourage you to test out the locks in your scenario, because the perf will depend on access ratios, contention patterns and the hardware it is being run on. I also encourage you to include the spin locks in .NET 4.0 in your comparison like System.Threading.SpinLock and System.Threading.SemaphoreSlim. That being said Joe Duffy has several posts on his blog which compare perf of particular locks, for example this one .
