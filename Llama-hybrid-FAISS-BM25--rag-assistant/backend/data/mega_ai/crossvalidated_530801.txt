To find the distribution of the distance between the origin, $(0,0)$ and a random point on the unit square $(0,1)^2$ we can integrate over the area that is within $d$ of the origin. That is, find the cdf $P(\sqrt{X^2 + Y^2} \leq d)$ , then take the derivative to find the pdf. Extensions to the square $(0,N)$ are immediate.
