No its not wrong to do at all. In fact, it would be considered leveraging code to achieve a nice finished product. A the recent jQuery Conference in Boston, we got to see a demo by the Filament Group (the people who built the theme roller) and they say they use the themed elements for their entire backend sites. Then, they use a bookmarklet when they are meeting with the client to change the entire theme on the fly. Just by changing the theme, the entire website shifts style and colors.
