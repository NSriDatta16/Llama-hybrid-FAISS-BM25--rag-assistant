Opencv is a library mainly for Computer Vision stuff and so there's no built-in function that let you process audio data. There's a similar question with a solution here on stackoverflow: Adding audio to video produced from OpenCV To take advantage of FFMPEG and it's related softwares take a look here: http://opencv.willowgarage.com/wiki/FFMPEG The Access Violation error you are facing using Emgu may be related to some emgu or opencv bugs, the 2.2 version has some known with video saving.
