One approach is to design for the densities and use the Icon Design guidelines for icons, and the dimensions specified in the " Range of Screens " area in the Supporting Multiple Screens guide for your background images. For the background images be sure to take the status bar dimensions into consideration. You can put your resources in the appropriate drawable-mdpi, drawable-hdpi, drawable-ldpi folders and they will be used appropriately.
