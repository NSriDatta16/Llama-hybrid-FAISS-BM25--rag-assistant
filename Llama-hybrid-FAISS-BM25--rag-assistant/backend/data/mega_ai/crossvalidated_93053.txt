Whether you use logistic regression or discriminant analysis you will get some sort of score for the likelihood that Y = 1. So Split your data into training/test (this assumes a relatively large sample) Apply the classification algorithm of your choice on the training data, as long as it gives a score of some sort. Look at $k*\alpha$ on the test data for various scores. Pick the one you like best
