Almost like you'd do it with fixed pipe. Set your bound texture's wrap mode to repeat before setting sampler uniform and texture coordinates outside 0-1 range will repeat the texture. glTexParameteri(GL_TEXTURE_2D, GL_TEXTURE_WRAP_S, GL_REPEAT); glTexParameteri(GL_TEXTURE_2D, GL_TEXTURE_WRAP_T, GL_REPEAT); In your case, texture coordinates will be s = 0-640 and t = 0-120. Fragment shader doesn't need anything special, just normal texture2D(tex, texCoord) will do.
