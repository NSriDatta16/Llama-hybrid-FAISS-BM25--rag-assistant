Instead of Delimiter (a char) and DelimitedText, you can also use LineBreak (a string) and Text: lst := TStringList.Create; try lst.LineBreak := '-$-'; lst.Text := '16.5.2003-$-12:09-$-anna-$-Organization created'; Memo1.Lines := lst; // or whatever you do with it finally lst.Free; end; Ans it works even the other way round.
