There are few alternatives to port an app from one platform to other. like Rhodes Mobile, Titanium and PhoneGap. In fact they did a good job and tried very well to remove fragmentation in smart phone app development. But according to Apple's SDK Agreement version 4.0, section 3.3.1 app must be developed in C/C++/JAVA script. At this stage convincing way is to write it separately.
