You probably want to add the child as supervisor. It is an entry in the childspec of a child of a supervisor. The "type" of the child can be set to "supervisor": http://www.erlang.org/doc/design_principles/sup_princ.html#spec Probably you can do it via the child starting a supervisor itself too but it is at least less elegant and it is less evident what you are doing. HTH, h.
