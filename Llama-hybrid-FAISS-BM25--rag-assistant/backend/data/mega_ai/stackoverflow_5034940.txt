It will ultimately depend on the amount of data in the table as to if you require the index. In many cases, the engine might just do a table scan and omit your index all together if it thinks that is faster. Do you have 100 users, or 100,000 users? On a bit/bool column you are not going to utilize a ton of storage space for the index, so it probably won't hurt unless you have a really high insertion rate.
