IDs must be unique within a page (html spec [1]); you have 2 #menu , #content , etc. Change them to e.g. and your selector to .menu -- that should work. Incidentally, you can simplify your hover callback with find [2]: function () { $(this).find('.menu, .dragable').show(); } 1: http://www.w3.org/TR/html401/struct/global.html#h-7.5.2 2: http://api.jquery.com/find/
