What is being stored at each sample, that can be applied at the end of the batch You store the prediction and error and then calculate the average after each cycle. GD - Update after all rows. It will give a correct direction to the weight update but will be very slow SGD - Update after each row. It will be fast but the direction may swing Batch - Update after a specific count. This approach gives a balanced solution to the two other approaches.
