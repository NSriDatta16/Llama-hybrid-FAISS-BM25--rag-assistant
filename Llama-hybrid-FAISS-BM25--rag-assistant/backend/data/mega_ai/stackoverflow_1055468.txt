i see this link , http://fragged.org/dev/changing-and-fading-body-background-image.php the idea is : apply your background to a div that's assigned a low z-index, absolute positioning and a background (think of it as a reverse / back modal). then produce your content into another layer on top of it with a transparent background.... you can now reference the bottom layer by id and change the opacity. all it needs is a stack / array of background mages to apply as a property to the layer...
