How do I find the number of local minima of a loss function?

Consider the loss function $L(A_1, ω_1, ..., A_d, ω_d)=\displaystyle\sum_{j=1} ^{m}(Y_j-\displaystyle\sum_{k=1} ^{d}A_k\sin(ω_kt_j))^2$ used to fit a time series to a sum of simple oscillations with frequencies $ω_j$ and amplitudes $A_j$ . $L$ has a local minimizer with distinct frequencies. How can I find the number of local minima if I increase $d$ (for example $d=3$ , $d=4$ , $d=5$ , ...)?
