Can you use prototype.js? If so you could use String.gsub, like var myStr = "a day in a life of a thing"; var replace = "a"; var resultString = myStr.gsub(replace, "g"); // resultString will be "g day in g life of g thing" It will also take regular expressions. To me this is one of the more elegant ways to solve it. prototypejs gsub documentation
