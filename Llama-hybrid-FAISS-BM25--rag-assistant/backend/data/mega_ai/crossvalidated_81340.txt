As mentioned, one way to solve this is through multivariable logistic regression. You can fit a model that predicts the probability of outcome (Status) from the other variables: fit There are several packages that can help: glm was already mentioned, and I like rms . Check out the documentation in rms because it has a detailed example for exactly your kind of problem, that you can work through. There are several books on logistic regression, including one by rms 's author.
