For your graph: Write an adjacency matrix $A$ whose coefficients are $1-p_{AB}$, etc. rather than 1. Set $A_{5,5}$ to 1. Then the answer is $A^4 \begin{bmatrix}1 \\ 0 \\ 0 \\ 0 \\ 0\end{bmatrix}$. In general, there is an elegant combinatorics solution.
