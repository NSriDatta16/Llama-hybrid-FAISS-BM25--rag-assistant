To examine the relationship between a continuous and categorical factor, a good start is to use side-by-side box plots, continuous on the left, categorical on the bottom. Are the means different? Use ANOVA to check. To examine the relationship between categorical factors, a good start is to use a mosaic plot, as well as a contingency table. You could group first then make separate plots. To predict occupants, ordinal logistic regression is probably the best way to go. To predict isLand, (binomial) logistic regression should do the trick. To predict distance, OLS regression will work.
