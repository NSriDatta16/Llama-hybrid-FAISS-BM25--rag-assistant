The facilities available are those detailed in the standard for PNG and the approved code modules for the file content which may involve using a container-file principle. The law for PNG also has precedence and content and storage are subject to the laws of England and other countries. PNG can mean postfix notational graphic or posterise normalised graphical or similar terms. In America their law has different words for the term PNG and the files are a different format.
