Here are the equations defining an AR($p$)-ARCH($m$) model for a time series variable $x_t$: $$ \begin{aligned} x_t &= \varphi_0 + \varphi_1 x_{t-1} + \dotsc + \varphi_p x_{t-p} + \varepsilon_t, \\ \varepsilon_t &= \sigma_t z_t, \\ z_t &\sim i.i.N(0,1), \\ \sigma_t^2 & = \alpha_0 + \alpha_1 \varepsilon_{t-1}^2 + \dotsc + \alpha_m \varepsilon_{t-m}^2. \end{aligned} $$ For the special case of $p=m$ just replace $p$ with $m$ in the top equation.
