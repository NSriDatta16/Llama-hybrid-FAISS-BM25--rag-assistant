The complexity of one generation will be O(N/2). In order to make the whole population the same, you will need N generations. My answer will be O(N**2). You can also evaluate the time complexity by actually measuring the run time and averaging. If you don't do crossover, mutation or replacement you are just selecting the best of your individuals.
