First of all, why? Correlation with time means that is a trend. For checking if there is a trend you can use this topic: How to test for presence of trend in time series? Correlation does not have sense in non stationary data. Obviously, time is not stationary. If distribution is changing in time, there is no point to measure anything across whole period.
