Numbers of participants in crowdsourcing tasks

Within the recent years, leveraging crowdsourcing for performing, mainly intelligent-intensive, tasks has become prevalent. An important question in this regard, that I could not find any specific answer for, is how many participants are adequate for running such a project? Is there any standard for that?
