The issue you are running into is called the cold start problem, "How do I make a decision without any data". I would start with the following: Bayesian Methods - specify a prior and update as new data comes in. This could be a combination of both business and psychological profiling. https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bayesian_inference
