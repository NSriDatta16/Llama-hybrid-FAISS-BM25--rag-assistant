It is definitely possible, but it may not be easy. Josh Smith posted this article that includes code for implementing this with a ListView . He's created an attached property, "IsUnderDragCursor," and shows a trigger where the item under the "drag cursor" turns blue. Obviously, you'd have to take the source and adapt it to work with your menu and NavButtons, but the principles are all there.
