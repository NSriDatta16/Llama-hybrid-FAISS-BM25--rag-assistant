Usefulness of Z-normalization in Machine Learning

Z -normalization means rescaling the feature $X$ by subtracting the average $\mu$ and dividing by its standard deviation $\sigma$, i.e., $(X-\mu)/\sigma$. What is the usefulness of normalizing data for machine learning?
