What are you really going to do with the list? Are you planning to block browsers you don't support? What if the user hacks the User-Agent response? Like others, I would strongly suggest going with something like Yahoo's "Graded Browsers" and, if possible, leveraging YUI or other libraries so you don't have to do it yourself.
