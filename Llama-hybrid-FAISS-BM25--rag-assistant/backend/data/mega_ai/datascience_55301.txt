Variance in statistics vs machine learning

In basic statistics, variance is a measure the variability of the data about its mean. In machine learning, variance is a measure of learning the training data too well/capturing the noise in the data/oversensitivity to the small local fluctuations of the data. Since machine learning is mostly refurbished statistics, how does these two concepts relate to one another
