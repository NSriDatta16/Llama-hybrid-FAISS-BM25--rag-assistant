Reason not to pregenerate: it slows down object creation. Reason to always regenerate: you save the storage needed to keep the hashcode around. For the record, I'd probably precompute the hashcode and store it for immutable objects unless profiling showed me it was expensive and I wasn't accessing the value very often.
