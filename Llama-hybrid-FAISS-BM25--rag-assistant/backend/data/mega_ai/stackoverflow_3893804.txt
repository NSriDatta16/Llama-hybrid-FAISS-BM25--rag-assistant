There are a number of other countries that display a different number of decimal places. 2 is the majority, 0 (no cents in their currency, e.g., Japan) is the largest minority, 3 is used in just a few. No other number that I know off. When exchange rates are quoted, more decimals are typically used. The currencies with 0 and 3 that I'm aware of are shown below. The ISO currency codes can be found at: http://www.iso.org/iso/support/currency_codes_list-1.htm http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/ISO_4217 or http://www.currency-iso.org/en/home/tables/table-a1.html .
