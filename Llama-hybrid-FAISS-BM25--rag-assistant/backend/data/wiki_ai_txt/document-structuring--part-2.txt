Note that narrative in this sense applies to factual texts as well as stories. Current NLG systems do not do a good job of generating narratives, and this is a major source of user criticism. Generating good narratives is a challenge for all aspects of NLG, but the most fundamental challenge is probably in document structuring. == References ==
