The DeepMind Ethics and Society board is also distinct from the mooted AI Ethics Board that Google originally agreed to form when acquiring DeepMind. DeepMind Professors of machine learning DeepMind sponsors three chairs of machine learning: At the University of Cambridge, held by Neil Lawrence, in the Department of Computer Science and Technology, At the University of Oxford, held by Michael Bronstein, in the Department of Computer Science, and At the University College London, held by Marc Deisenroth, in the Department of Computer Science.
