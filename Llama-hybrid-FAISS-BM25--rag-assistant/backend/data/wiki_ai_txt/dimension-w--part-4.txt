On 12 February 2016, it was announced that the anime would be broadcast on Adult Swim's Toonami programming block, replacing Akame ga Kill!, beginning on 28 February 2016. The series was later released on Blu-ray on 25 March 2016. The series was licensed in the UK by Anime Limited, however it was later delisted and cancelled. Episodes OVAs A collection of five 4-minute long non-canon original video animation (OVA) shorts called W Gate Online - Rose's Counseling Room (Wの扉 ONLINE 〜ロゼのお悩み相談室〜, W no Tobira Onrain ~Roze no o Nayami Sōdan-Shitsu~) were included on the Blu-ray release of the series. The shorts are animated in a 3D chibi style and revolve around Mira talking with Rose about her problems as a robot. On 21 March 2016, an OVA episode was announced. It was bundled up with the anime's sixth Blu-ray release, which released on 26 August 2016.
