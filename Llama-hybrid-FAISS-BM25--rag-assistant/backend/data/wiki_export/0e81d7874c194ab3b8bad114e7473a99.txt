KFSI (92.9 FM) is a Christian radio station in Rochester, Minnesota.

History
KFSI was started in 1981 by the Rev. Ray Logan. The Station plays contemporary Christian music as well as some teaching programs. The KFSI broadcast tower is located west of Rochester.
