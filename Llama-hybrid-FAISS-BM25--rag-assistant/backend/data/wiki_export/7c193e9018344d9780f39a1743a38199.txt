Loka Stadium () or ŠRC Loka () is a multi-use stadium in Črnomelj, Slovenia. It is used mostly for football matches and was the home ground of the former Slovenian First League side NK Bela Krajina until their disbandment in 2016. Since then, it has been used by their phoenix club NŠ Bela Krajina. The stadium holds 1,517 spectators. The original stadium opened in 1955, and the current one was built in 2001. It is the largest stadium in White Carniola.
