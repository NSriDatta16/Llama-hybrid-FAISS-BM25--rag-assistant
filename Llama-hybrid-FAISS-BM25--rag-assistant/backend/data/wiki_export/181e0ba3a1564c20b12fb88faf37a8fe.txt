Jinonice () is a Prague Metro station on Line B, located in Jinonice, Prague 5. It was opened on 26 October 1988 as part of the line extension from Smíchovské nádraží to Nové Butovice. General reconstruction took place in 2017.

The station used to be named Švermova, after the journalist and resistance fighter Jan Šverma.
