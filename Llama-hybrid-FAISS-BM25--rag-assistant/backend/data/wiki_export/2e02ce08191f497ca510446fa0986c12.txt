Bundang Jesaeng Hospital (), also known as Daejin Medical Center, is a general hospital located in Seohyeon-dong, Bundang-gu, Seongnam, South Korea. It is one of the Daejin Medical Centers and was built on 15 January 1995 by Daejin Medical Foundation which is owned by Daesunjinrihoe.

Trivia
A 2006 SBS TV series, Alone in Love took shots in this hospital.
