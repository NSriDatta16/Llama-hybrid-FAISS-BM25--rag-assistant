Austin Preparatory School is a co-educational Catholic school located in Reading, Massachusetts.  It is located in the Roman Catholic Archdiocese of Boston.  It was founded by the Order of Saint Augustine in 1961. The institution is colloquially referred to as Austin Prep and serves students in grades 6â€“12.

On January 20, 2022, the school announced that it would leave the Catholic Central League and Massachusetts Interscholastic Athletic Association to join the New England Preparatory School Athletic Council starting in the fall of 2022. The school will begin NEPSAC competition as an independent.
