Utrinski vesnik () is a daily newspaper in the Republic of North Macedonia. The paper was established in 1999. The first issue of Utrinski vesnik was published on 23 June 1999. Its current editor is Erol Rizaov. It is published every day except Sunday.

In Friday, an addition called Magazin+ comes out together with the newspaper.
