Marcus Glenn Cotton (born August 11, 1966) is a former American football linebacker. He was drafted with the 28th overall pick in the second round of the 1988 NFL Draft by the Atlanta Falcons.

High school career
Cotton prepped at Castlemont High School in Oakland.

College career
He played college football and was All-Pac-10 at the University of Southern California.

Professional career
Cotton played in the National Football League between 1988 and 1992.
