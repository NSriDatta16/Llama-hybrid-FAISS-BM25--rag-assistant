Terry Wooden (born January 14, 1967)  is a former American football linebacker who played for three teams, the Seattle Seahawks, Kansas City Chiefs, and Oakland Raiders from 1990 to 1998. He was drafted by the Seahawks in the second round of the 1990 NFL Draft.

Wooden played college football at Syracuse University and is currently a scout for the New Orleans Saints.  Attended Farmington High School in Connecticut.
