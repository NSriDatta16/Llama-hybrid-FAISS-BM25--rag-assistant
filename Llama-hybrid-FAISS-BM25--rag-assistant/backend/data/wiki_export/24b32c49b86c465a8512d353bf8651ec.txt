Rita Kieber-Beck (born 27 December 1958) is a politician from Liechtenstein who served as the Deputy Prime Minister of Liechtenstein from 2005 to 2009.

Kieber-Beck is a member of the Progressive Citizens' Party. She was the minister of foreign affairs for Liechtenstein from 21 April 2055 to 25 March 2009.
