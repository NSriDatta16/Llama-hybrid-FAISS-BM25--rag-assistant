Qalandar in Sufism is used as a title for some Sufis  who are considered especially spiritual. Some people for whom the title is used are Lal Shahbaz Qalandar, Bu Ali Shah Qalandar, Shams Ali Qalandar, Rabia Basri, and Qalandar Baba Auliya. 
 Among 21st century people called by the title are Nathar Vali, Baba Fakruddin, and Qalander Baba Auliya.
