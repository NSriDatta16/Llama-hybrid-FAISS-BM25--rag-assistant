Ladas (Λαδάς) is a small village in Greece, part of the municipality Kalamata, Messenia.  It is located on Taygetos, and is one of the six villages which make up Alagonia.  , the village had 85 residents.  The village was named for an athlete, Ladas, who took first place in the discus in the Ancient Olympic Games.
