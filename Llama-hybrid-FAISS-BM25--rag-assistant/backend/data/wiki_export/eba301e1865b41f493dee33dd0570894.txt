Gerald John Morrison Munro (November 20, 1897 â€“ January 28, 1968) was a Canadian ice hockey player who played two seasons in the National Hockey League for the Montreal Maroons and the Toronto St. Pats between 1924 and 1926. The rest of his career, which lasted from 1919 to 1931, was mainly spent in the amateur senior Northern Ontario Hockey Association and minor American Hockey Association.

Personal life
Munro was born in Sault Ste. Marie, Ontario and married Florence M. Cameron in 1925 at Sault Ste. Marie, Michigan. He died in Sudbury, Ontario in 1968 and was buried at Park Lawn Cemetery. His wife, Florence died in 1958.
