Tiran (, also Romanized as Tīrān; also known as Tehrān, Tihrān, and Tirūn) is a city in the Central District of Tiran and Karvan County, Isfahan province, Iran, and serves as both capital of the county and of the district.

At the 2006 National Census, its population was 15,673 in 4,431 households. The following census in 2011 counted 19,421 people in 5,871 households. The latest census in 2016 showed a population of 21,703 people in 6,880 households.
