Scott Starks (born July 23, 1983) is a former American football cornerback for the Jacksonville Jaguars of the National Football League (NFL). He was drafted in the third round of the 2005 NFL Draft by the Jaguars. Jason Fletcher of B&F Sports was his agent. He is known for recovering a Kyle Orton fumble and running it back for a touchdown to help the Wisconsin Badgers defeat the Purdue Boilermakers in the 2004 season. Starks was also a track star at the Wisconsin, where he competed in the 100 meters, posting a personal best of 10.64 seconds.
