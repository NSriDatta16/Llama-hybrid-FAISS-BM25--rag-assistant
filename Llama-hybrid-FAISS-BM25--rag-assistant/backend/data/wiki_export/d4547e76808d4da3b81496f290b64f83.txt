Jacoona fabronia, the pale grand imperial, is a small butterfly found in the Indomalayan realm including India that belongs to the lycaenids or blues family.

Subspecies

N. a. amrita S.Burma, Thailand, Peninsular Malaya, Singapore, Sumatra 
N. a. theodora Druce, 1885 Borneo
