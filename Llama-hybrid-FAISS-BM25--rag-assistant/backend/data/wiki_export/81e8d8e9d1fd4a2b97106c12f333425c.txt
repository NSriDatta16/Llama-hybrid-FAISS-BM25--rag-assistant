Arab slave trade refers to various periods in which a slave trade has been carried out under the auspices of Arab peoples or Arab countries. Examples include:
 Trans-Saharan slave trade
 Indian Ocean slave trade
 Barbary slave trade
 Slavery in Tunisia
 Slavery in Libya
 Slavery in Sudan
 Slavery in Mauritania
 Slavery in Yemen
