This discography is a comprehensive listing of official releases by British soul singer Beverley Knight. It consists of nine studio albums, three compilation albums (two greatest hits albums and one remix album) and one EP. Knight has also released twenty-eight solo singles (excluding re-releases) and twenty-six solo music videos. She has also collaborated on at least fifteen other projects.
