The OR Tambo District Municipality () is one of the seven districts of the Eastern Cape province of South Africa. It is within the Wild Coast Region. The seat is Mthatha. As of 2011, the vast majority (94%) of its 1,364,943 inhabitants spoke isiXhosa. The district is named after Oliver Tambo. The district code is DC15.

Geography

Neighbours
OR Tambo is surrounded by:
 Alfred Nzo District (DC44) to the north
 the Indian Ocean to the south-east
 Amatole District (DC12) to the south-west
 Chris Hani District (DC13) to the west
 Joe Gqabi District (DC14) to the north-west
