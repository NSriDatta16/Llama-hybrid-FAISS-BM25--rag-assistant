Saint-Pol-sur-Mer (, literally Saint-Pol on Sea; ; Picard: Saint-Po-dsu-Mér) is a former commune in the Nord department in northern France.  Since 9 December 2010, it is part of the commune of Dunkirk. In 2019 it had 20,250 inhabitants. It is the second-largest suburb of the city of Dunkirk, and is almost encircled by it.
