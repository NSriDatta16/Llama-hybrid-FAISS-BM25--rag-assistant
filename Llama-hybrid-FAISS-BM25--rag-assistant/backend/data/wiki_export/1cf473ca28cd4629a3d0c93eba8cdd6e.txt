Farsan County () is in Chaharmahal and Bakhtiari province, Iran. Its capital is the city of Farsan.

At the 2006 census, the county's population was 90,111 in 19,878 households. The following census in 2011 counted 93,941 people in 24,747 households. At the 2016 census the county's population was 95,286, in 26,914 households.
