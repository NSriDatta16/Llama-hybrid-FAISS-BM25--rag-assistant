Åmotsdal is a valley and small rural village in the municipality of Seljord in Telemark county, Norway. The villages is located about  to the northwest of the village of Seljord and about  to the northwest of the village of Flatdal. It is the site of Åmotsdal Church (Åmotsdal kyrkje) which was built in 1792.
