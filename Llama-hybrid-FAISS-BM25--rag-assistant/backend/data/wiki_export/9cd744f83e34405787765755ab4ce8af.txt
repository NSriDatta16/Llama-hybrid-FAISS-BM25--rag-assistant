The Eastern AAA Hockey League was a minor level ice hockey governing body.  The league was a sub-division of the Ontario Minor Hockey Association in the Canadian province of Ontario. The ETA playoffs are a best of 5 series. The final two teams advance to the OMHA Championships. It was established in 1990, before merging with the South-Central Triple A Hockey League in 2023.
