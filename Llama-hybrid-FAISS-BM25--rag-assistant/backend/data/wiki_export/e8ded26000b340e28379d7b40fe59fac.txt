Feleacu (; ) is a commune in Cluj County, Transylvania, Romania. It is composed of five villages: Casele Micești (Kaszoly), Feleacu, Gheorghieni (Györgyfalva), Sărădiș (Seregélyes) and Vâlcele (Bányabükk).
