Blenz Coffee (officially Blenz The Canadian Coffee Company) is a Canadian franchise chain of coffee shops. The first shop was opened in Vancouver, British Columbia in 1992. The chain has since grown to include a network of franchised locations, 64 in British Columbia Canada. The number of Blenz locations in the City of Vancouver has declined from 27 in 2013 to 12 in 2023.
