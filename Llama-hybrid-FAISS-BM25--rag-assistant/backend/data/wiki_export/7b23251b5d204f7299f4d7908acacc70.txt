Sarah Adler (; born 1978) is a French and Israeli actress with dual citizenship.

Career
Adler is now best known for her performances in the 2017 Israeli films Foxtrot and The Cakemaker; earlier in her career her notable films included Stones (2004), directed by her husband Raphael Nadjari; Our Music (2004), directed by Jean-Luc Godard; and Marie Antoinette (2006).
