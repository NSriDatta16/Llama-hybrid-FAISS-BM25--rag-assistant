Fiorella Viñas (born  1984) is the 2006 Miss Peru. She represented the Peruvian region of Lambayeque and won the title of Miss Peru Universe. She competed at the Miss Universe 2006 pageant held on 23 July 2006 in Los Angeles, California where she didn't place as a finalist.
