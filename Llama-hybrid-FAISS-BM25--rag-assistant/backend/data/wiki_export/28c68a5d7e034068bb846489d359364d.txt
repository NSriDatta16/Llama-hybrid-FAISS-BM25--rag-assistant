Sümeg () is a town in Veszprém county, Hungary. Sümeg is mostly known for Sümeg Castle. It is  north of Lake Balaton.

Twin towns – sister cities

Sümeg is twinned with:
 Aichtal, Germany

 Sovata, Romania
 Tapolca, Hungary
 Vobarno, Italy
