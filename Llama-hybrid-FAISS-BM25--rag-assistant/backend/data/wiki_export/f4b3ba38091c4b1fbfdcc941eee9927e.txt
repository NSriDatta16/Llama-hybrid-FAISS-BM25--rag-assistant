Sharswood is a small neighborhood in the North Philadelphia section of the city of Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, United States. It is located to the east of Brewerytown, north of Girard College, west of Ridge Avenue, and south of the Norman Blumberg Apartments. It is often grouped with Brewerytown, as in the Brewerytown-Sharswood Community Civic Association, and the Brewerytown-Sharswood NTI Planning Area.
