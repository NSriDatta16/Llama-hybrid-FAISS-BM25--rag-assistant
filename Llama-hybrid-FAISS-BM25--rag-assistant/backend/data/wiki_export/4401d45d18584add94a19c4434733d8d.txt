Orange Municipal Airport,  in Orange, Massachusetts, is a public airport owned by Town of Orange.  It has two runways, averages 95 flights per day, and has approximately 46 aircraft based on its field.

The airport offers Jet A and 100LL fuel, maintenance & repair facilities for piston and turbine power corporate aircraft.

Taxi services, rental cars, restaurants and a variety of options for lodging are available within close proximity to the airport.
