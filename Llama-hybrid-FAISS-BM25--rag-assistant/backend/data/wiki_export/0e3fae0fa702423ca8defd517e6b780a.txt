The Malaysian Trades Union Congress (MTUC; ), is a national trade union centre in Malaysia. It was formed in 1949 and was originally known as the Malayan Trades Union Council. It then changed its name to the Malayan Trades Union Congress in 1958, and then to its current name with the formation of Malaysia.

MTUC is affiliated with the International Trade Union Confederation.
