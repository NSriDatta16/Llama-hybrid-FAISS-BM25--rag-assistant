Saruhanlı is a municipality and district of Manisa Province, Turkey. Its area is 771 km2, and its population is 56,523 (2022). The town lies at an elevation of .

Agriculture 
Olive, walnut and almond cultivation is among the important agricultural activities of Saruhanlı.
