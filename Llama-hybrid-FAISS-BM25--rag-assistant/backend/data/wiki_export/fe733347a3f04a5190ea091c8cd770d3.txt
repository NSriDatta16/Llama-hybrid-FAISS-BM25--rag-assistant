John Edward Browning (born September 30, 1973) is a former American football defensive tackle.  He played in 24 regular season games (12 starts) for West Virginia University, tallying 78 tackles, 8 sacks, a fumble recovery and a pass deflection.  He was drafted in the 1996 NFL Draft in the third round by the Kansas City Chiefs.
