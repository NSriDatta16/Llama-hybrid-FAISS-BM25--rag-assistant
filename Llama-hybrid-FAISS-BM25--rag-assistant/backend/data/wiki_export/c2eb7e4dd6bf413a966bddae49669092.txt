Sulsted is a small Danish town with a population of 1,512 (1 January 2023) situated in northern Jutland, 14 km north of Aalborg and 3 km north of the closest other town Vestbjerg.

Church 
Sulsted Church was constructed –1200  and features a large number of frescos, all created in 1548 by Hans Maler from Randers.

Notable people 
 Knud Jespersen (1926 in Sulsted – 1977) a Danish politician, Chairman of the Communist Party of Denmark between 1958 and 1977
