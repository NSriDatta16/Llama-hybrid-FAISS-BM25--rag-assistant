The Wachs Arena is an 8,057 seat multi-purpose arena in Aberdeen, South Dakota, United States.
 
The Wachs Arena is home to the Don Meyer Court, named after long-time winning coach Don Meyer. The Arena is located in the Barnett Center; which holds swimming pool, wrestling room, weight room, training room, auxiliary gym, eight locker rooms, classrooms and offices, plus a 160-meter indoor track and a wooden basketball court. It was the Home of a Presidential visit to the Area by President George W. Bush October 31, 2002
