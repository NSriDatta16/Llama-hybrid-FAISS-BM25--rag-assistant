199 Park Lane was a British soap opera that aired on BBC1 in 1965. Airing twice a week, the series was set in a luxury block of flats in London.

A total of 18 episodes were broadcast, the first two with the titles "The New Tenant" and "Decision". The entire series was later wiped by the BBC and no episodes survive in the archives.
