Sipsey Fork is an unincorporated community in Monroe County, Mississippi, United States.

Sipsey Fork is located east of Splunge.

History
The community takes its name from nearby Sipsey Creek.

A post office operated under the name Sipsey in 1896.

Sipsey Fork is home to Center Point Church, which was founded in 1845 and once served as the community's school.
