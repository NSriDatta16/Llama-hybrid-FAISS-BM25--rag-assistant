Nurhak () is a municipality and district of Kahramanmaraş Province, Turkey. Its area is 1,028 km2, and its population is 12,257 (2022).

Composition

 Seyitaliler
 Tatlar
 Umutlu
 Yenimahalle

Demography 
The district is populated by Kurds and Turks.
