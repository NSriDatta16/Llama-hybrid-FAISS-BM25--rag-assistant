Constantine V () (11 January 1833 - 27 February 1914) was Ecumenical Patriarch of Constantinople from 1897 to 1901. His secular name was Konstantinos Valiadis () and he was born on January 11, 1833, at Vessa, on the island of Chios. 

He died on February 27, 1914. He is buried at  Hagia Triada Church in Istanbul.
