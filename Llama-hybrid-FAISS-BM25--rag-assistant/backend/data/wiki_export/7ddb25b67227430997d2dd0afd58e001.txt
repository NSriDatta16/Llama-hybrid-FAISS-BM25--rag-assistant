Rudolf "Rudi" Nierlich (20 February 1966 â€“ 18 May 1991) was an Austrian alpine skier. Born in Sankt Wolfgang im Salzkammergut (Upper Austria), he won a total of eight races in the Alpine Skiing World Cup, and was three times World Champion (1989 and 1991), in Slalom and giant slalom.

Death
He died  in May 1991 in a traffic collision in Sankt Wolfgang im Salzkammergut.
