Ramon D. Walker (born November 8, 1979) is an American football defensive back who played in the National Football League (NFL). Walker ranks seventh on the University of Pittsburgh's all-time list with 307 career tackles over just three seasons. He also deflected 14 passes and intercepted two.  He started in all 11 games at free safety in 2001 while earning first-team All-Big East honors. He was drafted in the 2002 NFL Draft by the Houston Texans.
