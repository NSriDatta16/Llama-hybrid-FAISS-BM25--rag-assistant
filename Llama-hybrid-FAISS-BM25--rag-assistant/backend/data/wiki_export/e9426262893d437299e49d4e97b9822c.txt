FC Ekibastuz () is a Kazakh professional football club based in Ekibastuz. Until 2008, the team represented Pavlodar and was called Energetik Pavlodar. In 2008, the team played as Energetik-2 Ekibastuz. In 2009, it was renamed to Ekibastuz FK. They are current members of the Kazakhstan First Division.
