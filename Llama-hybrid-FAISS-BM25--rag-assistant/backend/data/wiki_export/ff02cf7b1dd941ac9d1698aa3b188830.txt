Yigal Arnon & Co., founded by Yigal Arnon) in Jerusalem in the late 1950s, is one of the largest law firms in Israel. It has offices in Tel Aviv and Jerusalem, and more than 170 lawyers, of whom 61 are partners.  Its Tel Aviv offices are located in two of the Azrieli Towers.
