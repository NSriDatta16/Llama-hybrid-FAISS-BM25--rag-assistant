Lisa Aldenhoven (born c.1958) is an Australian film and television actress.

Career
Aldenhoven remains best known for her role in the television soap opera The Young Doctors as Nurse Julie Holland. She appeared in the series from 1978 to 1981.

Aldenhoven made a guest appearance in Prisoner, appearing in the first episode as ill-fated inmate Sally Lee. She was invited back several years later to play another guest role. She has also appeared in several movies, including Mad Max, The Getting of Wisdom, Blue Fire Lady, Death of a Soldier and Brilliant Lies.

She now works in PR and marketing.
