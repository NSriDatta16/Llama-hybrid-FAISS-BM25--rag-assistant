Hymns is the first solo studio album by Living Colour vocalist Corey Glover. It was released on April 7, 1998 via LaFace Records. Recording sessions took place at Teenage Dog Studio in Brooklyn and at BearTracks in Suffern, NY. Production was handled by Glower himself and The Family Stand, with L.A. Reid and Babyface serving as executive producers.
