KBFK-LP, virtual channel 34 (UHF digital channel 36), is a low-power Movies!-affiliated television station licensed to Bakersfield, California, United States. The station is owned by Cocola Broadcasting. MeTV was seen on 36.1, but was dropped in August 2014. Movies! was added to 36.2 on February 1, 2014. As of 2019, Cocola combined KBFK and KCBT's virtual channels in Bakersfield. Both channels are now located on channel 34.
