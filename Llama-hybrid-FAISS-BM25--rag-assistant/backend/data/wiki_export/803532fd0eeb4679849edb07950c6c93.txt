Mehrestan (), formerly known as Zaboli () also Romanized as Zābolī; also known as Qal‘eh-ye Zābolī (); formerly, Magas, is a city in, and the capital of, the Central District of Mehrestan County, Sistan and Baluchestan province, Iran, and also serves as capital of the county and of Zaboli Rural District.

At the 2006 census, its population was 7,672 in 1,560 households, when it was in the former Zaboli District of Saravan County. The following census in 2011 counted 10,112 people in 2,171 households, by which time the district had been separated from the county to become a part of the newly formed Zaboli County (later renamed Mehrestan County). The latest census in 2016 showed a population of 12,245 people in 2,969 households.
