The Mandokhail () is a Pashtun tribe primarily found in northern regions of the Balochistan Province and in southern Afghanistan. Most of the Mandokhails are settled in Zhob District of Balochistan Province, Pakistan; they live on both sides of the Zhob river.

Mandokhail has many subtribes:
There are Arabzai, Dawoodkhail, Surhkhail , Mamayzai, Shaikh.
Mandokhail tribe is found on the Northern west border of Balochistan which is also known as the Durand line.
