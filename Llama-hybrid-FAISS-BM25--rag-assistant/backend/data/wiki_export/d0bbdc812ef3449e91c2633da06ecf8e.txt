Le Monastier-sur-Gazeille (; ) is a commune in the Haute-Loire department in south-central France.

The official start of the Robert Louis Stevenson Trail (GR 70), a popular long-distance path, is at Place de la Poste in the centre of the village, marked by a plaque.
