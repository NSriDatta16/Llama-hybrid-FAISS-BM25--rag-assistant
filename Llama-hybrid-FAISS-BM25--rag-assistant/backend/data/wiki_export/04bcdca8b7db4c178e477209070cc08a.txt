Eryaman is a neighbourhood in the municipality and district of Etimesgut, Ankara Province, Turkey. Its population is 30,275 (2022). It is located 28 km west of the city centre of Ankara.

Göksu Park located here is a public park and neighborhood that surrounds the small natural lake
