Evangelismos () is a town near the southern coast of Messenia, Greece. It is located  southeast of Pylos and  east of Methoni. The town is the main settlement in Evangelismos Community which itself is part of the Methoni municipal unit within the Pylos-Nestor municipality. Nearby villages include Dentroulia and Kamaria.
