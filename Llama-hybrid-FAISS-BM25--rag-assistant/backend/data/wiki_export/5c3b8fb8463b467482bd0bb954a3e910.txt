Southbridge Municipal Airport  is a public airport located two miles (3Â km) north of the central business district (CBD) of the Town of Southbridge, a city in Worcester County, Massachusetts, USA. The airport is owned and operated by the Town of Southbridge and overseen by the Southbridge Municipal Airport Commission.  It covers , has a single runway, averages 142 flights per day, and has approximately 30 aircraft based on its field. The airport sustained damage on June 1 from the 2011 New England tornado outbreak.
