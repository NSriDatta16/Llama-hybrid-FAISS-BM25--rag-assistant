Get Squared is the second studio album by Nigerian musical duo P-Square. It was released in May 2005, by the duo's own label "Square Records". A breakthrough succes, the album spawned several hit singles including "Bizzy Body," "Temptation," "Say your love," and "Omoge Mi" 
On 15 May 2009, the album was released on iTunes.
