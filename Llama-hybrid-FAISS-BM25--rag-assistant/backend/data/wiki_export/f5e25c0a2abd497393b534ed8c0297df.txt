Foreglen (, "The Outer Glenn") is a village in County Londonderry, Northern Ireland. Around 1900 it was known as Ballymoney (not to be confused with the County Antrim town of Ballymoney).

Education
St Peter's and St Paul's Primary School

Sport
The local Gaelic Athletic Association club is O'Brien's GAC.
