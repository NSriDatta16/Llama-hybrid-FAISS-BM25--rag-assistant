Aznakayevo (; ) is a town in the Republic of Tatarstan, Russia, located on the Styarlya River (left tributary of the Ik),  southeast from Kazan. Population:

Geography
Chatyr-Tau range is located  east of Aznakayevo.

Administrative and municipal status
Within the framework of administrative divisions, Aznakayevo serves as the administrative center of Aznakayevsky District, even though it is not a part of it. As a municipal division, the town of republic significance of Aznakayevo is incorporated within Aznakayevsky Municipal District as Aznakayevo Urban Settlement.

Industry
Oil-producing industry is highly developed (oil and gas producing administration "Aznakayevskneft", JSC "RITEK-introduction"); there are some car-building and metal-working  enterprises (JSC "Aznakayevo plant "Neftemash"), food industry (Aznakayevo butter-making factory, AO "Aznakayevo Bread", AO "Aznakayevo foodstuff factory"), and light industry (JSC "Aznakayevo Clothes").
