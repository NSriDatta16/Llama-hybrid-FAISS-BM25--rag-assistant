Aspidoras rochai or loach catfish is a tropical freshwater fish belonging to the Corydoradinae sub-family of the  family Callichthyidae. It originates in inland waters in South America, and is found in coastal rivers in Ceará, Brazil.

The fish will grow up to  in standard length. It lives in a tropical climate in water with a 6.0–7.5 pH, a water hardness of up to 14 dGH, and a temperature range of . It feeds on worms, benthic crustaceans, insects, and plant matter. It lays eggs in dense vegetation and adults do not guard the eggs.
