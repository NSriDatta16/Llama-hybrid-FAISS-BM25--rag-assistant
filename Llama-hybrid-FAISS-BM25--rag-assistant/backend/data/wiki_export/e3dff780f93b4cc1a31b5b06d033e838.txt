Centro Universitario Angle Mexicano (English: Anglo Mexican University Centre) is a Mexican preparatory school affiliated with the Universidad Nacional Autónoma de Mexico (UNAM). It was inaugurated on September 4, 1974, in the residential San José Insurgentes neighborhood of Mexico City.
