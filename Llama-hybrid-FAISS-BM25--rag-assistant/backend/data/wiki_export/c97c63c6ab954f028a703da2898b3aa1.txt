WJNI-LD, UHF digital channel 31, is a low-power YTA TV-affiliated television station licensed to North Charleston, South Carolina, United States. Owned by Norsan  Media.

Previously, WJNI-LD was affiliated with the UATV network; however, UATV suspended operations indefinitely on May 1, 2006.
