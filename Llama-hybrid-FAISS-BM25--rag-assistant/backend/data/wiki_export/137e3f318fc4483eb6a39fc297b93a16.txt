Dickson Choto  (born 19 March 1981) is a Zimbabwean footballer who played as a center-back for Legia Warsaw from 2003 until June 2013. He was born in Wedza District.

International
He appeared for Zimbabwe in the 2004 African Cup of Nations, and was not called up for 2006 African Cup of Nations.
