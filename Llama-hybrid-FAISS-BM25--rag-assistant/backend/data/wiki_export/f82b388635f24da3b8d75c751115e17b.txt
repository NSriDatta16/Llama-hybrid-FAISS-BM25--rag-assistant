Susuz (, , ) is a town in Kars Province in the Eastern Anatolia region of Turkey. It is the seat of Susuz District. Its population is 2,044 (2022). The mayor is Oğuz Yantemur from the Republican People's Party (CHP).

Geography 
There is Lake Aygır to the west of the Susuz district center.
