Chen Hong (born December 13, 1968) is a Chinese actress and film producer. She married film director Chen Kaige in 1997 and they have two sons. In recent years, she has co-produced and acted in the films Together, The Promise and Forever Enthralled. She is also known for her role as Diaochan in the 1994 television series Romance of the Three Kingdoms.
