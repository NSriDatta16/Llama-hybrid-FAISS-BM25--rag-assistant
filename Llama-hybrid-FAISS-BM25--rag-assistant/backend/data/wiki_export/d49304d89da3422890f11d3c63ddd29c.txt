Bjølstad (also called Bjølstadmo) or Heidal is a village in Sel Municipality in Innlandet county, Norway. The village is located in the Heidal valley, about  northeast of the village of Skogbygda and about  southwest of the town of Otta.

The  village has a population (2022) of 376 and a population density of .

The village is the site of the historic Bjølstad Farm and the Heidal Church. Historically, this small village was the administrative centre of the former municipality of Heidal which existed from 1908 until 1965.
