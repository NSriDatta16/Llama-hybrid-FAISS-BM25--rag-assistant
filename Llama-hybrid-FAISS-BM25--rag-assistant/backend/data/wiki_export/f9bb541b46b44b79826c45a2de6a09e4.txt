Mir also spelled as Meer (Persian: مير) (derived from the Arabic title Emir 'general, prince') is a Kashmiri clan with ancestral roots from medieval Persia. The clan predominantly lives in Kashmir and with significant population living in Punjab.

Notable people
Hamid Mir, Pakistani journalist, columnist and author
Mir Khalil-ur-Rahman, Pakistani entrepreneur & founder of Jang Media Group
Ahad Raza Mir, actor and musician
 Professor Sajid Mir, Member, Senate of Pakistan (2015 - 2021)
