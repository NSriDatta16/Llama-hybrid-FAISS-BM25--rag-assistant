Nurlat (; ) is a town in the Republic of Tatarstan, Russia, located on the Kondurcha River,  southeast of Kazan. Population:

History
Nurlat was formerly known as Nurlat-Oktyabrsky.

It was founded in 1905 as a settlement around a railway station. Town status was granted to it in 1961.

Administrative and municipal status
Within the framework of administrative divisions, Nurlat serves as the administrative center of Nurlatsky District, even though it is not a part of it.
