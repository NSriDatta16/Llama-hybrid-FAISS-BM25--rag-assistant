Eugen Carl Kaufmann or Eugene Charles Kent (8 January 1892 â€“ 21 June 1984) was a German architect.

Early life
Kaufmann was born 8 January 1892 in Frankfurt am Main. From 1925, he was engaged at the New Frankfurt project under the leadership of Ernst May. Later, he joined in a city planning with E. May, at the invitation of the Soviets.

In London
Kaufmann moved to London and became a naturalized citizen on 28 June 1939.
He opened his own architectural office there.

He died on 21 June 1984 in London.
