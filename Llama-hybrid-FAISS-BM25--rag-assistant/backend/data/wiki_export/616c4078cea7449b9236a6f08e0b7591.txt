Paula Tiso is an American voiceover actress. She works in many areas  of voiceover including broadcast narration, news promo, radio imaging, commercial as well as video games and dubbing. 

She has narrated the true crime Oxygen series Living With a Serial Killer, season 2 and the ID channel series The Devil Speaks, as well as various series on The History Channel and the Smithsonian Channel.  

Paula also works in video games, appearing in Fallout 76 and Lego The Incredibles, and is known for voicing Lulu in Final Fantasy X and Final Fantasy X-2, and Sylvia Christel in the No More Heroes series of games.
