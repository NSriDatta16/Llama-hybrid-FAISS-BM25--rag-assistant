Çıldır (, Husenian; meaning "North"; , Chrdili; meaning "Shadow") is a town in Ardahan Province of Turkey. It is the seat of Çıldır District. Its population is 2,552 (2021). The town is populated by Turks.

The town consists of 9 quarters, including Aşıkşenlik and Yıldırımtepe.
