Early life
Biggar was born on November 5, 1861, in Saltfleet Township in Hamillton, Ontario, to Christopher Biggar and Ann Dean.

Personal life
Biggar married Charlotte Elizabeth Armstrong and had 6 children. 

On March 5, 1920, Biggar died and was buried in Hamilton Graveyard.
