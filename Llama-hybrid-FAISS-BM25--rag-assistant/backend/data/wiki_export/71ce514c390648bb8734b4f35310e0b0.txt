Beyram (, also Romanized as Bairam and Beyrom) is a city in, and the capital of, Beyram District of Larestan County, Fars province, Iran, and also serves as the administrative center for Beyram Rural District.

At the 2006 census, its population was 6,520 in 1,469 households. The following census in 2011 counted 7,379 people in 1,851 households. The latest census in 2016 showed a population of 7,300 people in 2,125 households.
