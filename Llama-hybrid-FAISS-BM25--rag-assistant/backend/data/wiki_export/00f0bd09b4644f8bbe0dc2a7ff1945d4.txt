Borujen County () is in Chaharmahal and Bakhtiari province, Iran. Its capital is the city of Borujen.

At the 2006 census the county's population was 113,795, in 27,963 households. The following census in 2011 counted 118,681 people in 32,649 households. At the 2016 census the county's population was 122,483, in 36,238 households.
