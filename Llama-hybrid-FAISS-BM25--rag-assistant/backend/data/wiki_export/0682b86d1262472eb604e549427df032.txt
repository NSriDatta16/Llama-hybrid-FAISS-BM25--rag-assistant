Sempah Pass (Malay: Genting Sempah; Jawi: ڬنتيڠ سمڤه) is a transit town and mountain pass in the Titiwangsa Range in Bentong District, Pahang, Malaysia, on the border with the neighbouring state of Selangor.  Genting Sempah is mostly known as a transit point to Genting Highlands, a famed hill station located on the peak of Mount Ulu Kali.
