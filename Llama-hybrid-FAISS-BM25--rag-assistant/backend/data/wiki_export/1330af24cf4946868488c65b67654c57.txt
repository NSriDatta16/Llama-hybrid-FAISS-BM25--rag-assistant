KNBA (90.3 FM) is a radio station in Anchorage, Alaska. The station is currently owned by Koahnic Broadcast Corporation and primarily airs an adult album alternative music format, while incorporating programming from National Public Radio, Native Voice 1, Public Radio International and APRN. KNBA also serves as the flagship station for National Native News, which is syndicated to hundreds of radio stations across the United States and Canada
