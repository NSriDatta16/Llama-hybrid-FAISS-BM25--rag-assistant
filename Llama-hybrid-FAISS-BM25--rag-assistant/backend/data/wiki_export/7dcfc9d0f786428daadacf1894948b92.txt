Arstaddalsdammen is a lake that lies in the municipality of Beiarn in Nordland county, Norway.  It is located about  southwest of the village of Moldjord.  The  lake lies in the southern end of the Arstaddal valley, and it has a dam on the northern end.  The dam was built in the 1960s.
