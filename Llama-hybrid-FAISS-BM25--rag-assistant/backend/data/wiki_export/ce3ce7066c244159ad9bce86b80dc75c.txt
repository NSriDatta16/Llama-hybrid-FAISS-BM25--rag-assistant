Zamira Khamatkhanovna Zaytseva (; born February 16, 1953) is a retired middle-distance runner who represented the USSR. She was born in Oltinko ªl, Uzbek SSR.

Her achievements include three championship silver medals over 1500 metres.

Her personal best times are 1:56.21 (800 metres) and 3:56.14 (1500 metres).
