Oprtalj () is a village and municipality in Istria, Croatia. Oprtalj is a community in the central northern part of Istria County, situated across the Mirna river valley from the village of Motovun, about 20Â km northwest of Pazin.

Oprtalj used to be a fortress surrounded by walls. In front of the former door, there is a large Venetian loggia with a lapidary.

Demographics
According to the 2021 census, its population was 748, with 75 living in the village proper. At the 2011 census it was 850.
