Clavelinidae is a family of tunicates in the order Aplousobranchia. It describes a group of marine animals.

Genera
, WoRMS recognizes the following genera in the family Clavelinidae:
 Clavelina 
 Eudistoma  Euclavella? 
 Nephtheis 
 Pycnoclavella
