Dendrobium cuthbertsonii is a species of orchid in the genus Dendrobium. It grows epiphytically at up to  above sea level in New Guinea and the Bismarck Archipelago. It is targeted by commercial collectors who harvest it for export. It has one of the longest floral durations of any orchid, with individual flowers remaining open for up to nine months.  Another source says ten months. Its stems are  tall and  wide; the flowers are  long,  wide, and extremely variable in colour., ranging from crimson through orangey-red.
