Akranesvöllur () (Known as Norðurálsvöllur for sponsorship purposes) is a football stadium in Akranes, Iceland.  It is currently used for football matches.  The stadium holds 5,550 and was built in 1935. It has 850 seats.

History

Akranesvöllur has a long and proud story, as one of iceland’s oldest current stadiums.

Currently going through renovations as of 2023 the stadium holds 5,550 with 850 seats.
