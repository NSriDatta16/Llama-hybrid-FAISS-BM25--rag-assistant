Nordagutu is a village in Midt-Telemark Municipality in Vestfold og Telemark county, Norway. The village is located along the river Sauarelva, just south of the southern end of the lake Heddalsvatnet. The village of lies about  south of the village of Holtsås, about  northeast of the village of Akkerhaugen, and just across the river from the village of Sauherad.

The  village has a population (2022) of 325 and a population density of .

Nordagutu train station is a part of the Bratsberg Line, and it serves as a junction between the Vestfold Line and Sørlandet Line.
