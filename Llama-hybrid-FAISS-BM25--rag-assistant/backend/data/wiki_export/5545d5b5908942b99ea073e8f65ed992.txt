is BoA's fifth single. It was featured on the hit anime, Inuyasha, as the fourth (in Japan) and the second (Korean) ending theme song. This was released on the same day as her Japanese debut album Listen to My Heart and reached number ten on the Oricon singles chart. Also, the Korean version of this song is listed in her Korean album, Miracle.

This single was also the first Copy Control release in Japan.
