The station has a single island platform with a fare mezzanine above. The mezzanine level connects to the Center City Pedestrian Concourse, which connects subway and regional rail stations in the Center City area.

Construction work to make the station accessible, including street to mezzanine and mezzanine to platform elevators, began in October 2020.
