Lisa Breckenridge is a former American television personality and news anchor. She currently runs a blog called happy.com.

Biography
Breckenridge dropped out from Pacific Union College in the Napa Valley in 1987 and struggled with issues relating to hair loss in the late 1980s.

She worked at KYMA-DT in Yuma, Arizona, then at KOLO-TV in Reno, Nevada and at KCRA in Sacramento, California, before settling at KTTV as a reporter, fill-in anchor, and lifestyle correspondent for Good Day L.A., a job she held for  years from 1999 to 2017. She was let go from the channel in January 2017, due to declining viewership on her segments.

She was married to talent manager Andy Cohen for 15 years; they announced their divorce in 2018.  Breckenridge is known for her unusual haircut which she has unsuccessfully tried to trademark.
