The Jamali () is  a Baloch tribe residing in Jaffarabad, Balochistan. The Jamali tribe lives in the southern part of Pakistan. They are mainly settled in the Balochistan and Sindh provinces of Pakistan.

The principal sections of Jamali are Taharani (also known as Sher Khanani), Shahaliani, Shahalzai, Mundrani, Sahriani (originally Khosa), Doshli, Zanwrani (also Khosa), Waswani, Bhandani, Babar, TIngiani, Manjhi, Ramdani and Rehanwala.
