Club de Fútbol Arenas de Armilla Cultura y Deporte is a Spanish football team based in Armilla, Granada, in the autonomous community of Andalusia. Founded in 1931 it plays in , holding home matches at Estadio Municipal de Armilla, with a 2,000-seat capacity.

Season to season

1 season in Segunda División B
16 seasons in Tercera División
2 season in Tercera Federación
