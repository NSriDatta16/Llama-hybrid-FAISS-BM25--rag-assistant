Vatndalsvatnet or Vatnedalsvatnet is a lake in the municipality of Bykle in Agder county, Norway.  The  lake lies about  west of the village of Berdalen and the river Otra. The nearby lakes Ormsavatnet and Store Urevatn flow into the lake through dams and canals.  The lake Vatndalsvatnet also has a dam on it and the water flows out of the lake from the dam into the Otra river.
