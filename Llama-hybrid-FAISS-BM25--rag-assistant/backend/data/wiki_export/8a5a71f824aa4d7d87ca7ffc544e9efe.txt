Kale () is a municipality and district of Malatya Province, Turkey. Its area is 237 km2, and its population is 5,571 (2022). The mayor is Murat Koca (AKP).

Demographics 
The district is fully Sunni Kurdish with the exception of one Kurdish Alevi village.
