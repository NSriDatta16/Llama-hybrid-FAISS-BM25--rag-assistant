Mirari Films was an animation and special effects company based in Los Angeles, with facilities in Brasov, Romania and New York City. In 2022, the studio closed down then the employees moved to any other animation faculties (including Bento Box Entertainment, ShadowMachine and Titmouse, Inc.).
