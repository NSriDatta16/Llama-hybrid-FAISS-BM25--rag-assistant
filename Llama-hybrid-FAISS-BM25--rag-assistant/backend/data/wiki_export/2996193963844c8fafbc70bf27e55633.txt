Spring Lake, originally named Edmonton Beach, is a village in central Alberta, Canada. It is located on the eastern shore of Spring Lake, approximately  and  west of the cities of Spruce Grove and Edmonton respectively. Hasse Lake is located  southwest of Spring Lake. It was known as the Summer Village of Edmonton Beach from 1959 to 1999.

Services 
There is a large RV park on the east side of the lake, which has a small camping store which sells candy, newspapers, and a variety of camping items. Other than this there are no commercial businesses in the village.
