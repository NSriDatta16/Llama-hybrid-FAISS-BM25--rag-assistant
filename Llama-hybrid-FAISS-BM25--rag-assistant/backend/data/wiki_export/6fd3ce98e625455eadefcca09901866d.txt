Olavtoppen, occasionally anglicised as Olav Peak, is the highest point of Bouvet Island, a volcanic island and dependency of Norway, and the remotest island on Earth. Olavtoppen is located north of the center of the island, immediately south of Kapp Valdivia, and rises  above sea level. It was first ascended on 21 February 2012 by Aaron Halstead, Will Allen, Bruno Rodi and Jason Rodi
