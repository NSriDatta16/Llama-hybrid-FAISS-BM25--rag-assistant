Graeme Watson (born 6 May 1986) is a Scottish professional footballer.

Watson began his career with Rangers and was first registered as a professional player in June 2003. He did not make an appearance for Rangers' first team, and after being released by them in the summer of 2006 he signed for Airdrie United.
