Yeşilyurt () is a municipality and district of Malatya Province, Turkey. Its area is 953 km2, and its population is 341,654 (2022). At the 2013 reorganisation part of the former central district of Malatya Province was added to the district. It covers the western part of the agglomeration of Malatya and the adjacent countryside. The mayor is Mehmet Çınar (AKP).
