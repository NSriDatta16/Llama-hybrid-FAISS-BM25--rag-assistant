David Berman, RGD, CGD, FGDC, CPWA is a Canadian communication designer and author who has worked on codes of ethics and standards for designers. He studied at the University of Waterloo from 1975 to 1980.

Publication
Do Good Design with foreword by Erik Spiekermann, AIGA Press/Pearson/Peachpit/New Riders (book, 2009).
