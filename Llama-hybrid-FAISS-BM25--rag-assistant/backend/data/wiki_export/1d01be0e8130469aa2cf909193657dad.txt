Antony and the Johnsons was the debut album by Anohni and the Johnsons, formerly known as Antony and the Johnsons, recorded in 1998 and released officially in 2000.

Release
Originally, the album was released in 2000 on David Tibet's label Durtro. Antony and the Johnsons was reissued by Secretly Canadian in 2004.
