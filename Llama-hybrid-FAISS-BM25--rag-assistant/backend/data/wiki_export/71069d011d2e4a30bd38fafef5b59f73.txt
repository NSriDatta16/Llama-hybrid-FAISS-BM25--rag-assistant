Julian Jenkins (born October 25, 1983) is a former gridiron football defensive end. He was drafted by the Tampa Bay Buccaneers in the fifth round of the 2006 NFL Draft. He attended Woodward Academy in College Park, Georgia, where he was a three-sport athlete in football, basketball and track and field.  Jenkins played both defensive end and defensive tackle in college at Stanford University. His father, Eddie Jenkins, was a former professional football player  for the 1972 Miami Dolphins. Julian Jenkins helped the Calgary Stampeders win the 2008 Grey Cup.
