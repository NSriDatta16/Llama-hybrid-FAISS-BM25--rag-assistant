Çatalpınar is a municipality and district of Ordu Province, Turkey. Its area is 101 km2, and its population is 13,025 (2022). The town lies at an elevation of .

The local economy depends on agriculture, particularly growing hazelnuts and grazing animals. There is a mineral water spring in the village of Elmaköy.
