Unión Balompédica Conquense is a Spanish football team based in Cuenca, in the autonomous community of Castile-La Mancha. Founded in 1946 it currently plays in Segunda División B – Group 3, holding home games at Estadio La Fuensanta, with a 6,000-seat capacity.

History
In the 2018–19 season, the club was relegated to Tercera División by finishing 18th in the Segunda División B, Group 3. Conquense had only 3 away victories and a total of 15 losses (4th worst result in the league) in that season. In June 2019 Nacho Alfonso was appointed head coach of the club. With him the club had a good start of the season, being just 4 points behind the leader after the first 15 matches.
