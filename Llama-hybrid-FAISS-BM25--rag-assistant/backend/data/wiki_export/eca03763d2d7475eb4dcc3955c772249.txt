Hin Hua High School () is a Chinese independent high school in Klang, Selangor, Malaysia. One of the four Chinese independent schools in Selangor, it was founded in 1947 by the joint efforts of parents, teachers and fellow students. The institution has undergone multiple restructuring and reformation activities since 1969.
