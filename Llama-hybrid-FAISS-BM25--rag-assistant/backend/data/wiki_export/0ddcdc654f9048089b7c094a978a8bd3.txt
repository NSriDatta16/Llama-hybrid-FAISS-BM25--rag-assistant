UTC+06:30 is an identifier for a time offset from UTC of +06:30. In ISO 8601 the associated time would be written as . It is 6.5 hours ahead of UTC, meaning that when the time in UTC areas is midnight (00:00), the time in UTC+06:30 areas would be 6:30 in the morning.
