The Communes of Burundi are divided into 2,639 collines. Colline means "hill" in French, one of the three official languages of the country. The collines are listed below, by commune:

Because of climate change, a report of March 28, 2023 states that the country is losing its capital base at an alarming rate, in a 2017 report of Country Environmental Analysis (CEA) the country loses 1.7% of its GDP in land degradation through soil erosion. However, a report of year 2020, states that Burundi loses 5.2% of area due to land degradation. The National Development Plan aims to restore the 16% degraded lands to the former ones, it is also predicted that by 2050 the country's land degradation will increase by 200%, including those 118 collines that have been identified as the country's hotspots.
