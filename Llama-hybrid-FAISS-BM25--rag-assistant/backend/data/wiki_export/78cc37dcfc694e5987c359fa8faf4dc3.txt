"Everytime I Think About Her" is a song by American R&B singer Jaheim, features additional vocals from rapper Jadakiss. Produced by Bink, it was recorded for his third studio album Ghetto Classics (2006). The song contains a sample from "The Sly, Slick, and Wicked" by the Lost Generation and "I Choose You" by Willie Hutch.
