Graswerth is a small, narrow island on the Rhine river near Koblenz, Germany, in the state of Rhineland-Palatinate. The two kilometer long island is uninhabited and can only be reached by row boat. It is a natural reserve, in which many rare species of birds live. The island is crossed by the A48 Autobahn via the Bendorfer Br√ºcke (Bendorfer Bridge).

Graswerth, together with the Island Niederwerth, forms the Municipality of Niederwerth.
