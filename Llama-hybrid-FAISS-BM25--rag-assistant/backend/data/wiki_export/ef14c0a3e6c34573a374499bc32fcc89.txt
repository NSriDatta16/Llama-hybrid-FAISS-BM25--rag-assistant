Raouf Bouzaiene () (born 16 August 1970) is a retired Tunisian footballer. He played as a left-back.

Club career 
Bouzaiene grew up in Laval where he began professional football in 1990. He holds French nationality. With Stade lavallois he reached the semi-final of the French Cup in 1993, against Paris Saint-Germain.

International career
Bouzaiene was part of the Tunisian national team at the 2002 World Cup, scoring Tunisia's goal in the 1–1 draw against Belgium.
