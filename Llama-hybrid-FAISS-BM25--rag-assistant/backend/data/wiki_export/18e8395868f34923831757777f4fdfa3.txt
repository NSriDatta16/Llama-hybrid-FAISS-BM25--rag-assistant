Kamil Ocak Stadium () was a multi-purpose stadium in Gaziantep, Turkey. It was used mostly for football matches and was the home ground of Gaziantepspor until 2017. The stadium hold 16,981 people. It was named after the Turkish politician Mehmet Kamil Ocak.
