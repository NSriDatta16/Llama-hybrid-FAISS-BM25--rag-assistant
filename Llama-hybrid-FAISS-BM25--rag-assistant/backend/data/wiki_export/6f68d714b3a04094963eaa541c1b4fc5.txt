School District 47 Powell River is a school district in British Columbia that is situated on the northern tip of the Sunshine Coast in the qathet Regional District. It provides public education to approximately 3,000 students in six elementary schools (Grades K-7), one secondary school (Grades 8-12), an alternate school and a Distributed Learning Program to support home-learning families called Partners in Education (PIE). 

SD47 has an elected board for five school trustees - Dale Lawson (Chairperson), Jaclyn Miller (Vice-Chairperson), Rob Hill (Trustee), Maureen Mason (Trustee), Kirsten Van't Schip (Trustee).
