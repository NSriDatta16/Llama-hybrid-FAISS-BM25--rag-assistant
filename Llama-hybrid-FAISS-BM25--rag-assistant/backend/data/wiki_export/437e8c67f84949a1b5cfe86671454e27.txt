Cala Sant Vicenç is a small resort town in north-eastern Mallorca, Spain. It consists of three small beaches, hotels, villas and apartments plus a few bars, restaurants and shops catering for tourists. Located at one end of the town is the hypogeum of Cala Sant Vicenç, perhaps the most spectacular hypogeum on the island.
