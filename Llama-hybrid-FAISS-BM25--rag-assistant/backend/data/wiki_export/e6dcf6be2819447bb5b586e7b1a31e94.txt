Fossingfjord is a fjord in Vestfold og Telemark county, Norway. The  long fjord forms part of the border between Kragerø Municipality and Bamble Municipality. The entrance to the fjord lies about  north of the island of Langøy. The small islands of Store Furuholmen and Hellesøy lie just outside the mouth of the fjord.
