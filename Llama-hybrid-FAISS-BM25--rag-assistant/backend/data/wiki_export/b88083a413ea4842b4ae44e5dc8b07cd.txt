Tranquil is an unincorporated community in Monroe County, Mississippi, United States.

Tranquil is located north of Aberdeen on U.S. Route 45.

History
Tranquil is named for the Tranuil Methodist Church, which was in turn named for a church in Newberry District, South Carolina. The church was originally built in 1847 and destroyed by a tornado in 1915. It was again destroyed by a tornado in 1922, rebuilt, and destroyed by another tornado on March 24, 2023.
