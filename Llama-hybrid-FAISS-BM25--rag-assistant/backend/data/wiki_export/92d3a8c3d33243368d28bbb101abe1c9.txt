State Route 332 (SR 332) is a west to east secondary highway in Knox County in the U.S. state of Tennessee.

The route is 14 miles (23Â km) long.  Its western terminus is in Farragut, Tennessee at State Route 1 (Kingston Pike).  Its eastern terminus is in  Knoxville at Papermill Road and Interstate 40/75.  The highway is known as Concord Road from Farragut to Concord and as Northshore Drive from Concord to and through Knoxville.
