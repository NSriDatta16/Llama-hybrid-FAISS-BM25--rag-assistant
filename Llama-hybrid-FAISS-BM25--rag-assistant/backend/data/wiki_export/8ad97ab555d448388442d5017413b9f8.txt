Justin Cooper (born November 17, 1988) is an American former child actor. He appeared in a Domino's Pizza commercial. After two years on the daytime drama General Hospital, he starred in the film Liar Liar and the sitcom Brother's Keeper. He serves as an executive producer of The Ben Maller Show.
