Lusty Beg Island () is an island located in Lower Lough Erne, in County Fermanagh, Northern Ireland, directly adjacent to Boa Island. The island is the location of Lusty Beg Island Resort and Spa, which operates an on-demand ferry service between the island and Boa Island.
