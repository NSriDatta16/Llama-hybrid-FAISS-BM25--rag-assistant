Nurabad (, also romanized as Nūrābād) is a city in the Central District of Delfan County, Lorestan province, Iran, and serves as both capital of the district and of the county. 

At the 2006 National Census, its population was 56,404 in 12,232 households. The following census in 2011 counted 61,142 people in 15,014 households. The latest census in 2016 showed a population of 65,547 people in 18,607 households.
