Formula SAE Australasia (FSAE-A) is the Australian event of the US-based Formula SAE and has been running since 2000 by SAE Australasia. The 2023 event will be held at Winton Motor Raceway, the rural town of Benalla, Victoria, Australia from 7-10 December.
