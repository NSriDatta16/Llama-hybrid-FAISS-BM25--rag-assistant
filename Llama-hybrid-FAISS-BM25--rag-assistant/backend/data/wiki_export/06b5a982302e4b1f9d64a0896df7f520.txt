Shard End Lake is a man-made lake created out of an old quarry, it is managed by Kingfisher Country Park. It is Located in Birmingham, this lake holds a variety of silverfish including roach to 1 lb, perch to 2 lbs, bream and tench to 6 lbs, and carp and pike to 30 lbs.
