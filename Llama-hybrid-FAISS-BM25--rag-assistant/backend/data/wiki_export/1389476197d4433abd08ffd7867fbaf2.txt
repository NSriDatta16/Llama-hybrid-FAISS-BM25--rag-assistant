KPVS and KLUA are commercial radio stations in the island of Hawai ªi, broadcasting to the Hilo and Kailua-Kona areas on 95.9 MHz FM and 93.9 MHz FM, respectively. The two stations simulcast as The Beat, a rhythmic CHR format.

On April 1, 2016, the simulcast adopted its present format, at the time retaining the "Native FM" name.
