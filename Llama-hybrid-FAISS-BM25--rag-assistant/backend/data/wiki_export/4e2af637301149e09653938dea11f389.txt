Tiger Child ( Tora no ko) is an experimental short film directed by Canadian filmmaker Donald Brittain and produced by Roman Kroitor and Ichi Ichikawa. The second film produced by Multiscreen, the precursor of IMAX, premiered at Expo '70 in Osaka, Japan at the Fuji Group Pavilion.
