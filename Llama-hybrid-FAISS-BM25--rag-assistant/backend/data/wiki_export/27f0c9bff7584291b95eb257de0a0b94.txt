Sarwani (Pashto شیروانی) is a Pashtun tribe.

Distribution
They are primarily found in Afghanistan with a significant minority found in Balochistan, Khyber Pakhtunkhwa and Punjab, Pakistan.

Origin
The Sarwani people originate from the city of Sherran or Current Iran-Afghan bordered Regions and from the Ghazni province.
