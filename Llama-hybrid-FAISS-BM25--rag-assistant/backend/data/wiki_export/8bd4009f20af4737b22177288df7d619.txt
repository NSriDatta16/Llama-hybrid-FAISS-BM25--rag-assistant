İmranlı (), is a town in Sivas Province of Turkey. It is the seat of İmranlı District. Its population is 2,823 (2022). The mayor is Özkan Demir (AKP). The town is located at 108 km to Sivas.

Demographics 
The town is populated by Sunni Turks and Kurdish Alevis.
