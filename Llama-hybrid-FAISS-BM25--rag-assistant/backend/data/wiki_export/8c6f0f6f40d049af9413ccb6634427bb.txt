Jon Fugler (born 13 October 1962 in St Austell, Cornwall, England) is an English electronic musician and one of the co-founders of the electronic music group Fluke along with Mike Bryant and Mike Tournier. He first met other members of the band in High Wycombe where he had been involved, along with Tournier, in the band Skin. Both Fugler and Bryant were students at Sir William Borlase's Grammar School in Marlow, Buckinghamshire.
