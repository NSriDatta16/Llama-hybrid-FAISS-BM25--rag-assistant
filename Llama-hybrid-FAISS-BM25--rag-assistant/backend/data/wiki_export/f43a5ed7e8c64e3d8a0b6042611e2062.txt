Tiran and Karvan County () is in Isfahan province, Iran. Its capital is the city of Tiran.

At the 2006 census, the county's population was 64,043 in 17,802 households. The following census in 2011 counted 69,047 people in 21,031 households. At the 2016 census, the county's population was 71,575 in 22,704 households.
