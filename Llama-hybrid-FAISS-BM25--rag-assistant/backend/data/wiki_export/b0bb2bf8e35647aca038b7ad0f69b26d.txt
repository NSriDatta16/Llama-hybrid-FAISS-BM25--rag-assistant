Daniel García Córdova (born October 28, 1971) is a Mexican race walker. He was born in Mexico City, Mexico.

Personal bests
 20 km: 1:18:27 hrs – Poděbrady, 19 April 1997
 50 km: 3:50:05 hrs – Atlanta, Georgia, 2 August 1996
