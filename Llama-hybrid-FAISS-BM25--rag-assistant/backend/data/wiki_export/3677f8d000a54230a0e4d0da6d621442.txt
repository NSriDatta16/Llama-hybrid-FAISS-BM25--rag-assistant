The Festa de Nossa Senhora do Rosário  translates to Festival of Our Lady of Rosário.

Background
It is the main celebration in the Brazilian city of Córrego Danta. Festa do Rosário, a  celebration which involves almost all of the Catholic people in Córrego Danta, starts with a mass in the main church and then a procession from the church to the Church of Our Lady of Rosary. It takes place in the city during the third weekend of August.
