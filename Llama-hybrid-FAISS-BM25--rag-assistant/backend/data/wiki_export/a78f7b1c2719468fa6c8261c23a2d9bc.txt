Paphinia subclausa is a species of orchid endemic to Costa Rica.

Taxonomy 
The classification of this orchid species was published by Robert Louis Dressler in Novon; a Journal for Botanical Nomenclature, 7: 121, fig. 1997 - St. Louis, MO, United States.
