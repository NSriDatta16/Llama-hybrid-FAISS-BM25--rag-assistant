Sir Tupua Leupena  (2 August 1922 â€“ 24 November 1996) was a political figure from the Pacific nation of Tuvalu. Leupena was the Speaker of the Parliament during 1978 in the prime ministership of Toaripi Lauti.

Governor-General of Tuvalu

Leupena served as the second Governor-General of Tuvalu from 1 March 1986 to 1 October 1990, representing  Elizabeth II, Queen of Tuvalu.

In common with most, but not all, of Tuvalu's llGovernors-general, Leupena accepted a knighthood, on 24 July 1986.

Sir Tupua Leupena died on 24 November 1996, aged 74.
