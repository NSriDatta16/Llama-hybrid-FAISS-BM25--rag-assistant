Miodrag Mišo Vlahović () (1924 — 2006) was a politician from Montenegro served as President of the Presidency of the Socialist Republic of Montenegro (within the Socialist Federal Republic of Yugoslavia) from May 1984 to May 1985. He was a member of the League of Communists of Montenegro.
