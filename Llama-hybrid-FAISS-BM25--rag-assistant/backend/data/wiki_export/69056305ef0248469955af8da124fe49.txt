Christopher D. Foote (born December 2, 1956) is a former American football center.

Foote was drafted in the sixth round (144th overall) of the 1980 NFL Draft. He played in the National Football League (NFL) for the Baltimore Colts (1980–1981), the New York Giants (1982–1983), and the Minnesota Vikings (1987 and 1990). Foote also played in the United States Football League (USFL) for the Los Angeles Express (1984) and the Tampa Bay Bandits (1984–1985). Foote played college football at the University of Southern California.
