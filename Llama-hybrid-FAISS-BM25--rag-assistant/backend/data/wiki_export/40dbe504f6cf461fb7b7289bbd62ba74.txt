Gülyalı, formerly Abulhayır, is a municipality and district of Ordu Province, Turkey. Its area is 62 km2, and its population is 8,425 (2022). The town lies at an elevation of .

Ulaş Tepe of the Republican People's Party was elected mayor in the 2019 local elections.
