Městský fotbalový stadion Srbská , currently known as the ADAX Invest Arena for sponsorship purposesis, a multi-purpose stadium in Brno, Czech Republic.  It is currently used mostly for football matches and is the home ground of FC Zbrojovka Brno.  The stadium holds 10,200 people and was built in 1926. The club moved into the stadium in 2001, having previously played at Stadion Za Lužánkami.
