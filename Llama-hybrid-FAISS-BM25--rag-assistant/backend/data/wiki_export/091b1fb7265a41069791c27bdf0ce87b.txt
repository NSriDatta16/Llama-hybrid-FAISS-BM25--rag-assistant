Elhanan is suffering from an incurable disease that causes him to lose his memory slowly, something like amnesia.  Elhanan tells Malkiel the story of his past before he forgets it all.  Malkiel is compelled to go to the village in Romania where his father failed to stop a crime from occurring, a memory that continues to haunt him.  Malkiel encounters the truth about his father and attempts to deal with the past.
