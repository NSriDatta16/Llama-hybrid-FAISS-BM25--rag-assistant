Berryessa station is a light rail station operated by Santa Clara Valley Transportation Authority (VTA). This station is served by VTA's Orange Line.

The station was opened on June 24, 2004, as part of VTA's Capitol light rail extension.

Service

Location 
Berryessa station is located in the median of North Capitol Avenue, just north of Berryessa Road in San Jose, California.
