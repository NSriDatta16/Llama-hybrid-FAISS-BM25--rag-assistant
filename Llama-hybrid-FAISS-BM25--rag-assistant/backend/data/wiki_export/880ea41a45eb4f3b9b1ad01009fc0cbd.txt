Sandvinvatnet is a lake in Ullensvang Municipality in Vestland county, Norway.  The  lake lies on the southern edge of the town of Odda. The Buardalen valley and Buarbreen glacier lie to the west of the lake. The Norwegian National Road 13 runs along the east side of the lake.
