Bińcze railway station is a railway station serving the village of Bińcze, in the Pomeranian Voivodeship, Poland. The station is located on the Chojnice–Runowo Pomorskie railway. The train services are operated by Przewozy Regionalne.

The station used to be called Bärenwalde (Kr. Schlochau).

Train services
The station is served by the following service(s):
Regional services (R) Słupsk — Miastko — Szczecinek — Chojnice
Regional services (R) Szczecinek — Chojnice
