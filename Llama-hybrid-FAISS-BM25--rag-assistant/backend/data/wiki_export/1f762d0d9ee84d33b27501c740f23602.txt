Ian Tarik Khan (born 12 May 1960) is a British racing driver. He took part in the FIA GT and FIA GT3 Championships, as well as the British Touring Car Championship.

Racing career
In 1992 he won the BRDC British Saloon Car Championship. The year after he continued to the British Touring Car Championship in a Vauxhall Cavalier, his most notable performance coming in the season-ending TOCA Shootout at Donington Park where he led for several laps thanks to the reverse-grid format.  In 1994 he competed again, firstly in a Toyota Carina and then his old Vauxhall for Maxted Motorsport.  From 1996 Ian raced in the Ferrari F355 championship and various endurance races. With races in the Renault Clio V6 Trophy between 1999 and 2001, from 2002 he has competed in the FIA GT Championship and the Le Mans 24 Hour.
