Teemore () is a townland in County Fermanagh, Northern Ireland, three miles south of Derrylin. The Erne West F5 region, of which Teemore is the sole settlement, has a population of 528 as of the 2021 census. It is situated within the Fermanagh and Omagh District Council area.

Places of interest 
The official opening of the Shannon-Erne Waterway took place at Corraguil Lock, near Teemore on 23 May 1994.
