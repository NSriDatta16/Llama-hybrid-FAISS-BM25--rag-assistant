Saint David is a county in Trinidad and Tobago which occupies  in the northeastern corner of the island of Trinidad.  The main town in the county is Toco.  It is bounded to the north by the Caribbean Sea, to the east by the Atlantic Ocean, to the south by Saint Andrew County and to the west by Saint George County.  Saint David County consists of a single Ward, Toco, although Tobago was formerly administered as a Ward of Saint David.
