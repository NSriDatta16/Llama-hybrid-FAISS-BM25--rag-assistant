The Muhammad Shahi are a Baloch tribe of Balochistan. They are from the Sarawan branch of the Baloch and reside in the Kalat region aswell as Quetta and Helmand. The tribe is further divided into subsections, Khidrari, Hajizai,Samakzai,Bimkazi,Dodai,Jicko,Dozanzai and Soorozai.
