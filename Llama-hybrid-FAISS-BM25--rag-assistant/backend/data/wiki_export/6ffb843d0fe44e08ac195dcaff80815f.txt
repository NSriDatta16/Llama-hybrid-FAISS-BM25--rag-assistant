Salim ibn Abd Allah (full name Sālim ibn 'Abd Allah ibn 'Umar ibn al-Khaṭṭāb al-'Adawī) was a well known narrator of hadith, many of which he related first hand from his father, Abd Allah ibn Umar. His paternal aunt was Hafsa bint Umar, one of Muhammad's wives.
