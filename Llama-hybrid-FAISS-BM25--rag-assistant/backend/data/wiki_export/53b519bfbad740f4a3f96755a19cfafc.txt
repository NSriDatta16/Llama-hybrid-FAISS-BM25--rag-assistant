Night Island is part of the Great Barrier Reef Marine Park west of Cape Melville, Queensland, Australia. It lies east of Coen between the first three-mile opening and the second three-mile opening of the Barrier Reef about 100Â km south-east of Lockhart River. It is part of the Islands North of Port Stewart Important Bird Area.
The indigenous people of the island were the Kawadji.
