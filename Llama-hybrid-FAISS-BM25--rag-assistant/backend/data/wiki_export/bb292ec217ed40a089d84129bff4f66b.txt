Cashion is a neighborhood that has been annexed into Avondale, Maricopa County, Arizona, United States. The zipcode is: 85329.  As of 2000, it had a population of 2,965. It is bounded in the north by Buckeye Rd., in the south by Durango St., on the west by 115th Ave., and in the east by 107th Ave.
