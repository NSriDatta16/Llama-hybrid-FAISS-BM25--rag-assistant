Yuldong Park () is a park located at Seohyeon-dong, Bundang-gu, Seongnam, South Korea.

Description 
Yuldong Park opened on September 1, 1999.

Yuldong Park has a bungee jumping tower that is  tall. Surrounding the reservoir, the park is well equipped with a promenade, a theater, flower gardens, children's park, badminton center, artificial rock wall, and many spots for a family picnic. A walking path and bicycle path circle the reservoir. There are many nearby restaurants.
