The Magna Græcia University of Catanzaro () is a university located in Catanzaro, Italy. It was founded in 1998 and is organized in three faculties.

Organization
The three faculties are:
 Faculty of Law
 Faculty of Medicine and Surgery
 Faculty of Pharmacy
