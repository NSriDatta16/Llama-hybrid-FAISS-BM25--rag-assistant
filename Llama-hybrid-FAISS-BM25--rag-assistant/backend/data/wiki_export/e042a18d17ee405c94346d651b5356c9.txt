Sural (, also Romanized as Sūrāl; also known as Surhāl) is a village in Sis Rural District, Bolbanabad District, Dehgolan County, Kurdistan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 241, in 56 families. The village is populated by Kurds.
