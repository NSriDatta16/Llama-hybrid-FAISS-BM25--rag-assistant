The Macquarie University Real Tennis Club played real tennis at Macquarie University in Sydney, Australia.  The current university facility is no longer associated with "Real Tennis", but offers Synthetic Grass, Clay and Hard courts. (Three (3) Italian Red Clay, One (1) American Green Clay, Six (6) Hard courts Plexicushion)'Plexicushion' is what the Australian Open uses.
