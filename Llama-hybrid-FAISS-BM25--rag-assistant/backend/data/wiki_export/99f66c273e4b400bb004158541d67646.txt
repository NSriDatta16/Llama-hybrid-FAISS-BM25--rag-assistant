During the rule of his brother Mohammad Shah Qajar (), Firuz Mirza had a prosperous political and military career. This continued under his nephew Naser al-Din Shah Qajar (), who appointed him to multiple governorships and other leading positions. Firuz Mirza was survived by his son Abdol-Hossein Farman Farma.
