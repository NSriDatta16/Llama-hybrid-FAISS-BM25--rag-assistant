Aleksei Ivanovich Bugayev (; born 25 August 1981) is a former association footballer who played defender.

Playing career
Bugayev has played for Tom Tomsk, Torpedo Moscow and Lokomotiv Moscow. In 2004, he was included in the Russia national team and played at Euro 2004.

Achievements
 2005 – Russian Super Cup, winner
 2005 – Russian Premier League, 3rd position

Personal life
On 28 October 2023, Bugayev was arrested in Krasnodar in possession of 495 grams of mephedrone and he is expected to be charged with illegal drug distribution.
