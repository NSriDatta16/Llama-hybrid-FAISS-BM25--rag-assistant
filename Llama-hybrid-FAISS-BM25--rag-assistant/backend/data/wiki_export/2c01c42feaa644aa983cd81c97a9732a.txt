The Clean Harbors 175 is a race in the NASCAR Craftsman Truck Series that takes place at the Milwaukee Mile in Wisconsin. The race was previously held from the series' first season in 1995 until 2009. The race was removed from the Truck Series schedule in 2010 and it returned to the schedule in 2023.

Past winners

2002 & 2004: Race extended due to a green–white–checker finish.
2009: Race postponed from Friday to Saturday due to rain.
