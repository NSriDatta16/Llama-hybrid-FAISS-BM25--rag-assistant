Niederdonven () is a small town in the commune of Flaxweiler, in the canton of Grevenmacher in south-eastern Luxembourg.  The town name refers to the crop Flax which once grew there.  , the town has a population of 305.

The municipality is made up of five villages: Flaxweiler, Gostingen, Oberdonven, Niederdonven and Beyren. The town of Buchholz also belongs to Flaxweiler.
