The Nová Paka Brewery () is a brewery in the town of Nová Paka, in the Czech Republic.

Beer
BrouCzech is a brand name for a range of beers made by Nová Paka.

The list includes:
 Brouček
 Kryštof
 Kumburak
 Granát
 Podkrkonošský speciál
 Podkrkonošský speciál tmavý (dark)
 Valdštejn
 Hemp Valley Beer
 BrouCzech beer
