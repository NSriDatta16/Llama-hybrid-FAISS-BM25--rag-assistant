J.U.F. is a collaboration between Gogol Bordello and Tamir Muskat released in 2004 by Stinky Records. The name—an abbreviation for Jewish Ukrainian Freundschaft—is inspired  by German industrial band Deutsch Amerikanische Freundschaft, who got their name from the GDR state organization Society for German–Soviet Friendship ().
