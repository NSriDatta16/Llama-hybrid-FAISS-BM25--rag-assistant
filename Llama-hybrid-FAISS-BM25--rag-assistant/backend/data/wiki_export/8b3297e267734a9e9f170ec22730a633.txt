Dark Medieval Times is the debut studio album by Norwegian black metal band Satyricon. It was recorded in August and September 1993 and released on October 25, 1993, through Moonfog Productions. On May 2021, Satyricon reissued the album, remastered with different artwork.

Track listing
All songs written by Satyr.
