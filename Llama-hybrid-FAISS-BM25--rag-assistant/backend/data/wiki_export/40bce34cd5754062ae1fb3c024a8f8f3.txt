Glentanar Junior Football Club are a Scottish football club based in Woodside, an area of the city of Aberdeen. Members of the Scottish Junior Football Association since 1998, they currently play in the North Second Division. The club are based at Woodside Sports Complex and their colours are yellow and blue. One of the founding members Alexsander frank Jackson (Jay) is still with the club today as acting president. He is supplemented in the day to day running of the club by Michael Crawford - Community Development, Jordan Gomez-Banderas - Club secretary and development, Campbell Murray- Treasurer

The team are managed since March 2015 by Graham McBeath.
